---
title: "Graft-vs.-Host Disease"
---
Abnormal bowel wall enhancement centrally, expected mucosal location
Corresponds to thin layer of vascular granulation tissue replacing destroyed mucosa
Location
Skin
Liver
Gastrointestinal tract
Size
Diffuse bowel involvement from duodenum to rectum
Morphology
Characteristic thin, central bowel enhancement
Loops of fluid-filled and dilated small and large bowel
Related to diarrhea &amp;/or ileus
Typically only very mild bowel wall thickening
