title: "Over the counter (OTC) drugs"
---
-available without a prescription
-drug interactions to watch for: interactions with other medications you are prescribed, people making other drugs out of them, overuse of OTC medications, masks other symptoms/conditions (having a cough for five weeks and it being lung cancer), look out for labels at the ingredients to make sure youre not giving too much of one medication (acetametaphin in multiple cold meds)
