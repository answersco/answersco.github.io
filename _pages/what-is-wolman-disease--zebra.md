---
title: "What is Wolman Disease (ZEBRA)?"
---
Big bilateral calcified adrenals
&quot;WOLly mammoth adrenals&quot; (fat metabolism error that kills within 6 months)

- vomiting and failure to gain weight in newborn with bilateral chalky adrenal calcs. 
- Genetic deficiency in lysosomal acid lipase --&gt; build up of cholesterol esterase in the organs --&gt; hepatosplenomeg
- symptoms in first few WEEKS of life
