---
title: "Thyroid cancers associated with MEN and XRT"
---
Medullary: associated with MEN II- calcitonin production
-Tendency toward local invasion, LNs, and hematogenous spread
-DOES NOT respond to I-131

Anaplastic: elderly patients, and patients who have had XRT.
Rapid growth, primary lymphatic spread.
DOES NOT respond to I-131.
