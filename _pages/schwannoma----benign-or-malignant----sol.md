---
title: "Schwannoma: - benign or malignant? - solitary or multiple? - classic look: - do they envelope the adjacent nerve root"
---
- benign
- usually solitary
- when multiple can be seen with NF2 or Carney Complex

- &quot;dumbell shape&quot; with skinny handle&quot;
- can be T1 dark, T2 bright with hemorrhage/central necrosis

- do NOT envelope the adjacent nerve root (opposite of neurofibroma)
