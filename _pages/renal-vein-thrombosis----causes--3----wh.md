---
title: "Renal Vein Thrombosis: - causes (3) - what can this mimic? - what is seen on doppler?"
---
Causes:
- dehyrdation
- indwelling umbilical venous catheters (neonates)
- nephrotic syndrome (adults)

- can mimic renal stone (flank pain, enlarged kidney, delayed nephrogram)

- reversed arterial diastolic flow and absent venous flow on doppler
