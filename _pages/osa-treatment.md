---
title: "OSA treatment"
---
1) Weight loss

2) Airway support -- airflow keeps airways open
-CPAP - continuous positive airway pressure
-BiPAP - bilevel positive airway pressure
-can have it with oxygen or without
-noncompliance bc it feels like they are suffocating - use sodium chloride nasal spray to shrink membranes to help breathe easier

3) Dental appliance - repositions jaw to keep airway open

4) Medications: CNS stimulant to decrease excessive daytime sleepiness
-Modafinil (provigil) for narcolepsy 

5) Surgery
-Remove part of soft tissue, soft palate or uvula or tongue

6) Sleep positioning - sleep on side
