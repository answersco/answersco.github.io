---
title: "Findings in ergotism"
---
Excessive use of ergot alkaloids (used for migraines or DVT prophylaxis) results in ergotism with GI, neurologic, and vascular symptoms.
Younger patients than those for atherosclerosis
Diffuse vasospasm (LE &gt; UE) results in symptoms of PAD. Other artery beds may also be affected

Angio: stenoses are seen as long and smooth, and in unusual locations
-Findings of athero usually absent
-Cessation of ergot use may result in complete reversal of the occlusive lesions
