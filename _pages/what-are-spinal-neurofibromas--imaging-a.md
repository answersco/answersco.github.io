---
title: "What are spinal neurofibromas? Imaging appearance? Assoc?"
---
Neoplasm containing Schwann cells, fibroblasts, myxoid material, and peripheral nerve fibers. Extradural/paraspinal
Intradural/extramedullary. &quot;Target&quot; sign on T2WI suggestive but not pathognomonic for NF. If plexiform, pathognomonic for NF. If PET avid then malignant. Assoc with NF1, but 90% of NF are sporadic.
