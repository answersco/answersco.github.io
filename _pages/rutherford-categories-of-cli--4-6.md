---
title: "Rutherford categories of CLI: 4-6"
---
Grade III:
-Category 4: ischemic rest pain
Resting ankle pressure &lt; 60 mmHg. Flat or severely dampened ankle or metatarsal PVR. Toe pressure &lt; 40 mmHg.

-Category 5: minor tissue loss; nonhealing ulcer; focal gangrene with diffuse pedal ischemia.
Resting ankle pressure &lt; 40. Flat or barely pulsatile ankle or MT PVR. Toe pressure &lt; 30.

-Category 6: major tissue loss extending above transmetatarsal level; functional foot unsalvageable.
Objective criteria same as category 5.
