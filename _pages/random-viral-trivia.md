---
title: "Random viral trivia"
---
Measles- multifocal GGO with small nodular opacities
 - complications are higher in pregnant and immunocompromised

Influenza: coalescent LL opacity, rare pleural effusion

SARS: LL predominant GGO

Varicella: Multiple peripheral nodular opacities
 -healed= round calcific lung nodules
 - 1/6 get PNA in kids or immunocompromized

Ebstein Barr: uncommon in the lung- lymphadenopathy and spleenomegaly
