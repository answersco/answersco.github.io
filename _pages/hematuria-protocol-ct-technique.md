title: "Hematuria protocol CT technique:"
---
CT urography: hematuria protocol
Typically includes ~1L water PO and either 250 mL saline IV or 10 mg lasix IV to distend ureters and bladder
Adults &gt; 40 years of age, CTU performed as three-phase exam
Unenhanced CTAP
Nephrographic phase through kidneys (100 seconds after injection)
Excretory phase abdomen and pelvis (15 minutes after injection)
Patients &lt; 40 years: split-bolus, dual-phase exam to &#8595; radiation exposure
Unenhanced CTAP
Combined nephrographic/excretory phase (8 minutes delay after first IV contrast bolus; 100 seconds after 2nd bolus)
