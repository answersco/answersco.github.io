---
title: "Precardiac anterior mediastinal masses:"
---
Precardiac anterior mediastinal masses: in contact with the diaphragm
Epicardial fat pad: silhouettes cardiac border on frontal XR and may simulate cardiomegaly
Pericardial cyst: benign, congenital cystic lesion, most at the right cardiophrenic angle
Imaging shows cystic lesion abutting the pericardium, and may change shape on subsequent studies.
Morgagni hernia: diaphragmatic hernia through the foramen of Morgagni, containing omental fat and often bowel
Usually occurs on the right
Omental vessels can be traced from the mass into the upper abdomen.
