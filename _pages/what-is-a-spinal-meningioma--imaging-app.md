---
title: "What is a spinal meningioma? Imaging appearance? Location? Epi? Association?"
---
A benign WHO grade 1 neoplasm.
Intradural, extramedullary round/ovoid tumor with a broad dural attachment usually dorsal. 
Avid homogenous enhancement. 
Thoracic (80%) &gt; &gt; cervical (16%) &gt; lumbar (4%). 
80% female, 50-60 yo. Assoc with NF2.
