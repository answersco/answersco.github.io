---
title: "Why do proximal obstructions (e.g. duodenal atresia) not result in microcolon (unlike ileal atresia, meconium ileus, or proximal colonic atresia, which all do)?"
---
The remainder of the normally developed small bowel will actually produce enough intraluminal mucus (&quot;succus&quot;) for the colon to develop normally!
