title: "Nissen Fundoplication"
---
Gastric FUNDUS wrapper around the lower esophagus'
 Early complications: obstruction either due to post op edema or wrap made too tight
Failure: (remember these are performed for either hiatal hernia or reflux). Failure defined as recurrence of either of the above. 
1. Most common reason for recurrent reflux = telescoping of the GE junction through the warp. This typically happens in people with a short esophagus (short esophagus = hiatal hernia that is fixed (cant ber educed) and greater than 5cm 
SLIPPED wrap will have a nissen fundoplication length of GREATER THAN TWO centemeters
