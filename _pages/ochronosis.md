---
title: "Ochronosis?"
---
Connective tissue manifestation of alkaptonuria which is a defect in homogentisic acid oxidase, causing homogentisic acid polymers to accumulate in the visceral organs, IV discs, and joints. Clinicially, homogentisic acid in the urine turns black when exposed to air.

Multilevel disc narrowing and dense disc calcification
