---
title: "Enteral tube nursing responsibilities"
---
1) always assess tube placement before use
2) if ordered, maintain suction
-shut off suction for abdominal assessment
-shut off for 30 min after administering meds
-disconnect when needing to ambulate but reconnect as soon as possible
3) maintain patency 
-irrigate with 30-60 ml of warm water
-carbonated beverage if the tube is clogged
-1 TBLS meat tenderizer in 30ml warm water
4) monitor I &amp; O
5) provide nasal and oral care
