---
title: "Pyelonephritis"
---
Acute infection of renal parenchyma; often difficult to distinguish from lower urinary tract infection or cystitis
Classic imaging appearance: Focal swelling and decreased perfusion of affected parenchyma visible on nuclear scintigraphy, sonography, CT, MR, and IVP
Permanent scarring more likely complication in children &lt; 2 years old
