title: "Skin calcifications and method to confirm:"
---
Skin calcifications &#8594; associated with sweat glands
Usually punctate or lucent-centered, most common medially.
A cluster of skin calcifications may project over the breast and appear more suspicious.
If skin calcifications are suspected&#8594; perform tangential view
Calcifications should be imaged using the alphanumeric needle localization grid &#8594; on tangential view, skin calcifications should be seen in the dermis immediately deep to the BB marker.
