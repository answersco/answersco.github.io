---
Tc comes out of generator as Na99mTcO4 (sodium pertechnetate) so reduced by adding SnCl2. 

Checking for FREE Tc with thin layer chromatography.

Limits for Free Tc:
95% for Na99mTcO4
92% for Tc99m sulfur colloid MAA
91% for all other Tc pharmaceuticals
