---
title: "Purpose of dressings"
---
1) protecting a wound from microorganism contamination
2) aiding hemostasis
-pressure dressings stop the bleeding
3) promoting healing by absorbing drainage and debriding a wound
4) supporting or splinting the wound site
5) promoting thermal insulation of the wound surface
6) providing a moist environment 
-different than promoting wound debridment
-would be done for a clean granulating wound
