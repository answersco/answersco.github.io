---
title: "Veins"
---
-thinner walls than arteries
-less smooth muscle
-larger lumen
-not as elastic
-valves to prevent backflow
-acts as a blood reservoir
-CVC in medulla stimulates vasoconstriction when demand increases
-pressure drops toward zero close to heart

-carry blood from the tissues to the heart
-beneath the surface of the skin
-less muscular
-contain valves
