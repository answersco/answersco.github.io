---
title: "Morton's neuroma"
---
Soft-tissue mass shown between 3rd and 4th MT heads is most likely Morton's neuroma
Compression/entrapment of plantar digital nerve by intermetatarsal ligament&#8594; thickening and perineural fibrosis
MRI: Show it on short-axis as T1 dark, dumbbell-shaped scar, usually unilateral in a woman.
