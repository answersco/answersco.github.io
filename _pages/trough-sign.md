Another sign of posterior shoulder dislocation
This looks like two vertical lines (parallel) near the superior emdial aspect of the humeral head from impaction fraction

Compressoin fx of the anteromedial aspect of the humeral head, also known as reverse Hill Sachs, from impaction of the humeral head on the posterior glenoid rim. (Posterior glenoid rim)
