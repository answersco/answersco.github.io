---
title: "What are the five dx criteria for rounded atelectasis?"
---
1) A pleural abnormality (pleural thickening/plaque/effusion) 2) Broad-based consolidation adjacent to the pleural abnormality
3) Volume loss
4) Swirling of bronchovasculature into mass-like consolidation, or the &quot;comet-tail&quot; sign
5) Acute angles with the pleura
