---
title: "Renal Ectopia And Fusion"
---
Isthmus or midline junctional zone of horseshoe kidney may contain functioning renal tissue or fibrotic nonfunctional tissue
Ultrasound typically sufficient to document location and gross morphology of ectopic and fused kidneys
Contrast-enhanced studies (nuclear, IVP, CT, or MR) used to assess drainage in hydronephrosis cases, document nephrolithiasis, and locate position of aberrant renal arteries and veins
