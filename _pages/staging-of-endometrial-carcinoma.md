---
title: "Staging of endometrial carcinoma"
---
Staging: FIGO criteria
Stage I: tumor confined to uterus
IA: &lt;50% myometrial invasion; IB: &gt; 50% invasion
Stage II: spread to the cervical stroma, but tumor still contained within the uterus
Involvement of the endocervical glands only is Stage I
Stage III: spread to adnexa or uterine stroma (IIIA), vagina (IIIB), pelvic lymph nodes (IIIC1), or para-aortic lymph nodes (IIIC2)
Stage IVA: spread to bowel or bladder mucosa
Stage IVB: distant metastases or inguinal lymph node spread
