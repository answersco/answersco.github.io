---
title: "What are the heterotaxy syndromes?"
---
Disturbance of normal left-right asymmetry in position of thoracic and abdominal organs.
Asplenia: Male neonate with severe cyanosis, susceptibility for infections, severe congenital heart disease
Polysplenia: Less severe cardiac disease, often presents later
High 1st year mortality: 85% asplenia, 65% polysplenia
