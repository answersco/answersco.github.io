---
title: "Splenic hamartoma:"
---
Splenic hamartoma: rare, benign lesion composed of malformed red pulp elements, possibly associated with tuberous sclerosis
Typically a well-circumscribed, iso- or hypoattenuating mass on unenhanced CT &#8594; enhances heterogeneously with contrast
MR: iso- to slightly hyperintense on T2, with heterogeneously early enhancement and relatively homogeneous delayed enhancement.
