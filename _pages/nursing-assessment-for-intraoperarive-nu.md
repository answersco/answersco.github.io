---
title: "Nursing assessment for intraoperarive nursing"
---
1) verification of client
2) necessary assessments &amp; documentation, &amp; procedure
3) client comfort
4) OR &quot;time out&quot; - everyone in the OR stops what they are doing and talks with each other about what is going on and everyone's role there - happens immediately before incision
5) all members of the OR team responsible for asepsis protocol
