---
title: "What are the threshold doses and latent periods for the acute radiation syndromes?"
---
Bone marrow: &gt; 2 Gy
-Latent period = 1-6 weeks
-Possible to survive

GI: &gt; 8 Gy
-Latent period = 5-7 days
-Death within 2 weeks

CNS: &gt; 20-50 Gy
-Latent period = 4-6 hours
-Death within 3 days

Total body dose of 0.75-1.25 Gy causes nausea ~30% of the time
