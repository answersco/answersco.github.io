---
title: "What are accepted terms/descriptors for US lesions: - shape - orientation"
---
Shape: (&quot;ROI&quot;)
- Round, Oval, Irregular

Orientation:
- Parallel (wider than tall), Not-parallel (taller than wide)

(descriptors are from good to bad)

Echotexture:
- Anechoic, Hyperechoic, Hypoechoic, Isoechoic, or Complex (cystic/solid)

Posterior Features:
- None, enhancement, shadowing
