title: "Uses for gallium scan"
---
Lung inflammatory diseases (PCP, CMV pneumonitis - not ILD)
Lymphoma
Chronic vertebral osteomyelitis
Cardiac amyloid
Sjogren's
Sarcoid or radiation changes to the parotid or lacrimal gland
Osteomyelitis (young children) and diskitis
Tumors / inflammation
Hepatoma

**Image 48 hrs post injections
Energy: 93, 184, 296, and 394 keV - high and low energy contribute to noise
