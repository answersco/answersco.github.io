---
title: "Is the following a superficial or deep vein?  - basal vein of rosenthal - vein of galen - inferior petrosal vein - superior cerebral veins - superior anastomotic vein (Trolard) - inferior anastomotic vein (labbe) - superficial middle cerebral veins"
---
Rosenthal, Galen, and inferior petrosal are deep veins
- superior and middle cerebral veins, Trolard, and Labbe are superficial

*<b> Labbe is the Large draining vein because it is Lower (gravity) </b>*
