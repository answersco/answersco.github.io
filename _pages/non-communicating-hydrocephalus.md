---
title: "Non-communicating hydrocephalus"
---
-Physical obstruction to CSF flow
1) Aqueductal stenosis
-congenital (web) or acquired (extrinsic mass - tectal glioma, pineal tumor)
2) Intraventricular mass (colloid cyst, meningioma, subependymoma)
3) Outlet obstruction of 4th ventricle
-Dandy Walker
-basilar meningitis, post hemorrhagic ependymitis
