---
title: "Temporal bone fractures and complications"
---
Longitudinal (long-axis of T-bone): more common
More common ossicular dislocation
Less facial nerve damage (~20%)
More conductive hearing loss

Transverse (short-axis of T-bone): less common
More common vascular injury (carotid, jugular)
More facial nerve damage (&gt; 30%)
More sensorineural hearing loss
