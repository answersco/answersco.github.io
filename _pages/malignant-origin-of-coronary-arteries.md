---
title: "Malignant Origin of Coronary arteries"
---
Most common and Malignant:
- LCA arises from R coronary sinus and courses between Aorta and Pulmonary Artery
- can get compressed and cause sudden cardiac death

- Anomalous right off the left cusp - repair if symptomatic
- Anomalous left off the right cusp: always repair
