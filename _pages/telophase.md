---
title: "Telophase"
---
-chromosomes uncoil or de-condense
-spindle disassembles
-nuclear envelope reforms

-chromosomes gather at each pole of cell
-chromatin decondenses
-new nuclear envelope appears at each pole
-new nucleoli appear in each nucleus
-mitotic spindle vanishes

-chromosomes gather at each pole pf cell
-chromatin decondenses
-new nuclear envelope appears at each pole
-new nucleoli appear in each nucleus
-mitotic spindle vanishes

the fourth and final phase of mitosis in which the chromosomes gather at opposite ends of the cell and lose their distinct shapes and two new nuclear envelopes form
