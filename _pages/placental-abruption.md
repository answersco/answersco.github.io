title: "Placental abruption"
---
-premature separation of placenta from myometrium
-causes: hypertension, cocaine
-disruption of retroplacental complex

Separation of placenta from uterine wall. US may be normal ultrasound. Typically ultrasound shows placental hematoma. Acute hematoma maybe isoechoic to placenta. Chronic is hypoechoic.
