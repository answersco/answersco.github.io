---
title: "Types 1 diabetes mellitus (IDDM)"
---
-5% to 10% of cases in US
-insulin is always used to treat type 1 by insulin injections, insulin pump, or dry insulin inhaler and monitoring blood glucose levels and controlled diet
-hereditary susceptibility 
-if susceptible individual is infected with certain viruses (rubella, cytomegalovirus), autoantibodies attack and destroy pancreatic beta cells
