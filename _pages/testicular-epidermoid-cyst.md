---
title: "Testicular epidermoid cyst"
---
Best diagnostic clue: &quot;Target/bull's eye&quot; appearance of an avascular testicular &quot;mass&quot;
Sharply circumscribed encapsulated round &quot;mass&quot;
Appearances vary with the maturation, compactness and amount of keratin present
&quot;Onion-skin&quot; appearance due to alternating hypo- and hyperechoic rings
Hyperechoic fibrous cyst wall &#177; shadowing from calcifications/ossifications
Hypoechoic cyst contents (laminated keratin debris)
Color Doppler: Avascular, no blood flow demonstrable
