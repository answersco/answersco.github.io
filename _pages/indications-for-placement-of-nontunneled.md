---
title: "Indications for placement of nontunneled CVC for exchange therapy (HD or apheresis)"
---
-Mgmt of acute volume overload not responsive to other medical therapy
-Mgmt of severe electrolyte disorders
-Mgmt of acute hyperviscosity disorders
-Short-term apheresis/HD tx
-Stem-cell harvesting
-Active infection or coagulopathy preventing more permanent device

PICCs do not permit exchange therapy or HD due to small lumen size and low flow rates.
