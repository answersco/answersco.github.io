---
title: "Ovarian Tosion: - what is it? - imaging features (5)"
---
Ovarian torsion is the rotation of the ovarian vascular pedicle --&gt; obstruction to venous outflow and arterial inflow

Features:
- MASS ON OVARY
- unilateral ENLARGED ovary (&gt;4cm)
- peripheral cysts
- free fluid
- lack of arterial or venous flow
