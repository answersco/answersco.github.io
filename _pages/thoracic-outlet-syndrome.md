---
title: "Thoracic outlet syndrome"
---
-Costoclavicular space:1st rib, clavicle, scalenus medius muscle, costoclavicular ligament
-Narrowed by cervical ribs, muscles, fascial slips, traumatic changes
-Angio: neutral position and stressed (hyprabduction) -&gt; focal stenosis, poststenotic dilatation, occlusion
-Rx: resection of 1st rib
