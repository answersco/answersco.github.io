---
title: "Nuc Med brachytherapy event?"
---
(a) a total dose to the tx site that deviated from the prescribed dose by more than 20%;
(b) a dose to the tx site from an individual fraction that deviated from the prescribed dose by more than 50%
(c) the wrong mode of treatment;
(d) the wrong patient or research subject;
(e) a leaking sealed source;
(f) a dose to a site or organ other than the treatment site that exceeded by 50 rem that which was expected must be incurred, except for the case of the migration of a permanent implant.
