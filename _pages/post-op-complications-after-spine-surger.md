---
title: "Post-op complications after spine surgery"
---
Recurrent residual disk: lacks enhancement (unlike scar)

Epidural fibrosis: scar, usually posterior, and enhances homogeneously

Arachnoiditis: &quot;clumped nerve roots,&quot; &quot;empty thecal sac&quot;
Enhancement up to 6 weeks post-op is considered normal
After 6 weeks, may be inflammatory or infectious
