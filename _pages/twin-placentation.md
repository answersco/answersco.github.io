---
title: "Twin placentation"
---
Monochorionic monoamniotic
-monozygotic
-split after 8 days
-no intertwin membrane
-rare

Monochorionic diamniotic
-monozygotic
-split &gt;4-8 days
-T-line

Dichorionic diamniotic
-monozygotic or dizygotic
-split 0-4 days
-twin peak sign

DIZYGOTIC can be different sexes
