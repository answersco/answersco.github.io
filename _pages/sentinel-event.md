Defined by joint commission as a skin dose &gt; 1500 rad (15 Gy) to any field during a CV or IR procedure. Must convene a root-cause analysis.

Sentinel Event
According to the JC, &quot;a sentinel event is an unexpected occurrence involving death or serious physical or psychological injury, or the risk thereof.&quot;
Serious injury specifically includes loss of limb or function.
