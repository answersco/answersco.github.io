---
title: "What are the MR appearances of focal cortical dysplasia?"
---
Abnormal cortical gyral pattern (broad gyri and large, irregular sulci). ddx Tuberous Sclerosis and Schizencephaly.

Blurring of gray-white junction (looks like partial volume)
Marked T2 prolongation of subjacent white matter which may taper towards the ventricle.
T1 shortening often seen in neonates and young infants.
