---
title: "What are the findings in asplenia syndrome?"
---
Asplenia syndrome = double right-sidedness (right sided isomerism)
Absence of spleen
IVC and aorta on same side
Bilateral SVC
+/- dextrocardia.
Bilateral tri-lobed lungs, and eparterial bronchi
High assoc with severe cyanotic CHD (AVSD, TGA, DORV, common AV valve, Pulm stenosis/atresia)
Type 3 TAPVR (&gt; 80%)
