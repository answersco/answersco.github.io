---
title: "PNA: klebsiella, H flu, legionella"
---
Klebsiella: &quot;bulging fissure&quot;
More common pleural effusions, empyema, and cavities than conventional PNA.
Alcoholic and nursing home patients

H flu: COPD and asplenic patients
Usually bronchitis
Sometimes bilateral lower lobe bronchoPNA (patchy opacities)

Legionella: COPDers and with bad air conditioners
Peripheral and sublobar airspace opacities
