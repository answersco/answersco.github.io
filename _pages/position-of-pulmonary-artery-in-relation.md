---
title: "Position of pulmonary artery in relation to aorta in D- and L-TGA"
---
NORMALLY: pulmonary artery should be to the LEFT OF AND ANTERIOR to the Aorta

* L-transposition = aorta is left and anterior of the pulmonary artery (flipped)

* D-transpotion = Pulmonary artery is left and POSTERIOR to aorta
