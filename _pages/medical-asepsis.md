---
title: "Medical asepsis"
---
clean technique: reduce or prevent the spread of microorganisms
EX. giving a bath, brushing someone's teeth

practice of techniques and procedures to:
-decrease # of organisms in an area
-decrease their likelihood of spreading
-protect ourselves, our families, &amp; clients
-if we do not do this, we see a risk of increase of pathogens which will delay or stop the pt's healing process
