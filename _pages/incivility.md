---
title: "Incivility"
---
-Any type of speech or behavior that disrupts the harmony of the home, work, or educational environment
-Other names in health care: Nurses eating their young, The doctor-nurse game, and Aggressive communication
-Pervasive in American society
-Increases when people are under stress.
-Can be viewed as a continuum of impolite behaviors. 
-They have a lot of overlap between them.
-All of the individual stages usually begin with some type of covert, subtle type of psychological behavior, however, they all can lead to physical violence if taken to their extremes.
