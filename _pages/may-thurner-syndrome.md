---
title: "May Thurner syndrome"
---
Compression of left common iliac vein by right common iliac artery
DVT of left common iliac vein

May-Thurner syndrome: venous thrombosis of the left common iliac vein caused by compression from the crossing right common iliac artery.
Treatment: endovascular thrombolysis followed by stenting.

-AKA \\\&quot;iliac compression syndrome\\\&quot; or \\\&quot;pelvic venous spur\\\&quot;
-left common iliac vein obstruction -&gt; reduction of blood flow and freqently DVT
-intraluminal spurs
-70-87% female
-approx 40 yo
