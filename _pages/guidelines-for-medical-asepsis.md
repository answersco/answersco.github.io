---
title: "Guidelines for medical asepsis"
---
-use high quality gloves when exposed to blood and bodily fluids
-choose the right glove for the job
-never re-use gloves
-use non-permeable gowns or aprons
-use appropriate fitting masks that fit snugly over nose and mouth (decrease talking to decrease moisture bc when mask is moist you need to discard it)
-wear protective eyewear for splattering
-keep nails clean and short (no artificial nails or chipped nail polish)
-do not wear jewelry that may tear gloves or harbor organisms
