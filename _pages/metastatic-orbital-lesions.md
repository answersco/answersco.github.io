---
title: "Metastatic orbital lesions"
---
Metastatic neuroblastoma: &quot;raccoon eyes&quot; on exam
Periorbital tumor infiltration with associated proptosis

Metastatic breast cancer: no proptosis
Instead, desmoplastic reaction and enophthalmos

Lymphoma: usually upper-outer orbit associated with lacrimal gland
Enhances homogeneously and restricts diffusion
