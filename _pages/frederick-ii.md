---
title: "Frederick II"
---
(r. 1740-1786) King of Prussia during the War of the Austrian Succession and the Seven Years' War

&quot;Frederick The Great&quot;-1712-1786;King of Prussia, aggressive in foreign affairs. Used military to increase power. Encouraged religious tolerance and legal reform and improved education. He reduced censorship and won praise for his &quot;enlightened&quot; attitude toward being king. He called himself &quot;the first servant of the state.&quot;
