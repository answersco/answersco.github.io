---
title: "PE symptoms"
---
Symptoms depend on size of clot and area affected:

1) Dyspnea

2) Chest pain

3) Sudden sharp stabbing / pleuritic pain

4) Anxiety

5) Fear 

6) Apprehension

7) Diaphoresis

8) Hemoptysis: sometimes is it pink and frothy

9) Syncope from lack of O2 

10) Tachypnea

11) Sudden death
