title: "Nervous system functions"
---
1) initiation/coordination of movement which is tied to musculoskeletal assessment
2) reception/perception of sensory stimuli: anything taken in from eyes, ears, mouth, nose, and touch and processes it (EX. If you touch something hot, your brain knows it's a bad thing and you quickly remove your hand)
3) organization of thought processes: ability to make sounds judgement
4) control of speech: controlled by cerebral cortex
5) storage of memory and whether they go into short-term or long-term memory
