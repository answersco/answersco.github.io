---
title: "Findings of impending aortic aneurysm rupture"
---
Draped aorta sign: indistinctness of posterior aortic wall from adjacent structures- drapes over vertebral column, and may indicate contained leak.

10 mm or more increase in size in a year

Focal discontinuity in circumferential wall calcifications

Hyperdense crescent sign: well-defined peripheral crescent of increased attenuation. Specific sign.
