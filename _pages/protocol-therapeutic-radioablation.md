---
title: "Protocol therapeutic radioablation"
---
*pregnancy test within 80 hrs - crosses placenta; cretinism
-stop breast feeding days before to lower breast dose; no breast feeding after (milk is hot for 80 days)
-Dismissed when dosimetry &lt; 7mRad/hr at 1 meter
-Image before leaving hospital to look for other mets
-need to be hypothyroid with elevated TSH &gt; 25 - helps transport iodine into thyroid

Complications: Bone marrow depression; Sterility if pelvic mets, leukemia, transient thyrotoxicosis (1-2 % of Graves; release of preformed thyroid hormone)
* wait 6 weeks if received iodinated contrast
