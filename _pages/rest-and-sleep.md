---
title: "Rest and sleep"
---
-stage 1 NREM is longer - takes them longer to fall asleep
-stage 3 deep sleep and REM sleep is decreased - do not feel rested
-need at least 7- 8 hours
-fragmentation of sleep - something is interrupting their sleep so naps are encouraged
-change in circadian rhythm - fall asleep sooner and wake up sooner (early morning is still dark)

-need between 8 - 9 hours sleep each night.
-getting sleep helps prevent illness
-good sleep is associated with good health.
