---
title: "Failure mode effects analysis (FMEA) vs Root cause analysis (RCA)?"
---
FMEA = Mechanism to PROSPECTIVELY identify error in a process
- very Subjective
- Takes into account &quot;SOD&quot; Subjective severity, Objective # of how often these occur, and Detection efficiency for these processes

RCA = RETROSPECTIVELY identify error in a process (for both active and latent errors)
