---
title: "Serfs"
---
In medieval Europe, an agricultural laborer legally bound to a lord's property and obligated to perform set services for the lord.

People who gave their land to a lord and offered their servitude in return for protection from the lord.

In medieval Europe, an agricultural laborer legally bound to a lord's property and obligated to perform set services for the lord. In Russia some serfs worked as artisans and in factories; serfdom was not abolished there until 1861. (pp. 254, 553)
