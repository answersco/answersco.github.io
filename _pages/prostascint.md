---
title: "Prostascint"
---
In-111 labeled to antibody capromab pendetide (Prostascint), which recognizes PSA antigen.

Performed in setting of rising PSA and negative bone scan, looking for mets outside the prostate bed (soft-tissue mets).
If they don't have distal mets, they can be offered salvage XRT to surgical bed.
Will localize to soft tissue mets, NOT BONE METS

Liver, marrow, bowel, blood pool, prostate +/- spleen
