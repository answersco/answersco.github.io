---
title: "What are genetic risk factors for breast ca?"
---
BRCA1 = Chromosome 17; breast, ovary, GI cancers
BRCA2 = Chromosome 13; males have higher risk with BRCA2

Li Fraumeni - no p53

Cowden = Breast Ca, Follicular thyroid, endometrial cancer, and Lhermitte Duclos (brain hamartoma)

Bannayan Riley Ruvalcava = assoc with developmental disorders at young age

NF-1 actually has moderate risk of breast ca
