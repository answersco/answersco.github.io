---
title: "What are concerning features for H/N lymph nodes including sizes? Retropharyngeal vs submandibular/cervical."
---
The upper limit normal size for retropharyngeal nodes is relatively small, around 6-8 mm, compared with 10-15 mm for submandibular and deep cervical lymph nodes

Necrosis within a node is usually a sign of pathology, even if the node is not abnormally enlarged.

Loss of the fatty hilum is concerning.
