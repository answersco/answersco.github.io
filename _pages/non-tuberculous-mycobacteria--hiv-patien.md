title: "Non-tuberculous mycobacteria: HIV patients, and HST pneumonitis"
---
HIV: in CD4 &lt; 100
GI infection disseminated in the blood
Hepatosplenomegaly
Often mixed with other pulmonary infections (e.g. PCP)- lungs can look like anything
Most common manifestation is mediastinal LAD

HST Pneumonitis: &quot;hot-tub lung&quot;
Aerosolized bugs- ill-defined, centrilobular ground-glass nodules.
