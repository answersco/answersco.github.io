---
title: "Posterior tibial or dorsalis pedis pulses that are normal, but decrease with plantar- or dorsiflexion"
---
Popliteal entrapment: symptomatic compression or occlusion of the popliteal artery by the medial head of the gastrocnemius of popliteus.

May see medial deviation of the artery.
Usually men &lt; 30
MRA or angiogram in rest and stress
