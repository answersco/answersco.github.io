title: "Tubular carcinoma?"
---
Low grade cancer presents as a small spiculated mass. Prognosis is better than IDC NOS.

Clinical: 
Radial scar may be a precursor.
Better prognosis than IDC-NOS
Imaging&#8594; slow-growing, typically presenting as a small, spiculated mass
Often stable across several studies.

This typically is going to be a small calcified mass
Going to have the best prognosis of any breast mass
