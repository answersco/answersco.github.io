---
title: "Neuroblastoma: Epi, origin, location, Ddx."
---
Epi: 3rd most common peds CA and most common extra-cranial solid tumor in peds, most common CA in infancy.
Origin: Primitive neural crest cells. 
Locations: Adrenal medulla (35%), Retroperitoneum (30-35%), Posterior mediastinum (20%).
Ddx: Wilms tumor, adrenal hemorrhage, other adrenal tumors (pheo, adrenocortical CA)
Spectrum of dz including ganglioneuroma, ganglioneuroblastoma and neuroblastoma.
