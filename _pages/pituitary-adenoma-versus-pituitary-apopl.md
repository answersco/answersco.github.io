---
title: "Pituitary adenoma versus pituitary apoplexy"
---
Pituitary adenoma: &gt; 1cm = macroadenoma
80% T1 dark and T2 bright
Enhance less avidly than normal parenchyma

Pituitary apoplexy: hemorrhage or infarction of pituitary, usually into enlarged gland (either pregnancy or macroadenoma)
Bromocriptine (or other prolactin drugs)
Sheehan syndrome in pregnant women
T1 bright (adenoma usually T1 dark)
Emergent due to potential hypotension
