---
title: "Findings (and signs) and lab abnormalities in sarcoid"
---
Classically black woman in 20s-30s
Non-caseating granulomas
Elevated ACE, hypercalcemia

1-2-3 sign: bilateral hilar and right paratracheal nodes
Lambda sign: same as 1-2-3, but on gallium scan.

Perilymphatic nodules with upper lobe predominance
Late changes: upper lobe fibrosis, traction bronchiectasis (honeycombing is rare), and aspergillomas.
CT galaxy sign: upper lobe masses (conglomerate of nodules) with satellite nodules.
