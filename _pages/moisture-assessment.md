---
title: "Moisture assessment"
---
-use back of hands to palpate for hydration and texture
1) hydration: oiliness and dryness
2) diaphoresis
3) flaking, crusting, or scaling: more common in elderly and those who overuse soap
4) dryness: seen in those who are dehydrated, smoke, stressed, and sun exposed
5) excessive dryness: eczema and dermatitis
