---
title: "Microraptor gui"
---
found in 1991 with fully feathered wing-like forelimbs, feathered hind limbs, and a long, feather-fringed tail; only one of Liaoning feathered theropods with feathers on its back leg; type 5 feathers that were capable of gliding among trees, possibly representing the intermediate stage between flightless non-avian theropods and birds
