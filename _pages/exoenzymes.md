---
title: "Exoenzymes"
---
-chemical secreted purposely by the microorganism
-degradative enzymes secreted by many types of bacteria
-used by microbes to gain better access to host &amp; gain nutrients
-many types (target connective tissue): 
1) lipases (fats)
2) collagenases (collagen) 
3) elastases (elastic fibers: in lungs)
4) hyaluronidase (hyaluronic acid: found in cell cement that glues cells together gaining better access down between and under cells - invasiveness)
