---
title: "What are the recs for holding meds or correcting anticoagulation prior to Low risk IR procedures?"
---
INR goal of 2.0: threshold for treatment (ie, FFP, vitamin K)
aPTT: correct heparin if &gt;1.5X control
Platelets: trfx for &lt;50k
Clopidogrel: hold 5 d before procedure
Aspirin: do not withhold
LMWH (therapeutic dose): withhold one dose before procedure.

INR: correct to 1.5
aPTT: correct heparin if &gt;1.5X control
Platelets: trfx for &lt;50k
Clopidogrel: hold for 5 d before procedure
Aspirin: do not withhold
LMWH (therapeutic dose): withhold one dose before procedure

INR: correct to 1.5
aPTT: correct heparin if &gt;1.5X control
Platelets: trfx for &lt;50k
Clopidogrel: withhold for 5 d before procedure
Aspirin: withhold for 5 days
LMWH: withhold for 24 h or up to two doses
