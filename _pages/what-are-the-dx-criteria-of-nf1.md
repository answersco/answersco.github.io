---
title: "What are the dx criteria of NF1?"
---
At least two of the following 7:
6 cafe au lait spots 5mm wide &lt;10yo or 15mm wide &gt;10yo.
Axillary or inguinal freckles
2 typical neurofibromas or 1 plexiform neurofibroma
Optic nerve glioma
2 iris hamartomas (Lisch nodules)
Sphenoid dysplasia or typical long-bone abnormalities such as pseudarthrosis
First degree relative with NF type 1
