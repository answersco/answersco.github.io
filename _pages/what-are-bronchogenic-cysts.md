---
title: "What are bronchogenic cysts?"
---
Bronchogenic cysts are a blind-ending, fluid-filled pouch or cyst, lined with ciliated epithelium, smooth muscle and cartilage found in the MEDIASTINUM in around 70-80% of cases. Slight male predominance. Usually no communication to bronchial tree. Sx may include mass effect with airway compression, recurrent infection. Rare malignant transformation. Tx is surgical removal at dx.
