---
title: "Risk factors for altered family health"
---
1) lifestyle risk factors
-lack of knowledge about nutrition
-living near factories etc.
2) psychosocial risk factors
-find out who they can talk to in the family
3) environmental risk factors
-agencies that can help them and they are not alone
4) developmental risk factors
-families with new babies, especially if family is not involved
-older adults living alone
5) biologic risks
-families with children that have birth defects or disabilities
