---
title: "Schizencephaly: associations and mimic"
---
Schizencephaly: grey-matter lined cleft that will extend through entire hemisphere

Associations:
Optic nerve hypoplasia (30%)
Absent septum pellucidum (70%)
Epilepsy (50-80%)

Unlike porencephalic cyst, open-lip schizencephaly is lined by gray matter.
