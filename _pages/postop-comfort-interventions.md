---
title: "Postop comfort interventions"
---
1) narcotics (opioids)/non-narcotic pain medications (analgesics)
-pain medications can be ordered PRN, continuously, or scheduled
-teaching/educating the patient about identifying pain and reporting it before it becomes unbearable are important 
2) PCA (Patient controlled analgesia)
3) back rubs
4) imagery
5) distraction
-nonpharmacological pain relief includes positioning, massage, distraction, emotional support
