---
title: "Treatment of facial or laryngeal edema:"
---
Give O2 6-10 liters/min via mask
Alpha agonist epinephrine SC or IM (1:1000) 0.1-0.3 ml (=0.1-0.3 mg) or, especially if hypotension evident, epinephrine (1:10,000) slowly IV 1-3 ml (=0.1-0.3 mg), repeating as needed up to maximum of 1 mg.
Call cardiopulmonary arrest response team if no response to therapy or obvious acute laryngeal edema.
