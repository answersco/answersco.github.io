---
title: "What is the SI unit for effective dose?"
---
Effective dose means application of tissue weighting factor based on organ exposure. It represents the stochastic health risk, which is the probability of cancer induction and genetic effects of ionizing radiation delivered to those body parts.[1][2] It takes into account the type of radiation and the nature of each organ or tissue being irradiated.Unit is also Sv or J/kg. The US unit is rem.
