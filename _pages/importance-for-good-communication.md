---
title: "Importance for good communication"
---
-the nursing profession recognizes communication as one of the cornerstones of its practice
-nurses must be able to communicate with clients, family members, physicians, peers, and associates in an effective and constructive manner to achieve their goals of high-quality care
-good communication is essential for good leadership and management
-without it, people would be unable to learn, direct their lives, or work together cooperatively.
