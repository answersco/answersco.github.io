---
title: "Peds pineal region tumors"
---
Germinoma: most common, almost exclusively in boys (suprasellar usually girls)
May have precocious puberty (hCG secretion)
Mass with fat and calcification, variable enhancement
Heterogeneous on T1 and T2
Engulfed calcification
May have CSF seeding or brain invasion.

Pineocytoma: rare in childhood
Well-circumscribed, non-invasive
Typically more solid, with enhancement of solid components
Exploded calcification

Pineoblastoma: occurs in childhood
Highly invasive
Associated with retinoblastoma
Heterogeneous with avid enhancement
Exploded calcification
