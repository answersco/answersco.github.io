---
title: "What is hepatoblastoma? Imaging findings? Associations?"
---
Malignant embryonic hepatic tumor composed of epithelial cells and occasionally a mixture of epithelial and mesenchymal cells. R&gt; L lobe. Large heterogenous mass. Elevated AFP. Metastasizes almost exclusively to lung. Most common primary liver tumor of childhood. 3rd most common abdominal malignancy after neuroblastoma and Wilms tumor.
