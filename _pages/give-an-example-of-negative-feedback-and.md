---
title: "Give an example of negative feedback and describe what is happening."
---
-body sense a change and activates mechanisms to negate or reverse it
EX. 1) Person rises from bed
2) Blood drains from upper body; creating homeostatic imbalance
3) Baroreceptors above heart respond to drop in blood pressure
4) Baroreceptors send signals to cardiac center of brainstem
5) Cardiac center accelerates heartbeat
6) Blood pressure rises to normal; homeostasis is restored
