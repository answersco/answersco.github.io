---
title: "General anesthesia"
---
-administration of drugs by inhalation or intravenous route
-entire body is affected by anesthesia
-diminishes reflexes and promotes relaxation
-puts you in a sleep-like state bc it suppresses the CNS
-administered by anesthesiologist or nurse anesthetist that requires very close and attentive medical focus including emergence, maintenance, and induction
-has the most side effects: severe N/V, neuro side effects bc its affecting CNS
