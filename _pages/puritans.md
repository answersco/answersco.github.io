---
title: "Puritans"
---
A religious group who wanted to purify the Church of England along Calvinist lines eliminating bishops, promoting sermons, policing people's behavior, and supporting Protestant states and interests in foreign policy

English Protestant dissenters who believed that God predestined souls to heaven or hell before birth. They founded Massachusetts Bay Colony in 1629.
