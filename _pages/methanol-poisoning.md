---
title: "Methanol poisoning"
---
Similar imaging features to carbon monoxide

- Atrophy of the optic nerves
- Hemorrhage and necrosis within the putamen and increased T2 signal (not always going to have the hemorrhage and necrosis component)
- Looks like there is slightly more involvement of the putamen

Hemorrhagic bilateral putaminal necrosis and optic nerve atrophy (this is what makes you blind)
