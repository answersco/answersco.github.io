---
title: "Name the 3 joint spaces in the knee. Which can be affected in RA? OA?"
---
medial tibiofemoral joint 
lateral tibiofemoral joint
patellofemoral joint

All 3 may be affected in RA
All 3 may be affected in OA

**however, OA tends to affected the medial tibiofemoral articulation first, before it affects the others
