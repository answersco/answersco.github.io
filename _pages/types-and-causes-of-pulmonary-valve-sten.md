---
title: "Types and Causes of pulmonary valve stenosis"
---
Supravalvular: 
- Williams syndrome (aortic and pulmonary stenosis)

Valvular: 
- most common: can lead to RV hypertrophy.
- Noonan syndrome (male version of Turner's) 

Subvalvular: 
- Tetralogy of fallot

Peripheral pulmonary stenosis: 
- seen with Alagille syndrome (kids with absent bile ducts)
