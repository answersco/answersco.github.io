---
title: "Findings in rotator cuff tears"
---
Rotator cuff tears: tear of articular surface 3X more common than bursal surface
Most commonly supraspinatus; least commonly teres minor
Partial tear &gt; 50% is what surgeon wants to know
Full-thickness: high-T2 signal in the expected location of the tendon, with Gad/fluid in the bursa, crossing over the cuff.
