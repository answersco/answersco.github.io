---
title: "Ureteropelvic Junction Obstruction"
---
Classic imaging appearance: Marked hydronephrosis that ends abruptly at ureteropelvic junction with normal caliber ureter downstream
Disproportionate enlargement of renal pelvis and variable drainage of collecting system
Morphology
UPJO has been likened to Hirschsprung disease of the ureter, with focal transition zone and aperistaltic segment
Tc-99m MAG3 diuretic renal scintigraphy show minimal function in this patient's massively dilated right kidney . The time activity curve for the right kidney shows progressive accumulation of counts , and no washout over the course of the entire exam, confirming UPJO.
