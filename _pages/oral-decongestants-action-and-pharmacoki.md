---
title: "Oral decongestants action and pharmacokinetics"
---
-taken orally to decrease nasal congestion related to the common cold, sinusitis, and allergic rhinitis
-usually used when more congestion than just in nose 
-takes 20-40 minutes to work 
-shrink the nasal mucous membrane by stimulating the alpha adrenergic receptors
-well absorbed, metabolized in the liver, excreted in the urine
