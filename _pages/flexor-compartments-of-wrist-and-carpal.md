---
title: "Flexor compartments of wrist and carpal tunnel"
---
Flexor compartment: includes carpal tunnel
10 total components of carpal tunnel
4 FDP tendons
4 FDS tendons
1 FPL tendon (flexor pollicis brevis is not in tunnel)
1 median nerve
Flexor carpi radialis is outside of the tunnel
Tunnel lies deep to the palmaris longus
