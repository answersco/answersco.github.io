---
title: "Tolerance"
---
-develops when a dose of opioid becomes less effective on repeated administration
-is not addiction
-involves physiologic changes related to drug metabolism and nervous system's adaptation to the medication
-need for increasing drug dosage may also indicate disease progression and new pathology

-decreased response to a medication
-body requires more and more of the same medication to achieve the therapeutic response
EX. Seizure, Parkinson's, migraine
