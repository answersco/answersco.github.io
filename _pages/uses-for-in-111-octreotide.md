title: "Uses for In-111 octreotide"
---
Locating neuroendocrine tumors - carcinoid, islet cell,
insulinoma, gastrinoma, motulinoma, pheochromocytoma, small
cell lung ca, medullary thyroid, paraganglioma, neuroblastoma,
glucagonoma, pituitary adenoma, meningioma, VIPoma

*many tumors express somatostatin receptors
**should never see bone: if you do --&gt; diffuse mets
