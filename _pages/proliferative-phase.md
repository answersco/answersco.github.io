title: "Proliferative phase"
---
-3-24 days
-wound fills in with granulation tissue
-collagen mixes with granulation tissue and supports re-epithelialization (new, red, fragile skin tissue, bleeds very easily, isotonic solutions are safe for granulation tissue)

-days 1 through 5
-build up of endometrial lining
-E2 stimulated mitosis in the uterine lining so cells keep building up and thickening the lining
-E2 tells cells &quot;express P receptors&quot; aka &quot;priming&quot;
