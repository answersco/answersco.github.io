---
title: "Excretion"
---
-elimination of the drug
1) kidneys-main way a medication is eliminated
-creatinine clearance (BUN or creatinine)-need to monitor to avoid drug toxicity 
-drugs must be water soluble to be excreted thru the kidneys
2) other ways to eliminate a drug:
-liver - bile
-GI tract -feces
-breast milk
-skin
-lungs
