---
title: "Sacroiliitis in seronegative spondyloarthropathies:"
---
Similar to OA, only the inferior aspect of the SI joint is affected in these because only the inferior aspect of the joint is a synovial joint.
Erosions first affect the iliac aspect of the joint.
Symmetric sacroiliitis&#8594; caused by IBD and AS (both start with vowels)
IBD sacroiliitis is associated with ulcerative colitis, Crohn's, Whipple disease, and after gastric-bypass.
Asymmetric sacroiliitis&#8594; caused by psoriatic arthritis and reactive arthropathy.
