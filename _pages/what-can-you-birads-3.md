---
title: "What can you BIRADS 3?"
---
- Findings consistent w/ fibroadenoma (e.g. circumscribed, noncalcified, oval or round mass)
- Focal asymmetry that looks like breast tissue (becomes less dense on compression)
- Grouped or clustered ROUND/punctate calcifications
* MRI: solitary focus (dot &lt; 4 mm) of enhancement with persistent kinetics on a baseline exam
