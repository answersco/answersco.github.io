---
title: "Interstitial lung disease (basics)"
---
1. Ideopathic interstitial pneumonia is NOT a disease entity (or diagnosis), this is a PATTERN of lung injury that has many difference causes
2. You are trying to figure out is this UIP or is this NON-UIP (each of which has different causes)
3. UIP tends not to get better with steroids
4. NON-UIP will often get better with steroids
