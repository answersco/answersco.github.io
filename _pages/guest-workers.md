---
title: "Guest workers"
---
Due to vast job openings throughout Europe, especially in the trades, many colonial migrants immigrated to Europe after WWII. During the &quot;Economic Miracle&quot; few tensions emerged as there was plenty of money and work for everyone. However, as many guest workers stayed permanently the ethnic and population tensions begin.

Workers who migrate to the more developed countries of North and West Europe, usually from South and East Europe or North Africa, in search of higher-paying jobs
