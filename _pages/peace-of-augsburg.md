---
title: "Peace of Augsburg"
---
1555 agreement declaring that the religion (Catholic or Protestant) of each German state would be decided by its ruler

temporary settlement of the religious wars within the Holy Roman Empire in 1555 that allowed the ruler of the state to determine which church the people of that state would follow -- Catholicism or Lutheranism; it established the precedent &quot;cuius regio, eius religio&quot;; it was overturned by the Thirty Years' War
