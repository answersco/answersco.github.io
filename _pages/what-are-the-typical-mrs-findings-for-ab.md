---
title: "What are the typical MRS findings for abscess and how to differentiate from neoplasm?"
---
Typical of abscess: LACTATE
Cytosolic amino acids at 0.9-1.0 ppm, Acetate at 2.0, Succs at 2.6
Are typically seen in abscess but not neoplasm.
Neoplasm typically has elevated Choline at 3.2 ppm and low NAA
