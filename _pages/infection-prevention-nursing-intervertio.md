---
title: "Infection prevention/nursing intervertions"
---
1) Hand hygiene 
-Hand Washing, Gloves
-Artificial Nails -- 16 neonatal deaths, (CDC,2000) attributed to one nurse and artificial nails

2) Sterility of access/equipment
-Have all equipment ready and make sure dressing is secured

3) Know agency policy

4) Do not use expired solution, tubing, fluids, add-mixtures
-If expired then equipment is not considered sterile
