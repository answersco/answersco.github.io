title: "Tc-sulfur colloid and WBC bone imaging"
---
Infected marrow should become photopenic on Tc-sulfur colloid, but this takes about a week after onset of infection.
In-WBC will accumulate in infection

Combined Tc-SC and WBC study is considered positive for infection if there is activity on WBC, without corresponding Tc activity on the BM image.

Gallium better than WBC for OM of spine.
