---
title: "Radiologic tests"
---
1) KUB: below diaphragm X-ray - kidney, urinary, and bladder
2) IVP: x-ray after injecting dye through IV veins and watching circulation through kidneys of that dye
3) Cystoscopy: visualization of insides lining of bladder
4) Urodynamic Studies: any procedures that examine the structure and function especial of GU tract
