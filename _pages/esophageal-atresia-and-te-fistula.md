---
title: "Esophageal Atresia and TE Fistula"
---
Intermittently air-distended, dilated upper esophageal pouch
Enteric tube tip near thoracic inlet in pouch
EA with TEF: Gas in stomach + bowel
EA without TEF: No gas in stomach or bowel
Displaced, bowed, narrowed trachea on lateral view due to distended esophageal pouch
Signs of other congenital anomalies
&#8593; cardiac size, abnormal pulmonary vascularity, vertebral anomalies, multiple dilated bowel loops
Side of aortic arch important for surgical repair (thoracotomy opposite to side of arch)
