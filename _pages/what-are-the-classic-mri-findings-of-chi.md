---
title: "What are the classic MRI findings of Chiari 2?"
---
Small posterior fossa
Elongation of the pons
Scalloping of the clivus
Elongation and inferior descent of the 4th ventricle
Towering cerebellum and wraping of the cerebellar tonsils around the medulla
Beaked tectum
Callosal dysgenesis (90%)
Hydrocephalus
Interdigitated cerebral gyri, Fenestrated Falx.
