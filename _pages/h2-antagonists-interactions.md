---
title: "H2 antagonists interactions"
---
1) decreased absorption of anything that needs acidic environment (Ex: calcium)
2) need to consider timing because can interfere with other medications - 2 hours before or after other meds and food
3) slows the metabolism of many drugs leading to increased serum levels
-warfarin
-anticoagulants
-phenytion
-beta blockers
-benzodiazepines
-many more
