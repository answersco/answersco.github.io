---
title: "Oncocytoma"
---
These are truly not distinguishable from RCC and therefore have to be removed
Three ways to show:
1. Solid mass with central scar on MRI or CT
2. Spoke wheel vascular pattern on US
3. HOTTER than renal cortex on PET

NOTE: RCC is usually colder than renal parynchyma on PET, oncocytoma is the OPPOSITE

Bilateral oncocytomas as well as chromophobe RCC are associated with birt hogg dube
