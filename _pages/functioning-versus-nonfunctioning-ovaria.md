---
title: "Functioning versus nonfunctioning ovarian cyst"
---
Functioning cyst (follicles) are affected by the cycle, usually benign, and are usually 25 mm or less, and usually change/disappear by 6 weeks.

If a cyst persists and either doesn't change or enlarges, it is considered a nonfunctioning cyst (not under hormonal control).

Simple cysts &gt; 7 cm may need eval with MR or surgery.
