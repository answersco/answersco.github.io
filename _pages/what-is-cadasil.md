---
title: "What is CADASIL?"
---
Cerebral Autosomal Dominant Arteriopathy with Subcrotical Infarcts and Leukencephalopathy

- Basically, think of as &quot;PRES in the Anterior Circulation&quot;
- 40 yo with migraines and dementia

* Confluent white matter hyper intensities in the frontal and temporal lobes &amp; basal ganglia, but sparing occipital lobes

* Spares the subcortical U-Fibers
