---
title: "Invasive lobular carcinoma (ILC):"
---
Invasive lobular carcinoma (ILC) &#8594; 10% of breast cancers
Difficult to diagnose&#8594; false-negative MM rate up to 21% due to infiltrating pattern of tumor spread.
Variable imaging appearance, including mass (with or without spiculation), and architectural distortion (often only seen or one view).
Microcalcifications are uncommon (vs IDC)
More often multifocal or bilateral than IDC.
