---
title: "Most common cyanotic CHD, and most common surgical complication."
---
ToF: VSD, RVOT obstruction (valvular obstruction), overriding aorta, RV hypertrophy

Degree of severity in symptoms depends on severity of RVOT obstruction.
Pentalogy of Fallot: also has ASD.
Very liely to have right arch.

Most common complication after surgery: pulmonic regurgitation.
