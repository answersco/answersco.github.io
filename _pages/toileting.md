---
title: "Toileting"
---
-use of the toilet, bedside commode or bedpan
1) urinal use
-keep off over bed table
-if urinal is full, assess amount of urine, color and clarity, empty and record output on I&amp;O sheet
-frequently associated with falls - mention using the bathroom (proactive) when you are in the room for another reason so they do not have to wait until the last moment where they will get up even if they cannot or should not 
2) assess patient's condition to assist 
3) check ambulation orders
