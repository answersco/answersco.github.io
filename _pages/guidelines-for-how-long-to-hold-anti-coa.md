---
title: "Guidelines for how long to hold anti-coag prior to Lumbar puncture"
---
coumadin - 4 to 5 days before should stop
Plavix - STOP 7 days before 
LMW heparin - HOLD for 12 hours before procedure
Heparin - HOLD for 2-4 hour prior to the procedure (document normal PTT)
ASA and NSAIDS - can continue
