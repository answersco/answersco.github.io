---
title: "Pulmonary artery aneurysm/pseudoaneurysm causes"
---
Iatrogenic from Swan-Ganz catheter&#8594; patient in ICU
Behcet's disease: &quot;Turkish descent&quot; or &quot;mouth and genital ulcers&quot;
Chronic PE

Hughes-Stovin Syndrome: similar to Behcets. Recurrent thrombophlebitis and PA aneurysm formation and rupture.

Rasmussen aneurysm: PA pseudoaneurysm secondary to pulmonary TB. Usually involves upper lobes in setting of reactivation TB.

TOF repair: patch aneurysm from RVOT repair.
