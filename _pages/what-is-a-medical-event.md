---
title: "What is a medical event?"
---
Dosage differs from prescribed amount by &gt;20% AND patient will incur &gt; 5 rem whole-body equivalent dose and/or &gt;50 rem dose to any one organ. Does not occur with an dx doses. Only for I-131 &gt;30mCi. Must notify pt and NRC in 24 hrs and NRC in 15 days in writing.
