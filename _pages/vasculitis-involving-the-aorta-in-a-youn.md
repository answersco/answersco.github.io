---
title: "Vasculitis involving the aorta in a young patient"
---
Takayasu arteritis: &quot;pulse-less&quot; large-vessel vasculitis
Classically Asian girls aged 15-30
Often show as vasculitis involving the aorta.
Acute: wall thickening and enhancement. May be occlusion of major aortic branches, or dilation of aorta and branches.
Aortic valve often involved- causes stenosis or AI.
Late: classically diffuse narrowing distally.
Commonly involves pulmonary arteries - peripheral pruning.
