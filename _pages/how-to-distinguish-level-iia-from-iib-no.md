---
title: "How to distinguish level IIA from IIB nodes?"
---
Posterior margin of internal jugular (and/or spinal accessory nerve) separates the two.

* IF Anterior, lateral, or medial to the IJ or INSEPARABLE from the IJ posteriorly, then call it 2A.

* IF Posterior to IJ with an intervening fat plane, then call it 2B
