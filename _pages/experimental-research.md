---
title: "Experimental Research"
---
-experimenter manipulates the variable of interest while holding all other factors constant
-CAN determine cause and effect
EX. Weight loss for class (half regular, half diet)
-each side goes 12 weeks
-exercise (yes or no)
-gender is same # in each group
-3 meals a day
-age
-starting weight (50lbs overweight vs. 5lbs overweight)
