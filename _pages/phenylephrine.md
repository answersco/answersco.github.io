---
title: "Phenylephrine"
---
-also called neosynephrine
-nasal spray causes vasoconstriction in nasal passages, decreasing blood flow in that area and decreased congestion
-check mucosa and lining to check if its intact bc continued use can cause damage to the mucosa and lining of the nose
-side effect: continued overuse causes nose to be &quot;addicted&quot; to it and if you stop using it can cause rebound congestion
-can be given in IV to treat hypotension and eye drops to dilate pupils
-is specifically alpha1 adrenergic agonist 
-cautions: hypertensive pt's, coronary artery disease, hyperthyroidism (hyperthyroid increases everything and so does a sympathomimetic)
