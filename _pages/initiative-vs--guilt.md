---
title: "Initiative vs. guilt"
---
initiative: 
-increasing dependability
-cooperates with other children
-negotiates solutions to problems
-assertiveness
-parents can encourage by giving child the freedom to play and use their imagination

guilt:
-sense of defeatism
-anger
-easily frightened
-feeling shameful
-preschoolers who are criticized or discouraged from taking initiative learn to feel guilty

-3 to 5-6 years
-children begin to forge their own interests, friendships, and take on some responsibilities
-extremely willing to help

-Success: develop initiative and willingness to contribute and help

-Failures: develop feelings of anxiety, guilt, and lack of initiative
