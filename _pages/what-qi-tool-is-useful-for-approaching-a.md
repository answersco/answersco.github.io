---
title: "What QI tool is useful for approaching a controversial topic?"
---
Nominal Group Technique (NGT): a more formal and structured approach to generating a list of ideas or merging them into a more sustainable number
&quot;Nominal&quot; is used to denote the minimal conversations or interactions among the team.
This approach can be quite effective for discussing controversial concepts
Also useful for defusing a domineering staff member
Promotes team ownership of decision-making.

2 stages:
Formalized brainstorming: everything discussed is written down. 
Decision-making: have members of the team make a list on an index card of selected ideas from the list ranked by priority. Collect and tally the votes.
