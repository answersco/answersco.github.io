---
title: "VSD: general info and CXR findings"
---
- Most common congenital heart disease
- Membranous type is the most common

- Outlet (infundibulum) subtypes must be repaired since the right coronary cusp prolapses
- small ones close on their own (70%)

CXR:
 - big heart, increase vascularity, small aortic knob
 - show a splayed carina (LAE)
