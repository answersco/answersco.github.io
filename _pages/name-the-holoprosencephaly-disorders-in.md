title: "Name the holoprosencephaly disorders in order of increasing severity  - what is the abnormality in holoprosencephaly"
---
- lobar (least severe)
- semilobar 
- alobar (worst)

Midline cleaving problem (brain failing to separate hemispheres)
- brain normally cleaves back to front (like myelination; opposite of corpus callosum) so in milder forms, posterior brain is normal
