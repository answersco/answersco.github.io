---
title: "Patient who is treated with I-131 vomits on clothing, contaminating them, but does not wish for them to be discarded. They are stored for self decay. After how long in storage can the patient obtain the clothing?"
---
Answer: 80 days
The items should be kept in storage for 7-10 half-lives. I-131 half-life is 7 days (10 half-lives equal around 80 days).
