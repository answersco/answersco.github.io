---
title: "Writing a nursing diagnosis"
---
-the problem or need based on the appropriate NANDA approved statements
-related to (R/T) is the etiology or the cause of the problem
-list the evidence (actual problem) or the supporting data 
EX.
1) impaired skin integrity R/T immobility - when a pt does not move in bed by themselves, the skin starts breaking down
2) risk for infection R/T invasive procedure - not showing any signs/symptoms of any infection but know their at risk due to his procedure
