---
title: "Heparin as treatment"
---
-requires frequent lab checks (PTT and weight) if being used as treatment rather than prophylaxis
-is weight based
-if sub q for prevention, don't need daily monitoring
-no bolus dose ordered is post-op pt bc will increase risk of bleeding
-loading bolus doses can be given to get therapeutic effect quickly 
-short half life, check every 4-6 hours and draw the aPTT lab tests (bc heparin is activated now)
-for short term use
-may be used as &quot;bridge&quot; if patient is on coumadin and requires an invasive procedure
