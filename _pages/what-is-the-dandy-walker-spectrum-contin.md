---
title: "What is the Dandy-Walker spectrum/continuum?"
---
A posterior fossa cyst in continuity w/ 4th vent, and abnl vermis. Primary sx is obstructive hydrocephalus and present early in life. Assoc w heterotopia, agen CC, holopros, schizenceph, syrinx; facial anom/cleft palate, poly-syndactyly, cardiac abnl; trisomies 13/18, tripoloidy. Ddx mega cisterna magna (normal vermis).
