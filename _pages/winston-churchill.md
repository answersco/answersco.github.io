title: "Winston Churchill"
---
A noted British statesman who led Britain throughout most of World War II and along with Roosevelt planned many allied campaigns. His speeches inspired the British at their lowest point, and he named the &quot;iron curtain&quot; that would separate Communist Europe from the rest of the West.
