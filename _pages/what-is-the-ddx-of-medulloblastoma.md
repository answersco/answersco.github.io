---
title: "What is the ddx of medulloblastoma?"
---
1) Atypical Teratoid/Rhabdoid Tumor (AT/RT), Indistinguishable by imaging
2) Ependymoma, Ca++ and hemorrhage more common
3) Cerebellar Pilocytic Astrocytoma (PA), cyst with enhancing nodule
4) Choroid Plexus Papilloma (CPP), less common in 4th vent
