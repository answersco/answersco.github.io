---
title: "Physical abuse in elders"
---
DEFINITION: Use of physical force that may result in bodily injury, physical pain, or impairment

EXAMPLES: Shaking, slapping, kicking, pinching, and burning. Improper use of physical restraints, inappropriate use of drugs, force feeding, physical punishment

SIGNS AND SYMPTOMS: Bruises, black eyes, fx, injuries in various stages of healing.
