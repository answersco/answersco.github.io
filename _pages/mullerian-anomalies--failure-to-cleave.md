---
title: "Mullerian anomalies: failure to cleave"
---
Septate: 2 endometrial canals separated by a fibrous (or muscular) septum
Fibrous vs muscular can be determined by MRI&#8594; changes surgical management
Increased risk of infertility and spontaneous abortion.
Early failure if implantation in septum.
Resection of septum improves outcomes.

Arcuate uterus: normal variant
Mild smooth concavity of the uterine fundus (instead of normal straight or convex).
Not associated with infertility or OB complications.
