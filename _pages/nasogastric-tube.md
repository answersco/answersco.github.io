---
title: "Nasogastric tube"
---
1) levin
-clear, larger, firm plastic tube
-manipulate to soften tubing
-used for feeding and decompression 
-check placement with gastric PH
-confirm placement only with X-ray
2) dobbhoff/corpak
-smaller tube diameter
-goes past stomach, past pyloric sphincter and to duodenum
-softer plastic -- soak in an ice bath to firm up plastic for easier insertion
-used only for feeding - longer term
-can not aspirate or tube will collapse
-confirm placement only with X-ray
