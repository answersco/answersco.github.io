---
title: "What are the contraindications to liver embolization?"
---
Liver parenchyma involvement of &gt; 50%
Hyperbilirubinemia (&gt; 2 mg/dL)
Child-Pugh class C liver disease
Hepatic encephalopathy; refractory ascites
Hypovascular tumors (ineffective)
PV thrombus
TIPSS
LDH &gt; 425 IU/L
AST &gt; 100 IU/L
