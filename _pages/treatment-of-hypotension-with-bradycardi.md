---
title: "Treatment of hypotension with bradycardia (vagal)"
---
Secure airway; give 02 6-10 L via mask.
Monitor vitals.
Legs elevated &gt;60 degrees or Trendelenburg.
IV LR or NS.
Atropine 0.6-1 mg IV slowly if patient doesn't respond to first steps, and repeat up to 0.04 mg/kg (2-3 mg in adult)
