title: "Secure"
---
-feels secure with parent of caregiver
-when separated they may or may not cry
-if they do cry, it will stop after a while
-when parent's return they actively seek the parent
-60% show this attachment
-will be more sociable and emotionally competent compared to the other attachment styles

-healthy
-infant plays with mom but also independently
-screams when mom leaves and is easily comforted when she comes back
