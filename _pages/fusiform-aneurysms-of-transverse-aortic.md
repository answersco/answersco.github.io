---
title: "Fusiform aneurysms of transverse aortic arch"
---
Almost always degenerative in nature, and contiguous with either ascending or descending Ao aneurysm
Common features:
Intimal calcification with bulky plaque or mural thrombus.
Tortuosity and elongation of arch
Deviation of mediastinal structures such as trachea or esophagus
Critical feature for operative repair = relationship of aneurysm to great-vessel origins
