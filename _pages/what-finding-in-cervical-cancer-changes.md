---
title: "What finding in cervical cancer changes management from surgical to chemo/XRT?"
---
Stage IIa: spread beyond cervix, but no parametrial invasion &#8594; surgery
Stage IIb: parametrial involvement or lower &#8531; of vaginal without extension to pelvic side wall&#8594; chemo/XRT
Parametrium: fibrous band that separates the supravaginal cervix from the bladder, extending between the layers of the broad ligament. It includes the uterine artery.
Cervix normally has a T2 dark ring on axial&#8594; disruption of this suggests parametrial invasion.
