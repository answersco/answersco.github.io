---
title: "What are the components and position of each extensor compartment of the wrist?"
---
Numbered from radial to ulnar direction
I: abductor pollicis longus and extensor pollicis brevis.
-Located along the lateral border of the radius.

II: extensor carpi radialis longus and brevis
-Rests along dorsal and lateral aspect of radius.
III: extensor pollicis longus
-II and III are separated by Lister tubercle, a bony ridge at the dorsal radius.

IV: extensor digitorum (communis) and extensor indicis tendons with a shared tendon sheath

V: extensor digiti minimi
-Sits between the articularion of the radius and ulna

VI: extensor carpi ulnaris
