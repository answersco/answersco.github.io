---
title: "Neurofibromatosis Type 2"
---
Bilateral vestibular schwannomas
Schwannomas of cranial nerves (CN) and spinal nerve roots
Meningiomas on dural surfaces (up to 50%)
Ependymomas in spinal cord and brainstem (6%)
Use high resolution T1 C+ through basal cisterns with fat saturation to evaluate cranial nerves
