---
title: "Prophase"
---
-replicated chromosomes (sister chromatids) condense
-microtubules organize into a spindle
-nuclear envelope and nucleolus break down

-chromosomes condense
-nuclear envelope breaks down
-spindle fibers grow from centrioles
-centrioles migrate to opposite poles of cell

the first and longest stage of mitosis in which the chromosomes become visible, the centrioles separate, a spindle begins to form, and the nuclear envelope breaks down
