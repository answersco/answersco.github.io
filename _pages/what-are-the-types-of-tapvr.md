---
title: "What are the types of TAPVR?"
---
Type 1 (supracardiac): innominate vein, subclavian vein, right superior vena cava, left superior vena cava, azygos vein, hemiazygos vein
Type 2 (cardiac): coronary sinus, right atrium, supradiaphragmatic IVC
Type 3 (infracardiac): subdiaphragmatic IVC, hepatic vein, ductus venosus, left gastric vein
