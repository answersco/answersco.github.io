title: "Polyarteritis nodosa"
---
This is more common in men
There is an association with hepatitis B 
This is a medium vessel vasculitis (think renal arteries, splancnic circulation, and cardiac circulation)
Within the kidney a lot of times you are going to see MICROANEURYSMS

Systemic necrotizing vasculitis of small and medium vessels causing multiple small visceral aneurysms
