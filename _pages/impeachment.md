---
title: "Impeachment"
---
An action by the House of Representatives to accuse the president, vice president, or other civil officers of the United States of committing &quot;Treason, Bribery, or other high Crimes and Misdemeanors.&quot;

the power delegated to the House of Representatives in the Constitution to charge the president, vice president, or other &quot;civil officers,&quot; including federal judges, with &quot;Treason, Bribery, or other high Crimes and Misdemeanors,&quot; which is the first step in the constitutional process of removing government officials from office
