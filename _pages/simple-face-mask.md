---
title: "Simple face mask"
---
-covers the mouth and nose
-provides the most consistent, effective delivery of oxygen
-interferes with talking, eating, and drinking
-may cause claustrophobic reaction so pt constantly taking them off 
-not suitable for COPD patients bc their body's are accustomed to less circulating oxygen
-40-60% -- 6-10 L/min (on the flowmeter)
