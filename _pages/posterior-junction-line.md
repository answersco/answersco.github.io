---
title: "Posterior junction line"
---
formed by the apposition of the visceral and parietal pleura of the posteromedial portion of the lungs posterior to the esophagus and anterior to the third through the fifth thoracic vertebra

Four layers of pleura, seen projection through the trachea on frontal view, more superior than the anterior junction line.

the line projects from the aortic knob and extends above the clavicles
