---
title: "Locations of gastric abnormalities:  - H pylori gastritis? - ZES? - Crohns of stomach? - Menetrier's? - Lymphoma?"
---
H pylori gastritis: antrum
ZES: jejunum ulcer (buzzword). duodenal bulb is #1 location. also in stomach. Secondary to gastrinoma (part of MEN1).
Crohn's: Antrum, although uncommon in stomach
Menetrier's: Fundus; classically spares antrum.
Lymphoma: &quot;crosses the pylorus,&quot; though reality: adenoCA does it more
