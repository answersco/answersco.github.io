---
title: "Assessing inner ear"
---
assess:
1) ear pain
2) itching
3) vertigo
4) tinnitus
5) hearing acuity
-whisper: stand behind pt, push down tragus on one ear, pt to repeat three words nurse says behind them
-refer to audiologist if does not pass whisper test
6) ototoxicity
-injury to the auditory nerve
-medications which may cause ototoxicity such as aspirin, streptomycin, furosemide, &amp; aminoglycosides
