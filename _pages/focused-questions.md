---
title: "Focused questions"
---
-Encourages client to give more information about specific topic of concern. 
-Encourages client to think about and describe a particular concern in more detail.

EX. 
&quot;Tell me more about . . .&quot;
&quot;Tell me about your pain.&quot;
&quot;What did your doctor say?&quot;
&quot;How has your family reacted?&quot;
&quot;What is your biggest fear?&quot;
&quot;That seems worth talking about more.&quot;
