---
title: "RhoGam"
---
-prevents formation of Anti-Ph antibodies
Occurs when an Rh- mother has an Rh+ child
1) first Rh incompatible pregnancy
-fetal Rh+ cells recognized as foreign
-mother's immune system attacks fetal cells
-produces a mild reaction, few antibodies present
2) second Rh incompatible pregnancy
-large response, plentiful antibodies
-destruction of fetal blood cells, much damage
