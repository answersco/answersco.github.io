---
title: "PCP with cystic lungs"
---
Typically GGO, predominantly in hilar and mid-lung zones.
Pneumatoceles in 30% - can have spontaneous PTX
Patients receiving aerosolized prophylaxis can get cystic form with bilateral, thin-walled, upper lung predominant cysts.
Gallium 67: diffuse uptake (thallium negative).
