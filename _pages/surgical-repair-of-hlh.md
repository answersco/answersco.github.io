title: "Surgical repair of HLH?"
---
1- Make the R ventricle supply systemic circulation by reconstructing aorta using main pulmonary artery. Excise atrial septum. Use blalock Taussig shunt- connecting R sublcavian artery to R pulmonary artery via prosthetic graft. 2- After 3-6 months wehn pulmonary arterial resistance is normal, do the bidirectional Glenn- connecting the SVC to the R pulmonary artery. 3- Modified Fontan- Once need more oxygenation connect IVC to pulmonary artery.
