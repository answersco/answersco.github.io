title: "What are the ages and bugs assoc with croup vs epiglottitis vs exudative tracheitis?"
---
1. Croup = Young (6 mo - 3yo); Parainfluenza virus; STEEPLE SIGN

2. Epiglottitis = (3.5 yo and teens); H flu; Thumbprinted epiglottis and asphyxiation from aryepiglotic folds (lower down before ventricle)

3. ET = older kids (6-10 yo): Staph; linear soft tissue felling defect/membrane in airway
