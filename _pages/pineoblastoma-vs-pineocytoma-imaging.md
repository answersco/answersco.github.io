---
title: "Pineoblastoma vs Pineocytoma imaging?"
---
Pineoblastoma = in KIDS! highly invasive (e.g. PNET of pineal gland); heterogeneous, avid enhancement; associated with RETINOBLASTOMA!

Pineocytoma = in ADULTS! Non-invasive, &lt; 3 cm well circumscribed solid mass that enhances avidly and MAY have cystic change
