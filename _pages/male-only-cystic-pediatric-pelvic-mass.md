Mullerian duct cyst
Prostatic utricle&#8594; normal midline structure that is the terminal remnant of the Mullerian duct, arising from the posterior urethra near the verumontanum (near seminal vesicle insertion)
Can become very large when associated with hypospadia (urethra opens along the ventral penile shaft)
