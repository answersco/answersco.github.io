---
title: "Von Hippel-Lindau: CNS manifestations"
---
Cerebellar (75%) spinal (25%). Autosomal dom or sporadic mutations. Sx balance/coordination issues and visual issues. Assoc w/ retinal hemangioblastomas, cystadenomas of endolymphatic sac, pheo, renal cysts, angiomyolipoma, clear cell RCC, pancreatic cysts, serous cystadenoma, and liver cysts. Ddx: Pilocytic astrocytoma, medulloblastoma, isolated hemangioblastoma, AVM, mets.
