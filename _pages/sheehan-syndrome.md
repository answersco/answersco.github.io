title: "Sheehan syndrome"
---
-pituitary hemorrhage in pregnant women
-T1 bright

Pituitary apoplexy in a lady who suffered from large volume hemorrhage during delivery 

MRI:
- Probs going to show T1 bright signal in the pituitary (more of an acute finding)
- Could also show ring enhancement around an empty sella (more of a late finding)
