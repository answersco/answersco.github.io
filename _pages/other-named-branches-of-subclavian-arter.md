---
title: "Other named branches of subclavian arteries"
---
Relatively constant in presence, but highly variable in origin.
-Vertebral arteries: from superoposterior surface of subclavian arteries.
-Thyrocervical trunk- arises just distal to internal mammary arteries from superior surface of subclavian, often lateral to vertebral artery. 
-Costocervical trunk
-Dorsal scapular artery
