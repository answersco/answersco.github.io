---
title: "Faith"
---
-generally refers to a confident belief in something for which there is no proof or material evidence. 
-it can involve a person, idea, or thing, and is usually followed by action related to ideals or values of that belief.
-used to describe a cultural or institutional religion such as Judaism, Islam
EX: If I have faith in my doctor, I am more likely to adhere to a treatment plan.
