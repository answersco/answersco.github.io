---
title: "Todani classification"
---
type I - extrahepatic cbd dilation
II - diverticula of the cbd.
III - choledochocele - intraduodenal portion of the cbd.
IV - multiple intra and extrahepatic (IVa). multiple extra - IVb.

Todani 1: Fusiform extra hepatic dilatation only (most common) = Excise for risk of cholangio!
2. Diverticulum of CBD
3. Choledochocele (protrusion of focally dilated CBD into duodenum)
4. BOTH intrahepatic and extra hepatic cysts
5. Caroli's (intrahepetic cysts only); central dot sign
