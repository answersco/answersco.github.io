---
title: "Ganglioglioma / ganglioneuroma"
---
Benign - children / young adults
-present with seizures
-Temporal &gt; frontal &gt; parietal
-cystic mass with calcifications,
-can erode inner table of skull
-can met through CSF pathways - small subarachnoid cyst
-in cerebellum, can mimic Lhermitte Duclos disease
