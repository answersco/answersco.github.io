---
title: "What is Crouzon Syndrome?"
---
autosomal dominant, mutation in fibroblast growth factor receptor 2 (FGFR2) on 10q25-26. Characterized by abnl calvarial shape /&quot;cloverleaf skull&quot;; shallow orbits with exopthalmos; mid facial hypoplasia; bifid uvula. Assoc w Chiari 1, hydrocephalus, c-spine fusion, stylohyoid lig calcs, elbow and hand deformities.
