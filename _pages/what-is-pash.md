---
title: "What is PASH?"
---
*PASH = BENIGN myofibroblastic hyperplastic process = big 4-6 cm, solid, oval shaped, with well defined borders (or asymmetric density)

* typically NOT associated with calcifications, spiculated mass, or architectural distortion

- annual 12 mo follow up recommended

- on US looks circ, hypoechoic, heterogenous mass with increased through transmission
* Should NOT have spiculated margin, calcifications, or architectural distortion

* IF biopsied and concordant, can return to screening mammo!
