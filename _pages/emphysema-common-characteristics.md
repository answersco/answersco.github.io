---
title: "Emphysema common characteristics"
---
1) Thin appearance

2) Increased respiratory rate to maintain adequate oxygen levels

3) Accessory muscle use

4) Barrel shaped chest

5) Purse-lipped breathing

6) Increased CO2 retention causing flushed, pink skin -- no cyanosis

7) Dyspnea

8) Ineffective cough

9) Hyper-resonance on chest percussion

10) Orthopneic

11) DOE

12) Prolonged expiratory time

13) Speaks in short jerky sentences

14) Anxious

15) Leads to right-side HF
