---
title: "PICC preferred locations"
---
Non-dominant arm
Above the AC fossa preferred, and may lower risk of phlebitis
Order of preference:
Basilic, brachial, cephalic, median cubital vein.

Basilic: dominant superficial vein of arm
-merges with axillary vein in mid- to proximal arm
-Best found in superficial fat of medial aspect of forearm
