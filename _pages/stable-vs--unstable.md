---
title: "Stable vs. unstable"
---
-behavior due to stable, unchanging factors
vs.
-behavior due to unstable, changing factors

EX. Lee gets a D on his sociology term paper. If he attributes the grade to the fact that he always has bad luck, he is making a stable attribution. If he attributes the grade to the fact that he didn't have much time to study that week, he is making an unstable attribution.
