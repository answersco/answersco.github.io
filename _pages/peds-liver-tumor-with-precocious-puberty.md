---
title: "Peds liver tumor with precocious puberty"
---
Hepatoblastoma: most common primary liver tumor of childhood (&lt;5)
Associated with hemi-hypertophy, Wilms, Beckwith-Wiedemann.
Prematurity is a risk factor.
Usually well-circumscribed RIGHT-SIDED mass that may extend into portal/hepatic veins and IVC.
50% with calcification
AFP elevated
May have precocious puberty due to b-hcg secretion.
