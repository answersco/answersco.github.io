title: "Galeazzi fracture"
---
Radial shaft fracture, dislocation of distal ulna at DRUJ

Distal radial shaft fracture and anterior ulnar dislocation at the junction with the radius

Fracture of DISTAL radius with dislocation of distal ulna.
(Fx of radial head w/ dislocation of ulna = Essex Lopresti)
