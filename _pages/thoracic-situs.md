---
title: "Thoracic situs"
---
refers to position of bronchi
Normal: situs solitus
 - LMB longer than RMB
 - L upper lobe bronchus inferior to left PA
 - R upper lobe bronchus superior to right PA
Situs inversus = opposite of above

Isomerism: Symmetrical development of heart / lungs
 - ie. left isomerism = 2 left lungs or LA
