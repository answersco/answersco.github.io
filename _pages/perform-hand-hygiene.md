---
title: "Perform hand hygiene"
---
hand hygiene:
-on arrival to hospital
-before eating
-after toileting
-before contact with clients susceptible to infections
-after caring for any client
-after touching any body substance (even when wearing gloves)
-before and after contact with wounds
-before and after invasive procedures (i.e. injections, suctioning, catheterization)
-after handling contaminated equipment
-before leaving the agency
-before putting on gloves
-after removing gloves
