---
title: "What is Boerhaave syndrome and how is it different from a Mallory-Weiss tear?"
---
Boerhaave syndrome refers to spontaneous transmural rupture of the esophagus after forceful emesis, you'll see a lot of gas in soft tissues, and fluid in the pleural space.
A mallory-weiss tear is a partial thickness tear after forcefull emesis usually with massive bleeding into the stomach common in cirrhotics with esophageal varices.
