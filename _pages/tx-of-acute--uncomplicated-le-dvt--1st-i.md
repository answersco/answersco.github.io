---
title: "Tx of acute, uncomplicated LE DVT (1st instance)"
---
Heparin (unfractionated or LMWH) used to initiate therapy, followed by oral Coumadin or LMWH for long-term therapy
-Duration of 3-6 months with target INR of 2-3
-Compression stockings for acute control of edema, and continued for at least 2 years to prevent post-thrombotic syndrome.
-IVC filters not indicated in uncomplicated DVT unless patient can't be anticoagulated, has had massive PE, or lacks cardiopulmonary reserve to tolerate PE.
