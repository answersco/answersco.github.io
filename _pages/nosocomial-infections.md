---
title: "Nosocomial infections"
---
-hospital acquired infections
-cause deaths in the tens of thousands/yr
-28-33 billion in additional heath care cost/yr
-UTI most common (post foley insertion) 
-followed by surgical site, bloodstream, respiratory

-also known as hospital acquired infections (HAIs)
-high number of them resistant to antibiotics
-1/25 pt's end up with HAIs
-usually brought on from breach of the cleaning technique
-can also occur due to pt's in the hospital w/ lower resistance of infection
