---
title: "Nursing interventions for respiratory"
---
1) cough &amp; deep breathe q 2 hrs.
2) hydration (2000cc/24 hours)
3) check for respiratory depression from amount of pain meds
4) quality of sputum: hydration helps to mobilize secretions and help cough up 
5) incentive spirometer q 1 hr. w/a
6) early ambulation
7) report S&amp;S infection
8) turn q 2 hrs. if client on bedrest
9) keep client comfortable
10) administer O2 as ordered &amp; monitor pulse ox.
11) pain control and comfort
