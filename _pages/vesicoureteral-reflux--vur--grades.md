---
title: "Vesicoureteral Reflux (VUR) Grades"
---
I: into ureter only w/ normal ureteral and calyces morphology
II: into non-dilated renal calyces
III: into collecting system w/ blunting of the calyces
IV: moderately dilated ureter
V: severely dilated ureter

* 90% of Grade I &amp; II spontaneously regress after few years
