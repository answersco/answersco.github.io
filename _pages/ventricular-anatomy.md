---
title: "Ventricular Anatomy"
---
- two lateral ventricles --&gt; foramen of Monro --&gt; 3rd ventricle -&gt; cerebral aqueduct (of Sylvius) --&gt; 4th ventricle --&gt; via foramen of magendi (medial) and luschka (lateral) --&gt; spinal cord

- most CSF will then rise through tentorial notch over the surface of the brain where it is reabsorbed by the arachnoid granulations into venous system
