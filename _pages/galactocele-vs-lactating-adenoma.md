---
title: "Galactocele vs lactating adenoma"
---
Galactocele: typically seen on cessation of lactation
Typically sub-areolar
Variable appearance
Aunt Minnie: fat-fluid level

Lactating adenoma: look like FA, usually multiple
Recommend follow-up 4-6 months post-partum, post-delivery, or after cessation of lactation via US
Can have flow on Doppler
Usually rapidly regress after cessation of lactation.
