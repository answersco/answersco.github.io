---
title: "Estrogen indications"
---
1) small doses for hormone replacement
2) for the discomfort of menopause such as hot flashes and vaginal dryness 
3) hypogonadism: decreased function of the gonads because for some reason the 1st stage of puberty was interrupted
4) oncology: need to be cautious and make sure that we are not giving estrogen to patients who cancer is ER+
