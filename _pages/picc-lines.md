---
title: "PICC lines"
---
-Used for long term therapy

-Can be inserted at bedside as a sterile procedure

-Can be inserted by a specially trained nurse 

-Use SAS or SASH (per agency policy)

-Multiple lumens - flushed as per agency policy

-Nursing vs. Nursing Assistant care for PICC -- NO BP in arm with PICC and NO venipunctures from the arm with the PICC (-only nurses can touch PICC lines - nurse techs cannot draw from PICC lines)

-Check site immediately after insertion to make sure it is not bleeding excessively
