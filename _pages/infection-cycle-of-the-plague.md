---
title: "Infection cycle of the plague"
---
-starts in animals (endemic reservoir hosts: small rodents like mice)
-will circulate in the mice population and spreads to larger rodents such as rats, squirrels, and chipmunks and they were called amplifying hosts because they tend to build up more disease in larger rodents
-the vector is the flea which can feed on animals and persons and the persons become the accidental hosts
-the fleas do get symptoms of the bacteria - blocks the GI tract
