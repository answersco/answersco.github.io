---
title: "Myocarditis"
---
Myocarditis: most commonly viral, due to Coxsackievirus B
Inflamed myocardium demonstrates hyperenhancement on EARLY gadolinium-enhanced T1W-images due to increased inflow of blood
DHE: enhancement in the mid-myocardium, often in a patchy pattern
May also be supepicardial

Bright T2-signal due to edema
