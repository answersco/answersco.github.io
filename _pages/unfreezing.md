---
title: "Unfreezing"
---
-first stage in stages of change
-when people have a sense of dissatisfaction, and they want something different.
-realizing there is a problem and seeks to make some kind of change. 
-in this stage it is important for the client to see the need for change

-recognize they have a problem 
-examine status quo, increase driving forces for change, decrease resisting forces against 
EX: overweight person realizes they need to lose weight
