---
title: "Findings in hypothenar hammer"
---
Blunt trauma (jack-hammer) to ulnar artery and superficial palmar arch, with impaction against the hook of the hamate.

Arterial wall damage leads to aneurysm formation with or without vessel thrombosis.

May get emboli with distal obstruction of digits (causes confusion with Buergers)

&quot;Corkscrew&quot; configuration of the superficial palmar arch, occlusion of ulnar artery, or pseudoaneurysm off the ulnar artery.
