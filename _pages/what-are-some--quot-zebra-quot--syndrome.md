---
title: "What are some &quot;zebra&quot; syndromes associated with male infertility (4)"
---
- Pituitary adenoma: making prolactin
- Kallmans syndrome: can't smell + infertility
- Klinefelter's syndrome: tall + gynecomastia + infertility
- Zinner syndrome: renal agenesis + ipsilateral seminal vesicle cyst
