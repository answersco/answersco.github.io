---
title: "What are the recs regarding removal of long term catheters in pts with catheter related blood stream infxn?"
---
If pt has severe sepsis; suppurative thrombophlebitis; endocarditis; or infxn despite &gt;72 h of antimicrobial therapy, or if there is infxn with Staph, Pseudomonas, fungus or mycobacteria.
