---
title: "What is a Geiger Muller Counter? Can it provide info on radiation type?"
---
Detects small amounts of radioactive contamination with its PANCAKE PROBE. Does NOT provide info on radiation type.

- Susceptible to its dead time (overloaded by large dose of radiation, then there's time before it can respond again)

- Max dose is 100 mR/h

* Calibrated ANNUALLY
