---
title: "What is neonatal PNA?"
---
Neonatal pneumonia is acquired during the perinatal period. Before birth, via placenta or ascend from the vagina and PROM. Gram negative bacteria (such as Pseudomonas aeruginosa) are more common in newborns than at any other age group. There is a high mortality, ~50%. Fever and WBC are not reliable.
