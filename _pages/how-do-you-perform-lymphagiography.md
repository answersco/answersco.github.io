---
title: "How do you perform lymphagiography?"
---
Inject methylene blue into subcut right 1st/2nd interspace. Look for blue vessels under skin. Cut down to vessel. Catheterize with 30g needle. Infuse lipiodol/ethiodol. Image up ankle, leg, pelvis, abd looking for vessels, LN and cisterna chyli to the right of L1-2. Thoracic duct from T12- to neck right side below T5-6 where it crosses to Left to enter L subclavian vein.
