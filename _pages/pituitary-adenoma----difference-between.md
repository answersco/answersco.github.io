---
title: "Pituitary adenoma: - difference between micro and macro-adenoma? - what do most secrete? - symptoms of men and women? - imaging characteristics"
---
- micro (&lt;10 mm); macro (&gt;10 mm)
- most functional adenomas secrete prolactin
- Women: menstrual irregularity, galactorrhea
- Men: vague (decreased libido)

Imaging:
- T1 dark, T2 bright
- enhance less than normal pituitary
