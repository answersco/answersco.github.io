Creutzfeldt-Jakob Disease (CJD)
Typical MRI appearance is cortical ribboning&#8594; ribbonlike FLAIR hyperintensity and restricted diffusion of the cerebral cortex.
Basal ganglia and thalami are also involved.
Motor cortex commonly spared.
Pulvinar sign&#8594; bright DWI and FLAIR signal in the pulvinar nucleus of the thalamus.
Hockey stick sign&#8594; bright DWI and FLAIR signal in the dorsomedial thalamus.
