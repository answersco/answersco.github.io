---
title: "TRNA"
---
0.1% codes for actual proteins

-transports specific amino acids to the ribosome for protein synthesis
-cloverleaf shape
-two business ends
 -- anticodon of tRNA hydrogen bonds with complementary mRNA codon
 -- 3' end of tRNA carries the amino acid specified by the mRNA codon

-made up of amino acids and anticodons which bind to specific codons in mRNA
EX. codon AUG = anticodon UAC
