---
title: "What medication (s) do you administer for contrast reaction leading to hypertensive crisis (SBP &gt;200, DBP &gt;120)"
---
labetalol 20mg IV slowly over 2 minutes (can double dose every 10 minutes) AND/OR lasix 20-40mg IV over 2 minutes

If no labetalol available, can give Nitroglycerine 0.4mg tab SL q 5-10 minutes
