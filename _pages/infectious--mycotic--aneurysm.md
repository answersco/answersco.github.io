---
title: "Infectious (mycotic) aneurysm"
---
-aneurysm arising from infection of arterial wall, usually bacterial
-Best diagnostic clue: Rapidly growing saccular aneurysm arising eccentrically from aortic wall
-Anywhere in aorta or other vessels
-Periaortic soft tissue stranding, edema and fluid frequently present
-In 25% of cases blood cultures are negative
-Acute rupture/hemorrhage seen in 75%
-Surgical resection/grafting following antibiotic therapy
-May need extra anatomic bypass grafting
