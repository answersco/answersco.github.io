---
title: "NM therapy for bone pain and contraindications"
---
3 approved agents for bone pain from metastatic breast and prostate cancer:
1. Sr-89-chloride
2. Sm-153-EDTMP
3. Ra-223-dichloride

Can be given during XRT
Absolute contraindications (for Sr and Sm):
-Pregnancy
-Breastfeeding
-Renal failure (&lt;30 GFR)
