---
title: "Posttransplant lymphoproliferative disorder"
---
Liver: hypo echoic nodular 1-4 cm lesions. may be infiltrative. periportal infiltration with biliary obstruction in liver transplant patients
Spleen: discrete hypoechoic lesions, splenomegaly
kidneys: usually unilateral and unifocal
Small bowel - cirfumferential thickening with aneurysmal dilation; intussesception

Spectrum of entities. Ranging from benign polyclonal lymphoid proliferation to aggressive non-Hodgkin lymphoma.
