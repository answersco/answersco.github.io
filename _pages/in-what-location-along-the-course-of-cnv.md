---
title: "In what location along the course of CNV is schwannoma most commonly located?"
---
Answer: Prepontine cistern

Meckel's cave (on the other hand): depression in anteromedial portion of petruos apex. In middle cranial fossa, it containes the CNB nerve ganglion, giving off VN1-3.
-It is posterolateral to the cavernous sinus.
