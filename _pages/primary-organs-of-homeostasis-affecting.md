---
title: "Primary organs of homeostasis affecting fluids"
---
-the cardiovascular system pumps and carries nutrients and water in body.
-lungs regulate oxygen and carbon dioxide levels of the blood.
-adrenal glands help the body conserve sodium, save chloride and water, and excrete potassium.
-the pituitary gland stores and releases ADH.
-the thyroid gland increases the blood flow in the body and increases renal circulation.
-the nervous system inhibits and stimulates mechanisms influencing fluid balance.
-parathyroid glands regulate the level of calcium in ECF.
-the GI tract absorbs water and nutrients that enter the body through this route.
