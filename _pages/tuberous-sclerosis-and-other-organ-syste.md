---
title: "Tuberous Sclerosis and other organ system associations: - Lung (1) - Cardiac (2) - Brain (3) - Renal (2)"
---
- Lung: LAM - thin walled cysts and chylothorax

- Cardiac: Rhabdomyosarcoma (involve cardiac septum) and rhabdomyoma (most common pedi cardiac tumor)

- Brain: Giant Cell astrocytoma, cortical and subcortical tubers, subependymal nodules

- Renal: AMLs, RCC (younger patients)
