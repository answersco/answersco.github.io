---
title: "What is the tumor that most commonly occurs SUPRATENTORIAL in KIDS and Infratentorial in adults? (e.g. the exception to the rule)"
---
Choroid plexus papilloma / carcinoma

Kids = Lateral ventricle atria
Adults = 4th ventricle

* Choroid plexus carcinoma is ONLY SEEN in KIDS; assoc with Li Fraumeni

* Both are very brightly enhancing
* Produce CSF so cause NON-obstructive hydrocephalus
