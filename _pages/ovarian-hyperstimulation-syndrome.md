---
title: "Ovarian hyperstimulation syndrome"
---
-large multicystic ovaries
-ascites
-pleural effusions
-can have pericardial effusions

complications:
torsion, hypovolemic shock

Due to VEGF dysregulation/capillary leak. Ovary larger than 5 cm, ascites/plural effusion, abdominal pain.
Plus elevated hematocrit, WBC, LFT, renal failure or dyspnea. 
Increased risk of ovarian torsion and ectopic
