Glomus tumors: Associated with MEN 1 and NF1, where they are often multiple.
Highly vascular and enhance avidly.
MRI&#8594; classic salt-and-pepper appearance due to intra-tumoral flow voids.

Glomus jugulare&#8594; usually middle-aged woman with pulsatile tinnitus and conductive hearing loss.
Moth-eaten destruction of bone centered around jugular foramen.
Schwannoma or meningioma may also occur around jugular foramen schwannoma typically features smooth bony remodeling while meningioma typically causes hyperostosis.
