---
title: "What is the N-staging for lung cancer?"
---
N0 = no local regional LN mets
N1 = ipsilateral peribronchial or hilar Nodes
N2 = ipsilateral mediastinal or subcarinal nodes
N3 = ipsilateral /contralateral scalene or SUPRACLAVICULAR nodes or contralateral mediastinal/hilar nodes

*only N0, N1, and N2 benefit from SURGERY
