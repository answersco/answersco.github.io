---
title: "War of 1812"
---
A war (1812-1814) between the United States and England which was trying to interfere with American trade with France.

War between Britain and US over British violation of US Maritime Rights. The war included numerous Native American conflicts as was as conflicts in the Great Lakes/Canada. This war ended with the Treaty of Ghent.

These major events are part of what war: Burning down the White House, burning down York, British blockading the Atlantic Ocean
