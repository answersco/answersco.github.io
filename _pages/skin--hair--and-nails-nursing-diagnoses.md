---
title: "Skin, hair, and nails nursing diagnoses"
---
1) risk for impaired skin integrity if Braden scale is low
2) impaired skin integrity -- becomes actual problem
3) impaired tissue integrity -- may be due to pt who is incontinent
4) bowl incontinence
5) ineffective individual coping -- a women with hirsutism or someone losing their hair
6) self-mutilation
7) disturbed body image: how do they feel about their disease
