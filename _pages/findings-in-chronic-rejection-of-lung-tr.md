---
title: "Findings in chronic rejection of lung transplant (&gt; 4 months)"
---
Bronchiolitis obliterans
Affects 50% at 5 years
Bronchiectasis, bronchial wall thickening, air-trapping, interlobular septal thickening.
Air trapping at expiration at or after 6 moths = chronic rejection.

COP may occur with chronic or acute rejection, and responds to steroids.
Upper lobe fibrosis may also be associated with chronic rejection.
