---
title: "Wound assessment"
---
1) identify the type of wound
-surgical
-traumatic
-pressure
-burn 
-other (dermatitis, vascular, arterial, etc.)
2) identify the location using anatomical location or bony prominence
3) remove dressing and examine wound
-visual and olfactory assessment
4) observe for drainage, signs of infection, signs of healing
5) determine size of wound, especially important for pressure ulcers
-measure length (head to toe), width (left to right), depth
-note any tunneling, undermining
6) observe the wound edges and surrounding skin
