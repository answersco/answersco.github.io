---
title: "Recordable and reportable events"
---
Limit for difference is prescribed and administered dose is 20% from NRC
Medical event (reportable): 2 criteria
1. F it up: wrong dose (&gt;20%), route, patient
-Or patient receives dose to part of body other than intended site that exceeds 50% the dose expected.
2. Harm: whole body dose &gt; 5 rem or single organ dose &gt; 50 rem.
-Must call NRC within 24 hours, letter to NRC in 15 days, and doctor within 24 hours (you or doc calls patient.).
-Keep recorded for 3 years

Recordable event: &lt; 5 rem whole body dose or single organ dose &lt; 50 rem
Requires institutional review and recorded locally
