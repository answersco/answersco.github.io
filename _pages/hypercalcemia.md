title: "Hypercalcemia"
---
diabetic bone scan w lung, cardiac and knee

-parathyroids are the regulatory gland for calcium, renal failure, pancreatic issues, wide spread organ dysfunction
-usually nauseous, can have constipation, slurred speech, confusion

-excessive calcium in blood
-desensitizes nervous and muscular systems
-muscles weaken, sluggish reflexes, emotional disturbances, cardiac arrest in extreme cases
-rare b/c if there's too much it'll just go back into blood
