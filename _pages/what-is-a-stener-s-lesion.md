title: "What is a Stener's Lesion?"
---
Avulsion of base of first proximal phalanx assoc with ulnar collateral ligament disruption in Gamekeeper's thumb

Stener Lesion = Adductor tendon gets caught in torn edges of the UCL and displaced ligament won't heal correctly --&gt; surgery

* Avoid stress views so you don't cause a Stener
