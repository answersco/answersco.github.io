---
title: "Internal and external iliac artery branches:"
---
Internal iliac artery branches:
Anterior division: supplies most of pelvic viscera
Inferior/middle rectal artery: anastomoses with IMA via pathway of Winslow
Uterine artery (females)
Obturator artery
Inferior gluteal artery

Posterior division: supplies musculature of pelvic and gluteal regions 
Lateral sacral artery
Iliolumbar artery (anastomoses with external iliac artery via the deep circumflex iliac artery)
Superior gluteal artery

External iliac artery branches: 
Inferior epigastric artery: anastomoses with the superior epigastric artery
Deep circumflex iliac artery: anastomoses with the internal iliac via the iliolumbar artery
Femoral artery
