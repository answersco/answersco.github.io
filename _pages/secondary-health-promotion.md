---
title: "Secondary health promotion"
---
-focus on screening for early detection of disease with prompt diagnosis and treatment of any found
-goal is to identify an illness, reverse or reduce its severity or provide a cure, thereby return the person to maximum health as quickly as possible

EX.
-assessing children for normal growth and development and encouraging regular medical, dental and vision examinations.
-screenings: BP, cholesterol and skin cancer
-recommending mammograms for women at appropriate ages
