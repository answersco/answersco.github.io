---
title: "Endolymphatic Sac Tumor: - what is it? - when you see this, think of ____ - imaging findings on CT and MRI and Angio"
---
- rare tumor of the endolymphatic sac and duct
- think VHL immediately

CT: amorphous calcifications
MRI: T2 bright, intense enhancement, flow voids
Angio: tumor blush
