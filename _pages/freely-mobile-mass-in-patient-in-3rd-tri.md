Lactational adenoma
Clinical: present in 2nd or 3rd trimester, or postpartum period
Freely-mobile mass, which may be tender
Benign&#8594; regresses when lactation ends.
MM&#8594; circumscribed, oval, isodense mass
US&#8594; circumscribed, hypoechoic, macrolobulated, parallel
