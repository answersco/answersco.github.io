title: "Jean-Jacques Rousseau"
---
A French man who believed that Human beings are naturally good &amp; free &amp; can rely on their instincts. Government should exist to protect common good, and be a democracy. In his &quot;Social Contract&quot; he explained an ideal society where each community member would vote on issues and majority would become one law.
