---
title: "Which of the following is not a sign of constrictive pericarditis? A) Pericardium thickness &gt;3mm B) Lack of pericardial slippage on tagged images C) Right ventricular dilation D) Right atrial dilation E) Diastolic septal bounce"
---
C. Right ventricular dilation. The other choices are typical findings of constrictive pericarditis.
