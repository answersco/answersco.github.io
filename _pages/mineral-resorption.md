title: "Mineral resorption"
---
-dissolve bone, release calcium phosphorus, and other ions into blood
-works by osteoclasts at ruffled border where bone is broken down
-Hydrogen pumped into space at bone surface and chlorine is electrically attracted creating a thin layer of hydrochloric acid that helps dissolve bone
-enzymes called acid phosphatases break down the collagen
EX. orthodontic appliances reposition teeth through resorption and deposit
