---
title: "Neglectful"
---
-parents have no control, no demands, emotionally detached
-may even reject the child
-are involved in physical/personal needs such as food, shelter, clothes but do not encourage, motivate, or play with them
-parents who are depressed often show this parenting style
-children feel unloved, detached, and decrease their cognitive and physical development, decreased self-esteem, decreased self-confidence
