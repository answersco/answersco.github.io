---
title: "Vascular products of each branchial arch"
---
Between 6th and 8th weeks, specific arches appear and regress, resulting in single aorta with 3 major noncoronary branches.
1st arch: forms maxillary and external carotid arteries
2nd arch: forms stapedial arteries
3rd arch: forms carotid arteries
4th arch: forms Ao arch and proximal subclavian arteries
5th arch: present in only 50% of fetuses, and regresses completely
6th arch: forms pulmonary arteries.
