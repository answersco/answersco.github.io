---
title: "What are the classic findings of VHL?"
---
Autosomal dominant familial syndrome with hemangioblastomas (HGBLs), clear cell renal carcinoma, cystadenomas, pheochromocytomas.
T2 bright cystic lesions with septations and nodules.
Location: Cerebellum 75%; Spinal cord (dorsal) 50%; Medulla 25%.
