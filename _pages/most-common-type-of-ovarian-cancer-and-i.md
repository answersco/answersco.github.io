---
title: "Most common type of ovarian cancer and imaging finding?"
---
Ovarian Serous Cystadenocarcinoma
- Unilocular w/ FEWER separations (frequently bilateral, with papillary projections suggesting malignancy)

- look for ascites (peritoneal involvement)


* Mucinous cystadenocarcinoma is Multi-locular (more septations) with FEWER papillary projections
