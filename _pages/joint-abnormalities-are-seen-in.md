---
title: "Joint abnormalities are seen in _______% of patients with SLE; the key radiographic finding of SLE is __________"
---
90% of patients with SLE have joint abnormalities

The key finding is REDUCIBLE subluxations of the MCPs and PIPs. 

MEANING - alignment is normal on a PA view when the hands are compressed against the radiographic plate, but, subluxations become apparent in the NORGAARD (&quot;ballcatcher's&quot; or &quot;you're in good hands with Allstate&quot;), OR the oblique views then the hand is not constrained
