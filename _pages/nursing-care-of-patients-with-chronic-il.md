title: "Nursing care of patients with chronic illness"
---
-Identify specific problems (trajectory phase)

-Establish and prioritize goals

-Defining plan of action and achieve desired outcomes

-Implement plan and interventions

-Follow up and evaluate outcomes

-Important concepts: b/c most care for pt is by the pt, caregiver, etc., in the home -- how the person will manage their problem
(home, community-based, transitional care, education, promotion of self-care)
