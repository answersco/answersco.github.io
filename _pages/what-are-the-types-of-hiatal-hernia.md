---
title: "What are the types of hiatal hernia?"
---
Surgical classification:
Type 1: GE jxn and gastric cardia intrathoracic (sliding)
Type 2: GE jxn normal, gastric fundus intrathoracic (paraesophageal)
Type 3: Both GE jxn and fundus in chest (paraesophageal)
Type 4: GE jxn and entire stomach in chest (paraesophageal)
