---
title: "Transmission of HIV"
---
-virus found in body fluids
-transmissible levels in: semen, blood, vaginal secretions, breast milk

Modes of transmission:
-sexual contact with infected person
-IV drug use with needle sharing
-passage from infected mother to infant 
-receipt of blood transfusions, blood product, organs from an infected person (products now screened - rate of transmission extremely low in developed countries)

NOT modes of transmission:
-casual contact, mosquito
