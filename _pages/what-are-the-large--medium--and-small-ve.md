---
title: "What are the Large, medium, and small vessel vasculitides?"
---
Large-vessel: Takayasu (&lt;20yo), giant cell arteritis (&gt;50yo)
Medium-sized: Polyarteritis nodosa (PAN), Kawasaki disease (coronary)
Small-vessel: Wegener granulomatosis, Churg-Strauss disease, microscopic polyangiitis, Henoch-Sch&#246;nlein purpura, lupus vasculitis, Beh&#231;et disease
