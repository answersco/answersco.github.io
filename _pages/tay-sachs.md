---
title: "Tay-Sachs"
---
-fatal neurologic disease
-baby will demonstrate signs and symptoms at six months
-has to do with the inability to break down enzymes or fatty substances
-fatty substances accumulate along nerve fibers and become toxic
-will damage all the nerve cells of the body
-child begins to lose function and see signs of blindness, deafness, and even paralysis
-results in death by age four and there is no cure
-affects Jewish communities a lot
-recommended genetic testing and counseling

patients lack an enzyme that should break down lipids --&gt; lipids accumulate in the brain and lead to neuro problems
