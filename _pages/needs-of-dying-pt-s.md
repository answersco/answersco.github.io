---
title: "Needs of dying pt's"
---
1) Physiological needs: 
-physical care (hygiene, pain control, nutrition)
2) Psychological needs: 
-sense of control over fear of the unknown and separation from loved ones 
-loss of dignity
-pain management
-coping with unfinished business &amp; isolation
3) Spiritual needs: 
-love and relatedness 
-forgiveness and hope 
-exploration of meaning and purpose
