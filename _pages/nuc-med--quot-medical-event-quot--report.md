---
title: "Nuc Med &quot;medical event&quot; reporting requirements?"
---
Reported verbally by telephone to the NRC within 1 calendar day after the medical event was discovered, and a written report must be submitted to the NRC and the referring physician within 15 days. In addition, the licensee must notify the referring physician within 24 hours, and must notify the patient unless (a) the referring physician informs the licensee that he or she will notify the patient, or (b) in the judgment of the referring physician, it would be harmful to notify the patient.
