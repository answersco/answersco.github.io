title: "Peutz-Jeghers syndrome:"
---
Peutz-Jeghers: AD syndrome with multiple hamartomatous pedunculated polyps, usually in SB&#8594; may cause intussusception.
Skin findings include perioral pigmented spots on lips/gums
Gynecologic neoplasms, as well as gastric, duodenal, and colonic malignancies

Hamartomatous polyps of the small bowel
Mucucutaneous pigmentation
Small and large bowel cancer
Pancreatic adenocarcinoma
Gynecologic cancer
Sertoli cell testicular tumors
