---
title: "Which sutures fuse in bracicephaly vs plagiocephaly/dolichocephaly?"
---
Brachycephaly = Coronal (Wide head)
- affects Girls (Ann Brown)

Scaphocephaly/Dolichocephaly = Sagittal suture (most common) = Long skinny head
- affects Boys (Cody Hargrove)

Turricephaly (&quot;tower&quot;) = both coronal and lambdoid fusion

Metopic suture closer = Trigonocephaly
