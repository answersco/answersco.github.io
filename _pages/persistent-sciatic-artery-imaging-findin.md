---
title: "Persistent sciatic artery imaging findings"
---
-Internal iliac artery continues as sciatic artery and then as popliteal artery
-Hypoplastic external iliac, common femoral, superficial femoral arteries
-Two types, based on degree of hypoplasia of femoral arterial system
-Aneurysms typically occur under the gluteus maximus muscle at level of greater trochanter
