---
title: "Extralobar sequestration"
---
Neonates
Extralobar, has own pleura
ALWAYS airless unless communicates with GI tract
Supply: thoracic or abdominal aorta
Drainage: Systemic: IVC, azygos, portal
Assd with diaphragm defect, pulmonary hypoplasia, cardiac anomaly, bronchogenic cyst

contained within its own little envelope of pleura.

No pleura, presents younger, drains to systemic vein
