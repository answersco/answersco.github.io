---
title: "What attaches to pubic symphysis &amp; ischial tuberosities?"
---
Pubic Symphysis = Adductor muscles (adductor longus, the anterior part of the adductor magnus, the pectineus, and the gracilis)

Ischial tuberosity = Hamstrings (Semimembranous, semitendinosus, Biceps femoris = medial to lateral)
