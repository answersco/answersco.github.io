---
title: "Enchondroma features?"
---
Contain calcified chondroid matrix, **except when in phalanges. May cause endosteal scalloping. No periostitis. Difficult to distinguish from chondrosarcoma (just say low grade chondroid lesion if you see one).
Ollier disease (multiple enchondromas (Only)). 
Maffucci disease (multiple enchondromas with soft-tissue hemangiomas - phleboliths!) (More stuff).
