---
title: "Immunotherapy"
---
-medical treatment used to amplify or redirect the immune response
-monoclonal antibodies (MAb) 
-useful for detecting and targeting one particular antigen 
-in 1975, British researchers devised a technology which mass-produces a single B cell (activated B cell fused to cancer cell = hybridoma)
-thus, preserving its specificity and amplifying its antibody type
