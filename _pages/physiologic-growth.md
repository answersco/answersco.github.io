---
title: "Physiologic growth"
---
There can be a periostitis related to physiologic growth
This shoudl ALWAYS occur around 3 months and resolve by 6 months
PROXIMAL involvement before DISTAL i.e. always happens in the FEMURS before the TIBIA
ALWAYS involves the diaphysis

NOT physiologic IF
1. Before 1 month age
2. Tibia before femur
3. Does not involve the diaphysis
