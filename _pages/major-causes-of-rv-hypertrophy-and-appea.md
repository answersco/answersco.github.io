---
title: "Major causes of RV hypertrophy and appearance in pulmonic stenosis:"
---
RVH: thickening of the RV wall of &gt; 6mm, measured in end-diastole.
Most common causes: pulmonary arterial HTN, pulmonic valve stenosis, and intracardiac shunt.

Pulmonic valve stenosis: characteristic enlargement of the main and left pulmonary arteries with relative sparing of the RPA.
