---
title: "Stage 1 pressure ulcer"
---
-persistent red, blue, or purple tones; no open skin area
-T&amp;P, barrier cream, pay attention to nutrition, moisture, protective dressings

-non-blanchable erythema of intact skin
-patient may complain of pain or itching at site
-look for difference in temperature, color, or sensation
-reversible within couple hours if pressure is relieved
-turn them, put heels on pillow, may apply dressing
-reddened area means capillaries have started to breakdown in that area so massaging the areas accelerates the breakdown
