---
title: "What is macrocrania?"
---
Aka macrocephaly. Head circumference &gt;95%. Seen with prominent Subarachnoid space. Dx by prominent extraaxial CSF spaces (normal is &lt;5mm), no mass effect, and bridging veins. Can be a normal variant called normal infantile subarachnoid spaces, benign external hydrocephalus, or benign extracerebral collections.
