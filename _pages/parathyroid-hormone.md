---
title: "Parathyroid hormone"
---
-located: osteoclasts in bone
-function: increase blood calcium levels by stimulating osteoclasts to break down bone matrix

-increase osteoclasts which breaks down food
-increase calcium resorption from digestion
-kidneys make calcitrol to increase rate of calcium resorption from gut
-not directly under pituitary control but responds to blood calcium levels
