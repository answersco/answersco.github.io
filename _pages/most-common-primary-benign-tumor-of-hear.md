---
title: "Most common primary benign tumor of heart and location?"
---
LEFT ATRIAL myxoma (attached at the septum)
- associated with MEN, Blue Nevi, and Carney Complex 
- T1 dark and T2 bright (due to myxomatous content!)
- Enhance with Gd and may calcify
- on CT, homogeneously hypodense
- Surgery to treat
