---
title: "Metered dose inhaler"
---
(MDI)
1) remove cover/shake inhaler.
2) have patient take deep breath/exhale.
3) hold inhaler 1-2 inches away from mouth, inhale slowly and depress canister.
4) instruct patient to hold breath for 5-10 seconds, then exhale through pursed lips. Wait 1-5 minutes before second puff (if ordered). If using a spacer, have patient place mouth on canister mouthpiece, depress canister into spacer, inhale slowly and breath through mouth.
