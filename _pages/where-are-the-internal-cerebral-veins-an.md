---
title: "Where are the internal cerebral veins and Vein of Galen on sagittal and axial images?"
---
ICV: formed from choroidal/septal and thalamostriate veins.
-Paramedian, and runs posteriorly in the roof of the 3rd ventricle.

ICV is joined by 2 basal veins of Rosenthal to form the vein of Galen under the splenium of the CC in the quadrigeminal cistern.

Vein of Galen joins the inferior sagittal sinus to form the straight sinus.
