---
title: "Imaging findings of DIG (desmoplastic infantile ganglioglioma)?"
---
Basically a PXA in an infant!

* Large cystic tumor involving superficial cerebral cortex and leptomeninges with DURAL TAIL!

* ALWAYS SUPRATENTORIAL and involving &gt;1 lobe
- Classically &quot;rapidly expanding head circumference&quot;

* Age &lt; 1 yo
