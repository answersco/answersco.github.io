---
title: "Terminal branches of brachial artery"
---
Radial artery: 
-Anomalous high origin from brachial or axillary artery in 15%

Ulnar artery (anomalous high origin in 3%)
Interosseous arteries
-Usually only the radial and ulnar arteries continue into the hand

Interosseous artery: divides into anterior and posterior branches
-&lt;2% it may continue into hand as median artery.
