---
title: "What does fibrocystic change look like?"
---
PREmenopausal women, most commonly with mastalgia, (focal/regional/or diffuse)
- It is not an indication for diagnostic mammography in absence of focal symptoms or imaging abnormalities
- Look for heterogenous dense parechyma with partially circumscribed masses (likely cysts); tea cup or round calcs in mulitple lobes
- no discernable mass
- small cysts in mammary zone on US
