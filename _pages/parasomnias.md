---
title: "Parasomnias"
---
-periods/patterns of wakefulness during sleep 

includes: 
1) somnambulism: sleep walking
2) REM behavior disorder (RBD): acting out dreams
3) sleep terrors: occur in deepest sleep stage
-mostly in children; sit up screaming but not able to reason; no other interventions than to keep them safe
4) nightmares: vivid and disturbing dreams
5) bruxism: teeth grinding
6) enuresis: bed wetting
7) sleep-related eating disorder: consumes food but has no recollection
