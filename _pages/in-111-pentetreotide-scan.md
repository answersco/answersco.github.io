---
title: "In-111 pentetreotide Scan?"
---
Octcreotide is an analogue to somatostatin: neuroendocrine tumors including carcinoid, gastrinoma, insulinomas, neuroblastomas, pituitary adenomas and medullary thyroid CA. Benign meningiomas are also seen. Normal uptake in normal pituitary, thyroid, liver, spleen, bladder, and bowel are also seen. Excretion is renal.
