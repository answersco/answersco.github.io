---
title: "Midgut Volvulus"
---
Malrotation: Abnormal position of duodenojejunal junction
Abnormal position of cecum by small bowel follow-through or barium enema
Volvulus: Corkscrew or Z-shaped appearance of duodenum, which does not cross to left of midline
US: Nonretroperitoneal location of 3rd portion of duodenum (not between aorta and SMA) suggestive of malrotation
Retroperitoneal location (between aorta and SMA) helpful in excluding diagnosis
