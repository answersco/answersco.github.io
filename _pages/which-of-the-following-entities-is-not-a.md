---
title: "Which of the following entities is not a cyanotic heart disease? A) Tricuspid atresia B) Tetralogy of Fallot C) Atrial Septal Defect D) Truncus arteriosus E)Transposition of the Great Arteries"
---
C. Atrial Septal Defect. ASD is an acyanotic heart disease; oxygenated blood from the left atrium goes through a defect in the septal wall to enter the right atrium. The other conditions listed are all cyanotic heart diseases.
