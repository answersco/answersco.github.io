---
title: "Paget's involvement of the skull"
---
Can have osteolysis as a well-defined, large, radiolucent area favoring the frontal and occipital bones.
Inner table affected more than outer table
&quot;Osteolysis circumscripta&quot;

Sclerotic phase: thickened, expanded skull

Complications:
-Deafness (most common)
-Cranial nerve paresis
-Basilar invagination --&gt; hydrocephalus --&gt; brainstem compression
-Secondary (high-grade) osteosarcoma.
