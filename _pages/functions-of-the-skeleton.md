---
title: "Functions of the skeleton"
---
1) support
2) protection -- spinal cord, brain, heart, lungs
3) movement -- anchor for muscles
4) electrolyte balance -- storage center for calcium and phosphorus: can break down bone or add extra to them
5) acid-base balance -- buffers blood from large changes by altering phosphate and carbonic salts
6) blood formation -- red marrow is chief produced of blood cells
