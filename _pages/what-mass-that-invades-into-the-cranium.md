---
title: "What mass that invades into the cranium classically has marginal cysts (between mass and normal brain)?"
---
Esthesioneuroblastoma (ENB) &#8594; commonly involves both the nasal cavity and anterior cranial fossa with a waist as it passes through the anterior skull base
Highly vascular with avid enhancement, bone destruction, and areas of less aggressive expansion and remodeling.
Presence of cysts at the interface between intracranial tumor and brain parenchyma (marginal cysts) is characteristic
