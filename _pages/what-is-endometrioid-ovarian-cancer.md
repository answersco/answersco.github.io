---
title: "What is endometrioid ovarian cancer?"
---
2nd most common (serous &gt; endometrioid &gt; mucinous)

Often a combination of both endometrial and ovarian cancer (concomitant in 25%; endometrial is the primary and ovary is a met)

- Granulosa theca cell tumor (produces estrogen)

- Can be 2/2 endometrioma conversion into endometrioid

- 15% bilateral
