---
title: "Kohler's AVN? Freiberg Infraction? 'Severs disease? Panner's disease?"
---
Kohler's: Navicular bone in boys age 4-6 (no surgery)

Freibergs: AVN of 2nd metatarsal head (adolescent girls --&gt; can lead to secondary OA)

Severs: AVN of the calcaneal apophysis (&quot;growing pain&quot;)
- Think of Severus Snape's heels!

Panner's: AVN of the capitellum (humerus part that articulates with the radial head) - in a 5-10 yo throwing kid
