---
title: "FAP and variants:"
---
Familial adenomatous polyposis (FAP): AD syndrome with innumerable premalignant adenomatous polyps in colon &gt; SB
Prophylactic colectomy to prevent cancer
Gastric polyps are hyperplastic, and not premalignant
Gardner syndrome: variant of FAP, with addition of: DOPE
Desmoid tumors
Osteomas
Papillary thyroid cancer
Epidermoid cysts
Turcot syndrome: another variant of FAP, with addition of CNS tumors (gliomas and medulloblastoma)
