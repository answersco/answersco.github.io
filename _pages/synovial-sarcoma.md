title: "Synovial Sarcoma"
---
NEVER in the joint (They are formed from synovial cells hence the name)
<b>Buzzword</b> Ball like tumor in the extremity of a young adult OR Soft tissue tumor in the foot of a young adult
Soft tissue calcifications + bone erosions is highly suggestive

For MCQ, never involves the joint. Can show as a baker's cyst looking thing thats way too complex and not between semiM and medial gastroc
