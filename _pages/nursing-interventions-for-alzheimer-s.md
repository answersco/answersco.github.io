---
title: "Nursing interventions for alzheimer's"
---
1) Cognitive enhancement techniques and social engagement

2) Avoid the use of restraints, both physical and chemical 

3) Maximize functional capacity

4) Address behavioral issues

5) Ensure a therapeutic and safe environment
-Adequate rest/sleep, fluid/nutrition, elimination, pain control/comfort measures

6) Encourage and support advance care planning
-Appropriate end-of-life care in terminal phase - best the pt's wishes are documented and is hard to think about when they will not be needing those wishes for another twenty years
-Provide caregiver education and support as well as pt's education and support 
-Integrate community resources
