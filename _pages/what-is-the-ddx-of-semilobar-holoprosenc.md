---
title: "What is the ddx of semilobar holoprosencephaly?"
---
Partial agenesis of the corpus callosum. In partial agenesis the genu and anterior body form, but the splenium and rostrum don't. In semilobar the splenium and body forms but the genu does not. And in syntelencephaly the middle portion does not form but the genu and rostrom do.
