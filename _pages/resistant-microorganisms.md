title: "Resistant microorganisms"
---
-mutated to develop resistant to one or more antimicrobial drugs -- probably only one drug left that treats these diseases well
*can prevent spread through strict handwashing and correct use of gloves
1) MRSA (Methacillin-resistant Staphylococcus aureus)
2) VRE (Vancomycin-resistant enterococci)
3) VISA (Vancomycin-intermediate Staphylococcus aureus)
