---
title: "What is LV noncompaction?"
---
- Genetically LOOSELY PACKED MYOCARDIUM (e.g. looks the same in systole &amp; diastole)
- LV with spongy apperance and increased trabeculations with deep intertrabecular recesses
- Noncompacted diastolic myocardium is 2.3x thicker than Compacted diastolic myocardium by MRI
- Systolic 2:1 ratio by ECHO
