---
title: "Normal position of IABP"
---
Tip should be located just distal to the takeoff of the left subclavian artery and 2-4 cm below the aortic knob. Inflation may be seen during diastole.

Complications: 
CVA (positioned too high)
Renal or mesenteric ischemia (too low)
Limb ischemia, infection
