---
title: "Endovascular treatment of thoracic aortic dissection:"
---
Fenestration: puncturing across the intimal flap, which creates a rent between the false and true lumina.

Stents: can be used to tack town the intimal flap or revascularize compromised branch vessels.

Stent-grafts: have been used to seal the proximal entry point of the flap.
