Stenosis/occlusion of proximal Left SCA, leads to reversal of flow in LVA to perfuse L arm. Left side (85%). US is dx with grade 0 normal VA flow, grade 1 blunted PSV in L VA, grade 2 biphasic flow, grade 3 severe with reversal of flow in VA to L SCA. Athero is most common cause with ddx of vasculitis, dissection, trauma, or vessel compression from adjacent neoplastic mass. Tx is bypass stenosis.
