---
title: "What are the frequencies of the three forms of holoprosencephaly and the prognosis?"
---
Alobar 64%, semilobar 24% and lobar 12% (from most severe to least severe). Worst prognosis for alobar with very high mortality especially if associated with chromosomal abnormalities (2% 1 yr survival). Without chromosomal abnormalities the survival is 35-50% at 1yr.
