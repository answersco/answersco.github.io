---
title: "Twin twin transfusion syndrome"
---
Mono chorion. Mono or diamniotic. Disproportionate bloodflow. Donor small with oligohydramnios. Recipient larger with polyhydramnios. Diagnosis:1-fetal sizes 2-amniotic fluid 3-mono chorionic. Treat with laser ablation AVF, therapeutic amniocentesis, selective coag of umbilical cord to less viable fetus
