---
title: "Treatment and prevention of HIV"
---
Prevention
-testing
-both screening and definitive tests - sometimes flags someone with having HIV when they do not so the definitive test is to double check that they are infected by the virus
-education - sexual education like using condoms
-vaccine - under development

Treatment - identify early b/c there is a better chance of treating the infection and keeping the patient from getting to the end stage
1) treatment of opportunistic infections: symptomatic - treat symptoms and passive immunization (immunoglobulin) 
2) antiviral drugs: Combination Therapy: Nitrogenous base analogs + protease inhibitors
-newest drugs block attachment or integration of viral DNA into host chromosome
