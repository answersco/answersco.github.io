---
title: "Indications for stent placement in aorta and iliac arteries"
---
Failed angioplasty (residual pressure gradient &gt; 10 mmHg at rest, 15 mmHg with distal vasodilatation, or residual stenosis &gt;/= 30%).
CIA origin lesions
Recurrent stenosis
Anastomotic stenosis
Occlusive dissection flap
Recanalization of total occlusion
Ulcerated plaque
Planned distal revascularization procedure
