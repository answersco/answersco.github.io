---
title: "What is the tumor most often arising in the fossa of rosenmueller?"
---
Nasopharyngeal carcinoma, strongly assoc with EBV. FOR is lateral pharyngeal recess. Keratinizing vs non-keratinizing. Non has better survival at 75% -5 y. Most often sx with met nodal disease, clogged hearing, epistaxis. MR is used for staging.
