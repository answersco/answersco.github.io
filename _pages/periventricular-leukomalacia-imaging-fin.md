---
title: "Periventricular leukomalacia imaging findings and time frame? Morbidity?"
---
Cystic areas in a periventricular distribution (seen 1-3 weeks post injury)

Occurs weeks after hypoxic ischemic injury due to impaired perfusion
- Risk factors = Premature and low birth wt &lt; 1500 g

Significant morbidity (50% develop CP) :-(
