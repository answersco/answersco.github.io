---
title: "Regions where the arachnoid projects into the venous system allowing for CSF to be reabsorbed = _____ - imaging characterstics on CT and MRI"
---
arachnoid granulations
- CT: hypodense and round or oval (clot will be linear)
- MRI: T2 bright and bright on Flair (can scallop the inner table from CSF pulsation)
