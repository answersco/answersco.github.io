---
title: "M (metastasis) staging of lung cancer:"
---
M (metastasis):
M0 = no metastatic disease
M1a: local thoracic metastatic disease (includes separate tumor nodule in contralateral lung, and malignant pleural or pericardial effusion, with 5 year survival of 2-3%)
M1b: distant or extrathoracic metastatic disease (6 months median survival; 22% 1-year survival).
