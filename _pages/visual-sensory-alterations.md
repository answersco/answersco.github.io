---
title: "Visual sensory alterations"
---
1) Presbyopia: unable to see closely, happens at age 40l normal alteration
2) Cataract: can be surgically repaired
3) Glaucoma: irreversible from intraocular pressure damage
4) Retinopathy: non-inflammatory damage to retina, mostly caused by diabetes
5) Macular Degeneration: loss of vision in center of vision field
