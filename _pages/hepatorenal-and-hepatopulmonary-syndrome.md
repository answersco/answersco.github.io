---
title: "Hepatorenal and hepatopulmonary syndromes"
---
Hepatorenal syndrome: renal failure in patients with advanced hepatic failure and portal HTN
-Characterized by very low GFR in absence of other causes of renal impairment.

Hepatopulmonary syndrome: triad of liver disease, hypoxemia due to right-to-left shunting, and intrapulmonary vascular dilations.
-Desaturate or become hypoxic when changing from supine to upright.
