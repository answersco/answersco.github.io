---
title: "Staging of RCC- Robson system."
---
1- Confined to renal capsule. 2- Tumor extends out of renal capsule but remaines confined within Gerotas Fascia. 3- Vascular or lymph node involvement. 3A- Renal vein or IVC. 3B- Lymph Node. 3C- Both. 4A- Tumor growth into Gerotas Fascia. 4B- Distant mets.
