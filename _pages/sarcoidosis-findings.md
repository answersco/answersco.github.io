---
title: "Sarcoidosis findings?"
---
Upper lobe predominant, Perilymphatic nodules (&quot;SSL&quot;); later on can see upper lobe fibrosis and traction bronchiectasis
- lambda sign = 1-2-3 sign (1-2-3 sign (b/l hilar LAD and R paratracheal LAD)) on Gallium scan
CT galaxy sign = upper lobe masses (conglomerate of nodules) w/ satellite nodules
