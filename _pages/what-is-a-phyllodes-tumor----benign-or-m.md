---
title: "What is a phyllodes tumor? - benign or malignant - what age? - features (4)"
---
fast growing breast mass that needs wide margins on resection
- benign but malignant degeneration risk is about 10% (so usually resected)
- usually 40-50 yr olds

Features:
- rapid growth
- hematogenous mets
- middle age to older woman
- mimics fibroadenoma
