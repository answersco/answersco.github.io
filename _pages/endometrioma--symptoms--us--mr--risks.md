---
title: "Endometrioma -symptoms -US -MR -risks"
---
-infertility, dysmenorrhea, dyspareunia

US: 
-round mass with homogeneous internal echoes
-INCREASED through transmission
-can look like hemorrhagic cyst (fluid-fluid levels, septations)
-does not go away or change on follow-up (vs hemorrhagic cyst)

MR: T1 bright, T2 shading (dark from iron), peripheral enhancement

-complications: bowel obstruction, infertility

-risk of endometrioid or clear cell carcinoma if
&gt; 45 yo OR &gt;6-9 cm
