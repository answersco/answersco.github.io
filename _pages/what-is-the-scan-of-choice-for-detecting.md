---
title: "What is the scan of choice for detecting a Meckel's diverticulum? What drug is also used for the scan? What is the sens/spec? False pos/False neg?"
---
Tc-99m pertechnetate scan. Cimetidine is used to augment accumulation in gastric mucosa. Sens 85% spec 95%. False positive Ddx urinary tract uptake, IBD, abscess, appendicitis, tumors, GI duplications. False negative due to small size/lack of gastric mucosa.
