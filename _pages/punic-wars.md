---
title: "Punic Wars"
---
Wars between the Romans and Carthaginians that marked Rome as the preeminent power in the eastern as well as the western Mediterranean.

a series of conflicts between Rome and Carthage over control of the trade routes in the Mediterranean Sea

Three major wars between Rome and Carthage in North Africa, fought between 264 and 146 B.C.E., that culminated in Roman victory and control of the western Mediterranean.
