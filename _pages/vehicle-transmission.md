---
title: "Vehicle transmission"
---
through food, water, drugs or devices such as IV bags and blood

-spread of disease from host to host by means of a non-living substance (vehicle) acting to transport the pathogen.
-type of vehicles: air, food, and water
-does not have to be in close proximity
-sources of pathogen:
1) infected organism sheds the pathogen: contamination of vehicle may be from either human or animal (zoonotic) source
2) inanimate object sheds the pathogen: air conditioning systems, fungi growing on walls, spores contaminate the air, then person breathes in the spores
EX. Stachybotrys
