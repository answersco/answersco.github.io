The method used by Caresteam for preprocessing is known as ___

-contraction of small intestine
-purpose is to mix and churn, not to move material along as in peristalsis
-mix enzymes/bile with chyme
-neutralize pH
-bring chyme in contact with mucosa for absorption
-distention (pressure on wall) triggers myenteric plexus, initiates process
-circular muscles contract, relax, controlled by pacemaker cells (9-12 contractions per min.)
