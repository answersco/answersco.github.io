---
title: "Physiology of sleep"
---
1) reticular activating system (RAS) &amp; Bulbar synchronizing region
-facilitates reflex and voluntary movements
-controls cortical activities related to state of alertness
-RAS extends upward through the medulla, the pons, the midbrain into the hypothalmus
2) hypothalamus
-control centers located here for sleeping and waking
3) interaction or neurotransmitters: important for excitation of CNS (serotonin, dopamine, histamine, acetylcholine, &amp; norepinephrine)
