---
title: "What is important about the first pre-radiation mammogram?"
---
Pre-radiation mammogram is important to identify residual disease
- *if you identify residual disease AFTER radiation therapy --&gt; you've condemned patient to mastectomy

Post radiation changes (skin thickening and trabecular thickening) usually get better after first post-RT mammogram

- if see WORESENING features, think of RECURRENT DISEASE, even inflammatory breast Ca
