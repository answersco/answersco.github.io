title: "Juvenile pilocytic astrocytoma (JPA)"
---
-posterior fossa in kids
-cystic mass with enhancing nodule
-can compress 4th ventricle
-can occur optic pathway in NF1

kid w/ cyst &amp; nodule, grade 1 BUT nodule enhances, posterior fossa or optic chiasm, optic gliomas in NF-1 are pilocytic astrocytomas that stop growing at puberty
