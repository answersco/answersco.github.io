---
title: "What are the quadriceps and hamstring muscles?"
---
Quad: Vastus medialis, lateralis, and intermedius
Rectus femoris

Hamstring: 
Semitendinosus: originates on ischial tuberosity and inserts on medial surfaceo f tibia
Semimembranosus: originates from ischial tuberosity, inserts on medial tibial condyle
Biceps femoris (long head): originates from ischial tuberosity, inserts on lateral side of head of fibula
Biceps femoris (short head): originates form linea aspera and lateral supracondylar line of the femur, inserts on lateral side of the head of the fibula (common tendon with long head).
