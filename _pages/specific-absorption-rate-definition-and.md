---
title: "Specific absorption rate definition and is proportional to?"
---
SAR = Average RF energy deposited in the body and converted to heat per unit mass &amp; time

SAR = B0^2 <b> Flip angle^2 </b>(1/TR) 

- Duty cycle = cool down; inversely related to TR
- If you double TR, then you half the duty cycle and half your SAR
