---
title: "Localizing breast lesions (seen on MLO view)"
---
If lesion is seen on the MLO view and on the LATERAL side of a CC view then this will be INFERIOR on a ML ('Lead sinks&quot;)
If lesions is seen on the MLO view and on the MEDIAL side of the CC view then this will be SUPERIOR on the ML (&quot;muffins rise&quot;)
