title: "Epididymitis"
---
-chlamydia/gonrrhea/E.coli UTI (older)
-enlarged, hyperemic
-acute scrotal pain

Inflammation of the epididymis
- Way in which it spreads: tail = most common effected (this is where it starts), then spreads to body and head
Hyperemia and increased size are what you're looking for

Frequently will see a reactive hydrocele as well as scrotal wall thickening
