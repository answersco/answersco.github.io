---
title: "Preclinical trials"
---
1) chemicals that may have therapeutic value are tested on laboratory animals
-purpose of this is to determine whether they have the effect on living tissue and to evaluate adverse effects
2) some chemicals are discarded at this stage:
-chemical does not have therapeutic activity
-chemical is too toxic - EX. subject is sent into liver or heart failure
-chemical is highly teratogenic
-safety margins are small
