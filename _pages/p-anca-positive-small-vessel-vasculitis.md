---
title: "P-ANCA positive small vessel vasculitis"
---
Churg Strauss (75%) and microscopic polyangiitis (80%)

Churg-Strauss: necrotizing pulmonary vasculitis in the spectrum of eosinophilic lung disease.
-Always have asthma and eosinophila.
-Transient peripheral lung consolitation or GG regions most commonly seen.
-Cavitation is rare (favors Wegeners)

Microscopic polyangiitis: affects kidneys and lungs
-1/3 with diffuse pulmonary hemorrhage.
