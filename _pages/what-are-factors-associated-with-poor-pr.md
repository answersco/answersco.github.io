title: "What are factors associated with poor prognosis/miscarriage of PGH?"
---
10-15% spontaneous abortion rate with any PGH
If living embryo + small PGH then &gt; 90% pregnancy success
If large PGH then 20% loss rate if living embryo seen
if any PGH and bradycardia then 80% loss rate
Cervical os dilatation &#8594; near 100% loss rate
