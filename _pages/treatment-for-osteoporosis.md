---
title: "Treatment for osteoporosis"
---
1) estrogen replacement therapy (ert): slows bone resorption, but increases risk of breast cancer, stroke, and heart disease

2) drugs like Fosamax and Actonel destroy osteoclasts

3) PTH slows bone loss is given by daily injection -- Forteo (PTH derivative) increases density by 10% in one year but may promote bone cancer so treatment is limited to just two years

4) treatment: best treatment; exercise and a good bone-building diet b/w ages 25 and 40
