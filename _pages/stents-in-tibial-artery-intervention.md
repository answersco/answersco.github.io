---
title: "Stents in tibial artery intervention"
---
Stents are increasingly used in this vascular bed
Drug-eluting stents may be important in these small-diameter arteries
Long-segment recanalization with subintimal techniques, atherectomy, or laser atherectomy improves procedural success, but lack long-term data.
Focal recalcitrant lesions can undergo PTA with cutting or scorring balloons.
