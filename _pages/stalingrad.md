---
title: "Stalingrad"
---
City in southern Russia and site of a Red Army victory over the Germany army in 1942-1943; it was the turning point in the war between Germany and the Soviet Union but at a horrendous cost to the civilian population

City in Russia, site of a Red Army victory over the Germany army in 1942-1943. The Battle of Stalingrad was the turning point in the war between Germany and the Soviet Union. Today Volgograd.
