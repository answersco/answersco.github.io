---
title: "Regional anesthesia"
---
-anesthetic agent injected near a nerve or nerve pathway or around operative site (ex: nerve block and epidural)
-medication we use for the peripheral nervous system
-produces a more localized anesthesia 
-less side effects and complications
-can also be used at post-operative period at a lower dose to manage pain
-common side effect: bc it blocks the autonomic nerves, it can cause lowered blood pressure
If in spine, there numbness below the line of administration
