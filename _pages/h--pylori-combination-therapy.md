---
title: "H. pylori combination therapy"
---
1) full 10-14 day therapy
-pt education to complete therapy even if they feel better
-can come back even worse if this is done
2) triple therapy
-amoxicillin or metronidazole (if allergic to penicillins)
-clarithromycin
-PPI
3) quadruple therapy
-metronidazole
-tetracycline
-PPI
-**bismuth subsalicylate: like pepto bismol
