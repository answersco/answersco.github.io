---
title: "Ganglioneuroma and ganglioneuroblastoma:"
---
Ganglioneuroma&#8594; benign neurogenic tumor that appears similar to neuroblastoma on imaging, but usually affects slightly older children (~ 6 years)
Only 50% are positive on I-123 MIBG
MRI: classically shows restricted diffusion in neuroblastoma, but not in ganglioneuroma.
Ganglioneuroblastoma&#8594; intermediate behavior between ganglioneuroma and neuroblastoma.
