---
title: "Host"
---
-a susceptible human or animal who harbors and nourishes a disease-causing agent
-the living species capable of being infected or affected by an agent.
-here are physical, psychological, and lifestyle factors (diet, exercise) that influence susceptibility and response to an agent

EX. genetic makeup, age, sex, nutritional choices, lifestyle factors, race, exercise level, health knowledge, motivation to achieve optimal wellness
