---
title: "Estrogen adverse effects"
---
1) GU tract
-mainly bleeding and an irregular period
2) fluid retention, electrolyte imbalance
3) headache, dizziness, N/V, abdominal cramps, bloating
4) acute pancreatitis, cholestatic jaundice (an obstruction and the bile is being blocked so it is not able to be excreted), hepatic adenoma (a hormone induced benign liver tumor)
