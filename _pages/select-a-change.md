Step 4 of the Planned Change Process
-best solution selected after all the alternatives have been analyzed
-to know what is to be accomplished a goal should be created in this stage.
-the goals should have these components: it should be specific, attainable and forgiving.
-consider an exercise goal here

EX. walking 30 minutes a day 5 days a week.
