---
title: "Left versus right heterotaxia syndromes"
---
Right-sided: 
2 fissures in the left lung (2 right sides)
Asplenia (normally left-sided structure)
Increased cardiac malformations
Reversed aorta/IVC

Left-sided: 
One fissure in right lung (2 left sides)
Polysplenia
Less cardiac malformations
Azygous continuation of IVC
