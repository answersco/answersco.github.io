---
title: "Whipple procedure"
---
-remove gastric antrum, gallbladder, pancreatic head, duodenum
-anastomose jejunum with pancreatic tail and remnant stomach

COMPLICATIONS:
-delayed gastric emptying (need NG tube &gt; 10 days)
-pancreatic fistula (amylase through surgical drain &gt;50 mL for 7-10 days)
