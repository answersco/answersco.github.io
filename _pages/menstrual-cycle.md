---
title: "Menstrual cycle"
---
-starts with the release of FSH and LH and stimulation of the ovarian follicles
-once a month the uterus grows a new lining to get ready for a fertilized egg, when there is no fertilized egg to start a pregnancy the uterus sheds the lining

includes proliferative phase, secretory phase, premenstrual phase, and menstrual phase

-discharge
-contains fibrinolysin that prevents clotting of blood in discharged tissue so it can all actually be expelled
