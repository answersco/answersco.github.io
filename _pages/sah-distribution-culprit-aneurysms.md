---
title: "SAH distribution Culprit Aneurysms?"
---
1. Anterior interhemispheric fissure: A comm aneurysm
2. Sylvian Fissure: MCA Trifurcation aneurysm
3. Pre-pontine, Interpeduncular Cistern, Perimesencephalic, or Intraventricular: Basilar tip aneurysm
4. Posterior fossa: PCA aneurysm
5. Ipsilateral basal cistern and SUPRASELLAR cistern: P comm aneurysm
6. Posterior fossa/Intraventricular: PICA
