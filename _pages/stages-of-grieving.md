---
title: "Stages of grieving"
---
1) denial: person refuses to believe prognosis
2) anger: occurs when the know the reality of the situation
3) bargaining: occurs when the person tries to enter an agreement with the postponement of death
4) depression: person becomes depressed about losing their life, worry about people leaving behind, become emotional about the situation
5) acceptance: person fully accepts they are going to die and will feel a sense of peace that they accepted their own fate but still need emotional support
