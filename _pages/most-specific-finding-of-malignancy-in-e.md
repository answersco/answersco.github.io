---
title: "Most specific finding of malignancy in endoemtrioma? What type of malignancy?"
---
Enhancing mural nodule
- Endometrioid or clear cell carcinoma

- Note Endometriomas can normally have echogenic foci adhering to the wall (just ensure it's not a true nodule)

- T1 bright and T2 dark! T2 shading sign without fat on MRI (due to internal iron)

- more risk if &gt; 45 yo or bigger than 6-9cm
