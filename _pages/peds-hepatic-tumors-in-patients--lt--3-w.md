title: "Peds hepatic tumors in patients &lt; 3 with elevated endothelial growth factor."
---
Hemangioendothelioma: often &lt; 1
High-output CHF
Aorta above the hepatic branches of the celiac is often enlarged relative to the aorta below the celiac because of differential flow.
50% with skin hemangiomas
Endothelial growth factor (VEG-F?) elevated
Can be associated with Kasabach-Merritt syndrome (platelet-eater)
Tend to spontaneously involute without therapy and calcify.
