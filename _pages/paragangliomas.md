---
title: "Paragangliomas"
---
Paragangliomas: hypervascular (intense tumor blush), salt-and-pepper on MRI, + on In-111-octreotide

Carotid body tumor: at carotid bifurcation &#8594; splays ICA and ECA

Glomus jugulare: at skull base&#8594; often destruction of jugular foramen

Glomus vagale: above bifurcation, but below jugular foramen

Glomus tympanicum: confined to middle ear (buzzword &quot;overlying cochlear promontory&quot;)
