---
title: "Thyroid mets: general info"
---
- &quot;microcalcifications in LN&quot; --&gt; papillary
- Nodes typically hyperechoic compared to regular nodes, hyperenhancing on CT, and T1 bright.
- Hemorrhagic brain mets.
- Miliary lung mets - lung mets may be occult on cross-sectional imaging.
- Pulmonary fibrosis: risk of treating with I-131 if patient has diffuse lung mets.
