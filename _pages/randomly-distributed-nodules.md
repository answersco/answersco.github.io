---
title: "Randomly-distributed nodules:"
---
Randomly distributed nodules: usually via hematogenous spread, with an angiocentric distribution:
Hematogenous metastases
Septic emboli
Pulmonary LCH: smoking-related lung disease that progresses from airway-associated and random nodules to irregular cysts. Usually distinguishable from other causes of random nodules due to the presence of cysts and non-angiocentric distribution.
