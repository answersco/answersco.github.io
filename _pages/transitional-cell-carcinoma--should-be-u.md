---
title: "Transitional Cell Carcinoma (SHOULD BE URINARY SECTION)"
---
Makes up about 90% of collecting system tumors
100X more common in the bladder than in the ureter, also more common in the renal pelvis than the ureter 
IN the ureter, 75% of TCCs involve the lower region (bottom 1/3)
IF you have a upper tract TCC, there is a 40% chance you will develop a bladder TCC
