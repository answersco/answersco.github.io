---
title: "What are the recs for checking and correcting INR prior to IR procedures?"
---
low risk: only check for pts on coumadin or with liver dz, correct to INR of 2.0
moderate: check on all pts, correct to 1.5
high risk: check on all pts, correct to 1.5

Check routinely for all patients on IV heparin for all procedures.
aPTT: correct heparin if &gt;1.5X control, especially for high risk.
