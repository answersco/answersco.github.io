Remember these should be triphasic
Abnormalities manifest in 1 of 3 ways:
1. Increased pulsatility: caused by right heart failure or tricuspid regurgitation
2. Decreased pulsatility: caused by cirrhosis of veno-occlusive disease
3. Absent: budd chiari
