---
title: "Most common vascular ring?"
---
Double aortic arch.

Double aortic arch&#8594; most common vascular ring
The arches encircle both the trachea and esophagus&#8594; may cause stridor
Right arch usually superior and larger in caliber than the left.
The non-dominant arch is typically ligated.
Lateral images: posterior impression on the esophagus, and anterior impression on trachea (right arch with aberrant left subclavian looks the same).
Frontal XR may show bilateral impressions on the trachea
