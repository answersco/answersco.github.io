---
title: "The genetic code"
---
-the correspondence between the chemical languages of mRNA and proteins
-a triplet code - three successive mRNA bases form a codon
-64 codons, including: 
 - one start signal (AUG) - carries methionine
 - three stop signals (UAA, UAG, and UGA)
-use a codon chart to determine the amino acid that is attached to the tRNA.
