---
title: "Subluxation of biceps tendon: associated injury"
---
Subluxation of biceps tendon:
Subscapularis attaches to lesser tuberosity, and sends a few fibers across the bicipital groove to greater tuberosity as transverse ligament.

Tear of subscapularis will disrupt the transverse ligament&#8594; biceps long-head tendon dislocation (usually medial)
