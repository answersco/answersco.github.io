---
title: "Mnemonics for congenital heart dz on CXR:"
---
&quot;Transpose the String Quartet&quot; - Egg on a string = transposition
&quot;Tappy the Snowman&quot; - Snowman = TAPVR (Supracardiac)
&quot;Coarc3tation&quot; = figure 3 coarctation
&quot;EbsBox&quot; = box shaped heart = Ebstein's (or other causes of high output failure like infantile hemangioendothelioma or Vein of Galen Malformation)
&quot;BooToF&quot; = Boot shaped heart = TOF
