---
title: "Respiratory defense mechanisms"
---
1) Clearance Mechanisms
-Cough
-Mucociliary system
-Lymphatic system: catches anything that enters through the blood until it goes back to the heart; helps with the immune response

2) Respiratory Epithelium: lined with mucus

3) Immunologic mechanisms
-Macrophages within lung tissue: eating debris
-Mast cells: deal with allergies
