---
title: "Low molecular weight heparins"
---
-action is same as heparin
-includes enoxaparin and dalteparin
-longer half-life
-can be used as &quot;bridge&quot; from Heparin to Coumadin 
-safer at home due to longer action
-much less lab monitoring
-self injectable, 1 or 2 x / day
-dose is based on weight and reason for giving
