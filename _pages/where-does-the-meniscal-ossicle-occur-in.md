---
title: "Where does the meniscal ossicle occur in the knee?"
---
Posterior horn of the medial meniscus

Other accessory ossicles:
- Fabella: accessory ossicle typically found in the lateral head of the gastrocnemius
- Camellia: rare sesamoid bone that exists as a normal variant within the popliteus tendon, characteristically located at the lateral aspect of the distal femur in the popliteal groove
