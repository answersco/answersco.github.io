---
title: "Hypotension with tachycardia treatment"
---
Anaphylactoid reaction (pulse &gt; 100, systolic BP &lt; 90)

Elevate legs 60 degrees or Trendelenburg position
O2 6-10L/min
Rapid 1L NS or LR

If unresponsive to fluids:
Epinephrine SC (1:1000) 0.1-0.3 mL (0.1-0.3 mg) OR
Epinephrine IV (1:10,000) 1-3 mL (0.1-0.3 mg) (especially if hypotensive)
Up to 1 mg epinephrine maximum
