---
title: "Home health care nursing"
---
-alternative to hospital- Patient being discharged from hospitals earlier in their recovery, many require skilled professionals at home
-services are delivered to persons at home
-involves Interdisciplinary professionals
-fastest growing healthcare system b/c more elders living alone
-collaboration with family more closely
-advocate for the Patient
Ex. Client is concerned about cost of home dialysis. Acknowledge client's concerns and recommend contacting the regional dialysis center and explore options to help reduce cost of home dialysis
