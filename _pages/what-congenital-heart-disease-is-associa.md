---
title: "What congenital heart disease is associated with asplenia?"
---
Tricuspid atresia: occurs with RV hypoplasia, almost always with ASD or PFO.

May have a right arch (though you should first think truncus arteriosus or ToF)
Usually has pulmonic stenosis- thus decreased vascularity.
