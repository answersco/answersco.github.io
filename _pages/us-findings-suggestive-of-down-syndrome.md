---
title: "US findings suggestive of Down syndrome"
---
CHD: usually AV canal or VSD
Duodenal atresia
Short femur length: non-specific
Echogenic bowel: non-specific (also CF, ischemia, obstruction, infection)
Choroid plexus cyst: non-specific (more common in trisomy 18)

Nuchal translucency: measured at 9-12 weeks between neck/occiput and skin- &gt; 3 mm in 3rd trimester suggests Downs or other chromosomal abnormalities

Nuchal fold thickness: measured in 2nd trimester
&gt; 6 mm nonspecific, also seen in Turner's

Echogenic focus in ventricle: non-specific
