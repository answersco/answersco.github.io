---
title: "What are some ways to tell difference between corpus luteum and ectopic pregnancy? (3)"
---
Ectopic:
- RI: &lt; 0.4 or &gt; 0.7
- THICK echogenic rim
- moves SEPARATE from the ovary (can push the ectopic away from ovary

Corpus Luteum:
- RI: 0.4 - 0.7
- THIN echogenic rim
- moves WITH the ovary (because in the ovary)
