aka Mucocutaneous lymph node syndrome
- idiopathic, febrile tx: aspirin, gamma globulin

Fever and cervical LAD
Desquamating rash on palms/soles
Vasculitis of coronary arteries with aneurysm, stenosis

(inflammation of blood vessles, hence the strawberry tongue) causes coronary artery aneurysms.
&gt; 8 mm coronary aneurysm is GIANT - these ones can cause MI
&lt; 8 mm may go away on its own

-Mucocutaneous lymph node syndrome
-Kawasaki disease (KD) is an acute self limited vasculitis of childhood that is characterized by multisystem disease that occurs in a characteristic progression
-Fever, nonexudative conjunctivitis, erythema of lips and oral mucosa, skin rash, and cervical lymphadenopathy
-Cardiac manifestations include myocarditis (50%), coronary artery aneurysms (3-15%), arrhythmias, and cardiac functional abnormalities related to ischemia
-Best diagnostic clue: Multiple fusiform and saccular coronary aneurysms
-Location: Multisystem disease affecting the skin, lymph nodes, mucous membranes, conjunctiva, myocardium, pericardium, coronary arteries, joints, bowel, gall gladder, kidney, urethra, and other systems
