---
title: "Kawasaki disease"
---
aka Mucocutaneous lymph node syndrome
- idiopathic, febrile tx: aspirin, gamma globulin

Fever and cervical LAD
Desquamating rash on palms/soles
Vasculitis of coronary arteries with aneurysm, stenosis

(inflammation of blood vessles, hence the strawberry tongue) causes coronary artery aneurysms.
&gt; 8 mm coronary aneurysm is GIANT - these ones can cause MI
&lt; 8 mm may go away on its own
