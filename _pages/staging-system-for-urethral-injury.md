---
title: "Staging system for urethral injury?"
---
1. Stretched (periurethral hematoma)
2. Rupture above UG diaphragm (extraperitoneal contrast present)
3. Rupture below UG diaphragm (extraperitoneal AND perineal contrast present)
4. Involves bladder and extends to the urethra
5. Injury to anterior urethra
