---
title: "What is the ddx of chondroblastoma?"
---
Osteoid osteoma ( usually cortex of meta/diaphysis)
Osteomyelitis (usually metaphyseal and more aggressive)
Langerhans cell histiocytosis (frankly erosive of cortex)
Metastatic disease (more aggressive multifocal)
Giant cell tumor (uncommon in skeletally immature)
