---
title: "Urethral trauma scale: - what are the based on? - what are the types? - describe each?"
---
- Type 1- Stretched (Not seen radiographically)

- Type 2_-Rupture above the urogenital diaphragm
- Extraperitoneal contrast is present

- Type 3- Rupture below the urogenital diaphragm
- Extraperitoneal and perineal contrast

- Type 4- Injury involved the bladder extending to the urethra

- Type 5- Injury to the anterior urethra
