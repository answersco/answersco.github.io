---
title: "Neutrophils"
---
-found in the blood stream but move out into the tissues
-do not live very long once they start to phagocytize microorganisms
-constantly circulating in blood but have the ability to know where infection is located so they come out of the blood and onto infection site

-phagocytize bacteria
-most numerous leukocyte

-60% to 70%
-aggressively antibacterial
-rise in number in response to bacterial infection
-barely visible in cytoplasm
