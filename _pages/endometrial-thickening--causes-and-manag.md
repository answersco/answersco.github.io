---
title: "Endometrial thickening: causes and management"
---
Measured without including any fluid in the canal

Premenopausal: normal up to 20 mm
Post-menopausal: &gt; 5 mm should be sampled (&lt; 5 mm usually due to atrophy in bleeding patient)

Estrogen secreting tumors&#8594; Granulosa cell tumors of ovary will thicken endometrium
HNPCC&#8594; 30-50x increased risk of endometrial cancer

Tamoxifen: causes endometrial cysts and polyps. &gt; 8 mm thickness in postmenopausal woman on tamoxifen needs biopsy.
