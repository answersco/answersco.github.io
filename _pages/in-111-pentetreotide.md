---
title: "In-111 pentetreotide"
---
In-111 pentetreotide: most commonly used agent for somatostatin receptor imaging.
-Classically for carcinoid tumors, gastrinomas, paragangliomas, merkel cell tumors, lymphoma, SCLC, medullary thyroid cancer, and meningiomas (also take up Tc-MDP).

Early phase (4 hours): bowel activity is absent
Delayed: clarifies that abdominal tracer is of GI origin
