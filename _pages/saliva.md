---
title: "Saliva"
---
-moistens mouth
-begins starch and fat digestion
-cleanses teeth
-inhibits bacterial growth
-dissolves molecules so they can stimulate the taste buds
-moistens food and binds it together into bolus to aid in swallowing
-hypotonic solution of 97.0% to 99.5% water and the following solutes: salivary amylase, lingual lipase, mucus, lysozome, IgA, and electrolytes
-pH = 6.8 to 7.0
