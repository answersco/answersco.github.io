---
title: "Latino/hispanic culture"
---
-good health associated with ability to work
-preventative measures not highly valued
-only by prayer
-believe health is result of external events or objects (e.g. hot-and-cold theory: cultural classification that divides diseases and substances into hot or cold in order to keep the body in balance)
-fear accidents, surgery
-male of household determines treatment
-curandero/cultural healer
-high incidents of diabetes, heart disease, and obesity
-do not seek medical care until condition has worsened
