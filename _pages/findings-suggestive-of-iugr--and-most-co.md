---
title: "Findings suggestive of IUGR, and most common cause of oligohydramnios in 3rd trimester"
---
IUGR suggested by:
Estimated fetal weight &lt; 10% 
Femur length / abdominal circumference &gt; 23.5
Umbilical artery S:D &gt; 4.0

IUGR in presence of oligohydramnios (AFI &lt; 5) or polyhydramnios strongly associated with poor outcome

Most common cause for developing oligohydramnios during 3rd trimester: fetal growth restriction associated with placental insufficiency.
