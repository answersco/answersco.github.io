---
title: "Findings in rickets:"
---
XR: expansion, fraying, and cupping of long bone metaphyses, often associated with adjacent metaphyseal periosteal new bone.
May have bowing of legs, osteopenia, skull demineralization, and fractures.
Rachitic rosary: anterior cupping of the ribs with widening of the rib epiphyseal cartilage.
Oncogenic rickets&#8594; variant seen with hemangiopericytoma or NOF due to tumor metabolites that impair vitamin D metabolism.
