---
title: "SAR Limits in MRI?"
---
Per the FDA, SAR should be limited to produce less than a 1&#176; C body-core temperature increase. In addition, the following significant risk criteria should be adhered to; less than:
&#8226; 4 W/kg averaged over the whole body for 15 minutes,
&#8226; 3 W/kg averaged over the head for 10 minutes,
&#8226; 8 W/kg in any gram of tissue in the head or torso for 15 minutes, or
&#8226; 12 W/kg in any gram of tissue in the extremities for 15 minutes.
