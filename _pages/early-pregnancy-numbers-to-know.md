---
title: "Early Pregnancy numbers to know"
---
1. Dating:
&lt;3 m : CRL
&gt;3 m : BPD

2. Fetal demise: 
CRL &gt;7 
MSD &gt; 25

3. Fetal anatomy:
*Normal Lateral ventricle &lt;10 mm 
*Nuchal lucency
*Nuchal thickness

4. <b>Timing:</b>
*Gest sac: 5 w
*yolk sac: 5.5 wks
*embryo: 6 w
*rhombo: 8 weeks
*gut returns: 12-13 wks
