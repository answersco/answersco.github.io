title: "Ependymitis granularis?"
---
Frontal horn periventricular hyperintensity on T2 weighted images due to intersitital CSF back up. (Not associated with inflammation despite name.

Normal anatomic finding. May mimic pathology. High T2 signal at tips of frontal horns. Due to porous ependyma allowing transependymal flow of CSF.
