---
title: "Throat inspection"
---
1) assess uvula and soft palate by putting tongue blade over tongue
-uvula moving up and down correctly correlates with CN IX and CN X (glossopharyngeal and vagus)
2) tonsils
-white patches on tonsils and back of throat usually means bacterial infection
3) pharyngeal tissue
-edema, ulcerations, inflammation, infection, abnormal lesions
4) drainage 
-clear or white = chronic sinus problems
-yellow or green = infection
5) odors
-sweet, fruity --&gt; diabetic ketoacidosis
-ammonia --&gt; kidney failure
