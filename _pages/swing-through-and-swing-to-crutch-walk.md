---
title: "Swing through and swing to crutch walk"
---
-frequently used by paraplegics or cerebral palsy pt's who wear weight supporting braces on their legs
-depends on stability of pt and what they are trying to do
-both crutches are moved forward followed by swinging both legs forward.
-swing through: advance feet past crutches, in forward motion
-swing to: advance feet to crutches, to move then stop
