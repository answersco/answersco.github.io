---
title: "Non-typhoid salmonella"
---
-taxonomy complex - several major groups (species) but each group with hundreds of serotypes (strain). 
-common species: S. enteritidis
-mainly zoonotic diseases, some human-human 
-intestinal infection
-symptoms: Prolonged diarrhea, cramps
-transmission: fecal oral
-asymptomatic carriers - no symptoms so you just don't know the animal is a carrier
