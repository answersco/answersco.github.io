---
title: "Named ankle fracture"
---
Tillaux fracture = SH-3 fracture through anterolateral aspect of distal tibial epiphysis
Triplane fracture = SH-4

Vertical component through epiphysis
Horizontal component through physis
Oblique component through metaphysis

Maisonneuve fracture: unstable fracture involving medial tibial malleolus and/or disruption of distal tibiofibular syndesmosis
Will show widened ankle mortis&#8594; get proximal leg XR
Common fracture of proximal fibular shaft
Does NOT extend into hindfoot
