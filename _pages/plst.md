---
title: "PLST"
---
-Framework for the nursing care of individuals with dementia

-Need alternating quiet and stimulating environments

-Behaviors have meaning - in pain, hungry, cold, hot, need restroom

-Check for physical problem, pain, and environment when trying to discover what their behaviors are
--Keep track of their baseline and notice if they do something different one day than understand something may be going on
--Do not be afraid to give a PRN bc they do not verbalize pain
--If condition is seen where it is painful and they are acting different, then think about administering meds
--Wong-Baker or thermometer scale are good scales used for non-verbals
