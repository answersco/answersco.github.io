---
title: "Indications for catheter interventions in DVT"
---
-Iliofemoral or femoral DVT (extensive, symptomatic, or progressive)
-Recent onset of symptoms (within 21 days)
-Threatened limb (phlegmasia cerulean dolens)
-Inadequate response to initial anticoagulation
-Low bleeding risk
-Good physiologic reserve
-Life expectancy &gt; 6 months
