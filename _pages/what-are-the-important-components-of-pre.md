title: "What are the important components of pre-treatment work-up/therapy prior to Y-90 TARE"
---
Lung shunt fraction: Tc-99-MAA to hepatic artery to determine the degree of pulmonary shunting
Shunt fraction that would deliver 30 Gy in a single treatment is a contraindication to Y-90.

Right gastric origin: need to prevent non-target therapy to stomach, leading to a non-healing gastric ulcer.
Usually perform prophylactic embo of the right gastric and GDA.
Right gastric origin is highly variable, and may come off of proper hepatic or left hepatic.
