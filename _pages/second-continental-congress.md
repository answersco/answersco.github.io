---
title: "Second Continental Congress"
---
Formed in May 1775 to give colonists the right to print paper money, create an army, and create a committee for foreign relations. Failed to reach a truce with George III to repeal the Coercive acts, and begin negotiations.

They organized the continental Army, called on the colonies to send troops, selected George Washington to lead the army, and appointed the committee to draft the Declaration of Independence.

meeting that convened in Philadelphia on May 10, 1775, at which it was decided that an army should be raised and George Washington of Virginia was named commander in chief
