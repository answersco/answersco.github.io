---
title: "Lipoma arborescens"
---
This is frond like fat deposit around the synovium

Synovium filled with frond like fat from chronic inflammation

Lipoma arborescens: affects synovial linings of joints and bursae in 50-70 yo patients
&quot;Frond-like&quot; deposition of fatty tissue, most commonly in suprapatellar bursa of knee
Can develop in normal knee, or associated with OA, chronic RA, or prior trauma, typically unilaterally.
MRI: behaves like fat&#8594; T1 bright, T2 bright, fat saturation &#8594; could show gradient with chemical shift at fat-fluid interface
US&#8594; &quot;frond-like hyperechoic mass&quot; and associated joint effusion
