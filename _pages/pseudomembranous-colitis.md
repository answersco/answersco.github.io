---
title: "Pseudomembranous Colitis"
---
Classic imaging appearance: Marked colonic submucosal edema causing wall thickening &amp; nodularity over a long colonic segment
Location
Usually pancolitis
Rectum &amp; sigmoid colon: Up to 90% of cases
Proximal colon only: 10-20%
Morphology
Plaque-like necrotic tissue with mucus on damaged colonic mucosa
