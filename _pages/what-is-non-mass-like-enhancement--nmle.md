---
title: "What is Non-Mass Like Enhancement (NMLE)? - different distributions (3) - what makes this suspicious?"
---
NMLE: 
not a mass, but more like a clump of tissue enhancement

Distributions:
- Segmental (triangular blob point to the nipple)
- Regional (a bigger triangle)
- Diffuse (all over the place)

Suspicious: 
- HETEROGENEOUS enhancement
