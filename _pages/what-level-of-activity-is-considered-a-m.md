---
title: "What level of activity is considered a major spill for Tc-99m, Tl-201, In-111, Ga-67, and I-131"
---
Tc-99m: major spill &gt; 100 mCi
Tl-201: major spill &gt; 100 mCi
In-111: major spill &gt; 10 mCi
Ga-67: major spill &gt; 10 mCi
I-131: major spill &gt; 1 mCi

Major spills require a call to radiation safety officer
Clear area, cover spill with absorbent paper (don't clean), indicate boundaries, shield source, call officer, decontaminate persons

Xenon leak: leave room quickly, close door
-Wipe test doesn't work
