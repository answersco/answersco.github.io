title: "Quality control"
---
Example: radiologist requests repeat CXR if apices are excluded
This includes the range of acceptability: 
-The accuracy (closeness of measurement to the true value)
-The precision (reproducibility of the measurement)
-The reliability (refers to both the accuracy and precision of a measurement)

Abbreviated QC
&quot;Checking to see the if the product meets certain parameters&quot;
This is looking at the END RESULT and saying &quot;good&quot; &quot;bad&quot;
