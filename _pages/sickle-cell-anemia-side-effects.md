---
title: "Sickle cell anemia side effects"
---
-cytotoxic drug (death of cells - always wear gloves and if pregnant do not give the medication), anorexia, N/V, stomatitis, diarrhea, rash, erythema, bone marrow suppression, headache, fever, chills, malaise
-increased risk of development of cancer - why they are not kept on the medications continuously
