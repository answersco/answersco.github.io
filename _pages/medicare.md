---
title: "Medicare"
---
-medical bills are paid from trust funds which those covered have paid into. 

-it serves people over 65 primarily, whatever their income and also serves younger disabled people with certain conditions and dialysis patients. 

-federal program run by the Centers for Medicare &amp; Medicaid Services (CMMS).

-more likely to cover acute hospital stays, equipment, meds and prescriptions

-less likely to cover long-term residential, some doctor office visits

-insurance plan for older adults (over 65)
