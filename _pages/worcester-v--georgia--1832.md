---
title: "Worcester v. Georgia (1832)"
---
Supreme court case overseen by John Marshall that declared Georgia had no right to rule in Cherokee territory. Jackson refused to use executive power to enforce this court case.

Cherokee fought the Indian Removal Act by taking issue to supreme court, supreme court ruled in favor of Cherokees, Cherokees should not have to leave their land, &amp; Andrew Jackson ignores and continues to move natives west of the Mississippi River
