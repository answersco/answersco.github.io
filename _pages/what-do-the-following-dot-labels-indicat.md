---
title: "What do the following DOT labels indicate: White 1; yellow 2; and yellow 3?"
---
White I: &lt; 0.5 mR/hr on contact. Transport index not required (TI = 1 mR/hr at 1 meter)

Yellow II: TI &lt; 1; &lt; 50 mR/hr on contact. Obliterate label before discarding.

Yellow III: TI &lt; 10; &lt; 200 mR/hr on contact.
