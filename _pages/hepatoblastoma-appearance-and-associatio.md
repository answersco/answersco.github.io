---
title: "Hepatoblastoma:appearance and associations"
---
Third most common abdominal tumor in children.

Heterogeneous enhancement completely replacing liver parenchyma. May have tumor thrombus. 
Right upper quadrant calcification - classic.

Associations:
Beckwith- Wiedenmann
Familial adenomatous polyposis syndrome (F APS)
Fetal alcohol syndrome

Elevated AFP/alpha-fetoprotein
