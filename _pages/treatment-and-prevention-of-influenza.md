---
title: "Treatment and prevention of influenza"
---
Treatment
-symptomatic
-antiviral prescription drug currently available
-&quot;Relenza&quot;, &quot;Tamiflu&quot;
-limits symptoms and duration of disease

Prevention
-vaccine available
-not always effective - so many variations that can cause influenza in humans
-a &quot;cocktail&quot; vaccine against several types - each year a new vaccine must be produced
