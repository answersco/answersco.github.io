title: "What is ARVC?"
---
Arrythmogenic right ventricular cardiomyopathy. A familial cardiomyopathy where the RV wall is progressively replaced with FIBROSIS AND FAT. The infiltration can interfere with electrical conductance resulting in arrhythmias and sudden death.

Arrhymogenic RV Cardiomyoapthy (ARVC): fibrofatty degeneration of RV --&gt; arrhythmia LBBB and sudden death

- Trick: Look for RV to sat out on Fat Sat images
