---
title: "What is a varicocele, how to dx, assoc, and tx?"
---
Dilatation of veins of pampiniform plexus &gt; 2-3 mm in diameter secondary to retrograde flow in internal spermatic vein. Seen with doppler US especially on valsalva. 80% are left-sided, bilateral in 15% of patients. Most frequent cause of male infertility. In the elderly consider L renal vein occlusion by tumor. Tx is embolization/surgical ligation if sx or low sperm count.
