---
title: "Fowlers"
---
-semi fowlers relaxes tension of abdominal muscles and facilitates breathing
-may want to do this when auscultating for lung sounds
-if coarse crackles are found then its possible pt has pneumonia and therefore fowlers will be better

-sitting straight up at 45-60 degrees
-used to help pt breathe and expand the lungs bc it promotes ventilation and helps feeding
