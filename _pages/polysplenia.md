---
title: "Polysplenia:"
---
Polysplenia: multiple spleens may be on the right or left, but are always on the same side as the stomach
Usually associated with severe CHD, and most die in early childhood
Associated with venous anomalies, including interruption of the IVC with azygos or hemiazygos continuation, or preduodenal portal vein.

Left isomerism
-moderate CHD
-DORV, ASD, PAPVR
-normal great vesseles
- malrotation
-multiple spleens
-azygous continuation of IVC
-abdominal situs ambiguous / inversus
**good prognosis
