Multilocular cystic nephroma&#8594; benign neoplasm
Bimodal distribution: young boys age 3 months-2 years, and middle-aged women.
Multiseptated cystic renal mass with enhancing septa, and can mimic cystic Wilms tumor.
Propensity to herniate into renal pelvis, causing hydronephrosis.

Bimodal age distribution 4 and 40 
By definition cystic with no solid components or necrosis
Buzz word = protrusion into the renal pelvis
