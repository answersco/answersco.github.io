---
title: "Sensory function"
---
-sensory receptors transmit messages to the spinal cord which from there travel to brain to be interpreted
-screening for sensory nerve dysfunction can be assessed during other exams such as the skin assessment
-assess both sides of the body
-have patients close their eyes
-pts identify sharp/dull sensation
-utilize dermatome zones to assess sensation related to specific spinal locations
1) pain
2) temperature
3) position
4) vibration
5) numbness/tingling
6) crude/fine localized touch
