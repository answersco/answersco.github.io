---
title: "False positive GI bleed studies"
---
Renal or bladder excretion (esp with hydro)
Transplant kidney (won't move)
Varices and angiodysplasia: won't move
Penis with blood in it
Hemangioma (will be over liver or spleen and not move)
Free Tc in stomach: look at salivary glands and thyroid to confirm free Tc and not actual bleed.
