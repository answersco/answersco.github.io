---
title: "Stool collections"
---
-medical aseptic technique is imperative
-hand hygiene, before and after glove use, is essential b/c fecal-oral transmission
-wear disposable gloves
-do not contaminate outside of container with stool
-obtain stool and package, label, and transport according to agency policy (in a plastic biohazard/sealable bag)
-test for occult (hidden) blood, culture and sensitivity to see what kind of bacteria, pus, ova and parasites related to parasitic infection, tested stool usually for c-diff
