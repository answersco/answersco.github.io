---
title: "Findings and Dx for cardiac imaging: - fixed defect (seen on stress and rest) - reversible defect ( seen on stress, better on rest) - fixed defect with reversible defect around it"
---
- fixed defect: scar (prior infarct)

- reversible defect: Ischemia

- fixed with surrounding reversible: Infarct with peri-infarct ischemia

- Dilated cardiomyopathy
- RVH
- if you see splanchnic activity, pt isn't exercising hard enough
