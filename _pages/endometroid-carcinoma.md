---
title: "Endometroid Carcinoma"
---
- Bilateral 15% of the time
- 25% of women will have an associated endometrial cancer 
- Endometriomas CAN turn into endometroid cancer

Gamesman ship: Ovarian mass WITH endometrial thickening
- THINK: 1) endometroid cancer (given likelihood of endometrial cancer) OR 2) granulosa-theca cell tumor (as these produce estrogen and cause endometrial hyperplasia)
