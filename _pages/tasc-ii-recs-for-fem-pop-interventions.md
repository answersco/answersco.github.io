---
title: "TASC II recs for fem-pop interventions: types C and D"
---
Lesion type C: surgical treatment used more often but insufficient scientific evidence
-Recurrent stenoses or occlusions that need treatment after 2 prior endovascular interventions
-Multiple stenoses or occlusions totaling &gt; 15 cm, +/- heavy calcification.

Lesion type D: surgery is tx of choice
-Chronic total occlusion of CFA or SFA (&gt; 20 cm), involving the popliteal artery
-Chronic total occlusion of the popliteal artery, proximal trifurcation vessels
