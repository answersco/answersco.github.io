---
title: "Types and treatments of femoropopliteal disease?"
---
Type A: Less than 10 cm (Endovascular treatment). Type B: Multiple lesions, each less than 5 or one greater than 15 (Still endovascular). Type C Multiple stenoses or occlusions greater than 15 cm. (Surgery is preferred, depending on patients comorbidities and preference). Type D: Chronic total occlusion. (Surgery is treatment of choice)
