---
title: "Hair and nails as a whole"
---
-hair, nails, and cutaneous glands are accessory organs of the skin
-hair and nails are mostly composed of mostly dead, keratinized cells
-pliable, soft keratin makes up stratum corneum of skin while compact, hard keratin makes up hair and nails b/c they are tougher and more compact due to numerous cross-linkages b/w keratin molecules
