---
title: "Malignant rhabdoid tumor of the kidney arises from where? Imaging findings?"
---
- Children &lt; 2 yo
- Arises from Renal medulla (indistinctly marginated soft tissue mass centered in hilum)
- Bad prognosis (80% metastatic at presentation to LNs, lung, liver, brain, bones)


* Contrast to clear cell sarcoma (also arises from renal medulla) - very similar; clear cell also occurs at age 2 yo
