---
title: "Healthcare provider's responsibilities"
---
-help people cope with alarm stage
-maintain resistance stage- keep from getting to exhaustion stage
-foster return to normal function or homeostasis or stability where they can cope and move on
-assess physical, emotional and cognitive state
-determine state of adaption
-teach client to recognize signs of stress and how to activate relaxation response
-counsel as necessary
-prevent or reverse exhaustion --stage
