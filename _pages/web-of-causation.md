---
title: "Web of Causation"
---
-the causal paradigm that emerged to explain the concept of multiple causation in order &quot;to explain the existence of health and illness states to provide guiding principles for epidemiologic practice&quot;
-For example, a combination of factors, and not only one factor, is the deciding factor in the development of poor health outcomes. Due to this concept, health care interventions have to be provided at a variety of levels, not just the individual levels
