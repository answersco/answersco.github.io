The last global pandemic in history that killed millions was that of _______ in 1918.

-an acute respiratory disease of warm blooded vertebrates
-reservoir - primarily wild birds
-often avirulent in reservoir meaning no symptoms
-hosts: domestic birds - ducks, chickens, swine, and humans
-occurs in epidemics and pandemics
-seasonal - usually when it gets colder
