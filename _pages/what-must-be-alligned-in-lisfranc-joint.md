---
title: "What must be alligned in lisfranc joint (4)?"
---
Lateral base of 1st metatarsal with lateral aspect of first cuneiform. Medial 2nd base with medial 2nd cuneiform. Medial third base with Medial 3rd cuneiform. Medial fourth base with medial cuboid. (Note that 3rd and 4th are easier to see on oblique). Also not ethat the dorsal profile of the Lisfranc should be uninterupted.
