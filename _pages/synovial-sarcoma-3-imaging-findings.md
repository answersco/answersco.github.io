---
title: "Synovial Sarcoma 3 imaging findings:"
---
Complex baker cyst appearance on US (also not in the typical location) - arises near joint or from tendon, but often not within the joint (young adults) 

Triple sign (high medium and low signal in the same mass on T2) - most often T1 dark and T2 bright to muscle; can have internal mineralization 

Bowl of grapes (fluid fluid levels in a mass)
