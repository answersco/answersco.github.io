---
title: "When would you consider using Tc-HMPAO-WBC over In-WBC?"
---
Kids: Tc-99 has lower absorbed dose and shorter imaging time
Small parts: Tc-99 does better in hands and feet

Downsides to HMPAO:
-Shorter half life (6 hours)- limits delayed imaging
-Has normal GI and gallbladder activity, limiting evaluation in these areas.
