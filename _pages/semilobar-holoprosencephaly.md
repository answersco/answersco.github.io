title: "Semilobar holoprosencephaly"
---
Partial fusion of hemispheres. Absent or dysgenic corpus callosum and septum pellucidum. High association with migration anomalies. Posterior portion of interhemispheric fissure and falx are usually formed.

FUSED at the thalami (posterior brain is normal)
Olfactory tract and bulbs are also gone 
- You are also going to have the problems that you have in LOBAR holopros (abscent septum)

Fused thalami. Everything anterior to that is uncleaved. Posterior brain should be normal
