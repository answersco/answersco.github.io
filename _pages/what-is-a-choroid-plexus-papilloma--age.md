---
title: "What is a choroid plexus papilloma? Age and gender predilection? Sx? Tx? Prognosis?"
---
WHO grade 1 intraventricular neoplasm, most common tumor below age 1yo, strongly enhancing, lobulated, intraventricular mass. Age: 2 yo (86% present by 5 yo) M&gt;F. Sx: Increased ICP. Tx is surgical resection with excellent prognosis: 5 year survival close to 100%.
