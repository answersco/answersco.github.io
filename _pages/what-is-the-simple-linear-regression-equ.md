---
title: "What is the simple linear regression equation (prediction)?"
---
y = a + bx

y = independent variable (y-intercept)
x = dependent variable (x-intercept)
a = constant (value of y when x = 0)
b = slope

r is NOT the slope
slope = &#916;y/&#916;x

multiple regression:
y = a + bx1 + bx2 ...
