---
title: "Mesenteric Lymphatic Malformations"
---
Best diagnostic clue
Cystic mass in small bowel mesentery
Location
Lymphatic malformations
Neck &amp; axillae (95%)
Mediastinum, omentum, mesentery, retroperitoneum, &amp; extremities (5%)
Mesenteric: Small bowel (60%), colonic (24%)
Retroperitoneum (14.5%)
Mesenteric lymphatic malformations may occur from duodenal to rectal mesentery
Most commonly ileal mesentery
Size
Few mm to 40 cm
Morphology
Unilocular, multilocular, or multiple
