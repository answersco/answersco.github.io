---
title: "Proton pump inhibitors (PPI's) action"
---
-inhibits the parietal cell H+/K+ ATP pump
-inhibits the release H+ (acid)
-greater inhibition, stronger, &amp; longer duration of action than with H2 receptor blockers - causing more significant side effects when taken long-term
-may take several days to feel effect 
-take in morning on empty stomach, 20-30 minutes before first meal
