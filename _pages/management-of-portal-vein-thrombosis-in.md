---
title: "Management of portal vein thrombosis in setting of HCC"
---
HCC has propensity to invade PVs and HVs and propagate within the lumen.
Enhancement of intravascular &quot;thrombus&quot; on CT or MRI is diagnostic of tumor
Arterioportal shunting due to tumor can result in variceal bleeding.
Embo of the tumor with permanent particles rather than TIPS is initial treatment.
