---
title: "Phospholipid"
---
-tails are hydrophobic and the heads are hydrophilic
-tails point towards each other automatically bc they are hydrophobic
-tails (fatty acids) point inward towards each other while heads (phosphate) point outward 
-micelles: lipid molecules orient with polar (hydrophilic) head toward water and nonpolar (hydrophobic) tails away frrom water
-function: structural formation of cell membranes and aid in fat digestion
