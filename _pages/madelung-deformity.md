---
title: "Madelung deformity"
---
Shortened distal radius with abnormal ulnar tilt of its distal articular surface.
Madelung deformity: often idiopathic, but can also be seen in the setting of prior trauma, Turner syndrome, or skeletal dysplasias such as hereditary multiple exostoses.

premature closure or defective development of the ulnar third of the distal epiphysis of the radius.

- Idiopathic
- Turner syndrome
- Skeletal dysplasia (multiple epiphyseal dysplasia, dyschondrosteosis)

= trauma
= infection / septic arthritis
