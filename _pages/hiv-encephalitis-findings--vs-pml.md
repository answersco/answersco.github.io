title: "HIV encephalitis findings (vs PML)?"
---
HIV encephalitis = Brain atrophy + SYMMETRIC; T1 normal but T2/FLAIR bright signal in deep white matter that SPARES the Subcortical U-fibers

PML = (CD4 &lt; 50; JC virus) = T1 hypointense, T2/FLAIR hyperintense lesions out of proportion to mass effect that AFFECT U-Fibers and are ASYMMETRIC
