title: "What causes LOW lung volumes in neonatal chest?"
---
GBS PNA and Surfactant deficiency

1. GBS = low lung vol in premature infant w/ granular opacities and eff

2. Surfactant deficient disesae = Hyaline membrane disease = RDS 
- PREMATURE baby
- low lung volumes with b/l granular opacities but NO pleural effusions
* treat with endotracheal administration of surfactant!
