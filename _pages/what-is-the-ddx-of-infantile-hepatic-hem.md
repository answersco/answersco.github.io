---
title: "What is the ddx of infantile hepatic hemangioma?"
---
Hepatoblastoma: solitary mass, rare in newborns, positive AFP.
Neuroblastoma Metastasis: usually multiple, look for adrenal mass.
Mesenchymal Hamartoma: multilobulated cystic mass
Angiosarcoma: looks very similar to hemangioma but presents later and does not respond to tx.
