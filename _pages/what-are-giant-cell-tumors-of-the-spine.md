---
title: "What are giant cell tumors of the spine?"
---
Lytic expansile lesion in sacrum or a vertebral body with narrow zone of transition &amp; usually non-sclerotic margins (can coexist with an ABC). 
Radiologically &amp; histologically identical to brown tumors, which occur in setting of hyperparathyroidism
Majority occur in 3rd to 5th decades
Ddx Chordoma and Plasmacytoma
