---
title: "Typical plain film findings in MI and papillary muscle rupture:"
---
Plain film evaluation of MI &#8594; usually normal
Most common abnormality is &#8593; pulmonary venous pressure or overt pulmonary edema (poor prognostic indicator)
Papillary muscle rupture due to MI: typically produces acute pulmonary edema
Classic XR finding is isolated RUL pulmonary edema due to acute papillary muscle rupture.
