---
title: "Tc-99m MAA microaggregated albumin"
---
- Dose - 4 mCi
- Action - becomes lodged in small caliber vessles
- Normal uptake - lungs
- Uses - VQ scan, diagnose right to left shunt (uptake in brain and kidneys)
- Special - 
o These particles are 10-50 microns in size; 200-500 thousand particles are injected
o Decrease the dose in children and in patients with a right to left shunt (b/c particles enter the systemic circulation)
o Relative contraindication is severe pulmonary HTN
