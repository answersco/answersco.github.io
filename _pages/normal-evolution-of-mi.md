---
title: "Normal evolution of MI"
---
Normal evolution of MI: 
Progressive thinning of myocardium in region of infarction
Hypertrophy of non-infarcted myocardium
Ventricular dilation

May see ventricular thrombus adjacent to akinetic myocardium &#8594; doesn't enhance on DHE images, and is within the LV, distinguishing it from MO, which involves the subepicardium
