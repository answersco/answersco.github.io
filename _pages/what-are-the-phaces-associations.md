---
title: "What are the PHACES associations?"
---
PHACES associations:
1) Posterior fossa brain malformations (Dandy-Walker malformation/variant, migrational anomaly)
2) Hemangioma of face and neck
3) Arterial abnl
4) Cardiovascular defects (aortic abnl, VSD)
5) Eye abnormalities
6) Sternal clefts
