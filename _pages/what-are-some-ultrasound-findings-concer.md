---
title: "What are some ultrasound findings concerning for Downs Syndrome? (8)"
---
- Congenital Heart Disease (AV canal/VSD)
- Duodenal atresia (most common intrabdominal pathology)
- Short femur length
- Echogenic bowel
- Choroid plexus cyst
- Nuchal Translucency (&gt; 3mm first trimester)
- Nuchal fold thickness (&gt;6mm second trimester)
- Echogenic intracardiac focus
