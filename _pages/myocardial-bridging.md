---
title: "Myocardial bridging?"
---
Band of myocardium overlying a segment of coronary artery- most commonly seen in the mid LAD

This is where you have an INTRAmyocardial path of the LCA (most commonly)

intramyocardial course of a coronary artery (usually LAD)

Intramyocardial course of a coronary artery (usually middle segment of LAD)
