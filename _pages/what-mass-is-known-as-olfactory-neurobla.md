---
title: "What mass is known as olfactory neuroblastoma?"
---
Esthesioneuroblastoma &#8594; bimodal age distribution (teens and middle-age)
Imaging: aggressive mass that is slightly hyperattenuating on CT, and intermediate on T1- and T2-weighted images. Calcification is often present.
Classic finding&#8594; peripheral tumor cysts that occur at the margins of the intracranial portion of the mass.
