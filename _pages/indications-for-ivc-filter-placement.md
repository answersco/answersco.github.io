---
title: "Indications for IVC filter placement?"
---
Therapeutic indications for inferior vena cava (IVC) filter placement include documented evidence of pulmonary embolism (PE) or IVC, iliac or femoropopliteal deep venous thrombus (DVT), AND at least 1 of the following: Contraindication, complication or failure of anticoagulation, inability to maintain therapeutic anticoagulation, recurrent PE despite adequate therapy, propagation of DVT during therapeutic anticoagulation, massive PE with residual DVT, free-floating iliofemoral or IVC thrombus, and severe cardiopulmonary disease with DVT. 

** Isolated calf vein thrombus is not included within therapeutic indications for filter placement.
