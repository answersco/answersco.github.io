---
title: "Population-Based Nursing"
---
-the definition of population &quot;is any group of people who share at least one characteristic, such as age, gender, race, a particular risk factor or disease&quot; 
-if a nurse works for a county or city health department, then the public health nurse will care for the population residing in that city or county
-Examples include smokers, breast cancer survivors, infants born in 2018.
