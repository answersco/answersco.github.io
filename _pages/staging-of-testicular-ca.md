---
title: "Staging of testicular ca?"
---
Two groups: low-stage or advanced-stage disease
Low-stage:Tumor confined to testis, epididymis, or spermatic cord. Mild-moderate adenopathy (no nodal masses &gt; 5 cm)

Advanced-stag: Tumors that invade scrotal wall. Adenopathy (nodal mass &gt; 5 cm). Visceral metastases.
