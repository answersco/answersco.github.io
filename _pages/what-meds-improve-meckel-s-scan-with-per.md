---
title: "What meds improve Meckel's scan with Pertechnetate? False negative causes?"
---
Pentagastrin (enhances uptake by gastric mucosa)
H2 blockers (blocks secretion of pertechnetate out of the gastric cells)
Glucagon slows gastric motility

False negative if: Recent in vivo labeling of RBCs or barium study (attenuates)
