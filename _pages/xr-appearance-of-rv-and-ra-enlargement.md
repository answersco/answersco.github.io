---
title: "XR appearance of RV and RA enlargement:"
---
Right ventricular enlargement: RV is most anterior cardiac chamber
RV enlargement causes displacement of cardiac apex in a leftward direction (in contrast to LV enlargement, which causes displacement in left-inferior direction)
May cause opacification of retrosternal clear space on lateral XR.
Right atrial enlargement: RA forms right heart border on frontal image.
RA enlargement causes lateral bulging or elongation of R heart border.
