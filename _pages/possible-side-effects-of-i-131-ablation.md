---
title: "Possible side effects of I-131 ablation, and contraindications"
---
Can cause pulmonary fibrosis if given to patients with lung mets (rare only with macro-nodular disease)

Sjogren's: higher risk of salivary gland damage

Overall, salivary gland damage is dose-related, so higher risk for cancer therapy patients.

Contraindications: severe uncontrolled thyrotoxicosis and pregnancy (both absolute)
