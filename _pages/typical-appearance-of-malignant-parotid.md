---
title: "Typical appearance of malignant parotid tumors:"
---
Mucoepidermoid carcinoma&#8594; 5% of parotid tumors, and most common primary parotid malignancy.
Low-grade tumor typically appears as enhancing mass that is hyperintense on T2-weighted images (indistinguishable from benign PMA).

Adenoid cystic carcinoma&#8594; 2nd most common parotid malignancy, and most common malignancy of submandibular and sublingual glands.
Tendency for perineural spread&#8594; CN palsy or paresthesia
Key feature is enhancing mass with perineural spread.

Carcinoma ex pleomorphic adenoma
In contrast to benign PMA, this tumor is hypointense on both T1- and T2-weighted images.
