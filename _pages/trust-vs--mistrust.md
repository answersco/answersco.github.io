---
title: "Trust vs. mistrust"
---
-the infant

trust: when taken care of and stimulated there is ease of feeding, depth of sleep, and contentment

mistrust: not stimulated or taken care of can cause poor sleeping &amp; eating, colic, failure to thrive, can effect infant in childhood and adulthood

-birth to 1.5 years
-helpless infants depend on others for fulfillment of physical and emotional needs

-Success: sense that world is predictable and friendly, learn to trust others, people will be there to help you

-Failures: sense that world is unpredictable and sometimes hostile, carry concerns of trust throughout life
