---
title: "What is neuromyelitis optica?"
---
Neuromyelitis optica (NMO), formerly known as Devic disease, is a demyelinating dz involving the optic nerves and spinal cord, usually sparing the brain. Antibodies to aquaporin-4 characterize the disease. Serum NMO-IgG is 90% specific for NMO and about 70-75% sensitive.
