---
title: "Types of urethral injury"
---
Type 1: stretched&#8594; periurethral hematoma is present. Not well seen on RUG.
Type 2: rupture ABOVE the urogenital (UG) diaphragm &#8594; extraperitoneal contrast is present.
Type 3: rupture BELOW the UG diaphragm&#8594; extraperitoneal and perineal contrast present.
Type 4: injury involves the bladder extending to urethra
Type 5: injury to anterior urethra.
