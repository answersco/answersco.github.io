---
title: "What are the mildest and most severe forms of DW spectrum and HPE?"
---
Mega cisterna magna: mildest form of DW spectrum.
-Occipital encephalocele is most severe.

Septo-optic dysplasia is most severe form of HPE.
-Hypoplastic optic nerves with absent septum pellucidum and hypothalamic-pituitary dysfunction.
