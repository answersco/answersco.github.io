---
title: "Unicorunate Uterus, definition, Epi, findings, and associations?"
---
Class II: More common on the right. 20% of MDAs with 30% being solitary, but 10-20% with rudimentary horns. Resection of a non-communicating horn is indicated, and as is a communicating horn due to risk of ectopic pregnancy and endometriosis.
40% of unicornuate also have renal abnormalities always ipsilateral to the rudimentary horn. 67% are Renal agenesis, w/ Ectopic, horseshoe, cystic renal dysplasia, and duplicated collecting systems.
