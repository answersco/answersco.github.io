---
title: "Indium-111 characteristics and uses"
---
Indium-111: produced in cyclotron
-Decays by electron capture- 2 photopeaks at 173 and 247 keV.
-T1/2 = 67 hours
-Acts like gallium, forming bonds with transferrin
-Can be bound to WBC, octreotide, or DTPA (CSF)
-Isolate WBCs prior to labeling because transferrin in blood will out-compete them.
