---
title: "Nursing diagnoses for neuro"
---
1) Cognitive-Perceptual Pattern 
-Acute/Chronic Confusion
-Impaired Memory
EX. pt's with dementia or alztheimer's
2) Role-Relationship Patterns
-Impaired verbal Communication
EX. Stroke pt with aphasia
3) Nutritional-Metabolic Pattern
-Impaired swallowing
-Risk for Aspiration
EX. CVA pt with dysphagia
4) Activity-Exercise Pattern
-Impaired mobility
-Ineffective cerebral tissue perfusion
-Self-care deficit
EX. Spinal cord injury pt's
