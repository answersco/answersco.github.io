---
title: "Where are parosteal osteosarcomas classically? What vegetable do they look like?"
---
Posterior distal femur metaphysis (may mimic cortical desmoid)
- Very densely ossified
- T1 and T2 dark due to dense ossification
- More dense centrally than peripherally (inverse of myositis ossificans which is more dense on the outside)

Cauliflower!
