---
title: "What is the ddx of recurrent PNA in different locations?"
---
1) Aspiration: Reflux, neuromuscular dz, TE fistula, esoph atresia, motility disorders, achalasia
2) Asthma, BPD
3) CF, down, Kartageners
4) Congenital Immune deficiencies: dysglobulinemias, SCID, DiGeorge, AT, malnutrition
5) Acquired immune deficiencies: Chemo, HIV, transplant
6) Anatomic or functional asplenia: sickle cell disease, splenectomy or asplenia (Streptococcus pneumoniae, Mycoplasma pneumonia)
