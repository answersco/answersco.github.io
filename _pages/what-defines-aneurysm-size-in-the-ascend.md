---
title: "What defines aneurysm size in the ascending, descending, and abdominal aortas? When to repair?"
---
Aneurysm: &gt; 4.0 cm ascending/transverse aorta; &gt; 3.5 cm descending; &gt; 3.0 cm abdominal aorta

Repair if: &gt;6.0 cm in the chest; 5.5 cm in chest w/ Collagen vasc dz; or 5.0 cm in abdomen
