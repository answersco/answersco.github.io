---
title: "Functional immaturity of the colon/small left colon"
---
A.k.a. meconium plug syndrome. (Not accurate name/confusing). It is not a cause of microcolon.

Temporary dysfunction of colonic ganglion cells. Failure to pass meconium.

Associated with preterm, magnesium for preeclampsia, diabetic mothers.

Typically transition in caliber at splenic flexure.

May see meconium filling defects

Tx and dx w enema. Unlike Hirschsprung disease will resolve with enema treatment and have distended rectum.
