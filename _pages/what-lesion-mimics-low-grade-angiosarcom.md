---
title: "What lesion mimics low-grade angiosarcoma on pathology?"
---
Pseudoangiomatous stromal hyperplasia (PASH) &#8594; rare entity of unknown etiology featuring stromal and epithelial proliferation
MM&#8594; ill-defined, round or oval mass, occasionally circumscribed
US&#8594; hypoechoic or mixed echogenicity, oval or irregular mass
Pathology: can mimic low-grade angiosarcoma&#8594; excisional biopsy is usually performed if PASH shows interval growth.
