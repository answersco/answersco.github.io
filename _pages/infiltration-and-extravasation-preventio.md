---
title: "Infiltration and extravasation prevention/nursing interventions"
---
1) Secure tubing and access device
-Position IV site to prevent dislodgment
-Protect IV tubing and site when ambulating patient

2) Check patency and position of access device prior to administration of all medications and fluids
-Flush with saline first

3) Assess site q1-2 hrs and as needed
-Check more frequently if potent medication or is pt has HF who is dehydrated b/c can have fluid overload

4) Be aware if antidote available

5) Discontinue IV if infiltration occurs
