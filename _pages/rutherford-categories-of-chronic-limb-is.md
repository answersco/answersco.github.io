---
title: "Rutherford categories of chronic limb ischemia: 0 - 3"
---
Grade 0:
-Category 0: asymptomatic. 
Normal treadmill or reactive hyperemia test.
-Category 1: mild claudication.
Completes treadmill test. Ankle pressure after exercise &gt; 50 mmHg but at least 20 mm Hg lower than brachial pressure.

Grade I:
-Category 2: moderate claudication
Objective criteria between cat 1 and 3
-Category 3: severe claudication
Cannot complete treadmill test. Ankle pressure &lt; 50 mmHg after exercise.
