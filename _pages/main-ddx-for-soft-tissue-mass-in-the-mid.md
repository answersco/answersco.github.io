---
title: "Main DDX for soft tissue mass in the middle ear with bony erosion:"
---
Cholesterol granuloma&#8594; will show restricted diffusion and will appear as a white mass on otoscopic examination. Typically hyperintense on T1-weighted images.

Cholesteatoma &#8594; congenital or acquired (middle-ear almost always acquired)
Typically appears as a well-circumscribed soft-tissue mass with adjacent bony erosion, blunting of the scutum, and erosion of the ossicles.
Hyperintense on DWI, and don't enhance (like epidermoids)
Intermediate to slightly hyperintense on T2-weighted imaging, and variable on T1-weighted images.
