---
title: "EVAR trivia"
---
Indication is an AAA that is larger than 5 cm (or 2x the size of adjacent normal vessel)
Indication can also be an AAA that is rapidly expanding &gt; 0.5 cm/6 months

Proximal landing zone must be:
- &gt; 10 mm long
- non-aneurysmal &lt; 3.2 cm
- angles less than 60 degrees (can't be tortuous)

Absolute contraindications:
- Landing sites that do not allow for exclusion of the aneurysm
- Covering a critical artery
