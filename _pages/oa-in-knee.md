---
title: "OA in knee:"
---
OA in knee &#8594; usually bilateral involvement.
Osteophytes determine whether OA is present
The degree of joint space narrowing determines the severity of OA
Typical pattern: asymmetrical involvement of the medial tibiofemoral compartment, with all 3 compartments involved in severe OA.
Degree of tibiofemoral cartilage space narrowing is best determined on standing weight-bearing views, often in flexion.
