---
title: "Transient osteoporosis"
---
Transient osteoporosis of the hip: classically female in 3rd trimester of pregnancy involving left hip.
More commonly bilateral in men
Joint space is normal
Self-limiting&#8594; resolves in a few months
XR&#8594; osteopenia
MRI&#8594; edema
Bone scan&#8594; focal increased uptake

Regional migratory osteoporosis: classic history of pain in a joint that resolves, followed by pain in another joint.
Associated with osteoporosis, also self-limited.
