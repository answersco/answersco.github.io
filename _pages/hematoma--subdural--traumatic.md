---
title: "Hematoma, Subdural, Traumatic"
---
Best diagnostic clue
Lobulated subdural mass with smooth margins
Location
May be located at any level of spine and extend into or from posterior fossa
Cervical is least frequent
Size
Focal or extensive
Variable degree of compression of spinal cord or nerve roots
Morphology
Biconvex or lentiform appearance on both sagittal and axial imaging
Along ventral, dorsal, &amp;/or lateral margin(s) of thecal sac
