---
title: "Urinary elimination"
---
-none as urination or voiding
-varies according to fluid intake: approximately 5X/day while awake
-assess color which is normally clear, odor (normal urine should not have a strong odor but some foods can change the odor of urine, also UTIs give urine a foul odor), amount (if &lt;30 ml/hr, may indicate increase renal diffusion or some kind of kidney issue so notify physician)
