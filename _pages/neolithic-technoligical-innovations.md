---
title: "Neolithic technoligical innovations"
---
These innovations include pottery, plows, woven textiles, wheels, and metallurgy (using metals such as iron, bronze, and copper to create tools). Technological innovations during the Neolithic era helped foster improvements in agriculture, trade, and transportation. The term &quot;Neolithic&quot; means &quot;New Stone Age&quot; and generaly refers to a period of development that happened after 8000 B.C.E.
