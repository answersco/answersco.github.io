title: "Subunit/acellular vaccines"
---
-contain only antigenic portion of the microbe
-bacterial capsules, cell walls, etc
-some produced through biotechnology
-contain only the single antigenic molecule

-ADVANTAGES
--few adverse reactions
--good to excellent immune response

-DISADVANTAGES
--newer vaccines expensive

-EXAMPLES
--Pneumococcus, HIB, Hepatitis B
