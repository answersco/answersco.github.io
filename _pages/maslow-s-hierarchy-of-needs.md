---
title: "Maslow's hierarchy of needs"
---
1) physiologic
2) safety
3) love
4) esteem
5) self-actualization

5 categories of needs; bottom level starts with the basic needs, physiological needs which is basic survival. Then Safety needs such as health insurance, then its social needs such as friends and the esteem needs which is job title and sense of self, and at the top is self-actualization needs which is the need to become everything one may become.
