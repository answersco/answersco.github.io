---
title: "Motivational interviewing"
---
strategy sometimes used in psycho-educational groups that emphasizes decision-making and resolves ambivalence about change

based of Carl Rogers' theory of therapy, psychology; more person-centered, goal-directed; resolve ambivalence, respectful approach to giving up/modifying substance use

a directive, client-centered counseling style for eliciting behavior change by helping clients explore and resolve ambivalence
