title: "P53"
---
-normal function is: determines if a cell repairs DNA replication errors or dies by apoptosis
-more than 50% of human cancers involve an abnormal p53 gene
-mutations occur only in somatic cells
-p53 cancers reflect environmental insults either from a virus, carcinogens, or radiation
-it work is preventing all cancers so if you lose your p53 gene, you can have just about any cancer there is
