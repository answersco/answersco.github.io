---
title: "What is the proposed mechanism of retroperitoneal fibrosis (RPF)?"
---
An immune reaction to atherosclerotic plaque within the aorta is felt to be an inciting agent in many cases of RP fibrosis. In fact, while the prognosis of patients with benign RP fibrosis is excellent, it is reduced in the long term because many of these patients die from cardiovascular complications.
