title: "Zygote stage"
---

-attaches to uterine wall
-protective layer forms = amniotic sac which protects the baby from any jolts the mom experiences, keeps temp constant, cushions the product of conception
-cells for placenta = the lifeline, connected by the umbilical cord, delivers blood, O2, nutrient, hormones, and removes wastes &amp; provides protection from many teratogens
-if really long umbilical cord, worry begins that it will wrap around baby's neck
