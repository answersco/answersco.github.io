---
title: "Syndromes associated with craniosynostosis"
---
Apert's syndrome: brachycephaly (bicoronal and/or bilamboid fusion) + fused fingers
Crouzon's: usually brachycephaly + first arch (maxilla and mandible) hypoplasia
Cleidocranial dysostosis: brachycephaly + Wormian bones + absent clavicles
