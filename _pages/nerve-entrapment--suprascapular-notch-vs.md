title: "Nerve entrapment: suprascapular notch vs. spinoglenoid notch"
---
Suprascapular notch vs. spinoglenoid notch
Cyst at level of suprascapular notch&#8594; will affect supraspinatus and infraspinatus (suprascapular nerve)
Cyst at level of spinoglenoid notch&#8594; will only affect the infraspinatus.
