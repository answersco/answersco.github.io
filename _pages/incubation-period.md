---
title: "Incubation period"
---
time between the pathogen's entrance &amp; appearance of symptoms
EX. Incubation of the flu is 1-4 days

-no symptoms
-varies with type of infection
-all the time it take for the microbes to get into the system and start reproducing
EX. common cold takes about 48 hours (acute) while Hep B takes about 30-60 days (chronic)
