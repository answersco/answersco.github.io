---
title: "Lymphoma of the breast:"
---
Lymphoma&#8594; primary breast lymphoma usually DLBCL
Can have a variable appearance
Usually palpable mass +/- axillary LAD
MM&#8594; mass with indistinct margins, but calcifications are rare
US&#8594; hypoechoic mass

In patient with known dx of lymphoma and new breast mass, the primary consideration remains breast cancer
