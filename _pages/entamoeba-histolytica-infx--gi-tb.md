---
title: "Entamoeba histolytica infx; GI TB"
---
Can cause abscess in liver, spleen, and brain
Colon: can cause toxic megacolon
Causes a &quot;coned cecum,&quot; referring to the change from normal, bulbous-appearing cecum to that of a cone.
Affects cecum and ascending colon most commonly.
Unlike many other GI infections, spares the TI.

Colonic TB can also cause coned cecum
Typically involves TI, with ulcers and regions of narrowing.
Fleishcner sign: enlarged, gaping IC valve.
Stierlin sign- narrowing of TI.
