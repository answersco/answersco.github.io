---
title: "Purpose of a binder"
---
-used to support tissue
-used to immobilize or splint
-used around the chest, abdomen, or pelvis
-used to hold a dressing in place
-designed for a specific body part
-generally made of cloth or elastic material
-can cause difficulty breathing and acid reflux when laying in bed
-sometimes it needs to be tighter when up and walking then when they are laying in bed
