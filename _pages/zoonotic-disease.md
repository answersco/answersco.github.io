---
title: "Zoonotic disease"
---
-spread of disease from animals to human
-type of contact transmission
-animal Bites: rabies
-contact with infected animal tissue, fluids, feces: leptospirosis (bacterial infection - in mice and rats and bacteria goes into urine), tularemia (rabbits)

-type of vector transmission
-infected animal --&gt; vector --&gt; person
EX. lyme disease and murine typhus
