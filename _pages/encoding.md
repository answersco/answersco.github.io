---
title: "Encoding"
---
&quot;writing it down&quot;
-1st step in memory: if you do not encode, it does not become a part of memory
-the process of taking information in through your senses and translating it into a form that your brain can &quot;write down&quot; and store for later use
-sometimes it happens automatically and sometimes it takes effort

changing information so that it fits into the preferred memory system
