---
title: "Hashimoto thyroiditis"
---
Autoimmune Hypothyroidism. nodular or coursened. Thick isthmus. Risk of lymphoma-growing nodule

Most common cause of goiter and primary hypothyroidism in adults in developed countries. Autoimmune disorder with circulating antithyroid antibody.

inflammatory, thyromegaly, hypothyroid, variable appearance
cold- lymphoid follicles/lymphocytes

antithyroglobulin antibodies - found in 55-90% of cases 2

thyroid peroxidase antibodies (TPO) - found in 90-95% of cases 2

present with hypothyroidism +/- a goitre
