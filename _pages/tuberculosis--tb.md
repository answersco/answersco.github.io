---
title: "Tuberculosis (TB)"
---
-Chronic and infectious disease of lungs

-Can spread to other tissues (Meninges, Kidneys, Bones, Lymph nodes)

-Mycobacterium: Increase in morbidity and mortality b/c people do not take all their abx (long term treatment and people do not comply) and abx hard to cross blood brain barrier

-Worldwide public health problem
--Associated with: poverty, malnutrition, overcrowding, substandard housing, inadequate health care

-Highly contagious via inhaled organisms

-Spread from person to person by airborne transmission
--Droplet nuclei
--Talking, coughing, sneezing, laughing, or singing
