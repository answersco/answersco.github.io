---
title: "Pulmonary AVM trivia?"
---
- HHT association
- Source of RIGHT to left shunt (therefore think of brain abscess and stroke 2/2 paradoxical emboli)
- More common in lower lobes
** Treat when afferent vessel is 3 mm
- Put coils in the feeding artery, as close to sac as possible
- Pleurisy is most common side effect
