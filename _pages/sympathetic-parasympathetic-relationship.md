---
title: "Sympathetic/parasympathetic relationship"
---
-antagonistic systems balance each other to support homeostasis - will not be all or nothing
-organs are innervated by both
-when one is blocked, the other has greater influence
-parasympathetic nervous system for males is erection and the sympathetic nervous system for males is ejaculation
-the normal functioning of the bladder is a combination of voluntary and involuntary control through both the sympathetic and parasympathetic nervous systems
