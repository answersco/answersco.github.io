---
title: "Quality of life issues"
---
1) Difficult to define b/c quality for one person could not be quality to someone else - very subjective
2) Factors affecting QOL
-presence of pain, social and financial support, caregivers
3) Many health care and life management decisions by older adult made based on perception of QOL
-do older adults want to continue with something intense or long - may be tired and not want to continue
