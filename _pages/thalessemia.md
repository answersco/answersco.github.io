---
title: "Thalessemia"
---
-caused by a different beta hemoglobin mutation
-too few beta globin molecules
-prevents formation of enough Hb molecules --&gt; RBCs die, release iron, which damages heart, liver, and endocrine glands.
-thalassemia minor (heterozygous)
-thalassemia major (homozygous for mutation and more severe)
