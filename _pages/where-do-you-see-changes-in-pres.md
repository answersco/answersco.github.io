---
PCA territory predilection
* SUBCORTICAL and cortical WHITE MATTER EDEMA (FLAIR bright, vasogenic edema, DOES NOT RESTRICT, e.g. NOT a stroke!)

- asssoc with HTN, Pre-eclampsia, eclampsia, Chemo (cyclosporine/cisplatin), DIC, TTP, SEPTICEMIA

Distractor: hypoglycemia - more cortical and gyral swelling (as opposed to subcortical disease in PRES)
