---
title: "Lithium nephropathy:"
---
Can lead to diabetes insipidus and renal insufficiency
Kidneys are normal to small in volume with innumerable tiny cysts (usually 2-5 mm diameter).
Will show on MRi with history of bipolar disorder.

Tiny microcysts and shrunken kidneys. Cysts are smaller than in acquired cystic dz of uremia
