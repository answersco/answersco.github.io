---
title: "Mnemonic for interpreting an angiogram?"
---
What, Where, When, VIA. What type of study (Angiogram, dsa, venogram). Where (Where is the cathether and what vessel, large vessel or superselective?. When (Early arterial, late arterial, parenchymal, or venous). Is there opacification of any veins during arterial phase? Vessels (Any contrast going where it shouldn't, do vessels have normal contour?). Interventiosn (Previously placed stents, grafts, filters, coils, clips, or drains). Anything else (Fx?)
