---
title: "What are the sx/complications of infants with holoprosencephalies?"
---
developmental delay and mental retardation; seizures (50%); hydrocephalus (alobar); microcephaly (semilobar/lobar); Feeding difficulties; dystonias; Endocrine dysfunction(75%) usually Diabetes insipidus (hypernatremia) and issues with homeothermy (33%).
