---
title: "Mazabraud Syndrome"
---
Combination of:
1. Polyostotic fibrois dysplasia
2. Multiple soft tissue myxomas

Myxoma = T2 bright with T1 signal slightly lower than muscle (see image for that REALLY LOW T1 signal)

Polyostotic fibrous dysplasia
Multiple soft tissue myxomas

Fibrous Dysplasia with intramuscular myxomas (Usually in same region of body)
