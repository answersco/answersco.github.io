---
title: "FIGO staging endometrial cancer?"
---
Stage IA: Confined to uterine wall (inner 50% myometrium)
Stage IIA: Confined to uterine wall (outer 50% of myometrium)
Stage IIB: Invasion of cervical stroma, but doesn't extend beyond uterus (requires RADIATION Pre-op)
Stage III: Tumor extends beyond the uterus but is confined to the pelvis
- IIIB = hydronephrosis or pelvic side wall extension
Stage IV: Tumors invaded adjacent organs like bladder/bowel or have distant mets
