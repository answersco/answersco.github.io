---
title: "Medication administration"
---
-liquid form of medication is preferred
-has to say where the administration is - if it says oral or through feeding tube
-many tablets can be crushed and dissolved in water
-do not crush enteric coated or time released capsules
-many capsules can be opened and dissolved in water, check drug guide
-if going through tube, flush with water, disconnect tube and draw medication, put medication in, then flush with water again
-if there was continuous tube feed, you would disconnect the tube feeding first but make sure to reconnect tube feeding after medication administration
