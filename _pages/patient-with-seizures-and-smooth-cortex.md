Lissencephaly: absent or &#8595; cortical convolutions, causing a smooth, thickened cortical surface, and seizures
May be caused by CMV, in which case calcifications are often present
Type I (classical): smooth cortex and hourglass or figure-of-eight configuration of the cerebral hemispheres.
Type II (cobblestone): finely undulating cortex
