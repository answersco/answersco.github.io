---
title: "HMG-CoA reductase inhibitors/statins patient teaching"
---
-take at bedtime b/c symptoms and works better with the circadian rhythm at this point
-must monitor liver function
-can cause myopathies
-report muscle weakness or pain (on alert for rhabdomyolysis which is serious adverse reaction)
-maintain low cholesterol diet
-note if avoiding grapefruit - liver toxicity
-avoid pregnancy
