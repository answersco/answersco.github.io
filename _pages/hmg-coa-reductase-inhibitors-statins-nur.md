---
title: "HMG-CoA reductase inhibitors/statins nursing instructions"
---
-teach lifetime commitment and dietary and exercise changes
-monitor liver enzymes,*** 
-report unexplained muscle tenderness or weakness (#1 reason for noncompliance), fever, and malaise
-check timing with food and bowel elimination
-follow eye checks for cataracts
-blurred vision , nausea &amp; vomiting
