---
title: "Hypoplastic left heart syndrome:"
---
Hypoplastic left heart syndrome: most severe form of CHD, presenting in neonatal period with CHF, cardiogenic shock, and cyanosis
Includes hypoplastic/atretic ascending aorta, AV, MV, and MV
Common PDA, juxtaductal coarctation
CXR: cardiomegaly with large RA and pulmonary venous congestion with interstitial fluid.
Small, thick-walled LV, dilated right-sided heart chambers
