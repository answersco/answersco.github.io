---
title: "What Lean Process Improvement tool focuses on standardization of work areas?"
---
Answer: Five S
The goal of the Five S tool in the Lean system is the standardization of work areas and simplification of the manner in which work flows across the system.

Value Stream Mapping: helps understanding of the components and information flow within a process.
Just-in-time: refers to a process whereby excess inventory is kept to a minimum.
Error-proofing: testing of a process such that sources of error are identified immediately in a new process so that immediate problem solving and variation reduction is possible.
