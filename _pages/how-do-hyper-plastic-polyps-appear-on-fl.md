---
title: "How do hyper plastic polyps appear on Fluoro?"
---
Sharply delineated circular filling defects (&lt; 2 cm) - may be sessile or pedunculated.
- usually body or fundus of stomach
- Most associated with Chronic atrophic gastritis and pernicious anemia and less so chronic H pylori or Crohns

- FAP &amp; Gardner's causes colonic polyps
- Peutz Jeghers causes small intestinal polyps
