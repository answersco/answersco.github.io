---
title: "Grading of renal trauma?"
---
1. Contusion of sub capsular hematoma w/o laceration
2. Superficial laceration &lt; 1 cm w/o extension to collection system
3. Laceration &gt; 1 cm in length w/o extension to collecting system
4. Laceration extends to renal pelvis with resultant extrav from collecting system or vessels
5. Shattered, devascularized kidney
