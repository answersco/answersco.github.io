---
title: "Treatment for dyssomnias"
---
1) nonpharmacologic therapy: Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)
-progressive muscle relaxation measures
-stimulus control
-sleep restriction (no naps); sleep hygiene measures (getting up at same time everyday, only go to bed when you're tired)
-biofeedback and relaxation therapy
2) pharmacologic therapy
-sedatives
-hypnotics
