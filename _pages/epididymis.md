title: "Epididymis"
---
-coiled duct system
-sperm mature in these coils
-continuous with vas deferens
-when performing this examination, we ask they palpate the posterior surface of the testes first to find the epididymis that is a cord like structure that can sometimes feel like a lump - when found on anterior or side of testicle then it need evaluated by a physician

transports and matures sperm to vas deferens

-sperm maturation
-gain mobility over the course of about two weeks
