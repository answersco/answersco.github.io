---
title: "Implanted ports administration guidelines"
---
1) Assure correct initial placement

2) Access port using non-coring needle

3) Establish blood return
-If no blood return, can use &quot;push-pull&quot; method which may eventually elicit a blood return.
-Important to know policy/procedure for facility concerning administration of chemotherapy without a blood return.
-Always thoroughly flush an implanted port to clear the port of drug and blood residue. 
-Huber needle has tail on end of it that needs primed with saline prior to placing needle in port
-Pull back for blood return then flush
-Put clear occlusive dressing over it
-If not being used, needs closed off with 100U of heparin

4) Assess site 

5) Use occlusive dressing
