---
title: "Normal BPP?"
---
8-10

1. Amniotic fluid (at least one pocket &gt; 2cm in vertical plane)
2. Fetal movement (at least 3)
3. Fetal tone (1 episode of fetal extension from flexion)
4. Breathing (1 episode of breathing motion lasting 30 seconds)
5. Non stress test = 2 or more fetal heart rate accelerations of 15 bpm or for 30 seconds
