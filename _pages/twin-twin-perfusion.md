---
title: "Twin-twin perfusion"
---
Occurs in monochorionic twins due to vascular communication in placenta
Donor twin does better, while the recipient develops hydrops and dies

Unequal fluid in amniotic sacs- donor (skinny) twin with severe oligohydramnios, often &quot;stuck to the wall of the uterus&quot;. The recipient has polyhydramniotic sac.

Donor twin has high resistance umbilical artery spectrum
