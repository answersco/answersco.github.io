---
title: "Routine premedication"
---
Works best for minor reactions

Method 1:
prednisone 50mg PO 13h, 7h, 1h prior
diphenhydramine 50mg (IV or PO) 1h prior

Method 2:
methylprednisolone 32mg 12h, 2h prior
diphenhydramine 50mg (IV or PO) 1h prior

Method 3 (if patient cannot take PO):
hydrocortisone 200mg IV 13h, 7h, 1h prior
diphenhydramine 50mg (IV or PO) 1h prior
