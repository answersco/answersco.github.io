---
title: "What are 2 lines on the elbow radiograph to assess for alignment"
---
Radiocapitellar line:
- line through center of radius should intersect middle of the capitellum on every view
- if radius is dislocated, the line with NOT pass through center

Anterior Humeral Line
- on a true lateral, line along anterior humerus should pass through middle third of capitellum
- supracondlar fracture: line will pass through anterior third
