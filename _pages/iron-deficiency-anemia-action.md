title: "Iron deficiency anemia action"
---
-many people get the iron they need through diet
-elevate the serum iron concentration, then converted to hemoglobin or stored to convert into usable iron for RBC production later
-some forms are given PO and some are administered parenterally
(Z track method (used when you do not want it to leak back or irritating solutions) or IV if pt's are low enough in iron they can give IV infusion)
