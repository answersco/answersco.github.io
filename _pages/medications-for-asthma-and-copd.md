---
title: "Medications for asthma and COPD"
---
1) Inhaled corticosteroids:
-most effective, more for maintenance to try and keep inflammation under control 
--Fluticasone 
--Budesonide 
--Flunisolide 

2) Short acting bronchodilator: 
-For RESCUE
--Beta 2 adrenergic agonists
--Albuterol 
--Levalbuterol

3) Long-acting B2 adrenergic agonist
-For maintenance or prevention
--Salmeterol 
--Formoterol 

4) Combos: 
--Fluticasone-salmeterol 
--Budesonide-formoterol
