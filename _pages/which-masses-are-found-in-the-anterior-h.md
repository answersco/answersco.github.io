---
title: "Which masses are found in the anterior half of the lateral ventricles vs the mass in the posterior half of the lateral ventricle?"
---
Posterior Half = Meningioma (b/c they arise from the arachnoid cap cells in the choroid plexus)

Anterior half:
1. Subependymoma (older male)
2. Ependymoma (young)
3. SEGA (near foramen of Monro in tuberous sclerosis)
4. Central neurocytoma (attached to septum pellucidum) in 20s yo
