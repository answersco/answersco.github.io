---
title: "What is a Brenner Tumor?"
---
Fibrous ovarian tumor that, unlike fibromas, ARE OFTEN CALCIFIED
- &quot;ovarian transition cell carcinoma&quot;
- T2 dark

Brenner tumors manifest as multilocular cystic masses with solid components or as small, mostly solid masses. At T2WI, the dense fibrous stroma demonstrates lower signal intensity similar to that of a fibroma.
