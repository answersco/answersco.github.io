---
title: "Thoracic surgery complications"
---
1) Immobility 
-atelectasis (collapse of airway, O2 drops, CO2 climbs)

2) Bleeding

3) Pneumothorax 
-collapsed lung due to too much air in chest cavity

4) Infection

5) Respiratory and/or Heart failure 
-primarily R-sided HF

6) Fistula formation 
-Opening between airway and other structure
