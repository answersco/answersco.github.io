---
title: "Stage 2 pressure ulcer"
---
-partial-thickness skin loss; presents as an abrasion or blister
-T&amp;P, not too dry or too moist, protective dressings

-partial-thickness skin loss involving epidermis or dermis
-ulcer is superficial, shallow
-looks like an abrasion, blister, or shallow crater
-if pt has one pressure ulcer, they are likely to have more so check all bony prominences
-can heal in weeks if covered and treated 
-t&amp;p, appropriate nutrition and hygiene, keeping skin dry and well moisturized
