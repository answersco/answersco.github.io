---
title: "Vaginal irrigation procedure"
---
1) fill the bag and connect the douche tip
2) lubricate the tip
3) prime tubing
4) clamp tubing
5) hang container 18-24 inches above the hips of the patient
6) insert tip 3-4 inches into the vagina angled toward the spine
7) unclamp the tubing
8) allow the fluid to flow, rotating tip periodically
9) when the fluid has been instilled remove the tip
10) keep the patient on a bedpan in a sitting position to allow drainage of all the fluid
11) provide pericare
