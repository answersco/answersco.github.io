---
1) reproductive history
2) history of sexually transmitted infections (STI)
3) history of sexual dysfunction 
4) sexual self-care behaviors
5) sexual self-concept
-any physical changes you're concerned about, are you comfortable with your partner, has anything interfered with your sex ability with your partner
-sexual identity
-sexual body image
-sexual self-esteem
-sexual role performance
6) sexual functioning
-has anything changed your ability in sexual functioning
