---
title: "What findings are diagnostic of pregnancy failure:"
---
CRL &gt; 7 mm and no heartbeat
Mean sac diameter &gt; 25 mm and no embryo
No embryo with heartbeat &gt; 11 days after a scan showed a gestational sac with a yolk sac.
No embryo with heartbeat &gt; 2 weeks after a scan showed a gestational sac without a yolk sac
