---
title: "What are the 6 competencies of MOC?"
---
Medical Knowledge is 1 of the 6 core competencies of the Maintenance of Certification (MOC) and relates to the detection, interpretation, and management of diagnoses. The other 5 competencies are (1) patient care (2) interpersonal and communication skills (3) professionalism (4) systems-based practice (6) practice-based learning and improvement.
