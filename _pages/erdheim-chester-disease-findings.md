---
title: "Erdheim chester disease findings?"
---
Non-LCH form of grnaulomatosis with b/l perirenal soft tissue, smooth pleural thickening, diffuse interlobular septal thickening, and b/l symmetric sclerotic lesions in femurs and tibias.

- Differential for perirenal and RP soft tissue/adenoapthy includes Erdheim chester, Rosai Dorfman, lymphoma, and RP fibrosis.
