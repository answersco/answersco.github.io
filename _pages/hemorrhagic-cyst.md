---
title: "Hemorrhagic cyst"
---
US: homogeneous, enhanced through transmission
-fishnet appearance
-goes away in 6 weeks

MR: T1 bright, no enhancement
-if old woman --&gt; cancer
-if fat suppression --&gt; teratoma

These can look similar to an endometrioma
They will have INCREASED through transmission (cancer will not, endometrioma will also have increased through transmission)
These will NOT usually do shading (T2 darkening)
Dopper flow will be absent and they may have a fishnet appearance (see image)
These will also usually go away in 1-2 menstrual cycles so wait 6-8 weeks
