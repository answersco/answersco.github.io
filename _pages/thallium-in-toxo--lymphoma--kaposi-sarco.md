---
title: "Thallium in toxo, lymphoma, kaposi sarcoma, tumor, and necrosis"
---
Toxo: thallium negative
Lymphoma: thallium positive
Kaposi sarcoma: thallium positive (gallium neg)
Tumor: thallium positive
Necrosis: thallium negative

Can use thallium in combination with perfusion tracers (HMPAO)
-Tumor: thallium hot, HMPAO cold
-Necrosis: thallium cold, HMPAO cold
