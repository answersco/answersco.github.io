---
title: "What is Reversible cerebral vasoconstriction syndrome?"
---
A reversible, multifocal cerebral artery vasoconstrictions, p/w severe headaches &#177; focal neurological deficits. DSA is dx with irregular diffuse/multifocal segmental narrowing &quot;string of beads&quot; or &quot;sausage strings of the large/medium arteries. ON NECT of the head there can be biconvex SAH without aneurysm.
