---
title: "Extensor compartments of wrist and locations of disease"
---
6 extensor compartments:
1st compartment (abductor pollicis longus and extensor pollicis brevis) &#8594; these tendons are affected in de Quervain's tenosynovitis
Presents with pain and thumb movement and point tenderness over radial styloid

1st and 2nd: involved with Intersecting syndrome

3rd compartment (extensor pollicis longus) &#8594; courses beside Lister's tubercle

6th compartment (extensor carpi ulnaris) &#8594; can get early tenosynovitis in RA
