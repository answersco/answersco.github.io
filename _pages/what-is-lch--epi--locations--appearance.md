---
title: "What is LCH? Epi? Locations? Appearance?"
---
Langerhans Cell Histiocytosis, &lt;30 yo with peak 5-10 yo. Skull, mandible, ribs (expansile lesion), femur, pelvis, spine. Well-defined lytic/erosive lesion without sclerosis. EG is bone or lung involvement. Hand-Scheuller- Christian Dz is chronic disseminated form. Letterer-Siwe is acute form with high mortality &lt;1 yo.
