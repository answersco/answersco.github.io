---
title: "Peace of Westphalia"
---
the peace treaty that ended the Thirty Years' War in 1648 allowing states in the HRE to establish their own foreign policies and state religion

the 1648 agreement ending the Thirty Years' War, fragmenting Germany states by allowing states in the Holy Roman Empire to establish their own foreign policies and state; it ended the CChurch's attempts to reestablish Catholic hegemony over central Europe; it included Calvinism along with Lutheranism and Catholicism as acceptable choices, and it granted independence to the Swiss Confederation and the Dutch Republic
