---
title: "How do you differentiate the three stages of empyema by US?"
---
Stage 1: Clear fluid or mild mobile sediment 
Stage 2: Particles, membranes and septations 
Stage 3: Fusiform shape, Obtuse angle with the chest wall, Separation and thickening of pleural layers
Increased blood flow and decreased resistive index in adjacent systemic (intercostal, diaphragmatic etc.) arteries
