---
title: "Gynecomastia: - what is it? - causes - mammo look"
---
- it is a non-neoplastic enlargement of epithelial and stromal elements

Causes:
- physiologic in adolescents
- drugs (spironolactone, psych meds, marijuana)
- cirrhosis
- testicular cancer

Mammo: flame shaped, behind nipple; can be bilateral but asymmetric
- can be painful
