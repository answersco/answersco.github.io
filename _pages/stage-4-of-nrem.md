---
title: "Stage 4 of NREM"
---
-deepest (slow wave) stage of sleep
-realistic dreams happen - if awoken during stage 4 NREM sleep it is difficult to determine whether dream was real life or not
-very difficult to awaken
-lasts 15-30 minutes
-newborns spend more time and elderly less time
-hormones related to restorative phase happen during this phase
-sleepwalking, enuresis, and night terrors occur during this phase
