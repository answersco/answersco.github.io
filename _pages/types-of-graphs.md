---
title: "Types of graphs"
---
1. ROC curve: sensitivy on the x acis and specificity on the y axis

2. Dashboard: continuous and easily visible display of progress

3. Fishbone: determining factors and root cause analysis

4. Control charts: your performance is charted against the average over time.

5. Flow chart: decision making branches and roadmap type tool

6. P something chart: determines the most important factors and ranks them from left to right with a bar graph and a superimposed line graph
