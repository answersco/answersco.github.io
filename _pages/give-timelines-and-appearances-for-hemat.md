---
title: "Give timelines and appearances for hematomas."
---
1) Hyperacute (0-6 hours)-Intracellular oxyhemoglobin- Diamagnetic- T1 Iso- T2 Iso to hyper (Dark Rim). 2) Acute Hematoma (6-72 hours)- Intracellular deoxyhemoglobin- Parmagnetic- T1 Iso or Hypo- T2 Hypo. 3) Early subacute(3 days to 1 week)- Intracellular methemoglobin- Paragmagnetic- T1 Hyper. T2 Hypo. 4) Late subacute (1 week to months). Paramagnetic. T1 and T2 Hyper. 5) Chronic- T1 iso or hypo. T2 Hypo.
