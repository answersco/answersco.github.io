---
title: "What is the most likely dx for an opacified frontal sinus with smooth bony remodeling? Locations? Imaging findings?"
---
Mucocele, due to obstructed ostium: Frontal (60-65%) &gt; ethmoid (25%) &gt; maxillary (5-10%) &gt; sphenoid (2-5%). &gt; 90% have ophthalmic signs &amp; symptoms. No central enhancement on CT/MR.
