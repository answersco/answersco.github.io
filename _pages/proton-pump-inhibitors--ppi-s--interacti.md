title: "Proton pump inhibitors (PPI's) interactions"
---
1) decreased absorption of anything that needs an acidic environment
2) increased levels
-benzodiazepines
-warfarin
-phenytoin
3) decreased levels
-ketoconazole
-theophylline
4) sucralfate
5) **clopidrogrel: antiplatelet medication that puts pt at risk for cardiac events when combined with PPI's
