---
title: "Pancreatic tumors: grandmother lesion"
---
Serous cystadenoma: benign tumor in elderly women&#8594; grandmother tumor
Consists of many small cysts (&gt; 6 cysts that are &lt; 2 cm) that may have a solid appearance on CT due to apposition of many cyst walls. MRI useful to show its cystic nature.
Hypervascular, which is unique among cystic pancreatic tumors.
No pancreatic ductal dilatation or tail atrophy
Classic feature: central stellate calcification
