title: "Selecting an antimicrobial agent"
---
1) type of microbe causing the disease
2) the medical condition of the patient
-life threatening vs. non-life threatening
-toxic side effects of the drug: aminoglycosides in high doses and the patient can suffer and damages kidney or allergic reactions
3) cost of the drug: newer and better drugs are more expensive
4) the susceptibility (sensitivity) of the microbe to the drug(s)
5) Kirby-Bauer method
6) MIC method
