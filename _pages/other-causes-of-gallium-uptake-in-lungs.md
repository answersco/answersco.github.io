---
title: "Other causes of gallium uptake in lungs"
---
Can show early drug reaction from chemotherapy (bleomycin) or other drugs (amiodarone).

Elevated in IPF and can be used to monitor response to therapy

Pneumocystic PNA: gallium hot
-Typically diffuse, bilateral uptake
-Kaposi sarcoma is negative on gallium, positive on thallium.

Bacterial PNA: intense lobar configuration without parotid or nodal uptake.
