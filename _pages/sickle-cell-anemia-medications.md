---
title: "Sickle cell anemia medications"
---
-Hydroxyurea (Droxia) - help to avoid the sickle cell crisis
-have to take for several months
-increases the amount of hemoglobin produced in the bone marrow and avoid the crisis from occurring
-results in less clogging of the small vessels and anoxic effects
-given PO, well absorbed in GI, metabolized in liver, excreted in the urine
-crosses placenta and can be excreted breast milk
