---
title: "Society of Jesus"
---
A Roman Catholic order founded by Saint Ignatius of Loyola in 1534 to defend Catholicism against the Reformation and to do missionary work.

the most important reforming religious order in the mid-16th Century who were also called &quot;Jesuits&quot;; they dominated seminaries and schools, missionary activities, and canon law (Church law) during the Spanish Inquisition
