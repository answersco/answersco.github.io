---
title: "How to distinguish the superior right intercostal vein from the azygos vein?"
---
Superior right intercostal vein is ABOVE the aortic arch (drains 2nd-4th interspaces) and drains into the azygos arch itself.

The azygos vein is BELOW the aortic arch and arches over the Right mainstem bronchus as the azygos &quot;arch&quot; into the SVC
