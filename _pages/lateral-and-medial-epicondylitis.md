---
title: "Lateral and medial epicondylitis"
---
Lateral epicondylitis: more common than medial &#8594; tennis players
Extensor tendon injury: classically extensor carpi radialis brevis
Radial collateral ligament complex&#8594; tears due to varus stress

Medial epicondylitis: golfers
Common flexor tendon and ulnar nerve may enlarge from chronic injury
