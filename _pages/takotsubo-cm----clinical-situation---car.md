---
title: "Takotsubo CM: - clinical situation - Cardiac Findings - Abnormal enhancement on MR?"
---
- Occurs in post-menopausal women after a stressful event
- associated with chest pain and EKG changes

Cardiac Findings:
- Transient akinesia or dyskinesia of the LV apex without coronary stenosis.
- Ballooning of the LV apex

- No delayed enhancement.
