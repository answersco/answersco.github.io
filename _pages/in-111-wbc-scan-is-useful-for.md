---
title: "In-111 WBC scan is useful for?"
---
1) Find infxn/ inflam in FUO.
2) Localize site in febrile post-op pt.
3) EOD for inflam bowel dz (99mTc may be preferred)
4) Osteomyelitis with hardware or ununited fx.
5) Osteo for DM, charcot, trauma or degen changes.
6) Mycotic aneurysms, graft infections, shunt infections.
7) NOT useful for vertebral/disc osteo (Ga-67)
8) NOT useful for chronic granulomatous infxn (Ga-67)
