title: "Whole body scan WITH bones.  How can spleen be used to distinguish the type?"
---
Bones + hot spleen = WBC (indium or Tc)

Bones + spleen (spleen = liver uptake)
-Sulfur colloid

Bones + light or no spleen = gallium

(Hint: if you see bones in a whole body scan, look at the spleen; Bones --&gt; look at spleen)
