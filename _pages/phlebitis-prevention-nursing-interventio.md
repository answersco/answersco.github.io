---
title: "Phlebitis prevention/nursing interventions"
---
1) Standard IV precautions
-Check IV frequently

2) Buffer irritating meds &amp; hypertonic solutions

3) If Phlebitis occurs:
-D/C IV &amp; restart (will need IV ATBs)
-Cold compresses 1st 24 hours, then warm compresses
-Culture site/device for infection (as ordered/ protocol)
-Monitor VS b/c fever can develop

5) Document
-Catheter was intact when removed, what did the IV site look like/feel like/discharge,
