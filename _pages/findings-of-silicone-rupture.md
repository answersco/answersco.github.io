---
- Silicone = very dense on mammo

1. Isolated intracapsular = occult on pysical exam: &quot;stepladder ultrasound&quot;, 
** Linguine sign or KEYHOLE sign on MRI 
* NORMAL ON MAMMOGRAM!

2. Intracapuslar wiht extracapsular rupture = dense silicone seen outisde the capsule with normal intact smooth implant contour
* Extracapsular silicone can be identified on mammography as dense masses outside the implant
- snow storm = echogenic w/o posterior shadowing
- on MRI: extracapsular silicone is T1 dark and T2 bright
