---
title: "Findings of amyloidosis and Wilson disease in the liver:"
---
Amyloid in liver: deposition of amyloid protein can cause focal or diffuse areas of &#8595; attenuation on CT

Wilson disease: high levels of copper accumulate in basal ganglia, cornea, and liver due to AR defect.
Liver may be hyperattenuating on CT with multiple nodules
Eventually leads to hepatomegaly and cirrhosis.
