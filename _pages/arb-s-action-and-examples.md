---
(angiotensin II receptor blockers)
-selectively bind to angiotensin II receptors in vascular smooth muscle &amp; adrenal cortex
-stopping the RAAS system so it will have same effect as ACE-I
-usually given if ACE-I gives you the ACE cough
-hard on kidneys at first but in the long-run will help the function
EX. -sartan meds
