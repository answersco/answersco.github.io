---
title: "Radiographic findings Hyperflexion Sprain"
---
o Focal kyphosis, mild anterolisthesis 
o Widened facet, interspinous/ interlaminar spaces 
o Widened space between posterior vertebral body and facet below
o Widened posterior, narrowed anterior disc space 
o Compression fracture often associated 
o All findings accentuated with flexion; MRI to confirm ligament injury
