title: "Renal Cancer Blitz"
---
Renal Cell Carcinoma: (adenocarcinoma)
Subtypes:
- Clear Cell: most common (enhances more)
- Papillary: 2nd most common (enhances less)
- Medullary: buzzword for sickle cell trait
- Chromophobe: buzzword for Burt Hogg Dube

Syndrome:
- Von Hipple Lindau: Multiple Clear Cells
