---
title: "Patriarchal society"
---
This kind of society is governed and dominated by men. Fathers are considered heads of the household, and everyone must submit to their authority. Women are excluded from positions of power and may not be able to own land, although they may have a few rights. Patriarchal societies first emerged as human beings organized themselves into families. In the Neolithic era, many cultures practiced patriarchy.

a society in which families become dominated by the male head of the family
