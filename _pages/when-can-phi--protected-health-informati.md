---
title: "When can PHI (protected health information) be released without to anyone other than the individual without that individual's authorization?"
---
1. For the delivery of care or treatment
2. Payment activities
3. Health care operations involving quality or competency assurance, fraud/abuse detection, or compliance
4. When required by law to public health authorities, to law enforcement, in cases of abuse/neglect, for judicial precedings, for worker's compensation
