---
title: "Xenon"
---
- Dose - 10-20 mCi
- Action - enters airways, alveoli
- Normal uptake - lungs
- Xenon
o Washes out fast; only have time for posterior images (posterior imaging is chosen to decrease breast attenuation)
o Allows assessment of air trapping because it washes out so fast 
o Requires a special exhaust system or charcoal trap
o Xenon is taken up in a fatty liver
