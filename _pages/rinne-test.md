---
title: "Rinne test"
---
-compares bone and air conduction
-bottom of tuning fork on mastoid, let it go until pt says they stop hearing the sound
-move off mastoid bone and in front of ear and they should still be able to hear a ringing
-if person hears bone conducted sound longer or for the same length of time = conductive hearing loss
-if person hears air conducted sound slightly longer or not at all = sensorineural hearing loss
