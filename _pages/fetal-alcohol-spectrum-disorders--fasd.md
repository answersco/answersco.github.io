---
title: "Fetal Alcohol Spectrum Disorders (FASD)"
---
-a cluster of problems that appear in the children of mother who drink alcohol during pregnancy
-defect depend on how much you drank, how often, and what periods of time the mom drank
-severe= physical and mental abnormalities: cleft palate, eyes are far apart, ears lower, heads shaped flat, hard to figure out time/money
