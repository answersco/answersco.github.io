---
title: "Perc chole: Seldinger technique"
---
Seldinger technique requires fluoro and US guidance- unsuitable for perc chole in ICU or at bedside.
-22-g needle from a one-stick needle system (Cook) inserted into GB under US-guidance
-GB opacified with dilute CM, and an 0.018 in guidewire inserted into GB using fluoro guidance.
-5-Fr sheath system placed over 0.018 wire into GB
-0.035-in J-shaped guidewire placed through sheath into the GB, with as much as possible coiled in GB to achieve adequate purchase.
-Track is dilated using 7 and 9-Fr dilators
-8-Fr pigtail catheter with a small pigtail is placed.
