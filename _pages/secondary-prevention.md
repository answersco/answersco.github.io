---
title: "Secondary Prevention"
---
-maximizing health and wellness through strategies that are set in place at the early and active chronic stages of pathogenesis of illness and injury
-CHN's perform interventions that seek to detect disease early in its progression, before clinical signs and symptoms become apparent, in order to make an early diagnosis and begin treatment.
-screening programs are necessary for diseases with few specific primary prevention strategies.
-occurs in community settings and in primary and secondary levels of care
EX. mammography to detect breast cancer, Pap tests to detect cervical cancer, colonoscopy for early detection of colon cancer

-disease identification
-screening and early diagnosis
-treating problem promptly
-shortening duration of severity of consequences
EX. -colonoscopy
-early detection, early cure
-catch it in its earliest stages
