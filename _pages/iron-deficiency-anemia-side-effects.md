---
title: "Iron deficiency anemia side effects"
---
1) GI irritation
-GI upset: nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, dark stools, **constipation (issue for noncompliance)
-pregnant women are normally constipated so they do not want to take iron supplements bc they are already constipated 
2) CNS: can see CNS depression, coma, and death
