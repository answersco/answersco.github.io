---
title: "Massive Retaliation"
---
Term coined by John Foster Dulles to imply that the US would use nuclear force in response to Communist aggression anywhere.

a military doctrine associated with Pres. Eisenhower's secretary of state, John F. Dulles, that stated that in the event of an attack by the Soviet Union (or any other hostile power), the U.S. would respond forcefully, including nuclear weapons; used to deter enemies from launching an initial attack
