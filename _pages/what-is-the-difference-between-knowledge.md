---
title: "What is the difference between Knowledge, Rules, and Skill?"
---
Knowledge:
- when you first learn to do something
- requires deliberate effort
- the cause of MISTAKES

Rules:
- middle man where you operate with an algorithm (if X, then Y)

Skill:
- repetitive things are just done from memory &quot;autopilot&quot;
- the cause of SLIPS/LAPSES
