---
title: "Myelination in full-term newborn:"
---
Pattern of myelination: generally assumes mature pattern by 2 years
Full-term newborn will have myelination of several structures at birth:
Posterior limb of internal capsule
Middle cerebellar peduncle
Ventrolateral thalami
Dorsal brainstem
Perirolandic regions
Myelin is bright on T1, dark on T2
