---
title: "Mucosa"
---
-goblet cells produce mucous
-epithelium specialized for absorption
-folded into villi to increase surface area
-microvilli on apical surface of columnar epithelia increase surface area more
-brush border enzymes in cell membranes (maltase, lactase, peptidases, nucleosideases, enterokinase)
-fatty acids, amino acids, sugars move across cells into lamina propria
-sugars, aminos enter blood
-fats enter lacteals, a type of lymphatic capillary

-lines the lumen (innermost layer) and consists of inner epithelium, lamina propria, and muscularis mucosa
-simple columnar epithelia folded up, formed gastric pits with gastric glands
