---
title: "IV assessment for phlebitis"
---
-inflammation of the vein 
1) signs and symptoms 
-pain
-edema 
-erythema 
-increase skin temperature 
-redness traveling the path of the vein
-vein feels hard like a electrical cord
2) infusion must be disconnected, raise the extremity, and apply warm compresses
3) risk of developing a blood clot
4) risk reduced by routine removal and rotation of sites
