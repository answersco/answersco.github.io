---
title: "Spleen"
---
mets (melanoma, lung, breast), lymphoma, abscesses (TB, cand)

-filters the blood
-capillaries of spleen are packed with white blood cells and B and T lymphocytes

-houses B cells and T-cells
-removes worn-out RBCs
-stores platelets
-damage/rupture means splenectomy
-red bone marrow, liver can pick up slack -- some decreased immune function
