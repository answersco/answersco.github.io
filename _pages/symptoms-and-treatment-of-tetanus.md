---
title: "Symptoms and treatment of tetanus"
---
symptoms:
-painful spastic paralysis of skeletal muscles
-results in tense contractions of muscles
-neck, face and limbs then respiratory muscles
-difficulty swallowing, breathing
-death due to respiratory failure

treatment:
-antibiotics get rid of bacteria and no new toxins are being released
-passive immunization - human tetanus immune globulin
-respiratory support

prevention:
-active immunization - tetanus toxoid
-very effective
-rare in developed countries - some cases in IV drug users from dirty needles

-common in developing nations
-especially neonatal umbilical stump infections
