---
title: "Lac operon"
---
-inducible operon when lactose present in a cell, it gets switched on, genes utilized, and turned into protein
-gene codes for proteins which process lactose for energy
-operon off = no lactose and repressor protein bound to operator and stuck in off position
-operon on = lactose present and it binds to the repressor to move it off the DNA so RNA polymerase can begin moving along and mRNA can be produced
