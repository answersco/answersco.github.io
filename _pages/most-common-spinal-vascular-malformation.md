---
title: "Most common spinal vascular malformation:"
---
Dural AVF&#8594; most common spinal vascular malformation
Typically affects older males with back pain and progressive myelopathy
Cognard type V dural AVF
MRI&#8594; shows flow-voids surrounding the cord. 
Cord is often swollen, with abnormal intramedullary T2-prolongation.
CT myelography&#8594; shows serpiginous filling defects in the subarachnoid space.
