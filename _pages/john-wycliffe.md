---
title: "John Wycliffe"
---
English preacher who declared that Jesus, not the pope, was the head of the Church. He was offended by the excesses of the clergy, and taught that the Bible (not the pope) was the final authority on Christianity; inspired a new English translation of the Bible.

(ca. 1330-1384) theologian from Oxford who questioned the fundamental teachings of the church
