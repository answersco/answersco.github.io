---
title: "Viral disease"
---
1) viruses effect every type of living organism but are not living cells themselves
-bacteria, protozoa, fungi, plants, animals

2) range of Disease
-benign to extremely malignant based on the specific viral infections
-skin warts all the way up to viruses that attack blood vessels and cause bleeding out

3) no definitive treatment for any viral disease
-hard to find selectively toxic drug

4) prevention is the main focus
-education
-quarantine sick people from healthy
-vaccination
