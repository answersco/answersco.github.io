---
title: "Indications for renal cortical imaging"
---
Acute pyelonephritis: can appear as...
-Focal ill-defined area of decreased uptake
-Multifocal areas of decreased uptake
-Diffusely decreased uptake in otherwise normal kidney
---Scarring and masses can also appear as focal areas of decreased uptake, although scarring also has volume loss usually

Column of Bertin vs. Mass
-Mass will be cold
-Column of Bertin (normal tissue) will take up tracer
