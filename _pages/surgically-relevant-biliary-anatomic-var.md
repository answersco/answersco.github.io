---
title: "Surgically-relevant biliary anatomic variants:"
---
Low insertion of cystic duct&#8594; common duct may be misidentified as cystic duct during cholecystectomy, possibly leading to inadvertent common duct ligation

Aberrant right posterior duct: only important if patient is a right hepatic lobe liver donor&#8594; the 2 right hepatic ducts need to be anastomosed separately in the recipient.
