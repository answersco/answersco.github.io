---
title: "Trace the pathway of a carbon dioxide gas molecule in the blood from the inferior vena cava until it leaves the blood stream as you exhale."
---
1) Inferior vena cave
2) Right Atrium
3) Tricuspid valve
4) Right ventricle
5) Pulmonary semilunar valve
6) Pulmonary Trunk
7) Pulmonary artery
8) Lobar artery
9) Alveoli (air sacs) of the lung
10) Pulmonary capillary beds
