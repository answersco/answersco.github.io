---
title: "Teratogens"
---
-any environmental agent that causes damage during the prenatal period
-includes smoking, medications, and addictive drugs

-cause birth defects during development
EX. thalidomide, cocaine, cigarettes, alcohol, some nutrients (vitamin A) and some viruses

-any agent that can cause birth defects
-mother can take in anything in her bloodstream that can reach her baby
-cannot harm baby in germinal period b/c infant is not attached to placenta
