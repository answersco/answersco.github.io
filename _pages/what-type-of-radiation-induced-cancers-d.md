title: "What type of radiation induced cancers do the following get: treated with radiation for ankylosing spondylitis Fluoroscopy repeatedly during management of tuberculosis Uranium workers Dial painters who work with radium Marshall Island inhabitants"
---
Radiation for ankylosing spondylitis - get leukemia
Fluoroscopy repeatedly during management of tuberculosis - get breast cancer
Uranium workers - get lung cancer
Dial painters who work with radium - osseous sarcomas and nasopharynx (licking brush)
Marshall Island inhabitants - thyroid tumors
