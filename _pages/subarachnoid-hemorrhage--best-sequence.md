---
title: "Subarachnoid hemorrhage (best sequence)"
---
-thunderclap headache
-Trauma
-Non-traumatic: aneurysm rupture, benign non-aneurysm perimesencephalic hemorrhage
-most sensitive sequence is FLAIR
-susceptibility artifact on GRE
-Fisher grade 2 &lt;1 mm; grade 3 &gt;1 mm; grade 4 - diffuse intraventricular or parenchymal extension
