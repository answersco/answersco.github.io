---
title: "Management of worsening encephalopathy after TIPS"
---
Worsening encephalopathy after TIPS occurs in 30-50%
More common in patients treated for ascites, less common with covered stents.

Treatment options include:
Diminishing shunt diameter by placing a &quot;shunt-reducing&quot; stent within the tract
Shunt occlusion
Liver transplantation
