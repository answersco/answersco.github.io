---
title: "Genus mycobacterium"
---
-gram (+) aerobic rods, often filamentous (bread crumb look)
-many free-living saprobes, some commensals (isolate around oral cavity and digestive tract)
-slow growers (long doubling times) 
-cell wall with high lipid content (wax-like material) (mycolic acids: protects this bacteria from phagocytic cells, are a virulence factor for this bacteria which is about the only one because they do not produce toxins or enzymes)
-includes tuberculosis
