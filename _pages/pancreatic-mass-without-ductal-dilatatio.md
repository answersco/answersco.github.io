title: "Pancreatic mass without ductal dilatation:"
---
Pancreatic mass without ductal dilatation:
Autoimmune pancreatitis
Groove pancreatitis
Cystic pancreatic tumor
Neuroendocrine tumor
Duodenal GIST
Peripancreatic lymph node
Pancreatic metastasis (e.g. renal cell, thyroid, melanoma)
Lymphoma
