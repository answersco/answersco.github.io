---
title: "Factors affecting wound healing"
---
1) individual factors
-age
-obesity
-smoking
-medication
-stress
2) systemic factors
-nutrition &amp; fluids (protein/albumin, vitamins A &amp; C, zinc)
-circulation and oxygenation (diabetes, smoking, and shock)
-immune cellular function
3) local factors
-nature of the injury (clean or contaminated)
-presence of infection
-local wound environment
