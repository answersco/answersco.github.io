---
title: "Thoracic surgery pre-op"
---
1) Assess functional reserve 
-how well they will be able to breathe afterwards
-will it be enough to sustain life and have some kind of quality of life

2) Determine likelihood of recovery and survival and if it is the right route 
-some pt's do not want to be on a ventilator 

3) Ensure optimal condition for surgery

4) Determine baseline

5) Pre-op testing: chest x-rays, CT scans, lab values (CBC, blood gases, electrolytes - BMP
