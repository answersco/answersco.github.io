title: "Status/People"
---
-most common measure of health in a community

Includes:
1) morbidity/mortality identifies the physical, emotional, and social determinants of health
2) vital statistics (births and deaths)
3) leading causes of death and illness
4) social determinants which are determined by suicide rates, crimes rate, high school drop out rates, graduation rates, and median income
5) demographic factors such as single, female-headed family households
