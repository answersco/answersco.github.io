---
title: "Jugular vein assessment"
---
1) visually inspected for distention 
-pt must be supine with head elevated 30-45 degrees
2) distention is influenced by:
-blood volume
-capacity of the right atrium to receive and expel blood to the right ventricle
-ability of the right ventricle to move blood into the pulmonary artery
3) normal finding is no jugular vein distention (JVD)
