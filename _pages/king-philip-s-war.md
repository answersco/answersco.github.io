---
title: "King Philip's War"
---
A series of battles in New Hampshire between the colonists and the Wompanowogs, led by King Philip. The colonists won with the Mohawks and it opened up Indian lands for expansion.

the most prolonged and deadly conflict between whites and Indians (English and Wampanoags) in the 17th century over imposing English laws on Amerindians and expanding into Indian territory
