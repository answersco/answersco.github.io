title: "Social learning theory"
---
imitation learning

sociological theory developed by Akers that emphasizes social reinforcement for drug use from influential others (imitation, group support) and individual reinforcement from physiological effects of drugs

theory that emphasizes that the modeling effects of drug-related attitudes and behaviors are prevalent in the individual's environment

theory that stresses the importance of learned behavior (Aker: Learning process is based on operant conditioning principles)
