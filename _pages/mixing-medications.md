---
title: "Mixing medications"
---
-determine compatibility of the medications
-do not contaminate one medication with another
-ensure the final dose is accurate
-maintain aseptic technique
-cannot over fill syringe bc you cannot push it back in to any vial bc it is already mixed
EX. Reg goes in syringe first
Push air into N
Push air into R
Draw up R
Draw up N
-it is ok if a little R gets in the NPH but not ok if the NPH gets in the R 
&quot;You can put white milk into your chocolate milk, but you can't put chocolate milk into your white milk&quot;
