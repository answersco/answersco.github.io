---
title: "Which is worse, gastroschisis or omphalocele?"
---
Omphalocele is WORSE in terms of outcomes
- associated anomalies include TRISOMY 18, cardiac disease, Turner's, Klinefelter's, Beckwith 

Gastroschisis = always on RIGHT side (rarely assocated with anomalies)
- Maternal AFP elevated &gt; omphalocele
