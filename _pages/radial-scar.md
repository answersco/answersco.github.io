title: "Radial scar:"
---
Radial scar (complex sclerosing lesion if &gt; 1 cm) &#8594; benign lesion of uncertain etiology
Clinical:
May be associated with tubular carcinoma, atypical ductal hyperplasia, and lobular carcinoma in situ
Treatment is surgical excision
Imaging&#8594; often identical to cancer
MM&#8594; spiculated mass or architectural distortion 
US&#8594; hypoechoic, shadowing mass
