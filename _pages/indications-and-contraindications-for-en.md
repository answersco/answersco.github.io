---
title: "Indications and contraindications for endografts in AAA repair"
---
Indications: 
AAA &gt;/= 5 cm
Rapidly expanding (&gt; 5 mm/year) smaller AAA
Contained rupture of AAA
Inflammatory AAA
High-risk for complications with open repair

Contraindications:
No suitable proximal/distal attachment site
Severe, uncorrectable iliac occlusive disease
Mycotic aneurysm (relative)
Marfan or Ehlers-Danlos
Indispensable IMA
Life-threatening contrast allergy
Poor compliance with follow-up

10-15% require intervention such as embo of IIA or accessory renal artery or iliac angioplasty in order to become anatomically suitable.
