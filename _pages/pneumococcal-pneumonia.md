---
title: "Pneumococcal pneumonia"
---
~70% of all bacterial pneumonia
-most fatalities in elderly or compromised patients
-check to diagnose patient by telling them to cough up sputum to look under microscope and what is shown are diplococcus bacteria (two dots together) or by x-ray to see how extensive the pneumonia is and it should be white from all the fluid filled up in your lungs instead of black space that is normal
-treatment is antibiotics and breathing treatment
