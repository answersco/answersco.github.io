title: "Polyhydramnios"
---
- fetal GI anomalies
- fetal CNS anomalies
- maternal diabetes

= idiopathic polyhydramnios
= fetal hydrops

GI obstruction, Neuro, DM, Twin twin transfusion

Most cases idiopathic with normal fetus.

Associated with chromosomal abnormalities, diabetes, structural defects (G.I.)

For G.I. obstruction/Atresia - laryngo-esophageal duodenal.
Secondary obstruction - diaphragmatic hernia, gastroschisos
CNS anomalies - swallowing disorders.
Twin twin transfusion syndrome.
Placental abnormalities - Choreo angioma (benign placental hemangioma)a
