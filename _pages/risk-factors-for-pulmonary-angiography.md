---
title: "Risk factors for pulmonary angiography"
---
Complete LBBB- requires temporary pacing to prevent transient RBBB and subsequent complete block.
Severe uncompensated right heart failure
Severe pulmonary HTN (with end-diastolic RV pressure of 20 mmHg or more)
Unstable ventricular arrhythmias
Acute MI
Pulmonary edema
Anaphylaxis to IV contrast
