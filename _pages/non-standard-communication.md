---
title: "Non-standard communication"
---
Level 1: life threatening or requiring immediate change in management
- 60 minutes if critical, requires doctor-to-doctor
- timely basis if not critical
- also applies to critical test, even when negative (eg. chest CT for PE)
Level 2: mortality or significant morbidity if not treated within 3 days
- 12 hours
- can have assistant call
Level 3: significant morbidity if not appropriately treated, not immediately life-threatening
- electronic communication, no time frame

Should include date and time of communication, person reporting findings, person receiving findings, time finding was observed for Level 1
