---
title: "Health"
---
state of complete physical, mental &amp; social well-being, not merely the absence of disease or infirmity

-state of complete physical, mental, and social well-being (World Health Organization, 1974) 
-not only absence of disease
-an active process in which a person moves towards one's maximum potential
-measured globally by morbidity and mortality

-not just an absence of disease
-a continuum with its own unique, individual definition
