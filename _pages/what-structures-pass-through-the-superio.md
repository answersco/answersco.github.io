---
title: "What structures pass through the superior and inferior orbital fissures:"
---
Structures that pass through the superior orbital fissure:
CN III&#8594; superior, medial, and inferior rectus; inferior oblique
CN IV&#8594; superior oblique (SO4)
CN V1 (ophthalmic division) &#8594; sensory to upper face
CN VI&#8594; lateral rectus
Superior ophthalmic vein&#8594; potential route of infection to brain

Structures that pass through inferior orbital fissure:
CN V2 (maxillary division) &#8594; sensory to inferior eyelid, upper lip, nose
Infraorbital artery
