---
title: "Hydrocolloid dressings"
---
-brand name is duoderm
-soft wafer that can be cut to desired shape and size
-absorbs small amounts of drainage
-autolytic debriding of necrotic wounds
-used on Stage I &amp; II pressure sores
-protects high friction areas, preventatively (heels)
-maintains moist environment for healing clean, shallow wounds
-can remain in place up to one week if kept clean and dry
-old-fashioned - sister is silicon dressing which is easier on the skin, able to open the dressing and look at the wound and it will stick back down
