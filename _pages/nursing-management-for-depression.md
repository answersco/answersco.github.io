---
title: "Nursing management for depression"
---
-Identify at-risk patients: the more stresses, the more loses, and more time passes without treatment, the more likely depression becomes an issue

-Assess and LISTEN: somatic complaints - my stomach hurts, I have a HA - very vague complaints that keep changing

-Request consult

-Review medications - look for side effects for fall risks bc of drowiness

-Assess for potential side-effects of anti-depressant medications - need to understand the timeframe

-Patient/caregiver education:
--Signs/symptoms
--Potential causes
--Lifestyle changes
--Understanding medical treatments
--Understanding time frame
--Understanding medications and side effects
--Community and online resources
