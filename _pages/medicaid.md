---
title: "Medicaid"
---
-an assistance program. 

-it serves low-income people of every age. 

-patients usually pay no part of costs for covered medical expenses. A small co-payment is sometimes required.

-medicaid is a federal-state program. It varies from state to state. 

-it is run by state and local governments within federal guidelines.

-federally funded public assistance program for people of any age who have low incomes; for the blind, and disabled
