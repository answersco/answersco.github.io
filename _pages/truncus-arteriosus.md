---
title: "Truncus Arteriosus?"
---
A single great artery arises from base of heart to supply systemic, pulmonary, and coronary circulations. It overides a VSD usually.

Single vessel (truncus) supplies aorta, PA, coronary cirulation; sits over a high VSD

CXR: RV and LV enlarged; increased pulmonary vascularity, enlarged aorta
