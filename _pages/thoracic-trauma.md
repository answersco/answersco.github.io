---
title: "Thoracic trauma"
---
Collar sign: hour glass appearing solid viscera

Dependent viscera: No lung between organ and chest wall

Tracheo-Bronchial injury: Near the carina get pneumomediastinum NOT PTX

Mackin effect: pneumomediastinum from alveolar rupture in the setting of blunt trauma

Beorhaave syndrome : complete rupture of the esophagus=Pneumomedastinum and medianstinitis

Flail chest: 3 ribs with segmental fractures
or one fracture in 5 or more adjacent ribs 
paradoxical motion with breathing

PTX: inversion or flattening of the ipsilateral diaphragm
