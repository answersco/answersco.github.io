title: "Theories of elder mistreatment"
---
1) Psychopathology of abuser: someone with mental illness or addiction problem - disease of psychology causing them to do

2) Transgenerational violence: when family deals with stress by resulting to violence

3) Learning Theory: learned behavior

4) Situational Theory: you are put into a situation where you are pushed to the limit and as a result strike out

5) Isolation Theory: more isolated the caregiver and person being abused, more likely to suffer from neglect or abuse
