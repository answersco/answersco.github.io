title: "Pca pumps"
---
-a frequently prescribed method for post-op pain relief
-appropriate candidates include: 
 -big surgery = big pain
 -cognitively intact
 -neuromuscular function of UE
 -large volume of concentrated pain medication. Many safety checks are required. Only the patient can push the dose button. Container is locked. Changes require verification by 2 RNs.
