---
title: "Skin changes in LE venous insufficency"
---
Edema due to chronic venous stasis indicates severe venous HTN.
Characteristic skin changes of venous insufficiency: thickening, scaling, and/or brown discoloration, almost always with associated edema and/or itching/burning. Color changes largely irreversible.

LE venous ulcers: most severe manifestation of venous valvular insufficency. 
-Shallow, irregular, associated with characteristic skin changes.
-Located in medial aspect of supramalleolar region. Incompetence of deep or perforating veins, with or without saphenous reflux, is present in &gt; 80%.
