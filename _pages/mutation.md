title: "Mutation"

-a change in DNA sequence present in &lt;1% of the population
-refers to the change in the genotype
-may affect any DNA sequence
-some sequences are more likely to mutate than others are
-may or may not be problematic

the process that causes changes in DNA sequence that distinguish alleles

any change in the base sequence of DNA like A, T, C and G
