---
title: "What is Communicating hydrocephalus?"
---
Obstruction at level of the arachnoid villi/granulations, which blocks normal reabsorption (e.g. ALL THE VENTS are dilated)
- NPH (upward bowing of corpus callosum)
- Blood (SAH), Pus (meningitis), and Cancer (carcinomatous meningitis)

&quot;Extraventricular&quot; obstructive hydrocephalus = obstruction is DISTAL to 4th ventricle outlet foramina at level of basal cisterns or arachnoid granulations (e.g. DIFFUSE SAH)
