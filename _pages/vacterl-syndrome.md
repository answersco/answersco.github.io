---
title: "VACTERL syndrome"
---
Group of disorders that are frequently associated together
VACTERL association is diagnosed when 3 or more of these anomalies affect a single patient 
HEART and KIDNEYS = most commonly affected 

V = Vertebral anomalies
A = Anal (imperforate anus) - This is associated with TETHERED CORD so you should get screening ultrasound
C = Cardiac 
T = TE fistula
R = Renal
L = Limb (radial ray)
