---
title: "What is atypical terratoid rhabdoid tumor? Age of dx? Location? associated findings?"
---
AKA AT/RT is a rare highly malignant peds tumor, most often posterior fossa (50%, usually off midline), with poor prognosis (mean 11 mo). Frequently seeds whole nervous system. Pt may also have synchronous renal or extrarenal malignant rhabdoid tumors
