title: "Renal lymphoma"
---
-typically hematogenous spread or direct extension (kidneys dont have primary lymphatic tissue).
-multiple hypoechoic, hypodense masses in both kidneys

Renal lymphoma: primary renal lymphoma is very rare
Several patterns of lymphoma in the kidneys
Multiple lymphomatous masses (most common pattern, in 50%)
Solitary renal mass
Diffuse infiltration&#8594; nephromegaly
Direct extension of retroperitoneal disease

Most common way to show is BILATERAL renal enlargement with small, low attenuation cortically based solid nodules or masses (see image)
