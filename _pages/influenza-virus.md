---
title: "Influenza virus"
---
-enveloped RNA virus (- sense) --
 RNA in 8 segments (codes for 11 proteins)
-two important envelope proteins that look like spikes: neuraminidase and hemagglutinin 
-three major types of influenza virus: A, B, C
-each major type has many variations
-type A causes most serious disease
-based on variations of neuraminidase and hemagglutinin
