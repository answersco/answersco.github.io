title: "Henry VIII"
---
English king who created the Church of England with the Parliament separating England from the Roman Catholic Church legally but retaining most of the Catholic beliefs; he produced an English Bible for all his churches and procured an annulment for his marriage to Catherine

(1491-1547) King of England from 1509 to 1547; his desire to annul his marriage led to a conflict with the pope, England's break with the Roman Catholic Church, and its embrace of Protestantism. Henry established the Church of England in 1532.

English Tudor monarch who broke with the Roman Catholic Church creating himself as the &quot;Supreme Head of the Church of England&quot;; he issued a Great Bible in English; his Archbishop granted him a divorce (refused by the Pope) that allowed him to marry Anne Boleyn
