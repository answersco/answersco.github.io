title: "Tuberous sclerosis findings and inheritance?"
---
Autosomal dominant hamartomatous disease

Lung: LAM, chylothorax
Cardiac: Rhabdomyoma &amp; Rhabdomyosarcoma (in LV)
Brain: Giant cell astrocytoma, cortical/subcortical tubers, SEGA
Renal: B/l AMLs, Oncocytomas
** No increased risk of RCC compared w/ general population but if it occurs, it's at a YOUNGER AGE in TS patients!
