---
title: "What is fibromatosis?"
---
Fibromatosis is a locally aggressive proliferation of fibroblasts and myofibroblasts (a.k.a. extraabdominal demoed).

- It usually arises from the pectorals fascia

- Wide local excision is standard. Local recurrence rates are 21-27% (usually within 3 years). There is NO metastatic potential.
