title: "Meckel Scan"
---
Tc-99mO4. Activity concentrates within right lower quadrant or mid abdomen in synchrony with stomach (ectopic gastric mucosa).

remnant of omphalomesenteric duct
Ectopic gastric mucosA (in 50%) which secrete Tc99mO4-
Pretreatment:
-treat with H2 blocker (cimetidine) to block secretion into bowel lumen and improve target/background ratio of Meckel's (give 2 days before)
-Pentagastrin - enhances uptake of Tc99mO-4
-Glucagon - decrease small bowel motility
