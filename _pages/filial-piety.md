---
title: "Filial Piety"
---
Concept is stressed in Confucianism. Reflected the high significance of the family in Chinese history.

In Confucian thought, one of the virtues to be cultivated, a love and respect for one's parents and ancestors.

According to Confucius, this was the <b>most important duty</b> of a person

The honoring of one's ancestors and parents, a key element of Confucianism.
