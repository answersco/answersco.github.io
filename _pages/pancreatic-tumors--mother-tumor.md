---
title: "Pancreatic tumors: mother tumor"
---
Mucinous cystic neoplasm: middle-aged women&#8594; mother tumor
Benign, but with malignant potential&#8594; typically resected
Consists of a single or a few large cysts (&lt; 6 cysts that are &gt; 2 cm), typically occurring in the pancreatic body or tail
MCN has a capsule (SPEN is only other pancreatic tumor with capsule)
