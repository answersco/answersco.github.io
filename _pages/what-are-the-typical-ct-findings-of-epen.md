---
title: "What are the typical CT findings of ependymoma?"
---
Soft, pliable tumors that fill the ventricular lumen insinuate through subarachnoid spaces, and classically extrude through the foramen of Luschka, Magendie, and foramen magnum

Isodense to brain
Calcifications common (40-80%)
+/- cysts or hemorrhage
Variable enhancement
