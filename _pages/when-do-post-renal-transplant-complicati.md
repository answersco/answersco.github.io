---
title: "When do post-renal transplant complications occur?"
---
Hematoma = Immediate post op
Urinoma = within 2 weeks post op
Lymphocele = 1-2 MONTHS after
- collection usually medial to the transplant causing ipsilateral lower extremity edema from femoral vein compression

Renal vein thrombosis = within first week
Renal Artery thrombosis = within first month
Renal artery STENOSIS = within 1st year
