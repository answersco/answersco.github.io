---
title: "Lillian Wand"
---
-first to use the term &quot;public health nursing&quot;
-thought that nurses in schools could follow up with the children to make sure they were properly treated in order to return to school. After one year, the number of children sent home from New York City schools and excluded from schools dropped from 10,000 to 1,000-established the hallmarks of public health nursing practice because of her emphasis on illness prevention and health promotion through health teaching, nursing interventions, and use of epidemiologic methodology
