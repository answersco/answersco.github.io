title: "Hypertrophic cardiomyopathy imaging findings?"
---
Hypetrophic cardiomyoapthy = asymmetric hypertrophy of the IV septum &gt; 15 mm (can be assocaited with LVOT obstruction --&gt; HOCM due to systolic anterior motion of MV)
- Patchy MIDWALL delayed enhancement of the hypertrophied muscle
- LV myocardium &gt; 11 mm at end diastole; RV &gt; 6 mm at end diastole
