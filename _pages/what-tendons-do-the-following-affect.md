---
title: "What tendons do the following affect: - subcoracoid impingement - posterior superior internal impingement"
---
Subcoracoid impingement:
- lesser tuberosity and coracoid do the pinching
- damage the subscapularis

Post Sup Internal Impingement:
- athletes overhead movements
- greater tuberosity and posterior superior labrum do the pinching
- damage infraspinatus
