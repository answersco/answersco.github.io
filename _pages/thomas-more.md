---
title: "Thomas More"
---
He was a English humanist that contributed to the world today by revealing the complexities of man. He wrote Utopia, a book that represented a revolutionary view of society, and he lost his head refusing to place the king above the pope

This English lawyer, humanist scholar, and friend to Erasmus wrote &quot;Utopia&quot; and proved his faithfulness to the Roman Catholic Church by defying Henry VIII's claim to supremacy -- an act which cost him his head
