---
title: "Vasculitis"
---
Etiologies include lupus, PAN, giant cell arteritis, and Sjogren.
MRI&#8594; numerous small foci of T2 prolongation in subcortical and deep white matter. 
Although the appearance may be similar to MS, foci of hemorrhage (best seen on GRE or SWI) may be present in vasculitis.
Vascular imaging&#8594; beaded, irregular appearance to the cerebral vessels.

infxn (TB, syphilis), inflam (angiits, PAN, sarcoid, Wegeners)

-multifocal areas of vessel narrowing, beaded appearance
-small focal areas of increased T2 signal in white matter (similar to MS)
