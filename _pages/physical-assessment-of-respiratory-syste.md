---
title: "Physical assessment of respiratory system"
---
(look, listen, feel)

-Symmetrical lung rise and fall

-Nail beds for cap refill and clubbing

-Depth of respirations

-Use of accessory muscles 

-Skin assessment 

-If coughing, assess sputum

-Listen for crackles, wheezes, rhonchi, rubs

-Feeling for crepitus and expansion
