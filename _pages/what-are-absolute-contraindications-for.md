---
title: "What are absolute contraindications for TIPSS?"
---
Right-sided heart failure with elevated CVP
Liver failure
Polycystic liver disease
Pulmonary hypertension
Hepatic encephalopathy

Right to left cardiopulmonary shunt (you're inevitably sending small PEs, and could become systemic)

Fistula infection

Uncorrectable coagulopathy
