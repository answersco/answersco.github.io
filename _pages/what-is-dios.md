---
title: "What is DIOS?"
---
Distal intestinal obstruction syndrome (DIOS), which is a functional obstruction of the distal small bowel by viscous, inspissated enteric contents , a phenomenon almost unique to patients with CYSTIC FIBROSIS, and which usually responds to aggressive administration of laxatives and cleansing enemas.

- adult equivalent of meconium ileum in the neonate
