---
title: "Why testicular self exam"
---
-testicular cancer is the most common cause of cancer in men 15-35 years old.
-7,500 new cases diagnosed each year.
-accounts for 3 % of all cancer deaths in this group.
-if discovered early, can be treated promptly &amp; effectively.
-surgery is preferred tx. - may be used together with radiation &amp; chemotherapy.
