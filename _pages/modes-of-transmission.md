---
title: "Modes of transmission"
---
1) contact (Direct, Indirect, &amp; Droplet): use hand hygiene, PPE;
-EX. C. diff (Curell)
2) vehicles: handshakes, garments, shoes
3) droplet: put masks before entering and leaving room
4) airborne
5) vectorborne: something else carries it such as ticks or mosquitos
