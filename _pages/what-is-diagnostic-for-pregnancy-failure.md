---
title: "What is DIAGNOSTIC for pregnancy failure?"
---
- CRL of 7 mm or more without heart beat
- Mean sac diameter of 25 mm or more without embryo

OLD guideline:
- No yolk sac when sac diameter &gt; 8 mm
- No embryo when sac diameter &gt; 16 mm
- No cardiac activity when embryo is &gt; 5 mm on TVUS
