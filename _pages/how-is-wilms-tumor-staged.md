---
title: "How is Wilms tumor staged?"
---
Answer: surgery
Stage 1: confined to kidney and completely resected.
Stage 2: extension through renal capsule, but completely resected
Stage 3: incomplete resection or residual disease in nodes
Stage 4: lung or liver metastases
Stage 5: bilateral disease 
60% are completely resected and associated with 95% 5-year survival
