---
title: "What are the three dx criteria of NF2?"
---
Bilateral cranial nerve 8/vestibular schwannomas
OR 1st degree relative with NF2 and a unilateral vestibular schwannoma 
OR 1st degree relative with NF2 and 2 of the following:
Neurofibroma, Meningioma, Glioma, Other Schwannoma, Juvenile lens opacity
