---
title: "Genus staphylococcus"
---
-gram positive
-many are facultative anaerobes
-some are resistant to common disinfectants and persist on fomites (inanimate object that can spread disease)
-harder to kill
-a major cause of Nosocomial infections - in hospitals 
-staph: pneumonia (15-20%) and surgical site infections (35%) because it is on skin and stays there making it easy 
-includes staphylococcus aureus, MRSA, and coagulase negative staphylococcus
