title: "Nucs appearance of hepatic adenoma, FNH, hemangioma, HCC, cholangiocarcinoma, mets, abscess, focal fat"
---
Adenoma: Sulfur colloid (SC) cold
FNH: 40% hot, 30% cold, 30% neutral
Hemangioma: SC cold, RBC scan hot
HCC: SC cold, gallium hot
Cholangio: SC cold
Mets: SC cold
Abscess: SC cold, gallium hot
Focal fat: SC cold, Xe hot.
