---
title: "MEN Syndromes"
---
MEN III = MEN 2b
Marfanoid body habitus
Coarse facial features with prognathism
GI tract abnormalities

MEN 1 (3 Ps): parathyroid hyperplasia (90%), pituitary adenoma, pancreatic tumor (usually gastrinoma)

MEN IIa (1 M, 2 Ps): medullary thyroid cancer (100%), pheo, parathyroid hyperplasia

MEN IIb (2 Ms, 1 P): Medullary thyroid cancer (80%), pheo, Marfanoid habitus/mucosal neuroma.
