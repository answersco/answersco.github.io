---
title: "Findings with posterior tibial tendon injury/dysfunction"
---
Posterior tibial tendon injury/dysfunction&#8594; progressive flat-foot deformity
PTT: primary stabilizer of the longitudinal arch
Chronic&#8594; tear usually behind the medial malleolus
Acute&#8594; tear most commonly at insertion onto the navicular
Acute flat arch&#8594; think PTT tear
Will also have hindfoot valgus deformity (unopposed peroneus brevis action)
Spring ligament (secondary supporter of arch by holding up talar head) &#8594; will thicken and degenerate without help of PTT.
