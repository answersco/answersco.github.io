---
title: "What is Parry Romberg Syndrome?"
---
Rare phakomatosis of facial &quot;hemiatrophy&quot; = unilateral facial atrophy of orbit, masticator space, superficial compartments (skin/muscle/skeletal structures)

- May see hemifacial pigmentation, trigeminal neuralgia, migraines, and seizures

&quot;Mitt Romberg only has half a face&quot;

Phakamtosis: progressive facial hemiatrophy involving the skin/muscles/bones.
