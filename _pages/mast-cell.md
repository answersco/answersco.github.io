title: "Mast cell"
---
-key component of inflammation
-release inflammatory mediators
-important actions of inflammatory mediators
-vasodilation and increase vascular permeability - histamine : allergies
-chemotaxis -- attraction of phagocytic cells to area - mast cell chemotactic factor
-pain -- prostaglandins : signals to surrounding nerves that something should be felt as pain to help not to move an injured ankle or wrist to maybe damage it more
