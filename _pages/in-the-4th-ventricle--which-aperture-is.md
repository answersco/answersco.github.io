---
title: "In the 4th ventricle, which aperture is anterior vs posterior?"
---
Foramen of Magendie is MIDLINE (just one) --&gt; POSTEROinferiorly from 4th ventricle to Cisterna Magna

Foramina of Luschka - curve ANTEROinferiorly from the 4th ventricle into cerebellopontine angle cisterns

&quot;LA MP&quot; = Luschka anterior; Magendie Posterior&quot;
