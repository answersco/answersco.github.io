---
title: "What is RDS and synonyms? Risk factors?"
---
Respiratory distress syndrome aka surfactant deficiency disorder (SDD), previously known as hyaline membrane disease (HMD).

SDD is the most common cause of respiratory distress in newborn. 

It mostly affects premature babies under 36 weeks of age, LBW, maternal diabetes, maternal/fetal hemorrhage and multiple gestations.
