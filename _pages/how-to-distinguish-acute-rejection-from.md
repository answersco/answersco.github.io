---
title: "How to distinguish Acute rejection from ATN in renal transplant?"
---
MAG3 nuclear scan!

ATN has normal perfusion 
Rejection has ABNORMAL PERFUSION 
- US rejection: Prominent pyramids, BIG kidney, urothelial thickening and elevated RI!

(Both have delayed excretion)
