---
title: "What is malakoplakia in the ureters?"
---
Inflammatory condition 2/2 chronic UTI (E. coli) in immunocompreossed female patients. NOT PREMALIGNANT

- Mucosal mass involving ureter or bladder; plaque-like or nodular intramural lesions

* NOT pre-malignant (unlike leukoplakia); improves with antibiotics
