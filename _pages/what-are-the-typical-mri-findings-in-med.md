---
title: "What are the typical MRI findings in medulloblastoma?"
---
Posterior fossa mass which is sointense to normal gray matter on T1, (Near gray matter signal intensity)
Reduced diffusion (2/2 tightly packed small round cells)
Uniform or patchy post-contrast enhancement
Hydrocephalus due to compression of 4th ventricle
Surrounding T2 vasogenic edema
Must image entire neuraxis to exclude subarachnoid metastases
