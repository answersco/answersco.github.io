---
title: "Nonspecific Interstitial Pneumonia"
---
LESS common than UIP
Histologically this looks like HOMOGENEOUS inflammation and/or fibrosis
*<b>This is the common pattern of injury in collagen vascular disease and drug reactions</b>*

Key Findings:
1. SPARING of the immediate subpleural lung
2. Ground glass is the NSIP equivalent of honey combing
3. Lower lobe, posterior, peripheral predominance
4. Micronodules

**This is the most common intersitial lung pattern in SCLERODERMA
