---
title: "What are enteric duplication cysts?"
---
Rare congenital abnl, arise anywhere from the tongue to the anus. Spherical &gt; tubular. Tubular may present as a complex thoracoabdominal mass.
Dup cysts have smooth muscle and are lined by GI or resp epithelium. Can be intramural-extraluminal. Foregut duplications include esophageal, gastric and duodenal (1st and 2nd parts only) cysts. Mid-and hindgut arise from the 3rd duodenum and distal.
