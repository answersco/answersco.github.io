---
title: "How do you differentiate pathologic extraxial collection from benign extraxial collection of infancy by US?"
---
In benign subarachnoid CSF you should see veins with color flow bridging the hypoechoic/anechoic collection. Subdural collections will compress the subarachnoid space, and separate them from the sagittal sinus, may also be mixed echogenicity.
