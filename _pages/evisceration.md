---
title: "Evisceration"
---
-happens only to abdominal wounds
-wound has dehisced and now abdominal organs are protruding through the incision
-is an emergency
-treated with sterile technique
-can cause necrosis from circulation stopping
-do not leave eviscerated pt alone or try to put organ back inside
-cover it with saline soaked sterile gauze to keep it moist
