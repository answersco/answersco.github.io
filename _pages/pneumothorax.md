---
title: "Pneumothorax"
---
-air or gas in the pleural cavity
-result of puncture through chest wall or pleura
-causes collapse of the lung requiring reinflation via chest tube 
EX. knife, bullet wound, or broke rib

Air in the pleural cavity. Black in color and contrasts with surrounding lung/tissue. In an upright film it is best seen in the apices. Pneumothorax is associated with lung collapse due to loss of surface tension.
