---
title: "Peripheral vascular assessment"
---
1) evaluates blood pressure
2) monitors blood flow and health of both arteries and veins throughout the body - checking all the pulses on body
-pulses in feet: dorsalispedis (found on top of foot in-between big toe and second toe) and posterior tibial (found on inside of leg behind ankle bone)
-if you cannot palpate the pulses, use doppler
3) palpate extremities for coldness and cyanotic
4) assesses the effectiveness of circulation throughout the body
5) reveals the effectiveness of the heart to pump blood to the periphery of the body
