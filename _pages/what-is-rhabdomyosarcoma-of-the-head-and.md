---
title: "What is rhabdomyosarcoma of the head and neck in peds?"
---
The most common soft tissue sarcoma, in the H&amp;N it is divided into the orbits, the parameningeal (middle ear, paranasal sinus, nasal cavity and nasopharynx- all associated w/ intracranial spread) sites, and all other. Three types: embryonal (most common), alveolar (worst prognosis) and pleomorphic (most rare).
