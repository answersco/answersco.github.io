---
title: "Temperature assessment"
---
-dependent on the amount of blood circulating through the dermis
-best assessed through palpation
-compare symmetrical body parts
-be alert to area of warmth and erythema (may be stage 1 pressure ulcer)
-increase/decrease in skin temp. indicates increase/decrease in blood flow
-skin should normally be warm
-check temp. on pt's that concern with temp/ such as diabetics
