---
title: "Watson model"
---
-a philosophical approach to explaining the care of patients; it is an attempt to show the connection between the art of caring and the science of delivering care

Client: individual - has needs, grows, and develops to reach a state of inner harmony

Health: dynamic state of growth and development leading to full potential as a human being

Environment: the client must overcome certain factors to achieve health

Nursing: the science of caring that helps clients reach their greatest potential
