---
title: "Four Key Characteristics of Nervous System"
---
1) Complexity: over 100 billion nerve cells with endless connections
2) Integration: happens separately but brain mends them together as a seamless conscience reality when they are in diff. areas
3) Electrochemical Transmission: communication from nerve cell to nerve cell 
4) Adaptability: Plasticity- the brain has a special capacity to change and adapt as we experience new things or encounter injuries
