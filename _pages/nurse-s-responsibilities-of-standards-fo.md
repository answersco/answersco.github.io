---
title: "Nurse's responsibilities of standards for blood administration"
---
-Verify order

-Blood product inspection

-Patient identification

-Expiration date

-Confirmation of compatibility: pt - donor
--If reaction to blood transfusion occurs, symptoms will be rash, fever, chills, chest pain, SOB, heaviness in chest, hives, feeling of loom and doom 

-Confirmation of informed pt consent

-Pt. education

-Monitoring during and after administration
