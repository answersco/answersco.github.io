---
title: "NPV and PPV of MRI for breast cancer and weakness compared to MM"
---
Negative predictive value of 98.9% for detection of breast cancer
Positive predictive value of 49.7%
One area of relative weakness compared to MM is comparative lack of sensitivity for microcalcifications &#8594; some larger ones are seen as susceptibility of artifact, but MM is superior for small calcifications.
