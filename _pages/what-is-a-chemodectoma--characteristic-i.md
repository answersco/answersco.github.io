---
title: "What is a chemodectoma? Characteristic imaging finding? Assoc? Ddx?"
---
A jugular paraganglioma or a carotid body tumor. Vascular mass splaying ECA and ICA. Assoc with VHL, MEN, NF1. Ddx: Lymph nodes, Schwannoma, Neurofibroma, paraganglioma (vagal or carotid body), Carotid Pseudoaneurysm.
