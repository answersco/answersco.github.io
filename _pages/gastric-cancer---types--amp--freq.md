---
title: "Gastric Cancer - Types &amp; Freq?"
---
Carcinoma (&gt;95%)
- Old person (70yo)
- RF: H pylori
- Ulcerated Carcinoma (penetrating cancer) looks like advanced cancer
- Mets spread to Ovary = Krukenberg Tumor
- Gastroenterostomy done for PUD (prior to PPI) has 2-6x risk of CA in gastric remnant

Lymphoma (&lt;5%)
- Primary (MALT) or 2/2 to systemic lymphoma
- Stomach is #1 extranodal site for NHL
- rarely causes obstruction
- clasically &quot;crossing pylorus&quot;; although Carcinoma more likely bc much more prevalent
