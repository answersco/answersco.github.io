---
title: "XR detection of middle and posterior mediastinal masses:"
---
Detection of middle mediastinal mass:
Distortion of the paratracheal stripes or convexity of the AP window suggests middle mediastinal mass

Detection of posterior mediastinal mass:
Distortion of azygoesophageal recess, posterior junction line, or paraspinal lines suggests paravertebral/posterior mediastinal abnormality.
