---
title: "What are the vascular arterial supplies to the colon? What supplies the rectum? What is the arc of Riolan?"
---
SMA: Middle colic and Right colic which is transverse and right colon.
IMA: Left and sigmoid colons.
Rectum is from internal iliac artery.
Arc of Riolan is anastamosis between middle and left colics (SMA to IMA).
