---
title: "Venous infarct causes"
---
Venous infarct: most commonly sagittal sinus
Baby&#8594; dehydration; older kid &#8594; mastoiditis; adult &#8594; coagulopathy and OCP.
Vasogenic + cytotoxic edema (just cytotoxic in arterial)
Heterogeneous restricted diffusion
Chronic venous thrombosis: can lead to dural AVF or increased ICP due to impaired drainage
