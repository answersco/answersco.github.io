---
title: "What to look for on fetal US for osteogenesis imperfecta?"
---
The ribs look &quot;beaded,&quot; and the long bones may be irregularly angulated due to fractures in OI. The underossified calvarium allows for very clear visualization of the brain in OI; this is described as seeing the brain &quot;too well.&quot; The contour of the head can be altered by direct transducer pressure in OI.
