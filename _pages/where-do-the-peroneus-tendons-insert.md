---
title: "Where do the peroneus tendons insert?"
---
Peroneus tendons: along lateral aspect of calcaneus, with the peroneus longus tenddon located lateral and posterior to the peroneus brevis tendon.
The peroneus longus tendon inserts on the base of the 1st metatarsal
The peroneus brevis tendon inserts on the base of the 5th MT.
