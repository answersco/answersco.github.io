---
title: "IMPORTANT: What are signs of BENIGN gastric ulcers?"
---
- Gastric folds extend all the way to the ulcer
- Ulcer projects beyond the lumen of the stomach
- Ulcer collar is smooth and sharply seen
- Tend to involve the LESSER curvature (&quot;Less bad&quot;)
- DEEPER than wide (they don't spread out like a cancer)
- Hampton's line
