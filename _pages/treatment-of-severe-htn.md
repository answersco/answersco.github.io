---
title: "Treatment of severe HTN:"
---
O2 6-10 L/min via mask
Monitor vitals
NG 0.4 mg tablet, SL (may repeat 3x), or topical 2% ointment.
If no response, consider labetalol 20 mg IV, then 20-80 mg IV q10 minutes up to 300 mg.
Transfer to ICU or ED.
For pheochromocytoma: phentolamine 5 mg IV (labetalol if not available).
