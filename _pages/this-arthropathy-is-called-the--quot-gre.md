---
title: "This arthropathy is called the &quot;great mimicker&quot; of other arthropathies"
---
CPPD (pseudogout) - because the clinical manifestions mimic a variety of other diseases

- pseudoosteoarthritis (common)
- pseudogout (10-20%)
- pseudorheumatoid (2-6%)
- pseudoneuropathic (&lt;2%)

20% are asymptomatic
