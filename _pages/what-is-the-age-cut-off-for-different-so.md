---
title: "What is the age cut off for different solid and cystic renal masses?"
---
4 yo! (remember, liver is 5 yo)

0-3 = Nephroblastomatosis, Mesoblastic nephroma (both solid) and multicystic dysplastic kidney

~ 4yo = Wilms (+ variants like Clear Cell and Rhabdoid), Multilocular cystic nephroma, lymphoma

Teen = RCC and lymphoma
