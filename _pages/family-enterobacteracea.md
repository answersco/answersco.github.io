---
title: "Family enterobacteracea"
---
-includes the enteric bacteria
-very large species group
-gram (-) facultative anaerobes b/c it is found in intestinal tracts which includes many levels of oxygen
-most are commensals of intestinal tracts

-opportunistic pathogens
-intestinal commensals that gain access to other sites
-common cause of urinary tract infections 
-common cause of nosocomial infections
-species: Escherichia coli (most common) and Klebsiella sp., Enterobacter sp., Proteus sp., others
