---
title: "What are the main testicular tumors?"
---
Germ cell are 95%, malignant, and include seminoma and the non-seminomatous: teratoma, embryonal, yolk sac, and chorioCA, and mixed types.

Stromal tumors (non-GCT) are 10%, usually benign (90%), and include leydig and sertoli cell tumors, or mixed types.
