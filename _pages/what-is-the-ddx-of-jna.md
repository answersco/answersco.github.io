---
title: "What is the ddx of JNA?"
---
Hypervascular Polyp (Does not involve SPF, PPF)
Rhabdomyosarcoma (Most common primary malignant head and neck tumor in children)
Epignathus- Nasopharyngeal teratoma
Nasopharyngeal Carcinoma (assoc with EBV) 
Encephalocele ( typically not hypervascular)
Fibrous Dysplasia (may mimic on MRI, but CT is dx with gg)
