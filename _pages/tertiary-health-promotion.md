---
title: "Tertiary health promotion"
---
-begins after an illness is diagnosed and treated.
-reduce disability and help to rehabilitate patients to a maximum level of functioning
-minimize complications

EX.
-teaching a patient with diabetes how to recognize and prevent complications
-a community nurse develops a plan of care focusing on diet, exercise, and glucose monitoring for an adolescent recently diagnosed with diabetes.
-using physical therapy to prevent contractures in a patient who has had a stroke or spinal cord injury
-referring a woman to a support group after removal of a breast due to breast cancer
