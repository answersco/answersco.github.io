---
title: "Korotkoff sounds"
---
I-clear, rhythmic thumping 
-systole - ventricle contracts &amp; forces blood into aorta, maximal pressure

II-blowing or whooshing sound
-audible as vessels distend with blood

III-softer thumping

IV-distinct muffling of sound

V-disappearance of sound
-diastole - heart is at rest
