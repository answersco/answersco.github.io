---
title: "Meckel's Scan  Agent: Dose: Image timing: Distribution: Critical Organ: Comments:"
---
Agent: 99mTc-Pertechnetate 
Dose: 
10 mCi (adult)
&gt;2 mCi (kids) 
Image timing: Get flow images, then scan for up to 30 min 
Distribution: Salivary glands/stomach/thyroid 
Critical Organ: Colon
Comments: 
Cimetidine before study fixes the tracer Pentagastrin increases uptake by gastric mucosa Glucagon decreases peristalsis (side effect of pentagastrin)
