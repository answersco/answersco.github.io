---
title: "OA in shoulder and foot:"
---
OA in shoulder:
Grashey view (obtained posteriorly in 40&#176; obliqued external rotation) shows the glenohumeral joint in profile and best demonstrates cartilage space narrowing.

OA in foot: 
Most commonly affected joint is the MTP joint of the great toe &#8594; may lead to hallux rigidus (stiff big toe) from dorsal osteophytes.
Also affects the talonavicular joint&#8594; cause of dorsal beaking.
