---
title: "What is the most common congenital cardiac abnormality associated with right arch with mirror branching?"
---
Tetralogy of Fallot
- 90% of patients with mirror-image right arch have TOF (6% Truncus)
- 33% of Truncus patients with mirror-image right arch
- 25% TOF patients with mirror-image right arch

Thus, most commonly seen with TOF, but most closely associated with Truncus.
