---
title: "Mammographic imaging of breast implants:"
---
Implant-displaced views (Eklund views): displace the implant posteriorly and pull the native breast tissue anteriorly to allow for improved compression and visualization of breast tissue

Saline implant: identified by presence of valve. The wall of the implant will be denser than the center.
Silicone implant: uniformly dense, with no valve present.
