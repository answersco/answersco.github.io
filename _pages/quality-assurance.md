title: "Quality Assurance"
---
Abbreviated QA
This is a STABLE process in which errors are handled in a reactive usually retrospective fashion
This is the set of activites for checking quality in the process of CREATING a product 
The goal is to PREVENT defects in the product. This is normally achieved through periodic audits of the operations of a system

-QA
-the maintenance of a desired level of quality in a service or product, especially by means of attention to every stage of the process of delivery or production.

system of policies, practices, and procedures implemented throughout the company to create and produce quality goods and services

Reactive, retrospective system for maintaining quality
Process oriented
Example: periodic audits of systems operations
