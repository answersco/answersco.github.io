---
title: "Otitis media"
---
-infection of the middle ear
-30-45% of cases due to Strep. Pneumoniae
-reddening and inflammation of the ear drum with fluid &amp; pus pushing on eardrum
-penicillin derivatives (amoxicillin, others) used for treatment
-17% resistance in S. pneumonia (beta-lactamase production)
-surgical treatment
