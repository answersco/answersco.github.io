---
title: "Pulmonary Langerhans cell histiocytosis"
---
Upper lobe predominant lung cysts with sparing of costophrenic sulci in a smoker

- Seen in YOUNG (20-30 yo) SMOKERS
- Findings: STARTS as centrilobular nodules in the UPPER Lobes, these then cavitate and turn into THIN WALLED CYSTS. The cysts then begin to coalesce and become odd shaped. SPARES the costophrenic angles
Location: UPPER AND MID LUNG
