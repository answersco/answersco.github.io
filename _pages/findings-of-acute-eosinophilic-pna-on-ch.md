---
title: "Findings of acute eosinophilic PNA on chest CT? How to distinguish from chronic eosinophilic PNA?"
---
CT = b/l Ground glass with interlobular septal thickening

Acute has positive Eos in bronchoavleolar lavage but NOT in the peripheral blood smear; tends NOT to recur after steroids; acute onset of symptoms or severe hypoxemia
