---
title: "Splenic trauma grades:"
---
Splenic trauma grading via CT:
Grade I: small (&lt; 1 cm) subcapsular hematoma, laceration, or parenchymal hematoma
Grade II: medium (&gt; 1 and &lt; 3 cm) subcapsular hematoma, laceration, or parenchymal hematoma
Grade III: splenic capsular disruption; or large (&gt; 3 cm) laceration or parenchymal hematomas
Grade IVA: active extravasation, vascular injury (pseudoaneurysm of AVF), or shattered spleen
Grade IVB: active intraperitoneal bleeding
