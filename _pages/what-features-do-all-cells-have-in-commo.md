---
title: "What features do all cells have in common?"
---
1. obtain nutrients; often obtain O2
2. convert nutrients to energy
3. eliminate waste
4. synthesize enzymes and structural parts
5. responsivity
6. membrane control: control transmembrane transport, intracellular composition
7. move materials within cell
8. growth, reproduction, repair
-many cells are highly specialized, yet all our cells have identical DNA
