---
title: "Paraduodenal Hernia"
---
- Through a congenital defect
- Left paraduodenal is most common
- left paraduodenal hernia: cluster of small bowel loops in the left anterior pararenal space. the cluster of small bowel loops is behind the inferior mesenteric vein (IMV) and behind the ascending left colic artery
- right paraduodenal hernia: cluster of small bowel loops is inferior to the third portion of the duodenum. the cluster of small bowel loops is behind the superior mesenteric vein (SMV), the superior mesenteric artery (SMA), and the right colic vein

Most commonly on the left, through the fossa of Landzert. Sac should contain IMV and left left colic artery
