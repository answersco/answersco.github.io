---
title: "Hyperventilation"
---
-ventilation in excess of that required to eliminate the normal CO2
-causes: anxiety, infection, drugs, hypoxia, fever, aspirin overdose, or diabetic ketoacidosis
-use brown paper bag
-expel too much CO2
-decrease balance of O2 and CO2

-under anxiety, excess can cause this
-CO2 expelled faster than produced
-pH rises, carotids contract
-dizzy, fainting
-rebreathe in a paper bag to fix
