---
title: "UAE post-procedure"
---
Patients experience severe pelvic cramping for 12-24 hours after procedure, followed by gradually decreasing cramping for 7 days.
Most managed overnight in hospital for pain. 
Should begin NSAIDS immediately after procedure (some begin several days before) and continue afterward.

Post-embo syndrome: common, manifested as fatigue, low-grade fevers, nausea, and anorexia.
-Gradually dissipates over same time period, but can persistn for many weeks.

Fibroid tissue can be expelled for up to a year after procedure.
