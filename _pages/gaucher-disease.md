---
title: "Gaucher disease:"
---
Gaucher disease: AR deficiency of glucocerebrocidase&#8594; accumulation of glucocerebrosides in the RES
Splenic manifestations: splenomegaly and multiple splenic nodules (&#8531;)
Bony findings: include Erlenmeyer flask deformity of distal femurs, femoral head AVN, and H-shaped vertebral bodies from endplate AVN.
