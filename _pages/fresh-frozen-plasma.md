---
title: "Fresh frozen plasma"
---
-Liquid portion of blood &amp; lymph 

-Helps reduce INR rate

-Also used for emergency surgery/procedure 

Used for:
1) Coagulation factor replacement (primary use)
2) Provides clotting factors
3) Reversal of warfarin

-Needs to be ABO compatible

-Administration:
1) Infuse over 1-2 hours, 4 hours max. - once over 4 hours it becomes contaminated
2) Standard blood administration set with blood filter
