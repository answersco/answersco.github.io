---
title: "Pancreatitis"
---
EtOH, stones, HLD, ERCP, viral, drugs (steroid, AZT, abx)

-Balthazar score: how much necrosis
-interstitial pancreatitis vs exudative pancreatitis vs necrotizing
-acute peripancreatic fluid collection vs pseudocyst (4 weeks)
-acute necrotic collection vs walled off necrosis

Know the vocabulary

NO necrosis
- less than 4 weeks: acute peripancreatic collection
- Greater than 4 weeks: Pseudocyst 

Necrosis
- Less than 4 weeks: Acute necrotic collection
- Great than 4 weeks: walled off necrosis
