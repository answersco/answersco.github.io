---
title: "Skin Dose Levels"
---
&lt; 2 Gy&#8594; no action needed
2-5 Gy&#8594; advise patient to watch for burns, especially 10 days post-procedure
&gt; 5 Gy&#8594; procedure and dose should be reviewed by physics
2 Gy: early transient erythema
3 Gy: temporary epilation (hair loss)
6 Gy: chronic erythema
7 Gy: permanent epilation
10 Gy: telangiectasia
13 Gy: dry desquamation
18 Gy: moist desquamation/ulceration
24 Gy: secondary ulceration
