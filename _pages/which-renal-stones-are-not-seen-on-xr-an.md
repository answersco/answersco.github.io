title: "Which renal stones are not seen on XR and CT?"
---
Uric acid&#8594; not seen on XR
Indinavir stones (HIV) &#8594; only stones not seen on CT
Calcium oxalate&#8594; by far most common type
Cysteine&#8594; rare, associated with congenital disorders of metabolism.
