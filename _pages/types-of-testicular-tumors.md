title: "Types of testicular tumors?"
---
GCT = seminomatous or non-seminomatous
- Non-seminomatous = Mixed GCT, teratoma, yolk sac tumor, choriocarcinoma
* Heterogeneous with larger calcifications

Sex Cord stromal = Sertoli Leydig (seen in Peutz Jeghers)
- look for gynecomastia

Lymphoma = esp. in immunosuppressed 
- multiple hypoechoic masses of the testicle
- usually NHL
