title: "What percent of posterior mediastinal masses are neurogenic? What are the associations with age for each of the neurogenic tumors?"
---
85% of posterior mediastinal masses are neurogenic.
Neuroblastoma, malignant, usually &lt; 4 yo.
Ganglioneuroblastoma is approximately 7 yo
Ganglioneuroma, benign, most often &gt;7 yo and adolescents
