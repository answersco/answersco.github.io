---
title: "Strep. pyogenes"
---
-causes most kind of infections for this group
-causes wide variety of disease
-skin infections
-reproductive tract (puerperal fever) - problem for women after childbirth; bacteria being on the doctor/midwifes hand or on the outside reproductive tract of the woman; fatal
-strep throat
-complications from strep. infection could develop these: (1) rheumatic fever (joints become inflamed, valves of heart damaged, once infection is cleared up and later in life, damage to heart catches up), (2) scarlet fever (high fever, aches and pains, bright red rash, usually recover on its own or with antibiotics), (3) glomerulonephritis
