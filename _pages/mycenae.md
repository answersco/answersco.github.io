---
title: "Mycenae"
---
Sea-faring Greek kingdom. A major center of Greek Civilization in the 1000s BCE, centuries before Greek's &quot;Golden Age&quot; of Athenian influence. It's center was located about 90 km southwest of Athens.

Site of a fortified palace complex in southern Greece that controlled a Late Bronze Age kingdom. In Homer's epic poems Mycenae was the base of King Agamemnon, who commanded the Greeks besieging Troy.
