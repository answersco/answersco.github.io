---
title: "What is a tectal plate glioma?"
---
Subgroup of intrinsic, focal, benign, nonprogressive midbrain tumors located at the dorsal mesencephalon (Tectum (aka quadrageminal plate) is dorsal portion of midbrain, dorsal to cerebral aqueduct and includes superior &amp; inferior colliculi &amp; periaqueductal gray matter)
