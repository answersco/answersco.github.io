---
title: "What were the five core beliefs of the enlightenment?"
---
1) Reason- truth discovered through logic and rationality
2) Nature- natural laws good and reasonable and knowable
3) Happiness- believed contentment/fulfillment was possible 
4) Progress- society can improve
5) Liberty- they envied the liberties that English had won in the Glorious Revolution; natural rights; live without oppression
