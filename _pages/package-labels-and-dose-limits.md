title: "Package labels and dose limits"
---
White 1: no special handling
-Surface dose rate &lt; 0.5 mrem/hr, 1 meter 0 mrem/hr

Yellow 2: special handling
-Surface rate &lt; 50 mrem/hr, 1 meter &lt; 1 mrem/hr

Yellow 3: special handling
-Surface dose rate &lt; 200 mrem/hr, 1 meter &lt; 10 mrem/hr
