---
title: "Neurofibromatosis type 1 imaging findings (chromosome 17)"
---
Altered signal in white matter and basal ganglia. Dural ectasia. Optic and other gliomas. Sphenoid dysplasia. Thinning long bone cortex (ribbon ribs). Plexiform neurofibromas. Caf&#233; au lait spots. Iris hamartomas (Lisch nodules). Vascular stenoses.
