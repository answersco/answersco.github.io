---
title: "Differential for Big Freaking Mass in kid's chest"
---
&gt; 10 yo = Askin Tumor (PNET/Ewing's) - eats up rib
- The associated soft tissue mass is frequently out of proportion to the radiographically visible bony findings.

&lt; 2 yo = Pleuropulmonary blastoma (can look like CCAM or be the result of one)

(In adult Pelvis: B.F.M = ovarian mucinous or serous mass, dermoid, or sarcoma!)
