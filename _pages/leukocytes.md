---
title: "Leukocytes"
---
includes neutrophils, eosinophils, basophils, lymphocytes, and monocytes

-least abundant formed element
-protect against infectious microorganisms and other pathogens
-conspicuous nucleus
-spend only a few hours in bloodstream before migrating to connective tissue
-retain their organelles for protein synthesis
-include granules

-white blood cells
1) neutrophils: attack bacteria
2) lymphocytes: recognize bacteria, toxins, and foreign invaders
