---
title: "Shading Sign"
---
This is something you see with endometriomas where HIGH T1 signal structure is LOW T2
Generally hemorrhagic cyst are T1 bright but will also be T2 somewhat bright
ALSO, look for layering, i.e., areas of the cyst that shade in a gravity DEPENDENT fashion
