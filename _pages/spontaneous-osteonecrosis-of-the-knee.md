Spontaneous osteonecrosis of the knee (SONK): actually an insufficiency fracture rather than osteonecrosis
Classically old lady with sudden pain after rising from seated position
Favors medial femoral condyle unilaterally
Associated with meniscal injury
Diffusely increased T2 signal (edema)
