---
title: "Malignant splenic masses"
---
Angiosarcoma: heterogeneous, poorly enhancing mass

Lymphoma: hypodense, T1 dark, PET hot

Mets: breast, lung

Angiosarcoma: can be a heterogenous area in an enlarged spleen. They can contain necrosis. Contrast enhancement is usually poor.
Lymphoma: Most common malig tumor of the spleen. SPLENOMEGALY is most common finding. You will sometimes see discrete nodules of tumor. They are going to be PET HOT.
