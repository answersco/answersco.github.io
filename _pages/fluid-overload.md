---
title: "Fluid overload"
---
-Inadvertent administration of excess fluid

-Increases blood pressure and central venous pressure (pressure found in right atrium, fluid is coming back into right atrium and cannot withstand the excess fluid)

-S/S: moist crackles, edema, weight gain, dyspnea, rapid/ shallow respirations, SOB, may turn greyish color, SATs will drop, look/verbalize fear of feeling like they are drowning

swelling, increased u/o, dyspnea, crackles, can cause a dilution of lab values
