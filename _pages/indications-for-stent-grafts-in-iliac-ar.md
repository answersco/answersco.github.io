---
title: "Indications for stent-grafts in iliac artery occlusive disease"
---
Balloon-expandable or self-expanding
Indications:
Primary treatment of ulcerated plaque or recanalization of occlusion
Angioplasty-induced iliac artery rupture
Treatment of in-stent restenosis of bare stents.

Kissing stent-grafts when performing bilateral CIA origin revascularization may reduce restenosis in this location
