---
title: "Hypoglycemia"
---
abnormally low level of sugar in the blood

-blood sugar less than 70 mg/dL
-results of starvation, over treatment of hyperglycemia
symptoms:
-headache
-hunger
-blurred vision
-weakness
-tachycardia
-diaphoresis
-appears anxious
&quot;Cool and clammy, need some CANDY!&quot;
-always carry around hard candy and make sure it is not sugar-free
