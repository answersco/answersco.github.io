---
title: "Postop nursing diagnoses and goals"
---
1) risk for aspiration related to decreased level of consciousness
-client will maintain a patent airway while in the PACU
2) risk for infection related to surgical wounds
-client will remain infection free postoperatively
3) urinary retention related to immobility
-client will void within 8 hours of surgery
-has to empty bladder before they are able to go home
-release of choline which triggers release ADH that can slow urine output
4) constipation related to pain medication
-client will resume normal bowel function when normal diet is resumed
