---
title: "Imaging and enhancement characteristics of chordoma"
---
CT findings:
Midline, expansile, multilobulated well-circumscribed mass
Lytic bone destruction with intratumoral Ca++
Variable enhancement

MR findings:
T1: Intermediate to low signal &#8776; brain
T2: Classically &#8593; &#8593; signal
T1WI C+: Moderate to marked enhancement
Enhancement is often heterogeneous.
