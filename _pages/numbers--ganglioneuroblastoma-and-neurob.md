title: "NUMBERS: Ganglioneuroblastoma and neuroblastoma are associated with elevated?"
---
VMA &amp; HVA 

HVA = more mature tumor
VMA = more immature tumor (&quot;Nurses who work at the VA are more immature&quot;)

Want VMA : HVA ratio &lt; 1 for favorable prognosis (e.g. more mature tumor)

(Can also have increased VIP)


* 90% of neuroblastoma are hormonally active (VIP, VMA, HVA)

Neuroblastoma &gt; Ganglioneuroblastoma &gt; Ganglioneuroma in terms of producing these
