---
title: "Leg, foot, and nail care"
---
-wash feet with lukewarm water
-dry thoroughly especially in-between toes
-apply lotion to dry areas
-always remove TEDs and SCDs (sequential compression device) before the bath and replace after completion.
-avoid massage of reddened areas
-provide diabetic foot care if indicated. Education is imperative. Request podiatric consult when indicated.
-concerned with neuropathy - pay attention to the feet especially due to decreased sensation of pain and such
