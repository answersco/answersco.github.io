---
title: "Hair care"
---
1) identify patient's usual hair and scalp care practices and styling preferences.
-note history of hair or scalp problems such as dandruff, hair loss, or baldness.
-treat any infestations, such as pediculosis and ticks.
-comb or brush hair
2) wash as appropriate
-shampoo and water/Waterless shampoo
-hair bags: be sure to open and check temperature.
3) shaving
-groom beard and mustache
-shave in the direction of hair growth
-note it patient is on blood thinners
-use electric razor
