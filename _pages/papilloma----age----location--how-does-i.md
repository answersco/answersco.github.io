---
title: "Papilloma: - age? - location? how does it look on mammo, US, galactography?"
---
- usually around 50s
- classically in subareolar region

- Mammo: normal (may show calcs)

- US: Well defined smooth walled hypo-echoic mass; may have cystic and solid components and associated duct dilation

- Galactography: solitary filling defect with a dilated duct
