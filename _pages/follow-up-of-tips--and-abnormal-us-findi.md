---
title: "Follow-up of TIPS, and abnormal US findings"
---
US useless for 1-2 weeks
Follow-up exams usually at 6 weeks, 3 months, and 6 months, followed by biannual exams.

US findings of TIPS dysfunction:
-Peak shunt velocity &lt; 90 or &gt; 190 cm/sec
-Change in peak shunt velocity: decrease &gt; 40 or increase &gt; 60
-Main portal vein velocity &lt; 30
