---
title: "How do you treat SVC syndrome (in malignant and non-malignant causes)?"
---
Want to return flow to at least one IJ

- Usually REMOVE offending agent (e.g. catheter)
- THROMBOLYSIS + AngioPLASTY

- Malignant = STENT
- Non-malignant = only stent if there's residual gradient (e.g. collaterals still light up)
