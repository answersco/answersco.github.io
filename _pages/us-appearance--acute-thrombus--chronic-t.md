---
title: "US appearance: acute thrombus, chronic thrombus, valve leaflet"
---
Acute thrombus: expanded vein, hypoechoic lumen, noncompressible vein
-Flow absent or around margins of filling defect

Chronic thrombus: contracted vein, lumen partially or completely filled with hyperechoic material.
-May have flow through center of lumen or small channels.

Valve leaflet: thin, linear, usually paired structure with mobile free margins in center of lumen.
