---
title: "Relationship with parents"
---
1) early: view parents as less powerful
2) middle: feel constrained by parents; often low point of parent/child relationship
3) late: have renewed respect and affection for parents

-parents remain a vital part of school-aged child
-age 6-8 seek parents approval
-age 9-12 think they're smarter than parents/adult &amp; questions adult authority
-should anticipate child to pull away from affection unless ill or threatened
