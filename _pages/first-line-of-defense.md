-the physical barriers

-unbroken skin
-mucous membranes and secretions
-waving cilia of the respiratory tract
-flushing effect of tears, saliva, urination, and diarrhea

-process that creates a hostile environment for pathogens
-sends in phagocytes that engulf and destroy pathogens via phagocytosis

-physical and chemical barriers
-genetic components
