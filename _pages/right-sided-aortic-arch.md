---
title: "Right-sided aortic arch"
---
Due to regression of left-sided rather than right-sided branchial arches
Most healthy adults with right-sided arches have variant great-vessel anatomy, commonly an aberrant left subclavian artery.
When the branching pattern of the right-sided arch is normal (&quot;mirror image&quot;), there is 90% probability of a severe congenital cardiac defect.
