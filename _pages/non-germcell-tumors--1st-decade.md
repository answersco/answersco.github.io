title: "Non germcell tumors (1st decade)"
---
Sertoli cell tumor: tumors are usually bilateral. Visualized on US as &quot;burned out&quot; tumors that are dense HYPOECHOGENIC that represent scar. There is a type of germ cell tumor that is associated with PEUTZ-JEGHER SYNDROME. (See image)

Testicular Lymphoma: Buzzword = multiple hypoechoic masses of the testicles. The testes are going to be enlarged.
