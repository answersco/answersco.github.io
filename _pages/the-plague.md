---
title: "The plague"
---
A really bad disease during the 14th century that spread through fleas &amp; trade, killed about 1/3 of Europe, helped end feudalism

-yersinia pestis - can do well growing and staying alive out of the digestive tract
-historically important 
-pandemics throughout history
-complex life cycle involving animal reservoirs and vectors
-present in rodent populations in south-west USA but very rare
-high fatality rate but treatable with antibiotics
