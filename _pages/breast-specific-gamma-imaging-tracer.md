---
title: "Breast specific gamma imaging tracer"
---
Tc-99m-sestamibi: concentrates in breast cancer 6X more than normal breast tissue, with sensitivity ~90%
-Give 20-30 mCi in contralateral arm then image 20 minutes later. Foot injection done if imaging both breasts.

Distribution is homogeneous regardless of density
Hormonal fluctuation can increase background uptake: lowest in mid-cycle for premenopausal woman.
