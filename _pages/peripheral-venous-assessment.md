---
title: "Peripheral venous assessment"
---
1) inspect for varicosities
-superficial dilated veins
-typically in the legs
-common in persons who stand for long periods
2) inspect for peripheral edema
-dependent edema is most common
-pedal/ankle edema (non-pitting &amp; pitting)
-indication of right sided heart failure if it is bilateral
-if just one extremity swollen, looking at possible trauma
3) phlebitis
