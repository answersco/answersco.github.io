title: "Necrotizing fasciitis: - Risk factors (4) - organism? - what do you see on imaging? - prognosis?"
---
- RFs: HIV, Transplant, diabetics, alcoholics
- usually polymicrobial (second is Group A Strep)

- they want to see gas in soft tissues (although only seen in minority of cases)
- Cross sectional: diffuse fascial enhancement

- kills very quickly
