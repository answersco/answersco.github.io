---
title: "Kawasaki's presentation?"
---
Coronary artery aneurysms
- bad when &gt; 8 mm 

Usually 6 mo - 8 yo (&quot;mucocutaneous lymph node disease&quot;)
- medium sized arteries
- fever, swollen tender LNs, truncal rash, erythema/desquamation of hands, conjunctivitis and strawberry tongue

* Treatment = Aspirin and IV gamma globulin
