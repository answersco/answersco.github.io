---
title: "Protocol for workup of problematic adrenal lesions?"
---
Small &lt;4cm homogenous uniformally enhancing adrenal mass. If measure &lt;10HU on noncon CT then dx of adenoma. If &gt;10HU on non-con, then do adrenal protocol with immediate enhancement phase and delayed 10-15minute phase. If washout is &gt;50% then adenoma. If washout &lt;50% then mets, lymphoma, pheo or carcinoma.
