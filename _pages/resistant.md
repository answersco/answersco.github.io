---
title: "Resistant"
---
-child is clingy, distressed, and angry when parent's leave
-as soon as they walk into an environment they start to get anxious bc they know their parent's are about to leave
-when parent's return, they are very angry like &quot;why did you leave me&quot; and might hit or punch parents as punishment for leaving them
-hard to comfort and do not explore
-10% show this attachment
