title: "NMLE internal enhancement:"
---
Heterogeneous&#8594; enhancement is confluent and non-uniform
Seen in 21% of DCIS cases

Homogeneous&#8594; more suggestive of benign lesion

Clumped&#8594; &quot;cobblestone&quot; or &quot;bunch of grapes&quot;
Most suggestive of DCIS, especially if in linear/ductal or segmental distribution.
Seen in 51% of DCIS

Stippled/punctate&#8594; usually benign

Reticular/dendritic&#8594; strand-like enhancement
May be associated with inflammatory carcinoma
