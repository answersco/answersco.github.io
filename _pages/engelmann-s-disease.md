---
title: "Engelmann's disease"
---
Progressive Diaphyseal Dysplasia
Fusiform bony elarngement
Sclerosis of long bones (usually tibia)
Bilateral and symmetric 
Hot on bone scan
Can involve skull (optic nerve compression)

AKA progressive diaphyseal dysplasia (PDD)
Fusiform bony enlargement with sclerosis of the long bones, beginning in childhood.
Bilateral and symmetric
Tends to involve long bones, esp tibia
Hot on bone scan
Can involve skull, causing optic nerve compression
