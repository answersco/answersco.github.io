---
title: "Inspection of breast"
---
-client position: sitting or lying
-size, shape, &amp; symmetry
-color &amp; texture
-edema, rashes, ulcerations, &amp; dimpling
-nipples &amp; areola
-teach pt to inspect and look for any of these changes during examination
-older clients have pendulous breast tissue and are worried about sagging breast tissue but this is usually normal caused by ligament in breast tissue being stretched over time - can occur to younger women who lose weight, breastfeeding, or those who do not wear good supporting bras
