---
title: "GBS: - underlying pathology - associated organism? - how does it look on imaging? - what is the most common cranial nerve affected? - what is the prognosis?"
---
- auto-immune disorder
- associated with Campylobacter

- enhancement of the nerve roots of the cauda equina (anterior &gt; posterior)

- CN 7 (facial nerve) is the most common CN involved
- typically improves in a few weeks
