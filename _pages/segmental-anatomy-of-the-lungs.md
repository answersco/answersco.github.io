title: "Segmental anatomy of the lungs"
---
Right lung: 10 segments (3 upper, 2 middle, 5 lower)
-RUL: apical, posterior, anterior
-RML: medial and lateral
-RLL: superior, posterior, lateral, anterior-basal, medial-basal

Left lung: 8 segments (4 upper lobe/lingula, 4 lower)
-LUL: anterior, apical-posterior
-Lingula: superior and inferior
-LLL: superior, anterior-medial-basal, lateral, posterior
