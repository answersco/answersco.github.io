---
title: "Phenol coefficient"
---
-compares effectiveness of phenol to other agents bc it was the first disinfectant shown to kill microorganisms

1) &lt; 1.0 = not as effective as phenol
EX. isopropyl alcohol and ethanol

2) = 1.0 is same

3) &gt;1.0 = better than phenol
EX. cetylpyridinium chloride is over 200 times more effective than phenol and lysol is twice as effective
