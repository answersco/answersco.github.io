---
title: "Glasgow coma scale"
---
-used to define a person's level of consciousness by assigning a numeric value to the person's level of arousal
-divided into eye opening, verbal response, and motor response
-normally sound and alert pt will have the max score of 15 - you can then provide orientation and teaching to pt bc you know they are A&amp;O
-pain responses for eye opening: sternal rub, push edge of nail hard, squeeze on top of shoulder
-any pt that has less than a 7 is considered comatose
