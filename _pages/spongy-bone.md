---
title: "Spongy bone"
---
-lattice of bone covered in endosteum with slivers of bone called spicules and thin plates that form trabeculae
-Found in center of ends and center of shafts of long bones and in middle of nearly all others
-Covered by more durable compact bone
-few osteons and no central canals
-all osteocytes close to bone marrow
-provides strength with minimal weight b/c trabeculae develop along bone's lines of stress

-porous appearance
-consists of delicate trabeculae
-trabeculae covered by compact bone
EX. head of long bones and middle of flat bones like sternum
