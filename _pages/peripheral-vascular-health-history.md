---
title: "Peripheral vascular health history"
---
1) leg cramps or pain: due to lack of oxygen
2) numbness, tingling in extremities
3) cold sensation in hands or feet
4) pedal/ankle edema: indicates venous insufficiency
5) cyanosis in feet, ankles, hands
6) sitting/laying with legs crossed: causes venous stasis which can lead to varicosities
7) smoking
8) exercise
9) nutritional problems
