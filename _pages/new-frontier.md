title: "New Frontier"
---
JFK's program. Many goals, but few passed Congress. What did pass:
-actions to boost economy
-increase nat'l defense
-increase international aid (like Peace Corps)
-space program

Kennedy's version of the new deal that included the passage of domestic policies improving education, civil rights, national health coverage, increasing social security coverage, the minimum wage. Introduced tax cuts, and increased spending to the military and NASA.
