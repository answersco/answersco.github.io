---
title: "Mucinous cystadenocarcinoma (ovary)"
---
- Again, OPPOSITE of what you see in the pancreas. These guys are usually MULTIloculated. 
- Compared to serous tumors of the ovary, these less frequently have papillary projections and are less likely to be malignant.
- Frequently will see low level echoes from mucin.
- Can cause pseudomyxoma peritonei w/ scalloping along solid organs
