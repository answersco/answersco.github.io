---
title: "Risk factors"
---
1) greatest risk of breast cancer is in fact being a women and older age (older than 50)
2) personal history of breast cancer - a woman who develops cancer in one breast has an increased risk of cancer appearing in the other breast
3) risk of breast cancer increases who have a women, sister, or daughter with a history of breast cancer
4) brca gene
5) women who have their period before age 12 and women who go through menopause before age 55 are at increased chance
6) environmental factors but are inconclusive
-overweight: more estrogen in fat cells 
-hormone replacement therapies for a long period of time
-exercise can decrease risk of breast cancer
