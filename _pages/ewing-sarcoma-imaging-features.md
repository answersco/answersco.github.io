---
title: "Ewing Sarcoma imaging features?"
---
Permeative lesion in child &gt; 5 YO (not in really young); soft tissue component and in a flat bone (e.g. pelvis)
- Femur &gt; Pelvis &gt; Tibia &gt; Humerus METADIAPHYSIS

* CAN metastasize to LUNGS

- aggressive lucent lesion with poorly defined borders and permeative destruction of cortex with aggressive periosteal newbone and SOFT TISS component

- The associated soft tissue mass is frequently out of proportion to the radiographically visible bony findings.
