---
title: "Pineal Cyst: findings and ddx."
---
Incidental finding; May cause mass effect on tectum w/ compression of the superior colliculi resulting in upward gaze paralysis (Parinaud's syndrome). Or may obstruct the aquaduct and result in hydrocephalus. IsoT1 high T2, enhance, no DWI. Cyst is most common pineal mass and cannot be distinguished from other pineal tumors on imaging alone. Ddx pineocytoma/blastoma, germ cell tumor, meningioma, astrocytoma.
