---
title: "Hydatiform mole (complete)"
---
non viable embryo that presents as a normal pregnancy - abnormally high HCG
- Most common type of mole 70%
- This involves the complete placenta i.e. no fetus seen
- These are BENIGN

First trimester: you will see uterus filled with highly echogenic material that is solid and extremely vascular, often described as a snow storm
Second trimester: Vesicles that make up the mole enlarge into individual cysts (2-30mm) and produce the &quot;bunch of grapes&quot; appearance
