---
title: "Melanoma"
---
-deadliest form of skin cancer
-most common cancer b/w ages 25 and 29
-begins as a small, mole-like growth
-encourage pt's to use the ABCDE rule to assess for warning signs
A-assemetry
B-border irregularity
C-color variations from blue to black
D-diameter greater than 6 mm
E-evolving: change in color, shape, elevation, may start itching, bleeding, or crusting over
-educate pt's to notify their HCP if lesions do not heal or change in color and appearance
