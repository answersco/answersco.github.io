---
title: "What is the typical appearance and ddx for gastric polyps?"
---
multiple small mural filing defects of nearly uniform size (&lt;1cm, fundus and body). Most common benign epithelial neoplasms of stomach (80-90%).
Ddx includes hyperplastic polyps (in the abscence of other hx), adenomatous polyps (FAP) or if solitary &gt;1cm, hamartomatous polyps (Peutz-Jeghers syndrome).
