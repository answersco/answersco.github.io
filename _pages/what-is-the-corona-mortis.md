---
title: "What is the corona mortis?"
---
anastomosis of inferior epigastric and obturator vessels sometimes ride on the superior pubic ramus
- can be injured during repair of hip fracture

Vascular connection between the OBTURATOR artery (internal iliac) arising from the inferior epigastric artery of the External Iliac (courses over superior pubic rim and can be damaged in pelvic trauma --&gt; hard to ligate)

* surgery at risk: Hip replacement surgery and inguinal herniorrhaphy
