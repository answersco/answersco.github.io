---
title: "Preoperational Stage"
---
-2 to 7 years old
-child develops abilities of symbolic thoughts and pretend play but not complex thoughts 
EX. match box car is a symbol for the car in the driveway and child pretend plays with it saying vrooom, daddy is going to work
-Hallmarks: language development, egocentrism, incapable of reversibility, incapable of conservation
