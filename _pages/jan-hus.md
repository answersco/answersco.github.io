---
title: "Jan Hus"
---
A professor from Bohemia (now Czechoslovakia) who taught that the authority of the Bible is higher than that of the Pope. He was excommunicated, tried as a heretic, and burned at the stake; inspired by John Wycliffe.

(ca. 1369- 1415) Czech theologian and reformer condemned as a heretic and burned at the stake.
