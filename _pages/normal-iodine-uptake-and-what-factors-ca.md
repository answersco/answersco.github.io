---
title: "Normal iodine uptake and what factors can affect the test?"
---
5 mCi of I-131 or 10-20 mCi of I-123 given for study

4-6 hours: normal uptake 6-18%

24 hours: normal 10-30%

Renal function: poor function increases iodine pool and reduces uptake numbers

Dietary iodine: variable and controversial

Meds: thyroid blockers, nitrates, IV contrast, amiodarone
