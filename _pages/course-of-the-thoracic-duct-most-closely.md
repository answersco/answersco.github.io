---
title: "Course of the thoracic duct most closely mimics?"
---
Azygos vein!

Originates as cisterna chyli at L2, then ascends between the thoracic descending aorta and azygos vein, crosses diaphragm thru aortic aperture, and ends in the neck where it drains into the junction of left subclavian and left jugular vein junction
