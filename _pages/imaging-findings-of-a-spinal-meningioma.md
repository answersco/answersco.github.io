title: "Imaging findings of a spinal meningioma vs schwannoma? Which is more common?"
---
Schwannoma most common intradural extramedullary mass!

Meningioma = Enhancing mass with dural tail and possible &quot;target&quot; sign with central calc and peripheral homogeneous tumor enhancement

Schwannoma = Peripheral origin that pushes adjacent axons, instead of infiltrating
