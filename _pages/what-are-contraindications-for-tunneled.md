---
title: "What are contraindications for tunneled chest wall port?"
---
Insufficient soft tissues ie s/p mastectomy or planned chest wall radiation. Consider arm port as an alternative.

Sepsis or unexplained fever &gt; 24 hours duration
Active skin infection
Coagulopathy/chronic anticoagulant use (relative)
Bevacizumab (Avastin) administration 2 weeks prior/post port placement
