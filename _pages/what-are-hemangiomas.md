---
title: "What are hemangiomas?"
---
Benign neoplasm with endothelial proliferation, presents in the first weeks of life but is typically not visible at birth. Hemangioma is the most common tumor of infancy. F:M of 3:1. 60% are H&amp;N and 80% are solitary lesions. Involute over 5-12 years. Ulceration can occur in 5%, rarely hemorrhage.
