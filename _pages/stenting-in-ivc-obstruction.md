---
title: "Stenting in IVC obstruction"
---
Stenting can be performed for symptomatic stenoses (normal pressure gradient &lt; 3 mmHg) or other occlusive lesions to prevent recurrent thrombosis.
-Can also be used for chronic occlusion of IVC
-Can be used in presence of chronically occluded IVC filter

Stents:
Usually diameters in range of 12-30 mm
Require large-diameter, self-expanding stents.
