---
title: "Vector transmission"
---
-biological: living creatures that carry pathogens (rats, insects, birds, mosquitos, west nile virus, malaria)
-mechanical: inanimate objects contaminated with infected body fluids (EX. central lines and ventilators)

-spread of disease from host to host by means of a living organism (the vector) acting to transport the pathogen
-types include non-communicable aand zoonotic diseases
EX. Mosquitos transfers the microbes
