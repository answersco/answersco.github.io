---
title: "What is infantile hemangioma?"
---
A true benign vascular neoplasm, not a vascular malformation. 60% in H/N, most often parotid space. Diffusely enhancing mass with high-flow vessels during proliferative phase and w fatty replacement during involuting phase. Inapparent at birth with median age at presentation 2 weeks. Involute 90% by 9 yo.
