---
title: "GCT of the tendon sheath (PVNS of the tendon) appearance?"
---
Usually Hand Palmar tendons
- Erosions of underlying bone
- Soft tissue density
* Blooms 2/2 low-signal hemosiderin bleed
* Avid enhancement

- T1 and T2 dark (unlike glomus tumor which is T2 bright and enhances); Bloom on GRE (fibroma doesn't)
