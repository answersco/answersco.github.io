---
title: "Which is the most appropriate statistical test for comparing mean values from continuous variables from 2 independent, normally distributed populations?"
---
Students t-test

ANOVA: Comparing more than 2 means from 2 or more independent groups with normally distributed continuous variables. Mann-Whitney U test: Compares means from 2 independent populations that are not normally distributed. Wilcoxon Signed Rank test: Compares means from paired samples that are not normally distributed. Chi-square (&#967;2) test: Compares observed frequencies of categorical data often found in a 2 x 2 table.
