assess pain 
-at regular intervals throughout treatment
-with each report of pain
-before and after non-pharmacologic treatment and medication administration
-look for consistency and changes 
-go back in after medication is given to make sure what you gave did what it was supposed to

1) onset
2) duration
3) location
4) intensity (pain scale)
5) quality
6) relief measures
7) contributing symptoms
8) behavioral effects
9) effects on activities of daily living (ADL's)

-have client describe pain
-acknowledge pain and take care of it immediately
