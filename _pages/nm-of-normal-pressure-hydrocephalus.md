---
title: "NM of normal pressure hydrocephalus"
---
NPH: wet, wacky, and wobbly (incontinent, confused, ataxic)
&quot;Ventricular enlargement out of proportion to the degree of atrophy&quot;

Scintigraphy:
Early entry (4-6 hours) of tracer into lateral ventricles.
Persistence of tracer in lateral ventricle &gt; 24 hours
Delay in ascent to parasagittal region &gt; 24 hours
