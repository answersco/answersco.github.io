title: "What are the top three ddx of anterior mediastinal masses in kids?"
---
85% of all anterior mediastinal masses originate from the thymus and are due to 1) Lymphoma, 2) Teratoma, and 3) Benign thymic enlargement. Also think of lymphatic malformations descending from the neck.
5Ts: thymus, teratoma, terrible lymphoma, thoracic aorta, thyroid. Thymic cysts and thymoma are rare in kids.
