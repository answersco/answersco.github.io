---
title: "Mediastinal teratoma  GERM CELL TUMOR"
---
Most common extragonadal germ cell tumor
Kids &lt; 1, and adults 20-30
Benign, with small risk of malignant transformation.
Immature subtypes are exclusively seen in men, while mature is equal M:F
Association between mature teratomas and Klinefelter's

Cystic appearance (90%) + fat
May have calcifications, including teeth.
