title: "What QI tool typically follows a brainstorming session that has generated many ideas?"
---
Multivoting: a simple, structured approach used by teams to select the most significant or highest priority item from a list.
Relies on popular opinion as a method of prioritizing projects or elements of projects
Usually follows a brainstorming session that has generated many ideas.
Steps to conduct a multivoting session
List the ideas generated during a brainstorming session
Agree upon one or more criteria for voting
As each team member to select &#8531; of the ideas
Eliminate the ideas with the fewest votes
Repeat voting and elimination process until a workable list of ideas remains.
Rank order the ideas from the final list.
