---
title: "Left-sided SVC"
---
Most common congenital venous anomaly in chest
Rarely cause right to left shunt
Only 10% seen in isolation
Most commonly associated with ASD, less commonly unroofed coronary sinus
92% drain into coronary sinus.

Duplicated SVC: usually seen in setting of left-sided SVC, with smaller right-sided SVC also present.
