---
title: "REPRO: How to distinguish septate uterus from bicornuate from arcuate?"
---
Look at the outer fundal contour! It's normal in septate and arcuate, but septate has a septum and arcuate does NOT (it's just concave in).

Next, the Bicornuate will have an indentation &quot;heart shaped&quot; outer fundal contour!
