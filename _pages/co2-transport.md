---
title: "CO2 transport"
---
CO2 + H2O &lt;--&gt; H2CO3 (carbonic acid) &lt;--&gt; HCO3- + H+ (bicarbonate)

-10% dissolved in plasma
-20% bound to Hb 
-70% (up to 90%) as HCO3- 
-red blood cells carry it
-in order to maintain electrical balance, Cl- leaves as HCO3- enters and vice versa (Chloride Shift)
