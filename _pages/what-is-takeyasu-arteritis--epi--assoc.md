title: "What is Takeyasu arteritis? Epi? Assoc? ddx? Tx?"
---
Granulomatous inflammatory vasculitis affects walls of medium and large vessels, especially Ao and branches. Age &lt; 30 yo in 90% pts. F&gt;&gt;M 8:1. ddx Giant cell/temporal arteritis, fibromuscular dysplasia (spares Ao), Kawasaki (coronary). Tx Corticosteroids. Angioplasty for focal narrowings.
