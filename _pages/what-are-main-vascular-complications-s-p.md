---
title: "What are main vascular complications s/p renal transplant?"
---
Renal vein thrombosis- Reversal of diastolic flow in the renal artery flow. Renal artery stenosis- Elevated velocities at site of stenosis with a parvus et tardus waveform distal to stenosis (Takes several weeks to months). Pseudoaneurysm is usually due to renal biopsy.
