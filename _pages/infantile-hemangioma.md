---
title: "Infantile hemangioma"
---
Appearance: super T2 bright with flow voids, diffusely vascular on doppler, the WILL enhance on CT (think about liver hemangiomas)
Phases: show up around 6 months of age and usually involute by 6-10 years of age
Treatment: usually none required, however, if large, rapid growth with mass effect on airway or adjacent vascular structures or compressing the eye
Tx: If going to treat, typically medical with propranolol
