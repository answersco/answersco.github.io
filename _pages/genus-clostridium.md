---
title: "Genus clostridium"
---
-gram (+) anaerobic, spore-formers
-many saprophytic; soils, water
-some commensals; in intestinal tract
-active fermenters b/c they are anaerobes and do not use cellular respiration
-some produce potent toxins - exotoxins 
-rod-shaped
-includes gas gangrene, tetanus, and botulism
