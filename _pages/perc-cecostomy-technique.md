---
title: "Perc cecostomy technique"
---
Anterior transabdominal approach is preferred.
-T-fasteners are used to perform a cecopexy (mandatory), securing anterior wall of cecum to anterior abdominal wall and preventing leakage into peritoneal cavity.
-T-fasteners inserted at corners of a 2-cm square around the needle entry site.
-After cecum is punctured, a 0.038 inch superstiff wire is inserted into cecum and manipulated up the ascending colon.
-Tract is dilated with serial fascial dilators
-14-16-Fr gastrostomy catheter placed through peel-away sheath
