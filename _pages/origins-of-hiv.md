---
title: "Origins of HIV"
---
-HIV-1 considered a &quot;new&quot; virus
-evolved (mutated) from existing virus ~1950s or earlier
-disease unknown before 1980
-early 1980's first cases
-evolved from the simian (apes and monkeys) immunodeficiency virus
-earliest patients were homosexual men, IV drug users and later persons receiving blood transfusions/organ transplants showing same disease
