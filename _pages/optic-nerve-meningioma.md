---
title: "Optic nerve meningioma:"
---
Optic nerve meningioma&#8594; typically middle-aged female with slowly progressive visual impairment, classically with preservation of the central visual field.
Imaging: the nerve-sheath tends to be circumferentially thickened, with uniform contrast enhancement.
Tram-track sign&#8594; enhancing peripheral tumor and nonenhancing, central optic nerve.

4th decade, 80% female
mass surrounding the optic nerve
calcifications common
tramtrack enhancement
sphenoid bone hyperostosis
