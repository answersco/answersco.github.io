---
title: "What is medullary sponge kidney?"
---
Congenital cystic dilatation of collecting tubules --&gt; Medullary nephrocalcinosis
* Especially UNILATERAL

&quot;tiny calculi forming within the ectatic renal tubules and periodically rupturing into the pelvocaliceal system + Tubular ectasia&quot;

- Associated with Ehler's Danlos &amp; Caroli's disease
* Also associated with Beckwith Wiedemann
