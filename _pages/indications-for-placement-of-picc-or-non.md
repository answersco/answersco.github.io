---
title: "Indications for placement of PICC or nontunneled CVC for infusion therapy"
---
-Rapid infusion of blood products or fluid for hemodynamic stability
-Infusion of vesicant solutions (chemo, cytotoxic or inotropic meds, pH &lt;5 or &gt;9, &gt; 500-600 mOsm, &gt; 10% dextrose, &gt; 5% amino acids)
-TPN
-Active infection or coagulopathy preventing more permanent device
