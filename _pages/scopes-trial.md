---
title: "Scopes Trial"
---
(1925) - Trial of John Scopes, Tennessee teacher accused of violating state law prohibiting teaching of the theory of evolution; it became a nationally celebrated confrontation between religious fundamentalism and civil liberties, Scopes found guilty and fined $100

1925 court case in which Clarence Darrow and William Jennings Bryan debated the issue of teaching evolution in public schools.
