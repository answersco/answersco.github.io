---
title: "Pulse assessment"
---
1) radial Pulse: inner wrist/thumb side
-lightly compress with index and middle fingertips
-count for 30 seconds; if regular, multiply by 2
2) apical Pulse: point of maximum impulse (PMI)
-fifth intercostal space/midclavicular line
-auscultate with stethoscope
-count for 60 seconds
