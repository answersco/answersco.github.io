---
title: "Hepatic veno-occlusive disease"
---
-main hepatic veins and IVC patent
-abnormal portal venous waveforms
-cause: chemoXRT

This is a form of budd chiari that occurs from occlusion of the small hepatic venules 
The main haptic veins and IVC will be PATENT
On doppler, the portal venous wave forms will be ABnormal with slow, reversed, or to-and-fro flow
