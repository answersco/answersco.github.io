---
title: "Lung info"
---
-green yellow mucous is sign of respiratory infection - won't get good perfusion
-incentive spirometer to inhale and inflate alveoli - helps with gas exchange 
-measure pulse ox 
-atelectasis - collapse of alveoli; don't hear anything with auscultation
-asthma - broncho constriction, tighten airways
-endotracheal tube - if aggravate anything in larynx will cause laryngospasms after it is removed
-fluid sinks to bottom of lungs and heard best as crackles in bottom and backside of lungs
-black means air on xray so if there is fluid in the lungs it will show up white
