---
title: "How is amniotic fluid measured, and what values are normal/abnormal?"
---
AFI: measured by taking the vertical height of the deepest fluid pocket in each abdominal quadrant and summing the 4 measurements.

Normal: 5-20
Oligohydramnios: AFI &lt; 5
Polyhydramnios: AFI &gt; 20 cm, or single pocket &gt; 8 cm.
