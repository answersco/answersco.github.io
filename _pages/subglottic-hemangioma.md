---
title: "Subglottic hemangioma?"
---
Most common pediatric subglottic tracheal mass. Benign vascular neoplasm that produces stridor in infancy.

Most common soft tissue mass in the trachea
Going to be loss of the normal shoulder on 1 side (as opposed to do)
Tends to favor the left side
50% associated with cutaneous hemangiomas (makes sense as its the same disease process)
7% have PHACES
