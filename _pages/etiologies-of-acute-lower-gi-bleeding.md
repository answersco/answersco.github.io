title: "Etiologies of acute lower GI bleeding"
---
Diverticulosis
Hemorrhoids
AVM/angiodysplasia
Postendoscopic polypectomy (as late as 14 days)
IBD
Ischemic bowel
Portal HTN (colonic, rectal, stomal varices)
Tumor
Vasculitis
XRT colitis
Infection (esp immunocompromised host)
Small bowel/colonic ulcers
Aortoenteric and graft-enteric fisula
Meckel diverticulum
