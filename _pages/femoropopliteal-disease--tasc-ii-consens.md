---
title: "Femoropopliteal disease: TASC-II consensus"
---
Femoropopliteal disease: classified by TASC-II consensus:
Type A: single stenosis &lt; 10 cm&#8594; endovascular therapy is treatment of choice
Type B: multiple lesions (stenosis or occlusion), each &lt; 5 cm; single lesion &lt; 15 cm &#8594; endovascular treatment preferred, depending on patient comorbidities and preference.
Type C: multiple stenoses or occlusions &gt; 15 cm&#8594; surgery preferred, depending on patient comorbidities and preference.
Type D: chronic total occlusion&#8594; surgery is treatment of choice.
