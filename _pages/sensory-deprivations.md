---
title: "Sensory deprivations"
---
-reduction or absence of usual and accustomed visual, auditory, tactile, or other stimuli 
1) S &amp; Sx: anxiety, depression, boredom, unsettled feeling, hallucinations
2) Causes: 
-altered sensory reception (e.g. spinal cord injury, brain damage, sleep deprivation)
-deprived environments (e.g. patients who are immobilized or isolated)
