title: "What are the types of Vasculitis: - Large vessel (3) - Medium vessel (2) - Small vessel (6)"
---
Large Vessel: (&quot;GCT&quot;)
- Giant Cell
- Cogan syndrome
- Takayasu

Medium Vessel:
- PolyArteritis Nodosa
- Kawasaki

Small Vessel:
- ANCA + (Wegener's, Churg Strauss, Microscopic Polyangitis)
- ANCA - (HSP, Behcets, Buerger's)
