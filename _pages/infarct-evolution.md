---
title: "Infarct evolution"
---
Mass effect peaks 3-5 days
T2 FLAIR starts at 6 hours
ADC resolves 5 days
DWI resolve 14 days
Enhancement 6 days - 6 weeks

If see mass effect + enhancement, NOT infarct (mass effect should resolve before enhancement in infarct)

Hyperacute: 0-6h
Acute: 6h- 3days
Early subacute: 1.5 days - 5 days
Late subacute: 5 days - 2 weeks
Chronic: &gt; 2 weeks
