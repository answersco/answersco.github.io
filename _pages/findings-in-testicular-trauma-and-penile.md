---
title: "Findings in testicular trauma and penile fracture"
---
Penile fracture: involves corpus cavernosum and surrounding sheath (tunica albuginea)

Testicular rupture: disrupted tunica albuginea
Heterogeneous testicle with poorly-defined outline

Testicular fracture: intact tunica, with linear hypoechoic band across the testicular parenchyma.
Well-defined testicular outline.
