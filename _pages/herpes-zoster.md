---
title: "Herpes zoster"
---
-initial infection is chicken pox, usually during childhood years
-kids recover from chicken pox
-virus is still in their system but has become dormant in nerves around spinal cord
-can stay in sensory nerves for up to the entire lifespan of a person
-when a person reaches middle-age and later, the virus can become reactivated and follows sensory nerves to the skin and begins to replicate and cause extensive damage to skin cells
-this later infection is called shingles
