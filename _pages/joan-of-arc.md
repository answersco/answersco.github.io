title: "Joan of Arc"
---
born in 1412, this warrior helped French win the Hundred Years' War

French heroine and military leader during the Hundred Years' War; as a young teenager she was inspired by religious visions to organize French resistance to the English and to have Charles VII crowned king. She was ultimately tried for heresy and burned at the stake.

(1412-1431) peasant girl who led French troops to victory over the English at Orl&#233;ans in 1429; later captured by the English and burned at the stake as a witch.
