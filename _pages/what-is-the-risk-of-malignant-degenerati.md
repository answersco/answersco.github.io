---
title: "What is the risk of malignant degeneration in osteochondroma and what finding suggests it?"
---
Osteochondroma: &lt; 5% risk of sarcomatous degeneration
Rapid growth and increasing pain, especially in a skeletally mature patient, are suggestive.
Bulky cartilage cap &gt; 2-3.0 cm is suggestive on CT or MRI.
