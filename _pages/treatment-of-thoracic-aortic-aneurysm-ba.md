---
title: "Treatment of thoracic aortic aneurysm based on location"
---
Aneurysms distal to left subclavian artery (35%): may be treated with stent-grafts or surgically.

Ascending Ao (40%) or arch (15%) aneurysms: generally repaired through median sternotomy with cardiopulmonary bypass, though branched endografts are becoming available.
