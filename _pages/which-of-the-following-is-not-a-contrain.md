---
title: "Which of the following is not a contraindication to Cardiac MRI?  A) Cardiac pacemakers B) Intraocular metal C) Coronary stents D) Intracranial shrapnel E) Cochlear implants"
---
C. Coronary stents are not a contraindication to Cardiac MRI as they are not made of ferromagnetic material. The other options listed are absolute contraindications to MRI as they can both induce electrical currents in the patient or be attracted to the magnet and result in damage to surrounding structures.
