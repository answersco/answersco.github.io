---
title: "Myocardial infarction"
---
-sudden death of a patch of myocardium resulting from long-term obstruction of coronary circulation
-interruption of blood supply to heart
-blood from heart gets blocked by blood clot or fatty deposits that clog arteries
-deprived of oxygen can result in the death of cardia cells in minutes
-some protection --&gt; alternate routes of blood flow called collateral circulation
