---
title: "Polypharmacy"
---
-use of multiple medications (prescribed and/or OTC) by a patient at one time. 
-may lead to drug interactions causing impaired cognitive function.
-increased risk in older adults.

-the administration of many drugs together &#8594; intensified drug effects &amp; interactions.

why this occurs: 
-multiple providers prescribing to client
-OTC and herbal self medicate, share meds
-discontinued drugs not stopped
