1. Localizer (some use SSFSE ex. HASTE)

2 . coronal SSFE (ex. HASTE) - T2 weighted, Fast Good overview, Minimizes motion and susceptibility artifact

3. T2 with fat saturation

4. Chemical shift imaging (In / Out of phase) - Fatty liver, adrenal glands, renal AML

5. Diffusion weighted imaging - Tumor?

6. T1 pre-contrast + dynamics - Ultra-fast gradient sequence; LAVA (GE), VIBE (Siemens)

7. Post-contrast T1 delays - Retention of contrast vs. Washout
