---
title: "What is popliteal entrapment? what is it caused by? - imaging finding? - age population - pulses normal?"
---
Symptomatic compression or occlusion of the popliteal artery due to developmental relationship with medial head of the gastrocnemius
- young men &lt; 30
- Medial deviation of the popliteal artery

- Normal pulses that decrease with plantar or dorsiflexion of the foot
