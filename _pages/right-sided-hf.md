title: "Right-sided HF"
---
symptoms include:
-weight gain
-JVD
-peripheral edema

*more body concentrated symptoms
1) hepatomegaly/splenomegaly
2) GI symptoms, anorexia
3) swollen legs/feet, dependent edema
4) ascites - buildup of fluid in the belly
5) decreased peripheral pulses
6) distended neck veins
7) nocturia
