---
title: "What is chondroblastoma? Location? Sx? Epidemiology?"
---
A benign bone tumor with immature cartilage cells (chondroblasts) in heterogeneous matrix. Epiphyseal or equivalent origin in &gt; 90%. 
Pain, tenderness, limpness, swelling, limited motion
Most common in patients 5-25 years of age with 70% being skeletally immature
