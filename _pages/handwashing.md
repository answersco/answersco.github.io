---
title: "Handwashing"
---
-#1 practice to decrease spread of organisms
-friction is the most effective component
-use soap and water as well as hand sanitizers

-pathogens are not as tightly attached as &quot;normal&quot; commensal bacteria meaning they are more prone to being removed from handwashing
-water and scrubbing does remove microbes
-water and soap actually kills microbes and removes oils, dirt, and grim where microbes where usually stick to
-water and antibacterial soap is same as water and soap but has residual effects
