---
title: "Epidermoid"
---
3rd most common CPA angle mass
Can be congenital or acquired
Key features:
- Follow CSF signal EXCEPT for they are FLAIR BRIGHT and the RESTRICT DIFFUSION (this is how you differentiate from an arachnoid cyst)
- These are normally going to be OFF midline

congenital or acquired s/p trauma (LP), usu off midline, restricts diffusion, bright on FLAIR

keratin filled, squamous lined epithelium
