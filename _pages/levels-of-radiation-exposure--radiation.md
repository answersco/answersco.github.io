---
title: "Levels of radiation exposure: radiation area, high radiation area, very high radiation area, restricted area, and unrestricted area?"
---
Radiation area: 0.05 mSv/hr at 30 cm
high radiation area: 1 mSv/hr at 30 cm
very high radiation area: 5 Gy at 1m
restricted: all radiation areas:
unrestricted: expected dose of &lt;20 microSv/hr
