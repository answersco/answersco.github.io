---
title: "PDA: general info, associations, and CXR findings"
---
PDA: 
- normally closes around 24 hours after birth (functionally)
- anatomically closes around 1 month.

PDA Associations - think:
- prematurity
- maternal rubella
- cyanotic heart disease (can be)

CXR (nonspecific):
-big heart, increased pulmonary vasculature, large aortic arch (&quot;ductus bump&quot;)
