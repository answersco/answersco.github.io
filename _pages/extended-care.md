---
title: "Extended care"
---
-provide medical and non-medical are for people with chronic illnesses or disabilities. 
-assist with ADL's for people of any age who are physically or mentally unable to care for themselves
-2012: 9 million people over the age of 65 used long-term care services; that number projected to increase to 12 million by 2020.
-most extended care is provided by family or friends: for example, 70% of older adults requiring extended care are not in any type of facility (Medicare, 2013)
-facilities: transitional care &amp; long-term care facilities
