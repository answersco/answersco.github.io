---
title: "Mgmt of exit site and tunnel infections"
---
Typically caused by skin flora with direct extension from adjacent skin, most commonly staph epidermidis
-Site and tunnel infx can by treated with systemic abx, with preservation of the site in most cases
-IV vancomycin typically effective for cellulitis or uncomplicated tract infection
-Abscess in tract requires removal + IV abx
-If patient is running out of access options, consider converting catheter with mild tunnel tract infection to a nontunneled catheter plus treatment of infx.
