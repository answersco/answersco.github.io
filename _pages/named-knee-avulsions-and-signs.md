---
title: "Named knee avulsions and signs"
---
Segond fracture: lateral tibial plateau
75% with ACL tear
Occurs with internal rotation

Reverse segond: medial tibial plateau
Associated with PCL tear and medial meniscus injury
Occurs with external rotation

Arcuate sign: avulsion of proximal fibula (insertion of arcuate ligament complex)
90% with cruciate ligament injury (usually PCL)

Deep intercondylar notch sign: depression of lateral femoral condyle secondary to impaction - associated with ACL tear.
