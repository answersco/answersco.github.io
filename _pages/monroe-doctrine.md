title: "Monroe Doctrine"
---
An American foreign policy opposing interference in the Western hemisphere from outside powers.

An announcement by President Monroe in 1823 that the Western Hemisphere was off-limits to European nations for colonial expansion. Created to get rid of pressure from European nations in the WH. Europe thought it was a meaningless statement.
