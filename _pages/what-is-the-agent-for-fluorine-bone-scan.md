---
title: "What is the agent for fluorine bone scan? Half life? Dose? Utility and comparison to FDG?"
---
Sodium Flouride-18. T1/2 of 110 minutes. Dose usually 5mCi. Image at 60 minutes. Useful for osteoblastic mets and no value for soft tissue mets. F-18 FDG is better for osteolytic and for soft tissue mets.
