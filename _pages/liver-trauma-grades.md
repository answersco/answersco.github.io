---
title: "Liver trauma grades:"
---
Grade I: superficial laceration or subcapsular hematoma &lt; 1 cm in size
Grade II: laceration or subcapsular/intraparenchymal hematoma &gt; 1 cm and &lt; 3 cm in size
Grade III: laceration or subcapsular/intraparenchymal hematoma &gt; 3 cm in size
Grade IV: massive hematoma &gt; 10 cm, or destruction/devascularization of one hepatic lobe
Grade V: destruction or devascularization of both hepatic lobes
