---
title: "Which medication is NOT associated with tricuspid regurgitation? (Methysergide, Pergolide, Fenfluramine, Bleomycin)?"
---
Bleomycin! (pulmonary toxicity/fibrosis)

Methysergide (migraine med), Pergolide (Parkinson's), and Fenfluramine (weight loss &quot;fen-fen&quot;) are all causes of TR!
