---
title: "Spatial resolution limited by what in FPD and II systems?"
---
FPD: limited by detector element size (around 2.5-3.0 lp/mm)
II limited by TV systems (1.0-2.0 lp/mm for GI, 2.0-4.0 lp/mm for angio)
