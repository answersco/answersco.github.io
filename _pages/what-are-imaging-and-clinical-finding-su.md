---
title: "What are imaging and clinical finding suggesting an epidermoid/dermoid?"
---
Skin stigmata, including a sinus tract opening, dimple, or tuft of hair, is present in up to 84% of dermoids, epidermoids, and dermal sinus tracts. Widening of the foramen cecum (&gt;10mm), and a bifid or dysmorphic crista galli are associated findings.
