---
title: "Hypotonic solutions"
---
-Exert less osmotic pressure than ECF

-Used to replace cellular fluid

-Cause the cells to swell

-Pulls fluid in

Types:
1) 0.45% NaCL
2) 2.5% dextrose and water
3) Sterile water

(0.25% NS, 
0.45% NS) Solute concentration less than cell/fluid moves into cell
