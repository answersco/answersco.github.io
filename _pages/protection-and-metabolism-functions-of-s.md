---
title: "Protection and metabolism functions of skin"
---
1) intact skin - protects against physical &amp; chemical injury
2) melanin - protects against sun's ultraviolet rays
3) sebum - acidic pH - slows microbial growth
4) skin flora - inhibits growth of pathogen, germs on your body all the time, bc it lives there, other things cannot; these can become a problem if there is an overgrowth of them (staphylococcus, streptococcus, yeast)
5) adequate circulation, hydration and nutrition maintain cell life 
6) resistance to injury can be affected by age, amount of underlying tissue and illness
7) synthesizes vitamin D from the sun
