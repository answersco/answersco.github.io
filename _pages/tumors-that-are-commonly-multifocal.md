---
title: "Tumors that are commonly multifocal:"
---
Multifocal: lymphoma, multicentric GBM, gliomatosis cerebri

Multifocal from seeding: medulloblastoma, ependymoma, GBM, oligodendroglioma

Syndromic:
NF-1: optic gliomas, astrocytomas
NF-2 (MSME): schwannomas, meningiomas, ependymomas
TS: supependymal tumors, IV giant cell astrocytoma
VHL: hemangioblastomas
