---
title: "Tracheobronchopathia osteochondroplastica (TBO)"
---
SPARES THE POSTERIOR MEMBRANE
Development of cartilaginous and osseous nodules within the submucosa of the tracheal and bronchial walls.

Multiple submucosal osseous and cartilaginous deposits within trachea and central bronchi of elderly men. Spares membranous posterior wall of trachea.
