---
title: "OB-GYN: What is DIAGNOSTIC for pregnancy failure? (NUMBERS:)"
---
- CRL of 7 mm or more without heart beat
- Mean sac diameter of 25 mm or more without embryo
* NO EMBRYO and heartbeat 2 weeks+ after scan showed gestation sac w/o yolk sac
* NO EMBRYO and heartbeat 11 days+ after scan showed gestation sac WITH yolk sac
- e.g. if you see a yolk sac, you're allowed only fewer days

OLD guideline:
- No yolk sac when sac diameter &gt; 8 mm
- No embryo when sac diameter &gt; 16 mm
- No cardiac activity when embryo is &gt; 5 mm on TVUS
