---
title: "Esophageal candidiasis and mimic"
---
Candidiasis: immunocompromised patient, or motility disorder (achalasia, scleroderma)

Discrete plaque-like lesions, nodularity, granularity, and fold-thickening. Severe disease has a &quot;shaggy&quot; appearance with irregular luminal surface.
UGI: longitudinally-oriented plaques
May see pseudodiverticula: nonspecific narrow, flask-shaped dilations of excretory ducts.

Glycogen acanthosis: mimic of candidiasis in asymptomatic elderly patient.
See multiple elevated nodules.
