---
title: "What are the CXR findings assoc with Type 1 TAPVR?"
---
Type I: &quot;Snowman&quot; heart and cardiomegaly with shunt vascularity.

Type II: Indistinguishable from atrial septal defect (ASD), cardiomegaly and shunt vascularity.

Type III: Small heart, edema/reticular pattern in the lungs.
