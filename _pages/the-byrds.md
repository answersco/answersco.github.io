title: "The Byrds"
---
jangly sound made up of David Crosby, Gene Clark, Roger McGuinn, Michael Clarke, and Chris Hillman; the American response to the British Invasion; McGuinn was the face of the band; begat Gene Clark (solo), Roger McGuinn (solo), Gram Parsons (solo), Crosby, Stills, &amp; Nash (&amp; Young), The Flying Burrito Brothers, and The Desert Rose Band
