---
title: "Timing of sequella related to SAH"
---
Hydrocephalus = early

Vasospasm = 7-10 days

Superficial siderosis = late
-From repeated episodes of SAH
-Stains surface of brain with hemosiderin
-Curvilinear low signal on GRE coating the surface of the brain
-Classic history = sensorineural hearing loss and ataxia.
