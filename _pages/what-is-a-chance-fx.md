---
title: "What is a chance fx?"
---
A Chance fracture is a flexion-distraction type of injury of all 3 columns, with all failing in tension. Usually compression injury of anterior column with distraction of middle &amp; posterior columns.

Seatbelt fx. Flexion distraction injurey caused by acute forward flexion of the spine during sudden deceleration. Horixontal splitting of the vertebra begining posteriorly in the spinous process or lamina and extending anteriorly through the vertebral body.
