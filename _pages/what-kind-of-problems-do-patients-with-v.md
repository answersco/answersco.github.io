---
title: "What kind of problems do patients with VHL get in following organs: - pancreas (3) - adrenal gland (1) - CNS (1)"
---
pancreas: 
- cysts
- serous microcystic adenomas
- neuroendocrine tumors (islet cell)

adrenal: pheochromocytoma (often multiple)

CNS: hemangioblastomas (cerebellum, brain stem, and spinal cord)
