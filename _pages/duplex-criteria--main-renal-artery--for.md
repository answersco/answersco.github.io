---
title: "Duplex criteria (main renal artery) for renal artery stenosis"
---
0% stenosis: 
Renal artery PSV &lt; 180 cm/sec
Renal artery PSV/aortic PSV: &lt; 3.5

&lt;60% stenosis: 
Renal artery PSV &gt;/= 180
Renal artery PSV/aortic PSV: &lt; 3.5

&gt;60% stenosis:
Renal artery PSV &gt;/= 180
Renal artery PSV/aortic PSV: &gt;/= 3.5
