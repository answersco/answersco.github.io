---
title: "What is a Non-ossifying Fibroma (NOF)? - size? what if it's smaller? - how do they look on imaging? - prognosis?"
---
eccentric lesion around the KNEE with a thin SCLEROTIC BORDER (unlike GCT)
- &gt; 3 cm --&gt; NOF (&lt; 3cm called a fibrous cortical defect)
- regress spontaneously over time (become more sclerotic before disappearing)
