---
title: "What are the NRC requirements to release an outpatient treated with I-131?"
---
NRC requirements to release an outpatient treated with I-131 include either:
Dose rate of &lt; 7 mrem/hr when measured from 1 meter away
Must have received a dose of &lt; 30-33 mCi, OR
Potential dose to a person maximally exposed to the patient must not exceed 0.5 mrem in 1 year
