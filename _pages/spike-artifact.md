title: "Spike artifact"
---
Spike artifacts&#8594; appear as a series of bands alternating between light and dark, superimposed on the image
Origin&#8594; come from a single &quot;spike&quot; or clump of data in k-space, elevated by noise.
The pattern in a spike artifact resembles an ensemble during phase/frequency encoding.

aka - bad pixel arifact
