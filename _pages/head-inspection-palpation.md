---
title: "Head inspection/palpation"
---
1) assess head position: should be held upright, mid-line, and motionless 
2) assess facial features for symmetry
-eyelids, eyebrows, nasolabial folds, mouth
-facial droop
-note what features are affected (left side of the face, just the left side of the mouth, just the left eye)
3) assess shape and size of skull 
-normocephalic, hydrocephalic
4) assess the temporomandibular joint (TMJ)
