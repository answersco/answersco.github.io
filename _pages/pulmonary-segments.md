---
title: "Pulmonary segments"
---
Left lung
The left lung is subdivided into two lobes and thereby, into eight segments:

left upper lobe
apicoposterior segment
anterior segment
superior lingular
inferior lingular
left lower lobe
superior segment
anteromedial segment
lateral segment
posterior segment
Right lung
The right lung is subdivided into three lobes with ten segments:

right upper lobe
apical segment
posterior segment
anterior segment
right middle lobe
lateral segment
medial segment
right lower lobe
superior segment
anterior segment
medial segment
lateal segment
posterior segment
