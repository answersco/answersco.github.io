---
title: "What is a contraindication to vasodilator pharmacologic Stress?"
---
2nd- or 3rd-degree atrioventricular (AV) block without a pacemaker or sick sinus syndrome

- you SHOULD use vasodilator stress agent when beta blocker/CCB on board (exercise won't achieve heart rate), Pacemaker, or LBBB (don't use dobutamine)
