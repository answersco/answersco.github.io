---
title: "OA in spine:"
---
OA only affects synovial joints: in the spine, OA only occurs at the facet (zygapophyseal), atlantoaxial, uncovertebral joints (C3-C7), costovertebral, and sacroiliac joints.
Only the inferior portion of the SI joint is a synovial joint&#8594; typical changes of OA only seen in the inferior portion.
