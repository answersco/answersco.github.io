---
title: "Erythema"
---
-skin is red
-acute sign of inflammation
-will be generalized to one area and not their entire body
-check temp. of skin b.c reddened areas should be warm
-look for it on sacrum, heels, greater trochanter and most other bony prominences as it may indicate stage 1 pressure ulcer
-look for especially in pt's who are immobile
