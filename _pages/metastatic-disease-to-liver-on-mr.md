---
title: "Metastatic disease to liver on MR"
---
T1: Lymphoma and metastases: Hypo. Melanoma is hyper due to melanin.
T2: All mets, Focal or diffusely hyperintense. &quot;Light bulb&quot; sign: cystic and neuroendocrine mets.
T1C+: Hypovascular mets, Low in center w/ rim enhancement may be tumor vascularity or hepatic edema. 
Hypervascular mets: Enhancement on arterial phase.
