---
title: "Flexion versus extension teardrop"
---
Flexion teardrop: fracture at anterior-inferior vertebral body
Impaction injury
Unstable, with common posterior subluxation of vertebral body
Associated with anterior cord syndrome: loss of motor function and anterior column sensation (pain and temperature), with preserved dorsal column sensation (proprioception, vibration)

Extension teardrop: anterior-inferior fracture with avulsion of ALL
Distraction injury
Usually stable
