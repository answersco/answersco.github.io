---
title: "Pay for performance"
---
-strategy using financial incentives to reward providers for the achievement of a range of payer objectives
-Hospitals Consumers Assessment of Healthcare Providers and Systems survey (HCAHPS) 
---first national, standardized publicly reported survey of patient's perspectives of hospital care.
---27 item survey measuring patient's perspectives of their hospital experience
EX.
-how well do the doctors and nurse communicate care?
-cleanliness and quietness of patient's room
-recommend this hospital to family and friends?

Incentive program that rewards employees for meeting specific individual goals
