title: "Zimmerman Telegram"
---
This message was sent by Germans to encourage a Mexican attack against the United States to prevent the US from entering WWI, and in return, Mexico was promised the return of NM, AZ and So CA; once publicized in the US this message helped convince US citizens to join the war against Germany

This was sent by Germans to encourage a Mexican attack against the United States. Intercepted by the US in 1917.
