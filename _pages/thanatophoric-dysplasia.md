---
title: "Thanatophoric Dysplasia"
---
lethal, telephone receiver femur, limb shortening, platyspondyly- flattening vertebral bodies, cloverleaf skull, normal bone mineralization

Lethal skeletal dysplasia with characteristic telephone receiver femurs, severe limb shortening and bowing and rib shortening.

lethal short-limb dwarfism characterized by a notable reduction in the length of the long bones, pear-shaped chest, soft tissue redundancy, and frequently clover-leaf skull deformity and ventriculomegaly.
