---
title: "What are some infratentorial tumors? (7) - are they seen in peds or adults?"
---
- Atypical Teratoma/Rhabdoid
- Juvenile Pilocytic Astrocytoma
- Diffuse Brain Stem Glioma (DBG)
- Ganglioglioma
- Medullosblastoma
- Ependymoma
- Hemangioblastoma (adults)

*<b> all are seen in PEDS </b>** (except hemangioblastoma)
