---
title: "Tech-99m HMPAO"
---
- Dose - 15-20 mCi
- Action - Perfusion agent that is extracted by the brain on first pass; is lipophilic and routinely crosses the BBB into brain tissue in proportion to flow; becomes trapped in brain cells and won't wash out for approximately 24h
- Normal uptake - brain
- Uses - brain death study, dementia imaging, seizure imaging (also used to label WBCs)
