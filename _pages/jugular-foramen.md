---
title: "Jugular foramen"
---
pars nervosa with the glossopharyngeal nerve CN9 and the pars vasculare - CN 10 and 11.

Two portions:

1. Pars Nervosa: This is in front and medial. This contains cranial nerve 9 (glossopharyngeal nerve) as well as a tympanic branch called jacobsons nerve

2. Pars Vascularis: This is going to be posterior and lateral. This contains the JUGULAR BULB as well as CN 10 (vagus nerve) as well as CN 11 (spinal accessory nerve)

Jugular vein, CN 9, 10, 11

Jugular vein, CN 10, 11 (pars vascularis), CN 9 (pars nervosa)
