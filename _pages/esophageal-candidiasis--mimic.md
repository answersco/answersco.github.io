---
title: "Esophageal candidiasis  Mimic?"
---
Candidiasis:

Discrete plaque-like lesions, nodularity, granularity, fold-thickening. 
(linear or irreg filling longitudinal filling defects separated by normal mucosa)

Severe disease has a &quot;shaggy&quot; appearance with irregular luminal surface.
(2/2 edema, mucosal inflamm)
RFs:
- immunocompromised pt (HIV, xplant)
- motility disorder (achalasia, scleroderma)


Glycogen acanthosis: 
- mimic of candidiasis in asymp elderly pt.
- multiple elevated nodules.
