---
title: "How to distinguish ectopic pregnancy from corpus luteum &quot;ring of fire?&quot;"
---
Corpus = THIN less echogenic ring and moves WITH the ovary (corpus is part of the ovary)

Ectopic = often NOT part of ovary so will separately from it; Thicker more echogenic ring
- RI &lt; 0.4 or &gt; 0.7
