---
title: "Maneuvering the stairs"
---
-use one crutch and handrail
-make sure pt is truly able to do this before ascending and descending stairs
-many times have a small set of stairs on the floor to practice movements
1) ascending
-stronger leg ascends steps first
-crutch and affected leg follows
2) descending
-place crutch on lower step
-move weaker leg to lower step
