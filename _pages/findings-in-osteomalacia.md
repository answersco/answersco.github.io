title: "Findings in osteomalacia"
---
Osteomalacia: usually vitamin D issues (renal, hepatic, or other)
Looks like diffuse osteopenia
Ill-defined trabeculae and corticomedullary junction
Bowing
Looser's zones (type of insufficiency fracture)- wide, lucent bands that traverse bone at right angles to the cortex: seen in osteomalacia and rickets, less commonly OI.
