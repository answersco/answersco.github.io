---
title: "Treatment and prevention of tuberculosis"
---
treatment:
-requires a combination of antibiotic
-isoniazid, rifampin, others
-long term therapy; 6-24 months

prevention:
-testing
-identify persons early in course of disease
-&quot;tuberculin&quot; or Mantoux test or TB skin test - intradermal injection of mycobacterial proteins, kinda like an allergy test to see how you react by the size of the bump that occurs
-vaccine (BCG vaccine) 
-high risk groups, other countries use this
