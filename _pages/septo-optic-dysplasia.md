---
title: "Septo-optic dysplasia?"
---
Optic nerve hypoplasia and agenesis of the septum pellucidum.

Mild form of lobar holoprosencephaly
* absent septum pellucidum and optic nerve hypoplasia

Can look very similar to LOBAR holoprosencephaly HOWEVER the frontal lobes are NOT going to be fused
You WILL have absence of the septum pellucidum
