title: "Most common cyanotic CHD? - features (4) / general info  - surgical procedure for correction - most common surgical complication after correction"
---
Tetralogy of Fallot: (PROV)
- Pulmonary Valve stenosis (RVOT obstruction)
- RV hypertrophy
- Overriding aorta
- VSD

- Pentalogy of Fallot: also has ASD
- Very likely to have right arch

Surgical correction:
- shunt procedures (Blalock-Taussig procedure)
- Most common complication after surgery: pulmonic regurgitation
