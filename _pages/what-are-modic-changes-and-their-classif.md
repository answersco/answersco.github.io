---
title: "What are Modic changes and their classification?"
---
Degenerative endplate changes.
Type I: Hypointense on T1, hyperintense on T2 (replaced by fibrovascular marrow)
Type II: Hyperintense on T1, isointense on T2 (replaced by fatty marrow)
Type III: Hypointense on T1 and T2 (replaced by sclerosis with little marrow)
