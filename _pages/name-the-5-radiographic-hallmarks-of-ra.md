---
title: "Name the 5 radiographic hallmarks of RA"
---
1) Marginal erosions, which FIRST occur in the &quot;BARE AREA&quot; - this is a region of exposed bone just within the joint capsule that is NOT covered by thick cartilage

2) Soft-tissue swelling

3) Diffuse, SYMMETRIC joint space narrowing

4) periarticular osteopenia

5) joint subluxations
