---
title: "US Findings of TIPS malfunction?"
---
Normal velocity: 90-190 cm/sec

- Elevated Max Velocity &gt; 200 cm/sec across narrowed segment
- Low portal vein velocity &lt; 30 cm/sec
- Temporal increase or decrase in shunt velocity by &gt; 50 cm/sec
- New or increased ascites
- Change in direction of portal flow from towards to away from stent

*Bad if PSG &gt; 12 mm Hg
