---
title: "What finding in aortic intramural hematoma (IMH) is the strongest predictor of eventual dissection, and how can you distinguish acute from subacute IMH on MRI?"
---
IMH: rupture of vaso vasorum in media without intimal tear.
Maximum aortic diameter &gt; 5 cm: strongest predictor of progression to dissection (same with penetrating AS ulcer)

Acute: T2 bright blood
Subacute: T1 and T2 bright.
