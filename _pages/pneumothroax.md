---
title: "Pneumothroax"
---
-Occurs when the pleural space is exposed to positive atmospheric pressure

-Air in thoracic cavity

-Too much pressure in chest cavity from too much air and causing lung collapse

-Emergency: Patient's condition can decline quickly

Symptoms:
1) Acute resp. distress
2) Pain - sudden, stabbing, pleuritic; can be similar to pleurisy, friction occurs
3) Tachypnea
4) Respiratory discomfort
5) Absent breath sounds

Treatment:
1) Chest tube
2) Oxygen if tension pneumothorax
