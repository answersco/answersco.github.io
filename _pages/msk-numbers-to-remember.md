---
title: "MSK numbers to remember"
---
1. Plantar fascitis: &gt;4 mm

2. Wrist Arthrogram: 4 mL total
3. Hip Arthrogram: 14 mL total
4. Shoulder Arthrogram: 12 mL total

5. Amount of Gad for arthrograms: always 0.1 cc
6. Amount of Lidocaine always for arthrograms: 4 cc

7. Osteochondroma cartilaginous cap: &gt;2 cm worrisome

8. Acetabular angle in DDH: &lt;30 is normal?
9. Other angle in DDH: &gt;60 is normal?

10. Atlanto-Dens interval Adults: 2.5 mm
11. Atlanto-Dens interval Kids: 5 mm

12. Acromegaly plantar thickening &gt;24 mm
