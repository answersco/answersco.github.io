---
title: "Fibroadenoma and variants:"
---
Fibroadenoma (FA) &#8594; firm, mobile mass
MM&#8594; oval or lobular, equal-density, circumscribed mass (non-specific)
Hyalinizing FA&#8594; usually older women, with coarse, &quot;popcorn&quot; calcification.
US&#8594; oval, circumscribed mass with homogeneously hypoechoic echotexture, parallel orientation.
Variants:
Complex FA&#8594; contains internal cysts, and carries slightly &#8593; risk of cancer.
Juvenile FA&#8594; seen in adolescents, and demonstrates rapid growth
Giant FA &gt; 8 cm
Larger FA may look identical to phyllodes tumor.
