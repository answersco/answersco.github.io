title: "Splenic artery aneurysm"
---

Splenic artery aneurysm: &#8593; risk in multiparous females and patients with portal HTN
&#8593; risk of rupture during pregnancy
Pseudoaneurysm: may be secondary to trauma or pancreatitis
Indications for treatment of aneurysm: symptoms (e.g. LUQ pain), size &gt; 2.5 cm, and prior to expected pregnancy.
Endovascular coil aneurysm is preferred treatment approach&#8594; coils placed distal to aneurysm neck (to exclude retrograde collateral flow), then proximally.
