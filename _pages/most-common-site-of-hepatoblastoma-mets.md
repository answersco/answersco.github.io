---
title: "Most common site of hepatoblastoma mets? Classic appearance?"
---
Mets to Lung

Classically: hypo to isoechoic mass with spoke wheel appearance due to fibrous septa (some have dense, chunky calcs that can shadow)

Associations: Beckwith Weidemann, hemihypertrophy, Trisomy 18 (NOT 21!), Gardner syndrome (FAP), Type 1A glycogen storage disease, and cholestatic syndromes
