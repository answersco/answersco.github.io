---
title: "What is a simple febrile seizure? What is the management recommendation?"
---
A febrile seizures that last &#8805; 15 minutes but does not recur within 24 hours. 2-5% of all children between the ages of 3 months and 5 years experience a febrile seizure. 1/3 of them will have at least 1 recurrence. Imaging is not indicated in a simple childhood febrile seizure.
