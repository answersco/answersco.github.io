---
title: "Five cardinal signs of localized injury/infection"
---
1.) Redness: arterioles dilate leading to increased blood in circulation
2.) Heat: increased blood produces heat
3.) Edema: fluid and cells (exudate) accumulate in interstitial spaces; exudate dilutes toxins &amp; restores homeostasis
4.) Pain: from change in pH, chemicals, &amp; swelling
5.) Altered Function: results from all above changes
