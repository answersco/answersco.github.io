---
title: "SLAP tears 1-4"
---
1- Isolated fraying of the superior portion of the labrum without a frank tear. Biceps tendon intact. 2- Most frequent type- Labral fraying in conjuction with stripping of the superior labrum. (Repetitive microtrauma) 3- Bucket handle tear of superior labrum. 4- Bucket handle with additional extension into biceps tendon
