---
title: "Requirements to use unsealed by-product materials (I-131)?"
---
Administering dosages of radioactive drugs to patients or human research subjects involving a minimum of 3 cases for which the individual is requesting Authorized User status includes (1) oral administration of less than or equal to 33 mCi (1.22 GBq) of sodium iodide I-131, for which a written directive is required, (2) oral administration of greater than 33 mCi (1.22 GBq) of sodium iodide I-131.

- The person must have completed residency training in radiation therapy, nuclear medicine, or related medical specialty
