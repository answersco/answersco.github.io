---
title: "What association should you consider when you see a bleeding aneurysm in the pancreatic arcade?"
---
Celiac artery stenosis

Known association of celiac artery compression (MALS) with dilated pancreaticoduodenal arcades with pseudoaneurysm formation.

Classically shown on angio of SMA- shows dilated collateral system and retrograde filling of the hepatic artery.
