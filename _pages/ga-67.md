---
title: "Ga-67"
---
Indications: spine and osteomyelitis, FUO, pulm infection, sarcoid/tb, retroperitoneal fibrosis, drug induced pulmonary toxicity. By 48 hr after injection, about 75% of the dose remains in the body and is distributed among the liver, bone and bone marrow, and soft tissues. The normal distribution is variable and also includes nasopharyngeal, lacrimal, salivary, breast (especially lactating or stimulated), thymus and spleen.
