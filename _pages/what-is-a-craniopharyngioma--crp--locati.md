---
title: "What is a craniopharyngioma (CrP) location, and demographics?"
---
A benign tumor arising from epithelial remnants of craniopharyngeal duct; located along stalk from floor of 3rd ventricle to pituitary gland. Suprasellar (75%), Mixed supra/intrasellar (21%), Intrasellar (4%). Hormonally mediated symptoms in at least 1/3 of cases. Bimodal distribution 10-14 yo (adamantinomatous = cystic) and 40-60yo (squamo-papillary = solid/mixed). WHO grade 1.
