---
title: "Phobias"
---
irrational fear that causes undue personal distress and/or interferes with normal functioning

-irrational, extreme fears
-maybe we acquire them through classical conditioning experiences as well
Q: if we acquire phobias through classical conditioning, then how might we get rid of them?
A: 1) extinction training: show person what their afraid of w/o them getting hurt
2) systemic desensitization: on step at a time
3) flooding: go big or go home
