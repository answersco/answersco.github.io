---
title: "Prior to LP/CT myelogram bleeding recs: - how many days should you stop Plavix - coumadin? - LMW heparin? - IV heparin? - ASA and NSAIDs?"
---
- Plavix: stop 7 days (1/2 life of platelets)
- coumadin: stop 4-5 days
- LMW heparin: 12 hrs
- IV Heparin: 2-4 hrs (document normal PTT)
- ASA/NSAIDs: do not need to stop
