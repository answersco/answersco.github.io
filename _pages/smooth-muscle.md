---
title: "Smooth muscle"
---
-not striated
-involuntary
-responds to stress such as the digestive system
-one nucleus per cell

-fusiform myocytes: oblong tapered shape
-lack striations
-short, one nucleus in center of each cell
-involuntary
-most is visceral muscle = walls of hollow organs
EX. digestion, dilate/contrat blood vessels
