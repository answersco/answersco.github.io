---
title: "Typical appearance of benign parotid neoplasms:"
---
Pleomorphic adenoma (PMA) &#8594; 80% of parotid tumors.
CT is insensitive for PMA
MRI: T1 and T2 characteristics similar to water, but typically enhances. 
Will grow if not completely excised, and can undergo malignant transformation to carcinoma ex pleomorphic adenoma.
Cannot be distinguished from malignant mucoepidermoid carcinoma by imaging alone.

Warthin tumor&#8594; 10% of all parotid tumors, 15% bilateral, usually older male smoker
No risk of malignant transformation.
Generally appears as a cystic neoplasm, and doesn't enhance.
