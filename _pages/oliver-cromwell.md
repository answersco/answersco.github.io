---
title: "Oliver Cromwell"
---
English military, political, and religious figure who led the Parliamentarian victory in the English Civil War (1642-1649) and called for the execution of Charles I. As Lord Protector of England (1653-1658) he ruled the new Commonwealth as a virtual dictator.

a general of the New Model Army who led the Parliamentary troops against King Charles I; he eventually ruled as a military dictator as the new republic called the Commonwealth struggled in the mid-1600s
