---
title: "Lung cancer (NSCLC) staging: what makes a lesion unresectable?"
---
Stage 3B is unresectable: implies N3 or T4 disease
-Supraclavicular, scalene, or contralateral mediastinal/hilar LAD.
-Tumor in same lung but different lobe from primary
-Malignant pleural effusion

Stage T3: 2 in same lobe
Stage T4: 2 in different lobes of same lung
Stage M1A: 2 in different lungs.
