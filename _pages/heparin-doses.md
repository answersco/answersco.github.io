---
title: "Heparin doses"
---
-dose determined by desired effect 
-available in several dosage/concentrations BE VERY CAREFUL as to not administer the wrong dose in the wrong route
1) low dose given Sub q used for prophylaxis of DVT post-op
2) very low dose may be used to keep central venous access lines patent
3) higher dose given continuous IV infusion with intermittent bolus doses if clot identified and to prevent &quot;growth&quot; of clot
