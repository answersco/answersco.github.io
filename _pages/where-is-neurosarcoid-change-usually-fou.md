---
title: "Where is neurosarcoid change usually found?"
---
Diffuse leptomeningeal nodular enhancement with dural thickening usually found in cortical/subcortical locations along penetrating arteries

* Neurosarcoid typically demonstrates leptomeningeal &amp;/or dural enhancement. Isolated parenchymal sarcoid is uncommon.

* Neurosarcoid can involve virtually any intracranial structure, including the dura, where it can present as a solitary or multiple lesion.
