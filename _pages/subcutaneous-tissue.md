---
title: "Subcutaneous tissue"
---
-between skin and muscle at 45&#730; to 90 &#730; angle
-not richly supplied with blood vessels
-few sensory nerve endings
-absorbs medication faster than most drugs administered orally and slower than IV

-fatty layer of tissue right underneath the dermis
-different from adipose tissue
-has larger blood vessels than the dermis
-function is to help support the skin
