---
title: "What are the borders of the mediastinum?"
---
Superior: inferior border is the oblique plane from the sternal-manubrial junction.

Anterior: posterior border is the pericardium.

Middle: includes the heart, pericardium, and bifurcation of the trachea.
Lateral CXR: posterior to the trachea, and anterior to the vertebral bodies (or 1 cm posterior to the vertebral bodies)

Posterior: from the back of the heart to the spine
Includes the esophagus, thoracic duct, and descending aorta.
