---
title: "Secondary lymphoid cells"
---
1) clonal expansion and hypermutation
2) selection by FDC and Helper T
3) improved affinity, differentiation and class switching
-follicular dendritic cells (FDC) are specialized as they only display antigens to the newly formed B cells
-they test the cells for their affinity to the antigen and select only the strongest adhering cells are allowed to proceed to differentiate into plasma cells &amp; memory cells.
