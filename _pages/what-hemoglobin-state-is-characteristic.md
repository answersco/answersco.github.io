---
title: "What hemoglobin state is characteristic of each stage of intracranial hemorrhage?"
---
Hyperacute (&lt;24 hours): intracellular oxyhemoglobin
Acute (1-3 days): intracellular deoxyhemoglobin
Early subacute (&gt; 3 days): intracellular methemoglobin
Late subacute (&gt;7 days): extracellular methemoglobin
Chronic (&gt; 14 days): extracellular ferritin and hemosiderin
