---
title: "How to distinguish ABC from Telangiectatic osteosarcoma from Osteoblastoma from GCT in the spine?"
---
ABC - from the PEDICLE; fluid fluid levels, Expansile soap bubble appearance; may invade vertebral body

Telangiectatic osteosarc = More permeative bone destruction and wide zone of transition

GCT = arises from the vertebral body itself (not the pedicle)

Osteoblastoma = Will see bony matrix
