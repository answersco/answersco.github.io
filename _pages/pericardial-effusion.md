title: "Pericardial effusion"
---
Tumor:
 mets (melanoma, lung, breast)
Inflammatory / Idiopathic:
 Rheumatic heart disease
 Collagen vascular disease
 Dressler syndrome
 Post pericardotomy syndrome
 Drug hypersensitivity
Infectious:
 Viral
 Pyogenic
 TB
Metabolic:
 Uremia
 Myxedema
Trauma:
 Hemopericardium
Vascular:
 Acute MI
 Aortic dissection
 Ventricular rupture

This is more than 50cc of fluid in the pericardial space
Look for &quot;oreo cookie&quot; sign on chest radigraph (2 lucent lines FAT with less lucent central line)
Look for rapidly expanding cardiac sillouhette 
Look for giant water bottle hear

Oreo Sign: 
Posterior Chocolate Layer: Pericardial Fat
Middle Cream Layer: Pericardial Effusion
Anterior Chocolate Layer: Epicardial Fat
