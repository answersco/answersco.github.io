---
title: "On axial view of L4-5, which facet is the 'bottom hamburger bun' (more posterior one)?"
---
At L4-L5, the lumbar facet is the Superior articular process of L5 (MORE ANTERIOR) and the Inferior articular process of L4 (MORE POSTERIOR)

- The inferior articular process of the higher up vertebral body is the &quot;bottom hamburger bun&quot;
