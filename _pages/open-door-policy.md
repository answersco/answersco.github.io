---
title: "Open Door Policy"
---
Statement of U.S. foreign policy toward China. Issued by U.S. secretary of state John Hay (1899), the statement reaffirmed the principle that all countries should have equal access to any Chinese port open to trade.

A policy proposed by the US in 1899 under which all nations would have and equal opportunity to trade with China.

Policy proposed by the United States giving all nations equal opportunities to trade in China
