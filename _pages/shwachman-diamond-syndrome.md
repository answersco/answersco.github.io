---
title: "Shwachman-diamond syndrome"
---
Second most common cause of pancreatic insufficiency in kids (1st is CF)
Clinical picture is kid with short stature, diarrhea, and eczema

Pancreatic insufficiency (2nd most common cause behind CF) - lipomatous pseudohypertrophy
Diarrhea
short stature (metaphysesal chondroplasia)
Eczema
