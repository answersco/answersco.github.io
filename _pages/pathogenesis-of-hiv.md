title: "Pathogenesis of HIV"
---
-virus infects multiple types of cells but mostly immune system cells such as lymphocytes, macrophages, dendritic cells, others
-main cell infected is T-helper lymphocyte
-TH cell count in the blood used to monitor progression of the infection
-normal count 800-1200 
-&lt; 200 patients considered to have AIDS or will develop AIDS within 1-2 years`
