---
title: "PIOPED Criteria for Low/Very Low probability of PE?"
---
Low/Very Low: 5-6% PPV
1) Non-segmental perfusion mismatch (enlarged heart, elevated diaphragm, CPA effusion)
2) Any perfusion defect &lt; CXR defect
3) Two or more matched defects with normal perfusion elsewhere
4) 1 to 3 small (&lt;25%) subsegmental mismatches
5) Single triple matched defect in mid/upper zone
6) Stripe sign
7) Pleural effusion 1/3 or greater
