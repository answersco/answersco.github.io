---
title: "Normal and abnormal values of hepatic vein pressure gradient"
---
Normal HVPG: &lt;/= 5 mm Hg
HVPG &gt; 5 mm Hg: indicative of cirrhosis
HVPG &gt; 12 mm Hg: elevated risk of variceal hemorrhage.

HVPG is normal in prehepatic and posthepatic causes of portal HTN.
-Presinusoidal causes may have normal or slightly increased.
