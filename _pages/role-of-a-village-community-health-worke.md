---
title: "Role of a Village Community Health Worker"
---
-local, respected member of community. 
-serves village/district people 1-2 hours per day with health service. 
-paid or unpaid. 
-trained in fundamentals of promoting health, preventing and treating most common health care issues. 
-instructed in basic first aid and assistance on simple treatments. 
-educated in areas of health teaching in areas of personal hygiene, safe water supplies, safe disposal of human waste/refuse, and nutrition
