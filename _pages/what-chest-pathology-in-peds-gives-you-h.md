---
title: "What chest pathology in peds gives you High or Normal Lung volumes?"
---
&quot;climb the High MTN&quot;
1. Meconium aspiration = term or post-term baby with ROPY asymmetric perihilar densities and PNX (ball-valve effect)

2. Transient Tachypnea of Newborn = C-section or maternal sedation; peaks at 1 day, gone by 3 days
- coarse interstiaial lung markings and fluid in fissures

3. Neoanatal &quot;Neumonia&quot; = Except for GBS (that's low lung vol)
- Granuloar opacities and pleural effusions
- Full term
