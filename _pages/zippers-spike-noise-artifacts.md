---
title: "Zippers/Spike Noise Artifacts?"
---
Appearance: Noise and zippers appear as a strip of increased signal. Zippers are a strip of varying intensity. Spikes appear as a series of bands alternating between light and dark, superimposed on the image.
Origin: Detection of extraneous RF signals during readout.
Direction: Predominantly along the phase-encoding direction.
Solution: Acquiring the image again or remove causal electronics.
