---
title: "What is the typical entrance exposure for radiography, fluoroscopy (rate), and IR/cardiac procedures (rate)?"
---
Radiography: typical entrance air kerma in the range of 3 mGy (typical doses in the range of 1-10 mGy, or ones)
Fluoroscopy: typical entrance exposure rate in the range of 30 mGy/min (typical doses in the range of 20-50 mGy/min, or tens)
IR: in the range of 300 mGy/min (doses in the range of 300-500 mGy/min, or hundreds)
