---
title: "Enchondromatoses:"
---
Enchondromatoses&#8594; multiple intraosseous benign cartilaginous tumors in asymmetric distribution
Associated with &#8593; risk of malignant transformation to chondrosarcoma (Maffucci &gt; Ollier)
Ollier&#8594; no associated abnormalities
Maffucci&#8594; venous malformations, which cause phleboliths that are evident on XR.
