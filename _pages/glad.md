---
title: "GLAD"
---
Labral defect, most mild form
Stands for glenolabral artricular disruption 
There is NO instability

Glenoid labral articular disruption- superficial tear of the anterior inferior labrum with adjacent glenoid articular cartilage injury. Not associated with instability even though at same location as Bankart, ALPSA, and Perthes.
