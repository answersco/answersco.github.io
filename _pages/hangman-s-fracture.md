Common when chin hits dashboard in MVA (hyperextension)
Fracture through bilateral pars of C2 (less commonly pedicles)
Get anterior subluxation of the body of C2
Cord damage is uncommon, as the acquired pars defect allows for canal widening
Often associated fracture of the anterior-inferior corner of C2 from avulsion of the ALL

Hyperextension fracture of both pars interarticularis of C2

what is this?

A fracture ofthe anterior arch of C2 due to hyperextension

Unstable, serious fracture of posterior elements of C2 usually with C2 anterolisthesis on C3. Caused by hyperextension and distraction (head against dashboard).
