Multiple myeloma: 
Usually discrete margins, and may be solitary or multiple
Classically vertebral body destruction with sparing of posterior elements
Bone scan often negative&#8594; skeletal survey better, MRI most sensitive
May manifest as diffuse osteopenia
Plasmacytomas may precede clinical or hematologic evidence of MM by 3 years
