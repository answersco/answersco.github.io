title: "Uses of fermentation"
---
1) food products: add starter cultures to show microbes were added
-bread: can add saccharomyces with waste product being CO2 and ethanol which creates air spaces and making the bread rise
-alcohol: uses saccharomyces but in diff. varieties -- type of alcohol being made uses diff. carbohydrates
-fermented milk products: yogurt and cheese
-fermented meats and vegetables

2) industry: production of alcohols
