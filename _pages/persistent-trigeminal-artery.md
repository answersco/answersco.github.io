---
title: "Persistent trigeminal artery"
---
-most common carotid-vertebrobasilar anastomosis
-tau sign on sagittal MRI
-Saltzman type I: anastomosis with basilar a.
-Saltzman type II: superior cerebellar artery

Connection from cavernous ICA to basilar. Look for tau sign on sag image. Increased risk of aneurysm.
