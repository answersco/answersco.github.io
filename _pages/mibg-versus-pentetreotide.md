---
title: "MIBG versus pentetreotide"
---
Adrenal pheochromocytoma
MIBG&#8594; generally considered 1st line agent 
Normal renal uptake of pentetreotide may obscure an adrenal lesion &#8594; 2nd line for adrenal pheo if MIBG is negative
Extra-adrenal pheochromocytoma (paraganglioma or glomus tumor)
MIBG and pentetreotide are roughly equivalent
