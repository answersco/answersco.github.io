---
title: "What are considered Moderate Risk IR Procedures for bleeding?"
---
Vascular: 
- Angiography, up to 7 F
- Venous interventions
- Chemoembolization
- Uterine &#64257;broid embolization
- Transjugular liver biopsy
- Tunneled central venous catheter
- Subcutaneous port device

Nonvascular
- Intraabdominal, chest wall, or retroperitoneal abscess drainage or biopsy
- Lung biopsy
- Transabdominal liver biopsy (core needle)
- Percutaneous cholecystostomy
- Gastrostomy tube: initial placement
- Radiofrequency ablation: straightforward
- Spine procedures
