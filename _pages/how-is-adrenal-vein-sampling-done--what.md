---
title: "How is adrenal vein sampling done? What drug is used?"
---
Catheter in appropriate vein. R adrenal directly to IVC and left adrenal to left renal vein. Obtain sample from right adrenal vein firs, then left, 15 minutes after administration of cosyntropin (ACTH) (0.25 mg bolus followed by infusion of 0.15-0.20 mg/hr). Compare with sample from iliac vein.
