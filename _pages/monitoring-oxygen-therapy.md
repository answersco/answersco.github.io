---
1) physical assessment
-color, respiratory rate, pt may be anxious or confused
2) pulse oximetry
-non-invasive: % of hemoglobin carrying O2 to peripheral tissues
-92-100%
-used to titrate O2
3) arterial blood gas
-O2
-pH (7.35-7.45)
-CO2 (35-45)
-HCO3 (22-26)
