---
title: "Preoperative risk factors"
---
1) age 
2) nutrition: 
-adequate balanced nutrition important for wound healing, healing time
3) obesity: 
-takes longer due to all the extra fat tissue
-have decreased lung capacity
-heart is stressed more at baseline, 
4) fluid &amp; electrolyte imbalance
-some diseases cause fluid imbalances
5) pregnancy
-may have unrelated issue that is not because she is pregnant
-try to push it off as possible
