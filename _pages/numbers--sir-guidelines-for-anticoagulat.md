---
title: "NUMBERS: SIR Guidelines for anticoagulation prior to procedure?"
---
1. Low Risk of Bleeding (Dialysis access, IVCF, Para/thora, Superficial aspiration/bx/drainage):
- INR &lt; 2, Platelets &gt; 50, HOLD plavix x5 days, ASPIRIN OK, Hold 1 dose of LWMH

2. Moderate Risk of Bleeding (UAE, TACE, 7F angio, Tunneled line or Port, Torso drainage/biopsy, G-tube, perc chole, RFA, vertebro/kyphoplasty)
- INR &lt; 1.5*, Platelets &gt; 50, HOLD plavix x5 days, ASPIRIN OK, Hold 1 dose of LWMH
- Turn off heparin for 2 hours (PTT 1.2x control or less)

3. High Risk of Bleeding (TIPS, Renal bx, PCN, complex RFA, PTBD)
- INR &lt; 1.5, Platelets &gt; 50, stop or reverse for aPTT&gt; 1.5x control<b>; </b>HOLD ASA AND PLAVIX x5 days, *Hold Fractionated Heparin 24 hours or up to 2 doses

* Different from the category before
