---
title: "Secular perspective"
---
Includes a set of positive values (love, honesty, truth) chosen by an individual that guides their life
&#8226; Emphasizes a search for inner peace rather than a relationship with a divine being
&#8226; Want to live a happy existence
&#8226; May include nurturing positive thoughts, emotions, words and actions
&#8226; Belief that everything in the universe in connected
