---
title: "Which nerve stays in the carotid space in the infra hyoid neck (after the low oropharynx)? Where does the sympathetic chain lie?"
---
Vagus nerve stays with the carotid space

CN 9, 11, 12 exit the carotid space in the low oropharynx

* The sympathetic chain is in the fascia between the lateral retropharyngeal space and the medial carotid space. It is not within the carotid space itself. 

* the reecurrent laryngeal nerve is in the tracheoesophageal groove in the visceral space.
