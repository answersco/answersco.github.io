---
title: "General catheter choice based on intended length of treatment"
---
Short-term access (&lt;2-4 weeks) for infusion, monitoring, or exchange therapy- PICC or small-bore nontunneled CVC is indicated

Long-term access (&gt;2-4 weeks: tunneled catheter or implantable port may be indicated.
-Port preferred for access &gt; 6 weeks to 3 months
