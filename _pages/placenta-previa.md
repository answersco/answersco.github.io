---
title: "Placenta previa"
---
-placenta covers internal cervical os
-painless bleeding in third trimester
-need empty bladder

Placenta should be greater than 3 cm from internal cervical os. 
Diagnose previa with empty bladder

This is a low insertion of the placenta where either the entirety or part of the cervical os is covered by the placenta 
Buzzword = Painless vaginal bleeding in the third trimester
