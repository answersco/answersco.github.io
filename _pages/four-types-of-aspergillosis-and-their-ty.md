title: "Four types of aspergillosis and their typical victim and imaging findings"
---
ABPA - asthmatics, finger in glove

Aspergilloma, immunocompetent patient with cavity from prior pathology, dependent nodule in cavity/air crescent

Semi-Invasive, diabetic or EtOH patient, nodule or consolidation near areas of prior scarring

Invasive, immunocompromised, CT halo sign, air crescent, peripheral wedge like consolidation
