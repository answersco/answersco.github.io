---
title: "Transverse metaphyseal lines"
---
Lucent (undermineralization):
 neonates: stress lines due to fever, congenital heart 
 disease, severe illness
 &gt;2 years: neuroblastoma mets, leukemia, lymphoma

Dense (repair):
 Neonates: growth recovery lines
 &gt;2 years: 
 lead (or heavy metal poisoning)
 healing rickets
 treated scurvy
