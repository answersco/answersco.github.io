---
title: "Myofibril"
---
-intracellular structure which are the bundles of contractile organelles
-bundles that fill the muscle fibers, filling most of the cytoplasm
-surrounded by sarcoplasmic reticulum and mitochondria
-has striated appearance due to orderly overlap of protein myofilaments

smaller fibers that are found in a muscle fiber; consist of thick and thin myofilaments
