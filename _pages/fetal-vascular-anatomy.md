1. Hypoglossal artery - C1-2 ICA to basilar artery via hypoglossal canal
2. Otic artery - Petrous ICA to replace middle meningeal artery via middle ear
3. Trigeminal artery - Cavernous ICA to basilar artery
4. Proatlantal intersegmental artery - cervical ICA to vertebrobasilar system
