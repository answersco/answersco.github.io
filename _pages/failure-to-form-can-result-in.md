title: "Failure to Form can result in?"
---
Mullerian Agenesis (Mayer Rotkitansky Kuster Hauser) - vaginal atresia, absent/rudimentary uterus (unicornuate or bicronucate), and normal ovaries
* Kidney agenesis or ectopic

Unicornuate uterus (with or without rudimentary horn or endometrial tissue)
- Renal agenesis ipsilateral to the rudimentary horn
