title: "Griffin"
---
two types of Streptococcus pneumoniae bacteria:
-R bacteria: rough coat - nonvirulent --&gt; no pneumonia
-S bacteria: smooth coat (capsule) - virulent --&gt; pneumonia
-resists phagocytosis due to capsule so it is more virulent
-coat type is associated with virulence
-to make capsule you need to have the DNA to make the capsule
