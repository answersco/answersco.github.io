---
title: "Indications and contraindications of vertebroplasty"
---
Indications: acute to subacute fracture with pain refractory to medical therapy, or unstable fracture with associated risk if further collapse occurs.

Contraindications: fractures with associated spinal canal compression, or improving pain without augmentation.

Risk of developing a new fracture in ~25% of cases.
Cement can embolize to the lungs.
