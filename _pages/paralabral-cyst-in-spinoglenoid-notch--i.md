---
title: "Paralabral cyst in spinoglenoid notch: involves what nerve and muscle? What about if it extends into suprascapular notch?"
---
Cyst in spinoglenoid notch: can impinge upon suprascapular nerve
-Denervation of infraspinatus muscle

Extension into suprascapular notch: supraspinatus and infraspinatus denervation.

Quadrilateral space syndrome: compression of axillary nerve
Atrophy of teres minor.
