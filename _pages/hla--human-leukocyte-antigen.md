---
title: "HLA (human leukocyte antigen)"
---
-link sugars to form branched glycoproteins that extend from cell surfaces
-class I are found on all nucleated cell types 
-class II are found on antigen-presenting cells (APCs) macrophages, dendritic cells, &amp; B cells
-HLA glycoproteins can recognize bacterial and viral proteins, targeting them for destruction (called antigen processing)
