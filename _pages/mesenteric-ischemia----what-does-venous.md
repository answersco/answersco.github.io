---
title: "Mesenteric Ischemia: - what does venous type look like? - nonocclusive? - strangulation"
---
Venous: (picture)
- Dilation with THICK bowel wall

Strangulation:
- closed loop bowel obstruction with severe dilation and fluid filled
- congested, dilated bowel

Nonocclusive:
- see in patients in shock or on pressors
- delayed filling of portal vein at 70 seconds
