---
title: "What are the major neurotransmitters in the brain and the main functions of each?"
---
-Acetylcholine: movement, learning, and memory

-GABA: primary inhibitory neurotransmitter; high amounts of GABA are released when drinking alcohol.

-Serotonin: sleep, mood, and appetite.

-Dopamine: movement, attention, short-term memory, planning, reward, self-control (being an adult)

-Endorphins are neural regulators that control our pain response.

-Norepinephrine: attention and arousal

-Glutamate: primary excitatory neurotransmitter
