---
title: "Super scans"
---
No abnormal focal uptake, but can't see the kidneys

Diffuse mets: most commonly breast and prostate

Metabolic: hyperparathyroidism #1, renal osteodystrophy, Paget's, or severe thyrotoxicosis.
-See asymmetrically increased uptake in the skull (not in metastatic super scan).
-May also have increased soft-tissue uptake

Mimic: horseshoe kidney in pelvis.
