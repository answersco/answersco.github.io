Peroneal vein
Anterior tibial vein
Posterior tibial vein

Deep veins of calf are paired structures that parallel each of the 3 tibial arteries.
PT and peroneal veins are larger than anterior tibial vein due to larger muscle mass.
Tibial veins reside in same fascial compartments as their companion arteries.
