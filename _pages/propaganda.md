---
title: "Propaganda"
---
information, especially of a biased or misleading nature, used to promote or publicize a particular political cause or point of view; in wartime these techniques villainized the enemy and glorified support for the national cause

biased information used to promote a particular political point of view, spread false information, or sway public opinion

one-sided information, especially of a biased or misleading nature, used to promote support for the war.
