---
title: "What is Sprengel's deformity?"
---
Congenital elevation of the scapula 2/2 failure of caudal migration of the scapula during early fetal development. (it's tethered by a fibrous band to the C-spine)

- An omovertebral bar (fibrous, cartilaginous and/or osseous connection between the scapula and cervical spine) is often present.
