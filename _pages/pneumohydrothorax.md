title: "Pneumohydrothorax"
---
1- Meniscus sign
2- Nodules are benign or malignant lesions less than 3 cm in diameter. Once a nodule is more than 3 cm in diameter it is considered a mass.
3- Metastatic disease refers to tumors or infected nodules which originate from an extra-pulmonary (and occasional from primary lung) source. They are usually multiple compared to a single lesion in primary lung cancer. 
4- Abscess. Pulmonary abscess is an infected (puss filled cavity) usually associated with an air-fluid level.
5- Pleural thickening leads to opacification of the pleural surface and may be related to chronic inflammation, infection or tumors of the pleura. 
6- CHF
