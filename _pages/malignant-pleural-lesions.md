---
title: "Malignant pleural lesions:"
---
Mesothelioma&#8594; most due to prior asbestos exposure, with &gt; 20 year latency
CT&#8594; nodular, concentric pleural thickening, often with pleural effusion.

Metastases&#8594; lung, GI and GU adenocarcinoma, and invasive thymoma

Multiple myeloma/plasmacytoma&#8594; rib lesions may appear to be pleural based, although the epicenter of a mass arising from a rib will be centered on the rib.
