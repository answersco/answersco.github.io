---
title: "Thyroid disease that classically follows an URI"
---
Subacute (De Quervain's) thyroiditis: classically female with PAINFUL gland after URI. Similar subtype in pregnant women, but usually painless.
Hyper- followed by hypothyroidism.
Gland recovers normal function
Radiotracer uptake decreased during acute phase.
