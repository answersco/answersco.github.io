---
title: "Imaging findings of myxopapillary ependymoma?"
---
An enhancing cauda equina mass extending from the conus with hemorrhage.
- Displaces and encases the nerve roots
- T1 bright due to mucin; T2 bright and T2 dark at margin due to hemosiderin

*this lesion is found almost exclusively in the conus and cauda equina area

* Occurs in younger patients 35 yo

See RP 47a 5/1
