---
title: "Treatment of bronchospasm:"
---
Give O2 6-10 l/min via mask, monitoring ECG, pulse oximeter, and blood pressure.
Give beta-agonist inhalers (bronchiolar dilators, e.g. metaproteronol, terbutaline, or albuterol) 2-3 puffs, repeating as needed, with SC/IM/IV epinephrine if not responsive to inhalers.
Epinephrine treatment as needed.
Call for assistance for severe bronchospasm of if O2 saturation &lt; 88% persists.
