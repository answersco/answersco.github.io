---
title: "Types of immune system cells"
---
1) APC -- macrophages and dendritic cells
2) mast -- histamine release in tissues
3) b cell -- antibodies when plasma cell
4) helper 4 (CD4) -- cytokines and activation
5) cytotoxic t (CD8) -- kills virus, cancer infected cells and transplanted tissues
6) natural killer -- innately kills virus and cancer infected cells
7) neutrophil -- bacteria not on APC
