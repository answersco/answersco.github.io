---
title: "Transudative vs exudative pleural effusion, and causes of each:"
---
Light criteria for diagnosis of exudate &#8594; any 1 of these 
Ratio of pleural fluid protein to serum protein &gt; 0.5
Ratio of pleural fluid LDH to serum LDH &gt; 0.6
Pleural fluid LDH &gt; &#8532; upper limit of normal for serum.

Causes of transudative effusion:
Systemic low-protein states
Heart failure
Nephrotic syndrome

Causes of exudative pleural effusion:
PNA with parapneumonic effusion
Empyema
Tuberculous pleuritis
Mesothelioma or pleural mets
Rheumatoid arthritis or other collagen vascular disease.
