---
title: "Qin Dynasty"
---
the Chinese dynasty (from 246 BC to 206 BC) that established the first centralized imperial government and built much of the Great Wall

(221-207 BCE) The first centralized dynasty of China that used Legalism as its base of belief.

This dynasty <b>overthrew the Zhou</b> Dynasty

A short-lived (221-206 B.C.E.) but highly influential Chinese dynasty that succeeded in reuniting China at the end of the Warring States period.
