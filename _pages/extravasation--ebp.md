---
title: "Extravasation (EBP)"
---
1) stop injection/infusion immediately
2) leave the needle/catheter in place
-slowly aspirate as much of the drug as possible
-so not apply pressure to the area.
-remove IV access while aspirating.
3) elevate the area for 48 hours to minimize swelling
-initiate substance-specific measures per physician order
EX. phentolamine (alpha blocker)
