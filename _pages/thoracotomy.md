---
title: "Thoracotomy"
---
-Creation of a surgical opening into the thoracic cavity


1) Indications:
--Diagnose lung disease
--Remove pus pocket that has developed
--Diagnose chest disease - remove part of lung due to tumor, etc.
--Obtain biopsy

2) Done to diagnose, cure, treat
--Wedge resection- for diagnosis; small piece of lung dissected and sent to path
--Lobectomy- taking out one of the lobes of the lung
--Pneumonectomy: taking out entire lung
--Decortication: taking out an area that has built up a lot of scar tissue
Removing scar tissue
