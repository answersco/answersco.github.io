title: "What are the 11 mammographic findings in breast cancer?"
---
1. Nothing, 10% of all breast CA are false negative.
2. Spiculated mass
3. Round mass
4. Mass with calcifications
5. Pleomorphic calcifications
6. Architectural distortion
7. Developing density
8. Asymmetry (focal, global)
9. Breast edema
10. LAD
11. Single dilated duct
