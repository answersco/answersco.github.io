---
title: "What are the fundamental principles of professionalism?"
---
Primacy of patient welfare. Based on dedication to serving the interest of the patient.

Patient autonomy

Social justice: includes fair distribution of resources.

Set of professional responsibilities:
Commitment to: professional competence; honesty with patients; patient confidentiality; maintaining appropriate relations with patients; improving quality of care; improving access to care; just distribution of finite resources; scientific knowledge; maintaining trust by managing conflicts of interest; professional responsibilities.
