---
title: "What are some indications for HIDA (5) - radiotracer? why use that tracer?"
---
Indications:
- acute cholecystitis
- chronic cholecystitis
- Biliary obstruction
- biliary atresia vs neonatal hepatitis
- bile leak

- use Tc99m labeled tracer - analog of iminodiacetic acid
- mimic bilirubin's uptake, transport, and excretion
