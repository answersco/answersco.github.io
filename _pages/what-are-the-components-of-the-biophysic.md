---
title: "What are the components of the biophysical profile (BPP)?"
---
Assesses for acute and chronic hypoxia
2 points for normal, 0 for abnormal: total of 8-10 considered normal.

Amniotic fluid: at least 1 pocket that measures 2 cm or more in vertical plane

Fetal movement: 3 discrete movements

Fetal tone: 1 episode of fetal extension from flexion.

Fetal breathing: 1 episode of breathing motion lasting 30 seconds

NST: 2 or more fetal heart rate accelerations of at least 15 bpm and or 30 seconds or longer.
