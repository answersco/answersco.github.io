---
title: "Scurvy vs Rickets in terms of bone findings?"
---
Rickets = fraying, cupping, irregular physeal margin (not before 1 month) - if present at birth, these imaging findings 2/2 HYPOPHOSPHATASIA (deficient alk phos)

Scurvy = Subperiosteal hemorrhage (lifts up periosteum), hemarthrosis
- not present before 6 months

* both can show rachitic rosary due to expansion of costochondral junctions
