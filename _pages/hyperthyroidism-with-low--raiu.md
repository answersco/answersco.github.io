---
title: "Hyperthyroidism with low %RAIU"
---
Subacute thyroiditis (AKA de Quervains or granulomatous)
Viral prodrome followed by hyperthyroidism with scan showing decreased %RAIU, this is de Quervain's.
During acute phase, it can mimic graves with low TSH and high T3 and T4
Uptake scan used to distinguish them. 
After gland burns out may stay hypo or recover.
