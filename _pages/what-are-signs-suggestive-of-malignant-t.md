---
title: "What are signs suggestive of malignant transformation of osteochondroma?"
---
Osteochondroma: may transform to chondrosarcoma

Signs:
-Growth or increased size, in a skeletally mature patient.
-Focal thickening of the cartilage cap to &gt; 1.5 cm
-Development of focal destruction with lucent foci within the interior of a lesion 
-Development of an irregular or indistinct surface of the lesion
