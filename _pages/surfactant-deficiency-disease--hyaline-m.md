---
title: "Surfactant deficiency disease (hyaline membrane disease or respiratory distress syndrome RDS)"
---
Demo: Disease of PREMATURE kids
Findings: LOW lung volumes AND granular opacities. NO pleural effusions (this is what makes it different than b-hemo strep)
NORMAL plain film @ 6 hours excludes the dx
Treatment: Delay pregnancy or spray artifical surfactant
Things to know:
1. Increased risk of pulmonary hemorrhage (probs due to shit elasticity)
2. Increased risk of patent ductus arteriosis (PDA)
