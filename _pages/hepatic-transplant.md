---
title: "Hepatic transplant"
---
Normal:
RI = 0.5-0.7
Hepatic artery flow &lt; 200cm/sec

If RI&lt;0.5
-Proximal stenosis: atherosclerosis, anastomotic stricture, median arcuate ligament syndrome
-Shunt: cirrhosis, iatrogenic injury, Osler-Weber-Rendu (AVM)

If RI&gt;0.7
-postprandial
-old age
-cirrhosis
-hepatic venous congestion
-transplant rejection
