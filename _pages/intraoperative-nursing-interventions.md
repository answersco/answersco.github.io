---
title: "Intraoperative nursing interventions"
---
1) monitor vital signs
2) safety
-needle and sponge count
-maintaining surgical asepsis
-monitor skin on grounding pad -used to prevent sparking
3) client positioning to prevent injury
-responsibility of surgeon, nurses, anesthesia
-use appropriate padding
4) monitor malignant hyperthermia
5) monitor surgical asepsis (if anyone breaks sterile field, they are responsible for telling that person
6) aid in wound closure
7) transport to post anesthesia care unit
8) report pertinent information to PACU nurse
