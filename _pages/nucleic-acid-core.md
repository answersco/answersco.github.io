---
title: "Nucleic acid core"
---
-DNA or RNA but never both which is how they are differentiated from our cells
-may be single or double stranded
-linear, circular, or segmented
-contains genes to instruct and take over host cell to make more viruses
EX. in influenza: lung cells do not operate correctly and so the viruses instruct them how to operate now that they took those lung cells over
