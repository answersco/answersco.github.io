---
title: "Rickets vs hypophosphatasia"
---
Rickets: &quot;fraying, cupping, irregularity along physeal margin&quot;
Increased risk for SCFE
&quot;Rachitic rosary&quot; - from expansion of anterior rib ends at costochondral junctions.
NEVER SEEN IN NEWBORN

Hypophosphatasia: looks like rickets in a newborn
Frayed metaphyses and bowel long bones
Due to deficient serum alkaline phosphatase.
