title: "Manifest Destiny"
---
Term first used in the 1840's to describe the god given right and duty of the US to expand westward.

The policy in the U.S. that led to its expansion from the Atlantic to the Pacific

A 19th century belief that the U.S. would inevitably expand westward to the Pacific Ocean.
