---
title: "Gastric ulcers: benign vs malignant"
---
Malignant: 
width &gt; depth
Located within lumen
Nodular, irregular edges
Folds adjacent to ulcer
Aunt Minnie: Carmen meniscus sign
Can be anywhere

Benign:
Depth &gt; width
Project beyond expected lumen
Sharp contour
Folds radiate to ulcer
Aunt Minnie: Hampton's line
Mostly on lesser curvature
