---
title: "Milk of calcium"
---
Sedimented calcifications in macro/microcysts. Amorphous on cranial caudal view. Semilunar, crescent, tea cup shaped on medial lateral oblique view.

On CC view, these calcifications look powdery and spread out
On MLO view, they should layer 
On ML view, they are going to even layer more in a more uniform pattern
These are due to fibrocystic change
BENIGN

Note: you may not always see this on biopsy as it must be viewed with polarized light to assess bire
