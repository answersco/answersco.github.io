title: "Gregor mendel"
---
-father of genetics
-research in plant breeding from 1857-1863
-no knowledge of DNA, cells, or chromosomes
-developed the laws of inheritance 
-described the units of inheritance
-described how these units pass from generation to generation
-when he bred plants, you would see different traits popping up and disappearing

individual who discovered the basic laws of heredity via experiments in cross-breeding pea plants of different colors
