---
title: "Normal retropharyngeal space thickness"
---
C2 &#9472; retropharyngeal space should be &lt; 7 mm 
C3 and C4 &#9472; retropharyngeal space should be &lt; 5 mm, or 
 Less than half the AP diameter of C3 or C4 
C6 &#9472; retrotracheal space should be &lt; 22 mm in adults 
 and &lt; 14 mm in children (under15 years)
