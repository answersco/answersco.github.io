---
title: "What are the vessels that come off the anterior division of the internal iliac artery (8)"
---
*<b> very variable </b>*
- umbilical
- superior vesicular (off umbilical)
- inferior vesicular
- uterine (if you have a uterus)
- middle rectal
- internal pudendal (gives rise to inferior rectal)
- inferior gluteal
- obturator
