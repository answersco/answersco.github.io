---
title: "What are the contents of the following foramina of the central skull base: optic canal, superior orbital fissure, inferior orbital fissure, and carotid canal?"
---
Optic canal: CN2, ophthalmic artery (a)

Superior orbital fissure: CN3, CN4, CNV1 &amp; CN6 &amp; superior ophthalmic vein (v)

Inferior orbital fissure: Infraorbital a, v, nerve

Carotid canal: ICA, sympathetic plexus

Foramen rotundum: CNV2, artery of foramen rotundum &amp; emissary veins

Foramen ovale: CNV3, lesser petrosal nerve, accessory meningeal branch maxillary artery &amp; emissary vein

Foramen spinosum: Middle meningeal artery &amp; vein, meningeal branch of mandibular nerve

Foramen lacerum: Not true foramen; cartilaginous floor of anteromedial horizontal petrous ICA canal

Vidian canal: Vidian artery &amp; nerve

Internal acoustic meatus: CN7, CN8, labyrinthine a

Hypoglossal canal: CN12

Foramen magnum: Spinal portion CN11, vertebral arteries &amp; medulla oblongata

Jugular foramen: Pars nervosa: CN9, Jacobson nerve &amp; inferior petrosal sinus

Jugular foramen: Pars vascularis: CN10, Arnold nerve, CN11, jugular bulb &amp; posterior meningeal a
