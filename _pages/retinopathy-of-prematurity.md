---
title: "Retinopathy of prematurity"
---
b/l small dense globes, oxygen therapy

Retinopathy of prematurity (ROP) &#8594; premature infants due to prolonged oxygen therapy.
Both eyes affected equally.
Bilateral microphthalmia, with &#8593; attenuation of the globes due to prior hemorrhage.
End-stage = phthisis bulbi&#8594; shrunken, non-functioning globe.
