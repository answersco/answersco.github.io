---
title: "Healthy People 2020 Leading Health Indicators"
---
-outcome metrics used to measure progress toward national public health goals and to measure the health of the nation

Includes:
1) Access to Health Services
2) Clinical Preventive Services
3) Environmental Quality
4) Injury and Violence
5) Maternal, Infant, and Child Health
6) Mental Health
7) Nutrition, Physical Activity, and Obesity
8) Oral Health
9) Reproductive and Sexual Health
10) Social Determinants
11) Substance Abuse
12) Tobacco Abuse
