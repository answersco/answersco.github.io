The incidence is inversely related to the gestational age at birth. It ranges from 50% in newborns at 26-28 weeks gestation and decreases to 25% in newborns at 30-31 weeks.

Only premature neonates, never after 36 wks gestation.

P/w tachypnea, chest wall retraction, nasal flaring and cyanosis. The presence of grunting, which is characteristic but not specific in SDD, reflects an attempt to increase alveolar distension by breathing against a partially closed epiglottis.
