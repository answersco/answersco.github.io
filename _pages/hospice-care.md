---
title: "Hospice care"
---
-the model for quality compassionate care for people facing a life-limiting illness or injury
-focuses on medical management, pain management, emotional and spiritual support

&#9643; Goal is to improve or maintain the quality of life until
death occurs.
&#9643; Emphasis is on physiological and psychological
support for clients who have terminal diseases.
&#9643; Services include medication administration, basic
treatments, and grief counseling.
