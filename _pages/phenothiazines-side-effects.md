---
title: "Phenothiazines side effects"
---
-may give opposite effect than expected in older adults
1) **drowsiness, dizziness, weakness
2) **tremors and agitation: in older adults
3) photosensitivity
4) hyper/hypotension
5) dysrhythmias
6) dry mouth, anorexia
7) **extrapyramidal symptoms: in older adults
8) urinary retention, urine discoloration (pink or reddish brown)
