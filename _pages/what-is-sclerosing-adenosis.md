---
title: "What is sclerosing adenosis?"
---
Benign proliferative lesion caused by lobular hyperplasia and formation of fibrous tissue distorting glandular elements

A benign combination of stromal sclerosis and adenosis. Most often appears as clustered or scattered, amorphous &#177; punctate calcifications, Nodular type associated with mass. If atypical than excision. If result of sclerosing adenosis is discordant with imaging, then rebiopsy.
