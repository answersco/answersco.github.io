---
title: "Odontoid fracture classification - what are the types - describe fracture patterns - stable/unstable?"
---
Type 1:
- upper part of odontoid
- may be stable
- rare

Type 2 (most common)
- fracture at base of odontoid process
- unstable

Type 3
- fracture through dens into body of C2
- unstable, but best prognosis
