title: "Regarding Duplicated Collecting Systems:"
---
Upper moiety: longer and inserts inferior and medial to the lower pole moiety ureter; tends to obstruct, due to stenosis at the ectopic UVJ, also ectopic ureterocele is common.
Lower moiety: inserts in a normal location on the trigone of the urinary bladder. Usually dominant, involving the lower pole and interpolar calyces.
