---
title: "Most common cystic lesion in hands and feet"
---
Enchondroma: most common cystic lesion in hands and feet
Usually as lytic lesion with irregularly speckled (arcs and rings) calcification of chondroid matrix
NO CHONDROID MATRIX IN FINGERS OR TOES
No periosteal reaction
No pain (present in low-grade chondrosarcoma)
