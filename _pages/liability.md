---
title: "Liability"
---
-occurs if a person is found guilty of any tort; generally results in the payment of damages.

--Compensatory damages: the actual costs incurred because of the negligent act.

--Punitive damages: money awarded beyond the compensatory damages to &quot;punish&quot; the violator and send a message that this behavior is unacceptable; tend to much greater amounts of money.

-To show legal liability, the patient must be able to prove the following
--A duty to use care as defined by a standard of care (local vs. national standards)
--Failure to meet that standard of care
--Forseeability of harm
--Actual harm to the patient
--If any conditions are missing, then there is no lawsuit.
