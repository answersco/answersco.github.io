---
title: "Endometrial cancer"
---
95% present with postmenopausal bleeding. Risk is estrogen exposure. Endometrium greater than 8 mm in post menopause - biopsy.

Basically all UTERINE cancers are adenocarcinoma
Imaging characteristics: typically enhances slightly less than background endometrium, WILL restrict diffusion, mildly bright on T2
Critical staging pearls:
- You are looking to determine stage 1 vs stage 2 which is defined as cervical stromal invasion (see image)
