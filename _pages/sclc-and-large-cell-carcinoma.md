---
title: "SCLC and large-cell carcinoma:"
---
Small-cell carcinoma: tends to occur in central bronchi with invasion through the bronchial wall&#8594; large hilar or parahilar mass
May cause SVC syndrome.
Large-cell carcinoma: wastebasket pathologic diagnosis for other primary lung cancers. 
Strongly associated with smoking, and carries poor prognosis
Often occurs in lung periphery, presenting as a solid mass.
