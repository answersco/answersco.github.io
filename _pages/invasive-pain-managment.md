title: "Invasive pain managment"
---
1) intraspinal -
 epidural/intrathecal
-used to control post-op pain, chronic non-malignant pain and severe cancer pain
-may be placed in the cervical, thoracic, lumbar, or caudal spinal regions
2) patient controlled anesthesia-PCA
-set pump for how much can be given
-pt can only push button
-used for alert pt who can decide when pain is getting worse
-records when requested and given
