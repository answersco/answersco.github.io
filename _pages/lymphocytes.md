---
title: "Lymphocytes"
---
the primary cells of the adaptive immune system present in the blood stream and lymphoid tissue which react to the presence of specific antigens

-mount immune response by direct cell attack or via antibodies
-nucleus takes up 90% of cell

-increased numbers in diverse infections and immune responses
-destroy cells (cancer, foreign, and virally infected cells)
-&quot;present&quot; antigens to activate other immune cells
-coordinate actions of other immune cells
-secrete antibodies and provide immune memory
