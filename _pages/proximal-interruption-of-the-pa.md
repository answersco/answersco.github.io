---
title: "Proximal interruption of the PA"
---
Congenital absence of the right (or left) PA- more distal pulmonary vasculature is present.

Classically shown with volume loss of one hemi-thorax
CT through heart showing only one PA

Seen on opposite side of arch (absent R PA with left-sided aortic arch)
Associated with PDA
Interrupted left PA associated with TOF and truncus arteriosus.

Congenital absence with present distal vasculature. Volume loss on the involved side. Usually on opposite side of arch.
