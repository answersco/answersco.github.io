---
title: "IR treatment in LE venous insufficiency:"
---
-Small varicose veins and telangiectasias can be treated with sclerotherapy.
-Larger varicosities can be either sclerosed or removed with mini-stab phlebectomy.
-Saphenous veins are usually treated with endovenous ablation (though traditional stripping still performed in some)
-Perforating veins: can be ligated, ablated with specialized probes, or injected under US guidance.
