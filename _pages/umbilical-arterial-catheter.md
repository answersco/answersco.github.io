---
title: "Umbilical arterial catheter"
---
Course: umbilicus &gt; down the umbilical artery &gt; iliac artery &gt; aorta
Major risk factor is renal artery thrombosis
In order to avoid this, either put the line HIGH T8-T10 or LOW L3-L5
Note: an omphalocele is a contraindication to placing an umbilical arterial line
