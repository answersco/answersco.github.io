title: "Insulin"
---
-hormone produced by beta cells (type 1 diabetics they do not have proper working insulin cells so they take insulin)
-releases incretins in response to food
-circulates through body
-reacts on insulin receptor sites
-stimulates transport of glucose into cells
-stimulates synthesis of glycogen (glucose stored for immediate release)
-converts lipids to fat stored in adipose tissue
-protein synthesis from amino acids
-released after meal &#8594; affects metabolism &#8594; body uses or stores nutrients

-located: most body cells
-function: decreases blood glucose levels by transporting glucose into cells

-produced by beta cells of Islets
-released when glucose levels are elevated in blood
-encourages the uptake of glucose into cells for storage
