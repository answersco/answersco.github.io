---
title: "What MELD and Childs-Pugh scores confer higher risk with TIPS"
---
MELD (bilirubin, INR, creatinine) &gt; 18 associated with higher risk of early death after elective TIPS.

Childs-Pugh (bilirubin, albumin, PT, ascites, hepatic encephalopathy) class B and C are risk factors for variceal hemorrhage.
