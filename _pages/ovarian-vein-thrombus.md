---
title: "Ovarian Vein thrombus"
---
Ovarian vein thrombosis is right-sided 80-90% of the time, possibly due to retrograde flow into the left ovarian vein preventing stasis. Cmmonly seen in post-partum patients, Less common are gynecological malignancies, pelvic inflammatory disease and pelvic trauma. The left ovarian vein typically inserts into the left renal vein while the right renal vein inserts directly into the IVC.
