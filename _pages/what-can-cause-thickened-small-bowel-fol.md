---
title: "What can cause thickened small bowel folds that remain straight (e.g. not nodular)?"
---
Inflammation, hemorrhage, radiation, ischemia

Low protein state, venous congestion, cirrhosis = more diffuse

Crohn's, Lymphoma, Mets, Infection = Segmental

Whipples, Lymphoid hyperplasia, lymphoma/mets, Lymphangiectasia = Diffuse
