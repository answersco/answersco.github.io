---
title: "How to conceptualize T3 vs T4 disease of bronchogenic carcinoma that's invading an adjacent structure?"
---
If it's invading a &quot;Vital&quot; structure, call it T4

ex) T3 = chest wall, mediastinal pleura, diaphragm, pericardium

T4 = mediastinal fat, heart, trachea, carina, esophagus, great vessels, recurrent laryngeal nerve, vertebral body
