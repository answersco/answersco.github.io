High Reliability Organization (HRO)
HRO: an organization that, despite operating in a high-stress, high-risk environment, continually manages its environment mindfully, adopting a constant state of vigilance resulting in the fewest # of errors.

5 characteristics of HROs:
Fixation on failure
Avoidance of oversimplification
Sensitivity to operations
Respect of expertise
Dedication to resilience
