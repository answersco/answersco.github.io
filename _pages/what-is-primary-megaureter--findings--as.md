---
title: "What is primary megaureter? Findings? Assoc? Tx?"
---
Primary obstructive megaureter refers to a functional obstruction occurring at juxtavesical segment of the ureter due to absent peristalsis (0.5-4cm). No true narrowing of distal ureter. Unilateral 67% affecting left ureter. 4X M:F. VUR &lt;10%. 70% Regress spontaneously by 7 yo w/o surg. If &gt;1.5cm diameter surgery may be needed.
