= Superior Sulcus Tumor, UE pain C8/T1/T2 ulnar pattern, Horner's (myosis/ptosis/anhidrosis)

squamous carcinoma posterior mediastinum; destruction superior cervical ganglion

Times when these tumors are unresectable:

- brachial plexus involvement above T1
- Diaphragm paralysis (c3-c5 invovlement)
- Greater than 50% vertebral body involvement
- Distal nodes or mets
