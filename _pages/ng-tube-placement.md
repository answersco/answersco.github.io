---
title: "NG tube placement"
---
1) place pt in Fowlers position
2) determine length by measuring NG tube from 
-tip of the nose to 
-helix or tip of the ear to 
-the xiphoid process. 
3) mark tubing or note striped marking on tube
4) glove, lubricate lube
5) have pt tilt head forward
6) instruct pt procedure will be uncomfortable.
7) advance tube while asking patient to swallow. 
8) secure to nose
9) follow hospital policy for placement verification: (x-ray, then pH, then measurement)
