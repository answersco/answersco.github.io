---
title: "Mouth and oral care"
---
1) assist with brushing and flossing. 
-patient on blood thinner = soft brush
2) use swabs for patients with stomatitis.
-stoma means opening and itis means inflammation : inflammation of the mouth
-caused by chemotherapy, mouth-breathing, antibiotics
3) patient with dentures:
-clean mouth
-clean dentures 
-line sink with wash cloth or towel
4) patient on oxygen
-mucous membranes dry out.
-keep moist and provide good mouth care
5) unconscious patient
-lower the head of the bed.
-place patient in the Sim's position
-head well towards the side
-use kidney basin and suctioning to prevent aspiration
