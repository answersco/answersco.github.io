---
title: "Hypothenar hammer syndrome: - complications (2) - imaging findings (3)"
---
Complications:
- aneurysm formation
- thrombus may form (+/- emboli): may confuse with Buergers

Findings:
- corkscrew configuration of superificial palmar arch
- occlusion of ulnar artery
- pseudoaneurysm of the ulnar artery
