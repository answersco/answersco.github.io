---
title: "Evaluating gallbladder polyp for malignancy? (6 things)"
---
Six S's. Size more than 10 mm (U/S usually cant see smaller than this). Single (Solitary more concerning). Sessile (Broad based is more suspicious, pedunculated is better). Stones (Increased malignancy due to chronic inflammation). Primary Sclerosing cholangitis increases risk of malignancy. Sixty (Age 60 or greater)
