---
title: "Incentive spirometry"
---
-encourages deep breathing
-measures inspiratory volume
-encourage client to breath in deeply
-should be used ten times and hour
-needs to be slow inhale, keeping the ball on the smiley face and not worrying about how much air they breathe in
-make sure they have good hydration bc it will dry out your mouth
-might have to splint if they have abd/chest incision
-may need to get pain under control first before they can use this
