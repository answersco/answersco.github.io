---
title: "The inferior portion of the SI joint is a ____________ joint; the superior portion is a ___________ joint.   Therefore, OA affects this part of the sacroiliac joint"
---
inferior portion of the sacroiliac joint: synovial

superior portion: dyndesmotic 

Hence, osteoarthritis only affects the INFERIOR part of the sacroiliac joint (OA only affects SYNOVIAL joints)
