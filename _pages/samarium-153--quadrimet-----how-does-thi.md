---
title: "Samarium 153 (quadrimet): - how does this work?  - how does it decay? - side effects? - primary method of excretion?"
---
- complexes with hydroxyapatite
- beta decayer (28% of decay is gamma, so can do imaging)
- transient bone marrow suppression (worse than Sr89, but recovers faster)
- excreted by kidneys
