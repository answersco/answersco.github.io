---
title: "Tuberculosis"
---
Primary - consolidation any lobe
Secondary - cavitation in upper lobe because higher O2 content

-M. tuberculosis: causes the most infection worldwide
-ancient human disease
-important world wide
-morbidity (how many people who became ill): 8-10 million/year
-mortality (how many people died): up to 3 million/year
-prevalence slowly declining in USA (10,521 new tuberculosis cases were reported in the U.S. in 2011)
-most people with natural resistance so bacteria cannot thrive in their system
-bacterium may be in person but disease won't develop

bacterial infection
