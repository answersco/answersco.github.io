---
title: "Spinal - intramedullary lesion"
---
Astrocytoma - most common for child, low grade usu, may be cystic, usu no hemorrhage, enhancement - tough to diff with Ependymoma
Ependymoma - m/c in adults, often hemorrhagic, heterogenous T2, enhance, usu with cysts, can have scalloping of vertebral bodies
Hemangioblastoma - rare, a/w VHL, enhance, flow voids, cysts
Demyelinating lesion - usu MS
