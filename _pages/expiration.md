---
title: "EXPIRATION"
---
median arcuate syndrome worsens during inspiration or expiration

1) the diaphragm &amp; intercostal muscles relax causing the thorax to get smaller and increases pressure in the chest
2) lungs push in
3) air is forced out of the lungs

-exhalation

1) Diaphragm, external intercostals relax
2) Thoracic cavity decreases in volume
3) Lungs follow suit, air forced out by elasticity of muscles and lungs and alveolar surface tension
