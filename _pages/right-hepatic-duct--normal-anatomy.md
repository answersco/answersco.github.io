---
title: "Right hepatic duct: normal anatomy"
---
RHD: drains segments 5-8
-Formed by right posterior duct (RPD) and right anterior duct (RAD).
-RAD: drains segments 5 and 8, and has a more vertical course on AP cholangiographic images
-RPD: drains segments 6 and 7, and has a more horizontal course.
-Normally, the RPD passes behind the RAD and joins the RAD on its medial side to form the RHD
