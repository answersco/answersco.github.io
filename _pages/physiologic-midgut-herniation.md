title: "Physiologic midgut herniation?"
---
During first trimester, back in by 12-13 weeks. 270 degrees counterclockwise around the SMA. Can't diagnose omphalocele or gastroschesis before 13 weeks.

This condition is considered normal unless visualized AFTER the 14th week. At 9-11 weeks this is completely normal.
