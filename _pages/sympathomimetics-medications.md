---
title: "Sympathomimetics medications"
---
1) Albuterol (Proventil)
2) Epinephrine
3) Levalbuterol (Xopenex)
-if pt has one of these breathing treatments will have increase heart rate; expected outcome; have to make sure it trends down when they leave hospital
-if go home on albuterol should notify provider if routinely higher than 110 bpm
-makes pts not feel great; if need to use more frequently than prescribed should go to hospital
