---
title: "What is the falciform ligament sign?"
---
aka: football sign, ovid collection of air

radiopedia: &quot;The football sign is seen in cases of massive pneumoperitoneum, where the abdominal cavity is outlined by gas from a perforated viscus. The median umbilical ligament and falciform ligament are sometimes included in the description of this sign, as representing the sutures.&quot;
