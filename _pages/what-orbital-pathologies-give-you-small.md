---
title: "What orbital pathologies give you small globes?"
---
- PHPV (+increased vitreal density; &quot;martini glass T2 dark against T2 bright globe&quot; = UNILATERAL)

- Coat's (No calcification; retinal telangiectasis causes leaky blood and sub retinal exudate; T1 and T2 bright from exudates)

- Retinopathy of prematurity (increased attenuation due to hemorrhage)
