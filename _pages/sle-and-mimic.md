SLE: Reducible deformity of joints without articular erosions
Ligamentous laxity&#8594; increased risk of patellar dislocation

Jaccoud arthropathy: very similar to SLE in hand
Non-erosive arthropathy with ulnar deviation of 2nd-5th fingers at MCP joint
History of rheumatic fever
