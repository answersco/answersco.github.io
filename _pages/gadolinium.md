---
title: "Gadolinium"
---
what agent is used for attenuation correction in nuclear medicine cardiac scans?

Chelated to DTPA to avoid toxicity
Seven unpaired electrons
Paramagnetic (increases magnetic field)
Shortens T1 (T1 bright)
At high concentrations T2 effects dominate -&gt; shortens T2 (T2 dark) (pseudo-layer in bladder)
Cannot pass intact blood-brain barrier
