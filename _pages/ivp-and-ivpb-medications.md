---
title: "IVP and IVPB medications"
---
1) Remember to check for: 
-Allergies
-Compatibilities
-Dilution
-Rate of administration

2) Prepare a medication label for syringe

3) Needleless syringes only in leur-lok

4) Clean port nearest to the patient 

5) Administer drug at recommended rate with constant flow
-Circulatory collapse or projectile emesis if drug such as morphine or dilaudid is not pushed over five minutes
-If furosemide is pushed too fast, you can cause the pt to go deaf (should be 20 mg/min)

6) Never administer IVP or IVPB meds into TPN or PCA Line!!!
