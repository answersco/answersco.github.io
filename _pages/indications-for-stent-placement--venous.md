---
title: "Indications for stent placement: venous and other"
---
Venous:
-Recanalization of chronic occlusion
-Extrinsic compression by malignancy
-Elastic, fibrotic, or recurrent stenoses

Other:
-Bridging mouth of aneurysm prior to coil placement in aneurysm
-Reinforce stent-graft
-Reinforce true lumen in dissection
