Hamartoma
Most common solid mass in neonate
Benign
Solid large intrarenal mass that involves the renal sinus
can have necrotic center

Most common renal tumor in newborn period &lt;1 yo. Usually benign. Imaging appearance identical to Wilms tumor, but Wilms is usually later 3yo, not infant.

Most common solid renal mass under 1. Key is age &lt;1 (often first month). Even if it looks like Wilms if theyre under 1 year old its this.
