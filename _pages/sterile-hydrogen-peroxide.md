---
title: "Sterile hydrogen peroxide"
---
-H2O2
1) used for mechanical debridement of open wounds
-causes too much trauma with effervescent action (bubbling) for deep tunneling wounds
2) removes blood clots
-not used for fresh bleeding wounds
-removes blood stains
3) no sustained antiseptic action
4) not used in full strength - combined with sterile normal saline, lactated ringers (isotonic solutions)
