---
title: "What is the Budd-Chiari syndrome?"
---
Hepatic venous obstruction. Three types. Type 1 is IVC occlusion +/- hepatic veins. Type 2 is major hepatic veins. Type 3 is small hepatic vein occlusion ie VOD. Enlarged caudate lobe and ascites with absent or reversal of flow in the hepatic veins.
