---
title: "What are methods for QI?"
---
Quality Improvement (QI) methodologies include Plan-Do-Study-Act (PDSA), Design-Measure-Analyze-Improve-Control (DMAIC), Lean, and Six Sigma. 

(NOT) Morbidity and Mortality (M&amp;M), which are conferences are usually peer reviews of mistakes occurring during the care of patients. M&amp;M conferences do not employ a QI methodology per se.
