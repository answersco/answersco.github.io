---
title: "Gallium- 67"
---
cyclotron produced, decay by electron capture, 78 hr half life
emits multiple gamma rays (roughly 90, 190, 290 and 390 keV)
Binds to transferrin in infxn/inflammation/neoplasm
Distribution: colon, bowel, liver, skull, salivary etc- obscures use in abdomen. 
Renal uptake persisting &gt; 24 hr means renal disease.
Diffuse pulmonary uptake is disease
Panda sign in nasopharynx, parotid, lacrimal: sarcoid (sjogrens, lymphoma, etc)

Actually use PET CT instead except for spinal osteomyl
