---
title: "Health history in females"
---
1) pain, tenderness: if pain lasts longer than a week after menstruation
2) hx. of lumps or skin changes: may indicate blockage of lymph nodes
3) discharge from nipples: unless breastfeeding
4) rashes
5) swelling: sudden enlargement of one breast compared to the other can indicate blockage
6) trauma
7) family hx. of breast disease
8) surgery 
9) self-care
10 axillary changes: pain, loss of hair, discoloration or swelling
