title: "What is NF 1?"
---
aka von Recklinghausen Dz. AD Mutation in NF protein results in multiple neurofibromas most often with white matter lesions (UBOs), optic gliomas and plexiform NFs. Also associated with vascular dysplasia (stenosis, moyamoya and aneurysm). Clinically Cafe Au Lait spots and axillary freckling.
