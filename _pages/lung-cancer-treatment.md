---
title: "Lung cancer treatment"
---
-Treatment depends on: 
1) Tumor cell type
2) Stage of disease
3) Overall status

-Treatment for cure or palliation of symptoms:
1) Radiation
2) Surgery
3) Chemotherapy
4) Palliative

Treatment-related complications:
1) Respiratory failure
2) Ventilator dependency
3) Pulmonary fibrosis
4) Pericarditis
5) Myelitis
6) Cor pulmonale
7) Pulmonary toxicity
8) Pneumonitis

**Need maximum psychosocial support and education about disease
