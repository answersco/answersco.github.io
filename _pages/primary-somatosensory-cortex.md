---
title: "Primary somatosensory cortex"
---
-in cerebrum
-receives all sensory input from the body
-neurons that sense feelings in our skin, pain, visual, or auditory stimuli, all send their information to the somatosensory cortex for processing

-located in postcentral gyrus of each parietal lobe
-receives sensory information about joint and muscle position
-receives sensory information about touch and pain and temperature from skin and muscle receptors to determine exact point of origin
