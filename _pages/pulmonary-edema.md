Pulmonary Wedge Pressure &lt;12mmHg normal
&gt;12 = vascular redistribution
&gt;17 = Kerley lines
&gt;20 = effusion on right
&gt;25 = air space opacity, perihilar

o2, elevate torso

furosemide 20-40 mg IV , slow push

consider morphine 1-3 mg IV

transfer to ICU or ED

caused when the left ventricle pumps less blood than the right, the blood pressure backs up into the lungs
