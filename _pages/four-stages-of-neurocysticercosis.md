title: "Four stages of neurocysticercosis?"
---
Viable/Vesicular (Tiny CSF intensity cysts, some with an eccentric dot representing scolex). Colloidal (Increased diffusivity ring enhancing lesions or other non specific findings). Nodular/Granular (Edema decreases as the cyst involutes and the cyst wall thickens. Calcified (Small parenchymal calcifications and some small foci of susceptibility)
