---
title: "Uses for heat application"
---
1) surgical wounds
2) hemorrhoids
-enlarged blood vessels of the rectum
3) episiotomies
-surgical incision in the perineum to help facilitate vaginal delivery
4) phlebitis and IV Infiltration
-both complications of IV therapy
5) arthritis
-heat loosens up joint to allow for more mobility
6) muscle spasms
7) cramps
8) low back pain
