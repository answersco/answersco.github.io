---
title: "What is the finding in pulmonary venoocclusive disease?"
---
PAH (&gt; 25 mm Hg) and NORMAL WEDGE (rules out post-capillary causes like left atrial myxoma, mitral stenosis, and pulmonary vein stenosis)

PVOD = affects post capillary pulmonary vasculature
- Occlusion of pulmonary veins by fibrous tissue in a patchy distribution
