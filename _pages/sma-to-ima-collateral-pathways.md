---
title: "SMA to IMA collateral pathways"
---
Conventional:
SMA &#8594; middle colic &#8594; left branch of middle colic &#8594; Arc of Riolan &#8594; left colic &#8594; IMA
Arc of Riolan: connects the middle colic of the SMA to the left colic of the IMA.

Marginal artery of Drummond: another SMA to IMA connection
Terminal branches of the ileocolic, right colic, and middle colic arteries of SMA &#8594; anastomose with the left colic and sigmoid branches of the IMA
Forms a continuous arterial circle or arcade along the inner border of the colon.
