title: "Ovarian mass that is T1 bright, T2 dark with &quot;shading&quot; and does not sat out with fat sat = ______ - what is the &quot;shading sign&quot;"
---
Endometrioma:
- T1 bright (from blood)
- T2 dark (from iron in the endometrioma)

Shading sign: describes T2 shortening (getting dark) of a lesion that is T1 bright
