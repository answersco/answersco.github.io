---
title: "How is biparietal diameter (BPD) measured, and what anomalies can falsely alter it?"
---
BPD: recorded at level of thalamus
From outermost edge of near skull to inner table of far skull
Affected by shape of fetal skull
Falsely elevated with brachycephaly
Falsely decreased in dolichocephaly.

Head circumference is recorded at the same slice. 
Does not include skin
Less affected by head shape
