---
title: "Family assessment"
---
1) find out family structure - who's living at home, communication and interaction between one another
2) socioeconomic concerns: do they have running water, do they have a lot of steps, their household area
3) religious and cultural background
4) health practices: some families do not place a high concern on health 
5) health habits (e.g. smoking)
6) how does family cope with stress?
7) support system
