title: "Respiratory assessment"
---
breathing capacity and lung volume decrease

1) rate:
-count respirations for 30 seconds; if regular, multiply by 2
-if irregular, &lt;12 or &gt;20, count for 60 seconds
-assess after the pulse while still holding the radial site or auscultating the apical, so client is unaware of assessment
2) rhythm
-regular
-irregular
3) depth
-shallow
-deep
-unlabored
-labored
