---
title: "Separation anxiety"
---
-first seen at a younger age but reoccurs at about age 2
-a lot of distress is seen especially when a babysitter or other caretaker comes to watch child
-depends on how or if the child gets over parents leaving do the attachment styles comes out
-goes away at around age 3
-anxiety can be decreased if parent says they will be back after something they understand such as &quot;I'll be back after Tangled&quot;
