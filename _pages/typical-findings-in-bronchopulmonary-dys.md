---
title: "Typical findings in bronchopulmonary dysplasia:"
---
Premature infants with oxygen dependence at 28 days of age and CXR abnormalities. 
Imaging: ill-defined, coarse, reticular and band-like opacities with intervening small lucencies. Findings are diffuse throughout both lungs, which are typically hyperinflated. Foci of air trapping are often seen on expiratory images.
