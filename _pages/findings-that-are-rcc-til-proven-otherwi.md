---
title: "Findings that are RCC til proven otherwise"
---
Enhancement (&gt; 15 HU)
Enhancement &lt; 10 HU is considered within the technical limits of the study, and not considered true enhancement &#8594; pseudoenhancement.

Calcifications in a fatty mass.
Fat-containing lesion in the liver COULD be a RCC metastasis.
RCC with macroscopic fat almost always has some calcification. If not, it is likely an AML.
