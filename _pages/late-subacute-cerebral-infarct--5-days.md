---
title: "Late subacute cerebral infarct (5 days - 2 weeks)"
---
Resolution of vasogenic edema and reduction in mass effect
Gyriform enhancement &#8594; 6 days-6 weeks after infarct.
&quot;2-2-2&quot; rule&#8594; enhancement may begin at 2 days, peak at 2 weeks, and disappear by 2 months.
DWI may remain bright, while ADC will either return to normal or show increased diffusivity.
