title: "Scleroderma on UGI compared to amyloidosis and GVHD"
---
Scleroderma of small-bowel
Fluoroscopy demonstrates bowel dilatation with narrow separation of the valvulae conniventes and normal fold thickness&#8594; &quot;hidebound&quot; pattern, associated with scleroderma, and, to a lesser degree, sprue
GVHD: &quot;ribbon&quot; appearance of SB with multi-segment areas of featureless/atrophic loops.
Amyloidosis: may cause diffuse nodular thickening of SB folds
