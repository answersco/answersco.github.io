---
title: "Right arch variants (2 most common)"
---
Right arch with aberrant left subclavian:
- last branch is the left aberrant subclavian artery
- vascular ring because of the ligament arteriosum on the left completes it.

Right arch with mirror branching:
- A/w CHD 
--most common: TOF and Truncus
-- 90% will have TOF
-- 6% will have Truncus
 
Truncus ==&gt; 33% have mirror right arch (TOF 25%)
