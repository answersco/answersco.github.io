---
title: "Physical assessment of abdomen"
---
1) inspection: observe contour, any masses, scars, or distention (full of air or fluid)
2) auscultation: listen for bowel sounds in all quadrants
-note frequency and character, audible clicks, and flatus.
-describe bowel sounds as hypoactive, hyperactive, absent or infrequent.
3) percussion and palpations: performed by advanced practice professionals
4) good assessment questions: any N/V, have you passed gas yet, have you had a BM, when was last BM, any recent changes to stool (size, shape, color, consistency), any constipation or diarrhea
