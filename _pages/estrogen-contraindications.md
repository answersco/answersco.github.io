---
title: "Estrogen contraindications"
---
1) allergies to estrogen
2) idiopathic vaginal bleeding, breast cancer, or estrogen-dependent cancer: cause exacerbation of any of these
3) thromboembolic disorders: a history of a CVA or a smoker may see an increased risk of a thrombus developing
4) hepatic dysfunction: have an affect on the excretion of the medication
