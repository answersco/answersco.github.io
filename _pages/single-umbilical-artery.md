---
title: "Single umbilical artery"
---
Associated with cardiovascular anomalies, trisomy 13, 18

Associated with fetal anomolies (usually CV) in up to 50% of fetuses.

This is also called a 2 vessel cord and it is absence of one of the umbilical arteries

US, 2 ways to show this:
- ONE vessel on doppler US running lateral to the bladder (image)
- Showing the cord in cross section with only TWO vessels
