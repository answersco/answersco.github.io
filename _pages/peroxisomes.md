---
title: "Peroxisomes"
---
-sacs with outer membranes studded with several types of enzymes
-break down lipids
-synthesize bile acids used in fat digestion
-detoxify compounds from free radical damage
-abundant in liver and kidney cells 
-adrenoleukodystrophy - 1992 film: Lorenzo's Oil, X-linked, damage from buildup of free radicals -- he got better from a special kind of oil - grapeseed oil
