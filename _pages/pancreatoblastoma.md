---
title: "Pancreatoblastoma"
---
Best diagnostic clue
Large, heterogeneous mass of pancreas in child
Location
40-50% arise in pancreatic head
13-25% arise in pancreatic body
13-37% arise in pancreatic tail
Size
Mean: 10.6 cm (range: 1.5-20 cm)
Morphology
Well-circumscribed, large mass of pancreas
May be difficult to distinguish organ of origin
