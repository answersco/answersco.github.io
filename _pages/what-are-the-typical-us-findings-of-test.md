title: "What are the typical US findings of testicular tumors?"
---
All masses have nonspecific appearance and are surgical lesions! Most will show vascularity.
DDx should include the epidermoid cyst which is avascular, spherical, with lamellated onion skin appearnce which can be by enucleation not orchiectomy. 
Ddx also hematoma, segmental infarct, focal orchitis, adrenal rests, rete testes.
