Tarsal tunnel syndrome: pain in distribution of tibial nerve (1st 3 toes)
Compression of tibial nerve as it passes through the tarsal tunnel (behind the medial malleolus).
Usually unilateral (unlike carpal tunnel, which is usually bilateral)
