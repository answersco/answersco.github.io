---
title: "Nuc Med administration event?"
---
(a) a total dosage that deviates from the prescribed dosage by more than 20%;
(b) administration of the wrong radiopharmaceutical
(c) employing the wrong route of administration; or
(d) administration to the wrong patient or research subject.
 effective dose equivalent exceeding 50 mSv or a dose equivalent to an organ or the skin exceeding 500 mSv must be incurred
