title: "FACTS"
---
-Forcing blood through tiny or damaged catheter may cause lysis of cells
--use 20g to give transfusion; may be 22g if absolutely necessary but smaller gauge = increased risk of damage to the blood cells

-If patient needs medication or solution administered while blood component is being administered, a 2nd IV site needed.
--cannot piggyback other meds into the blood - need separate IV line and site

-Blood &amp; blood products must include a standard blood filter.

-Maximum time for use of a blood filter is 4 hours.

**<b>Absolutely no meds or solutions other than 0.9% sodium chloride shall be added to blood or blood components!</b>**
