---
title: "Patella alta and baja and causes"
---
Patella baja: quadriceps tendon tear&#8594; unopposed pull from patellar tendon&#8594; low patella (baja = below)

Patella alta: patellar tendon tear&#8594; unopposed quadriceps pull&#8594; high patella (alta = above)
Classic association with patellar tendon tear = SLE (also elderly, trauma, athletics, RA)
Bilateral patellar rupture = buzzword for chronic steroids
