---
title: "Isotonic solutions"
---
-Osmolality close to that of the extra-cellular fluid (ECF), and do not cause cells to shrink or swell

Types: 
1) Normal Saline (0.9% NaCl) - only one used for rapid infusions
2) 5% dextrose in water (D5W)
3) Lactated Ringers

(0.9% NS, Lactated Ringers) homeostasis achieved equal solute. 
-expands ECF (maintenance fluid)
