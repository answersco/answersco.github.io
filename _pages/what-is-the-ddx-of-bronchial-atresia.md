title: "What is the ddx of bronchial atresia?"
---
Congenital: Congenital lobar emphysema (CLE), congenital pulmonary airway malformation (CPAM), bronchogenic cyst, pulmonary sequestration, pulmonary hypoplasia, pulmonary artery agenesis, scimitar syndrome

Obstructive:
Allergic bronchopulmonary aspergillosis (ABPA), endobronchial tumor
