---
title: "What are the locations of ependymomas and association with patient age?"
---
2/3 are posterior fossa (Floor of 4th ventricle, foramina of Luschka, posterior cerebral aqueduct, lateral medullary velum) and are more common in kids, mean age 6 yo

1/3 supratentorial (often located in parenchyma with cystic appearance) and are more common in adults 18-24 yo
