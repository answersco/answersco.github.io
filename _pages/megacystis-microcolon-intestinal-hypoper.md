---
title: "Megacystis microcolon intestinal hypoperistalsis syndrome"
---
Rare, loss of smooth muscle function of bowel and bladder, aperistalsis, microcolon, distended bladder. Usually fatal.

Megacystis microcolon intestinal hypoperistalsis syndrome:
Rare congenital loss of bladder and bowel smooth muscle function &#8594; causes absent intestinal peristalsis, microcolon, and distended non-obstructed urinary bladder. Usually fatal.
