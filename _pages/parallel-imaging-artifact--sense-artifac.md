---
title: "Parallel Imaging Artifact (SENSE Artifact)"
---
-Occurs when the FOV is to small for the parallel imaging factor selected.( AKA SENSE Artifact)
-Results in mis-representation of patien'ts tissue
-Typically in the central portions of the image
-Typically occurs in the PHASE ENCODING DIRECTION

can be reduced by..
-Increasing FOV or
-Reducing parallel Imaging Factor

Parallel Imaging artifact can be distinguised from Wrap around artifact (aliasing) by their location.
-Wrap around starts at the edges of the FOV and works its way in
-Parallel appears at the Center of the FOV
