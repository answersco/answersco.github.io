---
title: "What are risks and complications for infant RDS (surfactant deficiency disease)?"
---
Risk Factors: Prematurity (duh) and MATERNAL DIABETES!

Prolonged mech ventilation leading to chronic lung disease and pulm interstitial pneumonia

Primary potency of the PDA (due to poor oxygenation and elevated pulmonary pressures)

Pulmonary and cerebral hemorrhage
