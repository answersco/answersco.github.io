---
title: "What is the most likely dx for a non enhancing nodule of the lateral wall of the ventricle (or 4th ventricle obex) in an older adult?"
---
A subependymoma is a rare, benign, well-differentiated intraventricular ependymal tumor, often found incidentally in an older patient in the frontal horn of the lateral ventricle or 4th ventricular obex. SEGA would be enhancing assoc with TS and other tubers.
