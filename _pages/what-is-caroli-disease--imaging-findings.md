title: "What is Caroli disease? Imaging findings? Assoc? ddx?"
---
Nonobstructive communicating cavernous ectasia of intrahepatic ducts. &quot;Central dot&quot; sign on CECT: Strong enhancing, tiny dots completely surrounded by dilated bile ducts. Assoc 100X increased risk of cholangiocarcinoma. Ddx PSC, choledochal cysts Type 4a, hepatic abscess, recurrant pyogenic cholangitis, and polycystic disease.
