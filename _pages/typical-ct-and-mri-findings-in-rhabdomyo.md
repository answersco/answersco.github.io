---
title: "Typical CT and MRI findings in rhabdomyosarcoma:"
---
CT: Invasive soft tissue mass with variable contrast enhancement
Osseous erosion common but not seen in all cases
MRI: Isointense T1, hyperintense T2 relative to muscle
Variable contrast enhancement
Intracranial extension in parameningeal RMSa
Meningeal thickening and enhancement
