---
title: "Testosterone"
---
-responsible for many sexual and metabolic effects such as growth of the male, deeper voice (from thickening of the vocal cords), hair growth
-puberty and physical changes
-androgens-maintain changes caused by testosterone
-castration-removal of the testes

-located: testes, muscles, and other cells involved in male sex characteristics
-function: develop male characteristics, male sex drive, and regulate sperm production
