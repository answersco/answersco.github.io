---
title: "What are the 4 MRI zones? which ones are restricted?"
---
Zone 1: outside the MR building

Zone 2: waiting room/dressing room/
- screen patients and control access to Zones 3 and 4

Zone 3: control room where the MR Tech is
- should be a lock on the door (between zones 2 and 3)

Zone 4: actual MRI scanner room 
- room with the magnet

**<b> Zones 3 and 4 are restricted </b>**
