---
title: "Sidestepping subject"
---
-This eases nurse's own discomfort. It avoids exploring topic identified by client.

EX.
Client: &quot;I'm having a hard time with my family.&quot;
Nurse: &quot;Do you have any grandchildren?&quot;
Patient: &quot;I want to die.&quot;
Nurse: &quot;Did your doctor come to visit you?&quot;
