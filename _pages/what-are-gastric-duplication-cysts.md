---
title: "What are gastric duplication cysts?"
---
Rare, &lt;7% of foregut dup cysts. located on the greater curve. Female 2x &gt;male.
Sx: Abd pain, palpable masses, or sx of upper GI obstruction. 
If lined with gastric mucosa, Ectopic pancreatic tissue is found in 37% of gastric duplication cysts and can be c/b pancreatitis. 
Tx: surgical excision.
