---
title: "Triglyceride"
---
-made up of glycerol linked to three fatty acids
-each bond between a glycerol and fatty acid is formed by dehydration synthesis
-also called oils because liquid at room temperature
-function: energy storage, thermal insulation, filling space, binding organs together and cushioning them
