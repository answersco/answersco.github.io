---
title: "Winslow pathway"
---
Collateral pathway seen in the setting of aorto-iliac occlusive disease.
Subclavian arteries &#8594; internal thoracic (mammary) arteries &#8594; superior epigastric arteries &#8594; inferior epigastric arteries &#8594; external iliac arteries.

Collaterals in aortoiliac occlusive disease. Subclavian artery -&gt; internal mammary -&gt; superior epigastric -&gt; inferior epigastric -&gt; external iliac
