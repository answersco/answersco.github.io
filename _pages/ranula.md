title: "Ranula"
---
Ranula&#8594; mucous retention cyst arising from sublingual gland (thus arise from sublingual space).
Plunging ranula&#8594; extends from the sublingual space into the submandibular space by protruding posteriorly over the free edge of the mylohyoid or extending directly between a defect in the mylohyoid.
Dermoid cyst may also present below the mandible, but is typically midline, rather than eccentric location of plunging ranula.

Mucocele located in the floor of the mouth; most arise from sublingual gland (this is going to be lateral)
&quot;Plunging&quot; once it's under the mylohoid muscle
