---
title: "PE diagnostics"
---
1) Early recognition (Death can occur within 1 hour of onset of symptoms)

2) Clinical assessment of risk factors

3) Chest x-ray

4) ECG

5) Pulse ox

6) ABGs

7) V/Q scan - ventilation perfusion test

8) Spiral CT

9) D-dimer assay -- blood test
-If positive, can be indicative of PE but not all inclusive
-Just another clue to try and figure out diagnosis
