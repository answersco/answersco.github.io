---
title: "What entities are associated with higher risk of breast cancer? What entities are NOT?"
---
1.5-2x Risk: Usual ductal hyperplasia, fibroadenoma, sclerosing adenosis, papillomatosis, and radial scar

3.5-5x Risk: ADH and ALH

7-11x Risk: LCIS


NOT ASSOCIATED! PASH, Fibrocystic breast disease, mastitis, and squamous and apocrine metaplasia
