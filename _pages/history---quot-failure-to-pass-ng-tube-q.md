Choanal atresia: &quot;failure to pass NG tube,&quot; &quot;respiratory distress while feeding&quot;
Unilateral (&#8532;) or bilateral posterior nasal narrowing with thickening of the vomer
Associations: CHARGE, Crouzon's, DiGeorge, Treacher Collins, Fetal Alcohol Syndrome
Also association with piriform aperture stenosis, which is highly-associated with hypothalamic pituitary adrenal axis dysfunction.
