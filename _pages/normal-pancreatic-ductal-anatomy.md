---
title: "Normal pancreatic ductal anatomy:"
---
Pancreatic ductal anatomy:
Main pancreatic duct drains to the major papilla (ampulla of Vater) through the duct of Wirsung
Duct of Santorini drains to the minor papilla
Santorini is Superior and Small (minor)
Constant anatomy (always)
CBD always drains to major papilla, where it meets duct of Wirsung
Main pancreatic duct always drains the pancreatic tail
Duct of Santorini always drains to the minor papilla
