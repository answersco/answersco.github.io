---
title: "Well-counter QC"
---
The efficiency and energy resolution of the well counter should be evaluated at installation, annually, and after repair.
Background reading and daily constancy (sensitivity) test should also be performed, and inter-day constancy readings should agree to within + 5%
Suspected malfunction of a well-counter (or other counting system) &#8594; can be evaluated using a Chi-Squared Test &#8594; determines if the counting system is behaving as predicted by statistical theory.
