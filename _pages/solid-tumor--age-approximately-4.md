WILMS TUMOR
Most common solid tumor of childhood (does NOT happen in newborn)
Variants include:
1. Clear cell - likes to go to bones
2. Rhabdoid - associated with rhabdoid brain tumors 

Wilm's never:
1. NEVER BIOPSY: you can seed the tract which will upstage
2. NEVER occurs before 2 months of age
