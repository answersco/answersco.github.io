---
title: "Most common type of CHD to have a right-sided aortic arch:"
---
Truncus arteriosus &#8594; single great artery from base of heart supplies the systemic, pulmonary, and coronary circulations, and usually overrides a VSD
Most common type of CHD to have a right-sided arch (21-36% of cases)
Classic appearance is cardiomegaly with narrow mediastinum and pulmonary edema.
