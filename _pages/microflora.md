---
title: "Microflora"
---
-collection of microorganisms growing in or on your body
-human body surfaces habitat for many microbes like mouth, skin, intestines, etc.
-microbes either mutualists or commensals
-all may be mutualists indirectly by out competing pathogens for space and nutrients
-normally not parasites but instances where they can become pathogens
-most have preferred residence on the human body
-aerobes or anaerobes depending on where they will reside on your body
