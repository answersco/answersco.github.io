---
title: "Ventral respiratory group"
---
-(I)nspiratory and (E)xpiratory neurons take turns, inhibit each other
-I neurons stimulate diaphragm, intercostals
-About 2 seconds per stim in eupnea, E neurons inhibit I neurons, relaxation of muscles/exhalation
-Rhythmic depolarization, about 12 breaths/min
