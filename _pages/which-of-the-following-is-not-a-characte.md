---
title: "Which of the following is not a characteristic of atrial myxoma?  A) Usually located in left atrium B) Take up gadolinium contrast media C) Lower signal than blood pool on gradient echo D) Always same signal as myocardium on spin echo E) Attached to myocardium"
---
D. Always same signal as myocardium on spin echo (black blood). The signal of a myxoma is variable; hemorrhage can make it hyperintense while calcification makes it hypointense. The other items are all characteristics of atrial myxomas.
