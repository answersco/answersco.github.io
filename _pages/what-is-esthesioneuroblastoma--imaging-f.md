---
title: "What is esthesioneuroblastoma? Imaging findings? Epidemiomology. Tx?"
---
Malignant neuroectodermal tumor arising from olfactory mucosa in superior nasal cavity. Dumbell shaped with peripheral tumor cysts at intracranial tumor-brain margin is highly suggestive of diagnosis of ENB. Bimodal distribution in 2nd &amp; 6th decades. Tx is surgery and RT.
