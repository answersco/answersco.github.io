---
title: "Thoracentesis nursing interventions"
---
Monitor for:
1) Increasing resp. rate

2) Asymmetry in respiratory movement

3) Dyspnea

4) Diminished breath sounds

5) Anxiety or restlessness

6) Tightness in chest

7) Uncontrollable cough

8) Blood-tinged, frothy mucus

9) Rapid pulse

10) Signs of hypoxemia
