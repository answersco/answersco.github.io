---
title: "What findings are considered suspicious for pregnancy failure?"
---
No embryo &gt; 6 weeks after LMP
MSD of 16-24 mm and no embryo
No embryo with heartbeat 13 days after a scan that showed a gestational sac without a yolk sac.
No embryo with heartbeat 10 days after a scan showed a gestational sac and yolk sac.
