---
title: "What does In-111 Pentetreotide bind to?"
---
Binds to 2 of 5 Somatostatin receptors (neuroendocrine tumors)

No bones, HOT spleen and Kidneys (but also liver, GB, GI)

Critical organ = Spleen (&quot;Sploctreotide&quot;)

Classically: Carcinoid (&quot;Carcinoctreotide&quot;)
Gastrinoma, Insulinoma
Paraganglioma (e.g EXTRA-adrenal Pheo)
Medullary thyroid
Merkel cell tumor
Lymphoma, Small cell lung
* meningiomas
