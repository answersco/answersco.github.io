---
title: "TB risk factors"
---
1) Close contact with infected person 
-people who live with an infected person also have to be treated (prophylaxis)

2) Immunocompromised

3) Substance abuse 
-a lot of drug abusers are homeless and many homeless live near each other

4) Pre-existing medical conditions

5) Immigration from countries with high TB rates

6) Overcrowded population

7) Patient not identified or isolated adequately
-if you, as a nurse, if suspicious then put them in droplet isolation

8) Inadequate therapy (med coverage)

9) Inadequate ventilation of room

10) Isolation room not negative pressure air flow

11) Patient not masked when out of room (needs duck bill mask)
