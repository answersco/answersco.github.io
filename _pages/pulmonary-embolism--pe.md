---
title: "Pulmonary embolism (PE)"
---
-Obstruction of the pulmonary artery or one of its branches by a clot from the venous system 

-Common disorder

-Associated with trauma, surgery, pregnancy, heart failure, &gt;50 years, hypercoagulable states, &amp; prolonged immobility

-Over 60,000-100,000 deaths annually from PE - an emergency situation

-Risk factors
1) recent surgery
2) recent fracture
3) traveling for long periods of time
4) immobile
