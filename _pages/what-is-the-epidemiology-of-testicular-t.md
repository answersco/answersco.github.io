---
title: "What is the epidemiology of testicular tumors? What is the ethnic breakdown? Risk factors?"
---
Epidemiology: Most common cancer in men aged 15-34, and 1% of all male cancers. Seminoma: Avg age = 40.5yo and MGCT: Avg age = 30yo. Pure teratoma and yolk sac tumor more common in pediatric population.

Risk factors: Prior testicular ca, + FH, Infertility, cryptorchidism, Intersex.

Ethnicity: &gt; 90% Caucasians, only 3% in AA.
