---
title: "Skeletal dysplasia with complete or partial absence of the clavicles:"
---
Cleidocranial dysostosis &#8594; characterized primarily by abnormalities of the clavicles
Patients are of normal intelligence, but tend to be short
Key finding is complete or partial absence of the clavicles.
Strongly associated with wormian bones in the skull: also seen in hypothyroidism, OI, healing rickets, and Down syndrome.
Delayed ossification of the skull (neonatal)
Widened symphysis pubis (later in life).
