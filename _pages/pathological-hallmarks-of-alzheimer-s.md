---
title: "Pathological hallmarks of alzheimer's"
---
-extracellular deposition of B-amyloid (AB) plaques

-intraneuronal neurofibrillary tangles

-loss of cortical cholinergic neurones in AD accounts for memory impairment

-amyloid proteins are excessively produced and are accumulated which triggers immune response, tau protein comes and attack these proteins causes &quot;tangles&quot;
