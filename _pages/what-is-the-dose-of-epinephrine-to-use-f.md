---
title: "What is the dose of epinephrine to use for contrast reaction/bronchospasm?"
---
Reactions to iodinated contrast for which epinephrine treatment should be
considered include severe hives, diffuse erythema with hypotension, moderate or severe
bronchospasm, all forms of laryngeal edema, persistent hypotension, and those who are
unresponsive and pulseless. 

1 to 3 mL of 1/10,000 dilution IV 

0.3 mL of 1/1,000 dilution IM
