title: "Hypothalamus"
---
-neural and vascular control
-thermostat of the body

-tells pituitary gland to release hormones
-does this b/c it has releasing hormones and inhibiting hormones

-body regulation
-feel the behavior and affects behavior/reaction to those emotions
-boss of autonomic system

-in diencephalon
-responsible for the production of many of the body's essential hormones --&gt; pituitary gland

-associated with changes in emotional reactivity
-working memory
