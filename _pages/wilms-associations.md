---
title: "Wilms associations:"
---
Wilms: most common solid tumor of childhood, average age of 3.

Overgrowth:
Beckwith-Wiedemann: Macroglossia, omphalocele, hemihypertophy, cardiac, big organs, hepatoblastoma
Sotos: macrocephaly, MR, facial anomalies.

Non-overgrowth:
WAGR: Wilms, aniridia, genital, growth retardation
Drash: Wilms, pseudohermaphroditism, progressive glomerulonephritis.

Hemihypertrophy, WAGR, hepatoblastoma
