title: "Osteogenesis imperfecta (OI)"
---
Osteogenesis imperfecta (OI) &#8594; type II is lethal
Multiple fractures in ribs (causing accordion ribs), vertebral bodies, and long bones
Bowed long bones
Osteopenia
Wormian bones in skull

Differential of multiple fractures:
Osteogenesis imperfecta&#8594; only entity to cause antenatal fractures
Rickets &#8594; wouldn't expect antenatal injury, though fractures and osteopenia may be present.
Child abuse
