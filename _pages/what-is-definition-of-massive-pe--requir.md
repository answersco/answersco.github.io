---
title: "What is definition of massive PE (requiring pulmonary angiography w/ thrombolysis, aspiration, mechanical clot fragmentation, and/or stent placement)?"
---
Acute PE with sustained hypotension (SBP &lt;90 mm Hg for at least 15 minutes or requiring inotropic support, not due to a cause other than PE, such as arrhythmia, hypovolemia, sepsis, or LV dysfunction), pulselessness, or persistent profound bradycardia
