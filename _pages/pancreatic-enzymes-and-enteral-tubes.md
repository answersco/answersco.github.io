title: "Pancreatic enzymes and enteral tubes"
---
-pt's on enteral feeding through gastrically or jejunally placed tubes may require pancreatic enzyme replacement to ensure proper pH and alkaline enviroment
-can be given with nectar like juices to thicken things up and make less acidic
-need administered with lots of water
-to get alkaline environment, you may need to administer bicarbonate at the same time
