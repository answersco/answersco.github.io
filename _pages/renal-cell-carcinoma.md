---
title: "Renal Cell Carcinoma"
---
T4: Extends beyond Gerotas fascia

T3c: IVC above the diaphragm

T3b: IVC below the diaphragm

T3a: Renal vein

(T3 also includes the perirenal fat and the adrenal gland)

T2: Confined to the kidney

Things that make you think RCC until proven otherwise
1. ENHANCEMENT &gt; 15 HU
2. Calcifications in a fatty mass (REMEMBER clear cell renal cancer has FAT which almost always calcifies) 
- The calc point of fat is in opposition to AML where their fat typically DOES NOT calcify
