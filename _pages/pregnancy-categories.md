title: "Pregnancy categories"
---
-usually found out when a mother takes the medication and it is not know what it does during pregnancy
A: no risk to the fetus in any trimester
B: no adverse effect demonstrated in animals; no human studies
C: studies with animals have shown adverse reactions; no human studies; given only after risks to the fetus have been considered
D: definite fetal risk; given only if life threatening condition exists
X: absolute fetal abnormality; not to be used anytime in pregnancy
