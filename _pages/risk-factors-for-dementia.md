---
title: "Risk factors for dementia"
---
1) Advanced age

2) Mild cognitive impairment

3) Cardiovascular disease

4) Genetics: 
-Family history of dementia
-Parkinson's disease
-Cardiovascular disease
-Stroke
-Presence of ApoE4 allele on chromosome 19 (found in early-onset Alztheimer's)

5) Environment
-Head injury / alcohol abuse

6) Immunological
-Viruses
-Infections

*<b>Need huge physical and entire history to determine the type of dementia bc there are so many different symptoms with different kinds</b>*
