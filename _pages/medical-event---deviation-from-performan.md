---
title: "Medical event = deviation from performance of a diagnostic or therapeutic procedure resulting in excessive radiation exposure, reportable to NRC or agreement state authorities. Criteria:"
---
Total dosage that deviates from prescribed dose by &gt; 20%
Wrong patient, radiopharmaceutical, or route.
Must involve effective dose &gt; 50 mSv to an organ or skin &gt; 500 mSv
Report via phone to NRC/Agreement State within 1 day, written report to NRC and referring physician within 15 days.
Notify referring physician and patient within 24 hours unless determined otherwise by referring physician.
