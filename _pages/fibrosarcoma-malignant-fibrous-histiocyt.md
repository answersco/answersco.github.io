---
title: "Fibrosarcoma/Malignant fibrous histiocytoma (MFH)"
---
Fibrosarcoma: primary or secondary (Paget's, infarct, etc.)
Lytic lesion that DOES NOT produce osteoid or chondroid matrix
Almost always lytic (permeative or moth-eaten)
Not T2-bright (which most tumors are)

MFH: more common than fibrosarcoma &#8594; look the same
Sarcomatous transformation of bone infarct could be MFH
