---
title: "What is normal portosystemic pressure gradient, and what gradient defines portal HTN (and other findings)"
---
Normal different between portal vein and IVC = 3-6 mmHg
Portal HTN: defined as pressure in portal vein &gt; 10 mmHg or PSG &gt; 6 mmHg

Enlarged portal vein (&gt;1.3-1.5 cm), enlarged splenic vein (&gt;1.2 cm), splenomegaly, ascites, collaterals, reversed flow in portal vein.
