---
title: "What are the 4 WHO astrocytomas?"
---
- WHO I: Pilocytic astrocytoma

- WHO II: Diffuse Astrocytoma = T2 hyperintense with heterogeneous enhancement

- WHO III: Anaplastic Astrocytoma: hypointense to white matter, variable enhancement 

- WHO IV: GBM (dont forget Turcot syndrome with Gi polyps and GBM)
