---
title: "Wegener granulomatosis and sarcoidosis of trachea:"
---
Wegener granulomatosis&#8594; 20% with large airway involvement
Most commonly manifests as subglottic tracheal stenosis with circumferential mucosal thickening.
No sparing of posterior membranous trachea.

Sarcoidosis&#8594; rare, usually in advanced disease
Variable appearance, ranging from smooth stenosis to nodular or mass-like appearance.
No sparing of posterior membranous trachea.
