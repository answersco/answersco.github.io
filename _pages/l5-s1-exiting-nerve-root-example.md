---
title: "L5-S1 exiting nerve root example:"
---
In the Thoracic and Lumbar Spines, the FIRST NUMBER corresponds to the EXITING nerve at that level (laterally via neuroforamina)

- Therefore at L5-S1, The L5 root exits via the neuroforamen and is compressed by a foraminal disc protrusion

- At L5-S1, the S1 roots are descending, and will be smashed by a paracentral or central disc protrusion
