---
title: "How does esophageal candidiasis present?"
---
HIV/Transplant/immunocompromised patient or Achalasia/scleroderma (dysmotility patient).

- Discrete plaque-like lesions (longitudinal linear or irregular filling defects)
- Nodularity/granuliarty/focal thickening
- &quot;Shaggy irregular&quot; luminal surface
