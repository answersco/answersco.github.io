title: "NUMBERS: CHF stages?"
---
Stage 1: Wedge 13-18 mm Hg = cephalization, big heart, vascular pedicle big

Stage 2: Wedge 18-25 mm Hg = Kerley lines, interstitial edema/peribronchial cuffing; indistinct central vessels

Stage 3: Wedge &gt; 25 mm Hg = Alveolar edema, airspace opacities and pleural effusion
