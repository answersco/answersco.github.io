---
title: "Which of the following about cardiac thrombi is incorrect? A) Acute thrombi have a higher signal than chronic thrombi on gradient echo imaging B) Chronic thrombi are darker than myocardium on gradient echo imaging C) Thrombi are brighter than the surrounding blood pool with gradient echo imaging D) Cardiac thrombi do not take up gadolinium contrast media E) Cardiac thrombi are associated with areas of myocardial akinesis"
---
C. Thrombi are actually darker than the surrounding blood pool. All of the other choices are correct.
