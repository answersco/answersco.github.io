---
title: "Enchondromas"
---
These are benign bone lesion that are almost always INTRAMEDULLARY
They are going to be located in tubular bones (fingers) and femur, tib, fib 

Appearance:
- Look for ring and arc calcificaiton
- These are going to have sharp zone of transition
- These are NOT going to break through cortex (if you see this then you need to be thinking of chondrosarcoma)
