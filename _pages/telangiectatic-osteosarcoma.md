Osteolytic destructive sarcoma

Rare, histologic variant of osteosarcoma containing large, blood-filled spaces. 25% present with pathologic fracture. Appears as purely lytic lesion that occasionally obliterates the entire cortex. Differential inludes ABC; MRI shows fluid-fluid levels and extensive surrounding edema.
