---
title: "Pancreatic lipomatosis versus pancreatic agenesis"
---
Pancreatic lipomatosis: most common pathologic condition of the pancreas
Causes: CF (most common cause in kids), hyperlipidemia, Cushing Syndrome, chronic steroid use, HLD, Schwachman-Diamond Syndrome (may also cause lipomatous pseudohypertrophy)
Has a normal duct

Dorsal pancreatic agenesis:
No duct
Predisposes to DM (most beta cells in tail)
Associated with polysplenia
