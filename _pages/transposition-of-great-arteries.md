---
title: "Transposition of Great Arteries"
---
Cyanotic with increased vascularity
-most common CHD with cyanosis in first 24 hrs of life

Complete transposition: D Transposition
 - aorta from RV
 - PA from LV
 - Normal position of atria and ventricles: AV concordance
 - aorta anterior and to the right of the PA

L transposition
 - inversion of ventricles: AV disconcordance
 - aorta anterior and to the left of PA

CXR: Egg on Side; narrow upper mediastinum

TX: Prostaglandins prevent closure of pDA
