---
title: "Hemorrhage"
---
-first sign is low urine output
-increased drainage, hematoma formation
-low BP, Increase HR
-cool, clammy skin, pale
-thready pulse
-dizziness
-increased oxygen demand

-bleeding
-can cause shock (pale, unresponsive, cold, BP drops, pulse goes up, O2 saturation drops, demand for O2 goes up, urine output drops)
-always look at dressing to determine if they're bleeding and how much

excessive bleeding
