---
title: "Tubular ectasia of the rete testis?"
---
Nonpalpable, asymptomatic, cystic dilatation of the tubules at the mediastinum testes caused by epididymal obstruction. May be bilatera. Common in older patients.

Benign obliteration of the efferent ducts (bilateral in older men)
- Cystic dilatation NEXT TO MEDIASTINUM testis
