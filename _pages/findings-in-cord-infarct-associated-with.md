---
title: "Findings in cord infarct associated with aortic stent graft"
---
Cord Infarct: most commonly anterior spinal artery involvement (e.g. with stent-graft or bronchial artery embolization)
Anterior spinal artery involvement&#8594; central cord/anterior horn cell high T2 signal (gray matter more vulnerable)
&quot;Owl's eye&quot; appearance of anterior cord infarct
Usually long-segment, &gt; 2 segments.
