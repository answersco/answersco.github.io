title: "MRI of the cervix: normal cervical T2 zonal anatomy"
---
MRI of the cervix: normal cervical T2 zonal anatomy
Endocervical canal: T2 hyperintense due to mucin
Cervical mucosa: intermediate T2 signal intensity
Inner cervical stroma: very hypointense on T2 (analogous to uterine junctional zone). The superior aspect of the inner cervical stroma is continuous with the junctional zone of the uterus.
