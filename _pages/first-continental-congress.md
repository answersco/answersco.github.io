---
title: "First Continental Congress"
---
Legislative assembly created in 1771 to resist acts of Parliament and defy the king, especially the intolerable acts.

colonies met to discuss their rights and unite as one. Agreed to send petition to the king. Nothing was really accomplished

meeting held in Philadelphia from September 5 to October 26, 1774, in which fifty-six delegates (from every colony except Georgia) adopted a resolution in opposition to the Coercive Acts
