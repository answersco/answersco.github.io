---
title: "Modifications of healthcare in nursing"
---
-During hospitalization - do whatever needs done to the pt to make sure they are receiving proper treatment

-Health promotion and prevention: do not hold anything back but also bc of their disability, have more issues that need taken care of

-Home, community-based and transitional care: resources, equipment, and transitional care are available

-Gerontologic considerations: normal changes with aging, the way to provide education, how to deliver long-term care out of hospital setting
