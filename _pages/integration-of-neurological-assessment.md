---
title: "Integration of neurological assessment"
---
-evaluate cranial nerves while assessing the head and neck
-assess mental and emotional status during the intake interview by watching their speech patterns and how they carry themselves
-focus the neurological assessment: patient health status &amp; patient concerns
-requires time and attention to detail - seeing minute changes in a pt's neurological function can mean a big deal
-do they have problems walking, does the pt have an concerns about their emotional health, balance, numbness, tingling, headaches, loss of function in extremities, etc.
