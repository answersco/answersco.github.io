---
title: "Planes separating the left and right hepatic lobes; segment 2 and 3 from 4; right posterior from anterior; superior from inferior."
---
Left and right lobes: separating from plane passing through IVC and gallbladder fossa along path of middle hepatic vein.

Segments 2 and 3 are separated from 4 by the ligamentum teres.

Right posterior (6 and 7) and anterior (5 and 8): separated by plane of the right hepatic vein

Superior (7, 8, 4a, 2) from inferior (5, 6, 4b, 3): by the planes of the right and left portal branches.
