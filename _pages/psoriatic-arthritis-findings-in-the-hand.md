---
title: "Psoriatic arthritis findings in the hands and feet"
---
Distal predominance. Proliferative erosions. Soft tissue swelling. Periostitis (fuzzy). Ivory phalanx. Pencil in cup. Sausage digit (fusiform soft tissue swelling). FINGER ANKYLOSIS. Mouse ears. Acral osteolysis. IP's affected, can also have &quot;ray pattern&quot; (MCP, PIP, DIP of same finger). Spares 1st MCP. Arthritis mutilans (non-reducible subluxation et al)
