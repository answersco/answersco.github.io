---
title: "Fertility drugs contraindications"
---
1) primary ovarian failure: your body is not able to be stimulated with this condition
2) ovarian cyst: can result in overstimulation and an increase in the size of the cyst
3) uterine bleeding: can be exacerbated
4) thyroid or adrenal dysfunction: can be exacerbated 
5) breast feeding: can cause negative effects on the baby
6) thromboembolic disorders
