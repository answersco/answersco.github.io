---
title: "Neisseria meningitidis"
---
-causes meningococcal meningitis
-the most serious infection of meninges 
-bacterium and/or endotoxin (any gram negative bacteria has this in their cell wall) often invades the bloodstream
-many persons with natural immunity against it and can easily fight it off or do not even come down with the infection
-children/teens/young adults most susceptible
-tends to occur in outbreaks or epidemics -- military personnel, crowded cities, schools
-gets in through the nasal cavity and follows pathway to brain from the tiny holes in back of nasal cavity
