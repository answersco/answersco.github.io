---
title: "HIV Encephalopathy"
---
Diffuse atrophy is most common manifestation. White matter lesions are seen with AIDS dementia complex. Diffuse pattern of increased T2 signal in deep white matter or multiple small punctate white matter lesions.

Manifests as diffuse cerebral atrophy and symmetric T2-prolongation in the periventricular and deep white matter.
Unlike PML, HIV encephalitis spares the subcortical U-fibers, and tends to be symmetric.

rapidly progressing atrophy, b/l symmetric white matter dz
