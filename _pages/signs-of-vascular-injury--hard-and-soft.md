---
title: "Signs of vascular injury: hard and soft"
---
Hard: 
-Active arterial hemorrhage
-Thrill or bruit
-Expanding hematoma
-Extremity ischemia
-Pulse deficit

Soft:
-Adjacent fracture
-Adjacent nerve injury
-Stable hematoma
-Delayed or decreased capillary refill
-History of hypotension or bleeding
-Extensive soft-tissue injury
