---
title: "Ficat stages?"
---
1- Normal radiograph with AVN seen on MRI or bone scan (reduced uptake). Treat with core femoral head drilling. 2- Cystic and sclerotic changes, same treatment as stage 1. 3- Loss of normal spherical shape of the femoral head due to subchondral collapse, may see crescent sign on x ray. 4- Flattening of the femoral head and secondary OA.
