---
title: "What are the imaging characteristics of osteochondroma?"
---
Metaphyseal stalk of marrow and cortex, all continuous with normal underlying bone. Femur (30%) &gt; tibia (20%) &gt; humerus (10-20%). Cartilage cap &lt;1.5cm; if &gt;2.5 cm much higher risk of malignant txs to Chondrosarcoma.
