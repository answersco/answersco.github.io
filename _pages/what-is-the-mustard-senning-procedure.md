---
title: "What is the Mustard/Senning procedure?"
---
Atrial level repair of TGA, an intraatrial baffle created which routes systemic venous blood return to embryologic LV and to PA. Then Pulmonary venous blood return is routed to embryologic RV and to aorta. 

Mustard uses autologous or synthetic pericardium to form baffle; whereasSenning uses native atrial tissue.
