---
title: "Septic emboli"
---
IV drug user, organ transplant, cyanotic heart disease, AIDS, lung AVMs

Classic appearance is numerous small foci of restricted diffusion
May result in abscess, mycotic aneurysm (most commonly distal MCAs)
Favors gray-white interface and basal ganglia
Edema surrounding the tiny abscesses

History: parenchymal bleed in patient with infection.
