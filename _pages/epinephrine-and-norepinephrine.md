---
title: "Epinephrine and norepinephrine"
---
-located: body cells in fight or flight
-function: fight or flight response

-not apart of the CRH--&gt;ACTH--&gt;(-) loop
-release of them driven by sympathetic nervous system
-flight or flight response
-rapid stress response
-increase alertness, metabolism of glucose &amp; fatty acids, increase in heart rate and blood pressure, shuts of digestion
