title: "Medulloblastoma syndromic associations"
---
Medulloblastoma&#8594; associated with Basal Cell Nevus Syndrome (Gorlin's) and Turcots Syndrome
Heterogeneous T1 and T2, homogeneous enhancement, 20% calcification (less than in ependymoma)

Gorlin syndrome: medulloblastoma + thick dural calcifications
Get odontogenic cysts and basal cell skin cancer after XRT
