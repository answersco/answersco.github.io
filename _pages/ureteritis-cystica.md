---
title: "Ureteritis cystica:"
---
Ureteritis cystica: benign response to chronic urinary tract inflammation (e.g. chronic infection or stone disease) &#8594; no malignant potential
Several small subepithelial cysts are found unilaterally in the proximal &#8531; of the ureter and renal pelvis
Imaging: multiple tiny filling defects in the renal pelvis or ureter
Same entity in the bladder is called cystitis cystica: shows multiple rounded-contour filling defects at the base of the bladder
