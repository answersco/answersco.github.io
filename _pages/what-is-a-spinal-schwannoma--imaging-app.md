---
title: "What is a spinal schwannoma? Imaging appearance? Location? Epi? Associ?"
---
Benign WHO grade 1 neural based tumor, intradural intra/extramedullary. An enhancing dumbbell shaped mass through the neural foramen. Cystic spaces common. Thoracic &gt;cervical=lumbar. Most common intradural-extramed tumor. M= F, 30-60yo. Assoc with NF2.
