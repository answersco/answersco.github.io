---
title: "Ross procedure"
---
Performed for diseased aortic valves in children.
Aortic valve is replaced with the patient's pulmonic valve.
PV replaced with a cryopreserved pulmonary valve homograft.

- Performed for diseased aortic valves in children.

- Pulmonary Valve replaced with a cryopreserved pulmonary valve homograft
