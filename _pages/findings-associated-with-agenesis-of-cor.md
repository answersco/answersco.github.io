---
title: "Findings associated with agenesis of corpus collosum?"
---
Absense of cavum septum pellucidum. Colpocephaly. Widlely separated ventricular frontal horns. Parallel configuration of lateral ventricles. Concave medial borders of lateral ventricles (Protrusion of Probst bundles). Midline interhemispheric cyst-superior herniation of the third ventricle.
