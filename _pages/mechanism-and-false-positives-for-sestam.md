---
title: "Mechanism and false-positives for sestamibi parathyroid scan"
---
Dual phase technique: Tc99-sestamibi administered, and both early (10 minutes) and delayed (3 hours) imaging is performed.

Sestamibi imaging depends on mitochondrial density and blood flow

False-positives:
Thyroid nodules
Head and neck cancers
LAD
Brown fat
