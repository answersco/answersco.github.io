---
title: "Insulin syringes"
---
-always has orange cap 
-100 units (1mL)
-low dose 30 (0.30 ml) or 50 units (0.50ml)
-not measured in mL!!
-can only use insulin syringes for insulin
(U-100 most common. Means 100 Units of insulin/1 mL. Must use U-100 syringe with U-100 insulin. All syringes pictured are U-100)
