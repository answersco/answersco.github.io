---
title: "What are the facial features of the holoprosencephaly spectrum? How often are they seen?"
---
Cyclopia, Ethmocephaly, Cebocephaly, single central incisor, Hypotelorism, cleft lip and palate. 80% of the holoprosencephalic babies have facial abnormalities. However, if the face is normal, that does not rule-out a holoprosencephalic syndrome.
