-Bolus leaves mouth --&gt; Oropharynx --&gt; Laryngopharynx --&gt; esophagus
-deglutition occurs:
-bolus pushed posteriorly by tongue (voluntary)
-respiratory pathway closes (reflex)
-uvula/soft palate close off airways, respiration ceases
-epiglottis closes over larynx

common pathway for food/air
