---
title: "What is the criterion on imaging for atlantoaxial subluxation"
---
Atlanto-axial subluxation is present IF:

the ADI (atlanto-dental interval) is &gt; 2.5 mm in adults or &gt; 5 mm in children

**the atlanto-dental interval is the distance between the anterior aspect of the dens and the posterior aspect of the anterior ring of C1, as measured @ the inferior aspect of the C1-C2 articulation
