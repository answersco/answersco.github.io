---
title: "Prior to brain SPECT imaging for dementia, what do you instruct the patient to do?"
---
1) Place the patient in a quiet, dimly lit room 
2) Instruct the patient to keep eyes open 
3) Ensure that the patient is seated or reclining comfortably 
4) Place intravenous access at least 10 min before injection to permit accommodation 
5) Instruct the patient not to speak or read 
6) Have no interaction with the patient before, during, or for 5 min after injection
