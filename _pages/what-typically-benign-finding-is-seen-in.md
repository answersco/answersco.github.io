---
title: "What typically benign finding is seen in MFH?"
---
No periosteal reaction.
Pleomorphic sarcoma that predominantly occurs in soft-tissues, but may occur primarily in bones, peaking in 4th decade.
Most are in metaphysis of long tubular bones.
Can be spontaneous or occur in setting of XRT, surgery, trauma, or pre-existing benign bone lesions.
Usually no sclerotic margin, common soft tissue mass.
