---
title: "What QI tool is used to generate a list of ideas about an issue or process, and what are the major steps?"
---
Brainstorming: an organized process for generating a list of ideas about an object or process. Sessions are used to identify all issues; understand and clarify the process; generate potential solutions or action plans, and determine collection issues.
Major steps:
Explain the subject of the session to the team.
Set a time limit.
Identify a note-taker to list the ideas on a flip chart
Ask participants to call out one idea at a time
Build and expand on ideas, and encourage creativity.
