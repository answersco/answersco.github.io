---
title: "Popliteal vein: anatomy and tributaries"
---
Popliteal vein: formed from confluence of tibial veins in the upper third of the calf.
-Smaller tributaries: gastrocnemius, soleal, and sural veins, as well as small saphenous vein (SSV).
-In relation to the anterior surface of the leg, the PV lies posterior the popliteal artery.
-In relation to the skin of the pop fossa (posterior surface of the knee joint), it is more superficial
-PV is duplicated or complex in 35%
