---
title: "Essential elements of LeFort fractures and ZMC"
---
All involve pterygoid processes
LeFort 1 (floating palate): Lateral nasal aperture
LeFort 2 (pyramidal): Lateral orbital rim and orbital floor
LeFort 3 (craniofacial dissociation): Zygomatic arch and lateral orbital rim/wall

ZMC fracture (tripod): most common
Zygoma, inferior orbit, lateral orbit
