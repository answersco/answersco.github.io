---
title: "Plantar fasciitis"
---
Pain worsened by dorsiflexion of the toes
Buzzword = &quot;most severe in the morning&quot;
XR: may show heel spurs
MRI: thickened fascia (&gt; 4 mm) and increased T2 signal, most significant near insertion on calcaneus
Bone scan may show increased tracer in region of calcaneus due to periosteal inflammation
