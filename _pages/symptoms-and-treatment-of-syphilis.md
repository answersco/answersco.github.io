---
title: "Symptoms and treatment of syphilis"
---
Signs and Symptoms
-Primary: chancre on genitals
-Secondary: fever, headache, enlarged lymph nodes, rash
-Tertiary: internal and external gummas, headaches, convulsions, altered mental states

Diagnosis
-darkfield microscopy on active lesions
-serologic testing (antibody detection)

Treatment 
-pre-antibiotic era treatments - mercurials, arsenicals which were poisonous but was the only chance of treating 
-penicillin
