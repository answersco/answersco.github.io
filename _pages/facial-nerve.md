---
title: "Facial nerve"
---
Exits PONS, through CPA and IAC
-then in temporal bone -
 labyrinthine segment (to geniculate ganglion)
 -greater superficial petrosal nerve (lacrimation)
 - Stapedius nerve
 - chorda tympani (taste)
 tympanic (horizontal) segment
 mastoid (vertical) segment
-then through the stylomastoid foramen
 5 branches extracranial
