---
title: "Familial adenomatous polyposis"
---
-affects 1 in 5,000 people in the U.S.
1) causes multiple polyps at an early age
-cells do not die on schedule and instead pile up into polyps
2) several mutations contribute
-first APC genes is deleted, &#946;-catenin is not silenced
-activation of oncogenes(E.g. K-Ras)
-mutations in TGF-&#946;, p53, and other genes
