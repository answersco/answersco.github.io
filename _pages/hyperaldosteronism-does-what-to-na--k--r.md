---
title: "Hyperaldosteronism does what to Na, K, Renin, Angiotensin, and pH status?"
---
Aldosterone acts to reabsorb Na, secrete K &amp; H.

Therefore you become hyperNa, HypoK, ALKALOTIC with feedback inhibition (low) Renin and Angiotensin
