---
title: "What is passive hepatic congestion?"
---
Stasis of blood within liver parenchyma as result of impaired hepatic venous drainage usually 2/2 Heart failure, constrictive pericarditis. Look for reflux of contrast into IVC and hepatic veins. Chronic disease results in cirrhosis and the ddx is cirrhosis viral and EtOH, Budd-Chiari syndrome.
