---
title: "Hypervascular liver mets, and mets with calcification:"
---
Hypervascular metastases (best seen in arterial phase): 
Neuroendocrine tumors (including pancreatic and carcinoid)
RCC
Thyroid carcinoma
Melanoma
Sarcoma

Calcifications in liver metastases: 
Mucinous colorectal tumors
Ovarian serous tumors
