Adenocarcinoma:

95% of the time it is going to be adencarcinoma and it mainly arises from the proximal small bowel (duodenum)
What you can see if a focal circumferential area of bowel wall thickening

Lymphoma:

Usually non-hodgkins variety
Key piece of trivia is that it usually DOES NOT obstruct even with massive circumferential involvement (image)
