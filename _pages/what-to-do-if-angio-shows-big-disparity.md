---
title: "What to do if angio shows big disparity in time it takes for contrast to reach the knees"
---
For big disparity in the time it takes contrast to reach the knees, reactive hyperemia effectively decreases the transit time on the slower side by half.
-Performed by inflating a BP cuff to a suprasystolic pressure at the ankle for 203 minutes
-Cuff is then released and removed just before contrast injection.
