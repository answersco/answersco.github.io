---
title: "Large, unilateral, cystic lesion lateral to prostate"
---
Seminal vesicle cyst- unilateral, and lateral to prostate
Can appear midline if very large.
Congenital or acquired

Congenital:
Associated with renal agenesis, vas deferens agenesis, ectopic ureter insertion, and PCKD

Acquire: 
Often obstruction from BPH, or chronic infection/scarring
Classic history is prior prostate surgery
