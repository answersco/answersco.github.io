---
title: "What are the permissible radiation limits in restricted areas, radiation areas, high radiation area and non-controlled areas?"
---
Restricted area: &gt;2 mrem per hr (&gt;20 &#181;Sv/hr) 
Radiation area- 5 mrem per hour at 30 cm or 100mrem (1 mSev) per week
High Radiation area: 100 millirem per hour dose at 30 cm
Non-controlled - &lt;2 mrem (0.02 millisievert) in any one hour
