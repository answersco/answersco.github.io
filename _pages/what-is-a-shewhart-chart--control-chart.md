---
title: "What is a Shewhart Chart (Control Chart)?"
---
Process behavior type of chart with upper and lower controls
- untying OUTSIDE the expected variation (outside the controls) are considered a &quot;SIGNAL&quot; (special cause of variation) and should be investigated

- Upper and lower controls are chosen to distinguish between normal variation (noise) and special or unusual variation (signal)

- Staying between the controls demonstrates a stable process
