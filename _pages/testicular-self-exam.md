---
title: "Testicular self exam"
---
-teach client how to examine scrotum and testicles:
1) roll each testicle gently between the thumb &amp; fingers of both hands.
2) note presence, size, shape, mobility, contour, &amp; consistency of nodule or lump
3) report any masses, swelling, or tenderness 
4) any unusual discharge after compressing tip (urinary meatus) 
-if you find any hard lumps or nodules, notify your physician promptly.
