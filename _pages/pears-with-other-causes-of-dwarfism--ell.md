---
title: "Pears with other causes of dwarfism: Ellis-Van Crevald, pseudoachondroplasia, pyknodysostosis"
---
Ellis-Van-Crevald: dwarf with multiple fingers
Pseudoachondroplasia: not present at birth. Spares the skull.
Pyknodysostosis: osteopetrosis is a dwarf with a wide-angled jaw and acro-osteolysis
