---
title: "Hospice services"
---
-provides physical, psychological, social and spiritual care for dying people, their families and other loved ones
-manage patient's pain and symptoms, provide needed drugs, medical supplies and equipment
-instruct family how to care for loved one
-to be eligible for hospice benefits from Medicare and Medicaid, the patient must have a serious, progressive illness with a life expectancy of 6 months or less
