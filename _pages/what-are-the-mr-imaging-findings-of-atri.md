---
title: "What are the MR imaging findings of atrial myxoma?"
---
85% LEFT atrium. Pedunculated mass. T1-weighted spin echo/dark blood isointense to myocardium but may be variable due to calcifications (common) and hemorrhage. 
GRE (bright blood) usually dark compared to blood pool.
T1 post con, heterogenous enhancement.
Often move in SSFP cine and can obstruct the mitral valve.
