---
title: "Why questions"
---
-Asks client to justify reasons.
-Implies criticism and makes client feel defensive. 
-Better to focus on what happened and encourage telling the whole story.

EX.
&quot;Why did you do that?&quot;
&quot;Why do you feel that way?&quot;
&quot;Why did you eat that when you know it gives you stomach pain?&quot;
&quot;Why did you go back to bed?&quot;
