---
title: "What would a CT neck, CTA, MRI, and MRA of the spine look for?"
---
-CT neck: better used for soft tissue mass, goiter, thyroid nodule, retropharyngeal abscess
-CTA: look at neck vessels-carotids and vertebral arteries
-MRI: used to look at spinal cord and ligamentous injuries
-MRA: contrast of vessels that is not CTA
