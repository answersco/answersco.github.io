---
title: "Osteoporosis in females"
---
-estrogen maintains bone density in both sexes and inhibits resorption by osteoclasts
-postmenopausal white women at greater risk b/c ovaries cease estrogen secretion
-can be seen as young as 35, and by the age of 70, 30% less bone mass which increases fractures
-in young female athletes with low body fat causing them to stop ovulating and decrease estrogen secretion which can develop osteoporosis
-risk factors: age, gender, race, smoking, diabetes, poor diets
