title: "Medication error statistics"
---
-more than 250,000 deaths per year are due to medical error in the U.S. 
-in 2013, according to the CDC, 611,105 people died of heart disease, 584,881 died of cancer, and 149,205 died of chronic respiratory disease 
(the top three causes of death in the U.S.) 
&#8212; the newly calculated figure for medical errors puts this cause of death behind cancer but ahead of respiratory disease.
