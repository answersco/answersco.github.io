---
title: "Leukoplakia"
---
Squamous metaplasia secondary to chronic inflammation
Bladder &gt; ureter
Mural filling defects

Premalignant: CANCER IS SQUAMOUS CELL

Malakoplakia not premalignant, and usually improves with antibiotics

This IS premalignant (and associated with squamous cell carcinoma)
This is usually related to chronic inflammation as well and will be shown as filling defects
More commonly happens in the bladder relative to the ureter
