---
title: "Medication management for depression"
---
1) Evaluate current medications

2) Treatment of underlying medical condition, if applicable

3) Antidepressants
-Most common (safest): Selective serotonin reuptake inhibitors (SSRI's)
-Serotonin-norepinephrine reuptake inhibitors (SNRI's)
-Serotonin modulators and stimulators (SMS's)
-Serotonin antagonists and reuptake inhibitors (SARI's)
-Norepinephrine reuptake inhibitors (NRI's or NERI's)

4) Others
-Tricyclic (TCA's)
-Monoamine Oxidase Inhibitors (MAOI's)
-Adjunct: Two antidepressants or antipsychotic with antidepressants
