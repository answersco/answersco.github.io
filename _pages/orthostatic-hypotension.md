---
title: "Orthostatic hypotension"
---
-getting up too fast and getting dizzy
-decrease in blood pressure of 20 mmHg or more with position change
-blood pressure is measured with the patient laying down, sitting, and standing with 1-3 minutes of rest between measurements
-volume deficit --&gt; decreased BP &amp; increased pulse
-inadequate sympathetic response decreased BP &amp; decreased pulse: seen in parkinson's
