---
title: "Heat stroke"
---
-temperature: 106-113&#176; F or 41.1-44.9&#176; C
-etiology: excessive environmental temperature, high humidity, medications, &amp; excessive exercise
-signs/symptoms: hot, dry skin, giddiness, confusion delirium --&gt; unconsciousness, excessive thirst, nausea, muscle cramps, &amp; increased pulse and lowered blood pressure
