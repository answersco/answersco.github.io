---
title: "Pericardial effusion:  - amount of mL - causes (3) - CXR appearance on XR (3) -- classic sign on lateral"
---
Pericardial effusion: &gt; 50 cc between the layers

Causes:
- Uremia (renal failure) most common
- Lupus
- Dressler Syndrome (post-MI inflammatory process)

CXR: 
1. big heart (normal on comparison)
2. Giant water bottle heart
3. Lateral with &quot;oreo cookie sign&quot;: 2 lucent lines (epicardial and pericardial fat) with central opaque line (pericardial fluid)
