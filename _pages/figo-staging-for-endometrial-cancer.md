---
title: "FIGO staging for endometrial cancer?"
---
Stage IA: Confined to uterine wall (inner half myometrium)
Stage IIA: Confined to uterine wall (outer half of myometrium)
Stage II: Invasion of cervical stroma, but doesn't extend beyond uterus (requires RADIATION Pre-op)
Stage III: Tumor extends beyond the uterus but is confined to the pelvis
Stage IV: Tumors invaded adjacent organs like bladder/bowel or have distant mets
