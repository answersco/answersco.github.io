---
title: "Rectal Cancer Important Facts"
---
- T2 weighted images are by far most important (don't really even need contrast)
- T3 tumor is very important decision point as they get chemo/rads first (this is when cancer invades the serosa into the perirectal fat)
- T4 is when tumor goes through the mesorectal fascia
- Nodes &gt;5mm are ABNORMAL in the perirectal fat
