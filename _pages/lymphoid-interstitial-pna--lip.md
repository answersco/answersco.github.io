---
title: "Lymphoid interstitial PNA (LIP):"
---
Lymphoid interstitial PNA (LIP): exceptionally rare as an isolated idiopathic disease, and more commonly associated with Sjogren syndrome or HIV.
Imaging findings: include diffuse or lower-lobe predominant ground-glass
Scattered thin-walled perivascular cysts are often present, thought to be due to air-trapping.
May be complicated by PTX in advanced disease.
