---
title: "Hematocrit"
---
Men: 42-52%
Women: 35-47%
-percentage of the total blood volume that consists of RBCs, about half of the blood volume is RBCs
(often call about a H&amp;H which is hemoglobin and hematocrit)

-centrifuge blood to separate components
-components include erythrocytes, white blood cells and platelets, and plasma

-packed cell volume
-percentage of whole blood volume composed of RBCs
-men 42% to 52% cells
-women 37% to 48% cells
