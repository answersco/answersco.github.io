---
title: "Treatment of splenic artery aneurysm?"
---
Coil embolization-- place distal to neck then come proximal

Probably warranted in any pregnant patient with splenic artery aneurysm, or any symptomatic aneurysm
2 cm recommended as threshold for treatment in young women

Stent-graft: preserves in-line flow to spleen
Embo: easier when artery is tortuous
-Pack aneurysm with coils, and/or placement of coils or plugs distal and proximal to aneurysm.
