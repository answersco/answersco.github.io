---
title: "Kawasaki disease imaging findings"
---
-Echocardiography is ideal technique with high specificity and sensitivity for detecting proximal aneurysms of coronary arteries
-Size, number and distribution of aneurysms
-Intravascular coronary ultrasound demonstrates findings in the wall of the vessels
-Coronary angiography demonstrates the aneurysms, size and extent of stenosis
-Cardiac MR in older children and adults for assessing function and aneurysms
