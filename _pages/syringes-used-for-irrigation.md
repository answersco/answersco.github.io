---
title: "Syringes used for irrigation"
---
1) Asepto or bulb syringe
-bulb at the distal end
2) Catheter-tip syringe
-catheter-tip without a needle
3) Bulb syringe aspirator
-used to suction newborn nasal passages

-benefit to bulb syringes is it is easily controllable so you can irrigate gently or hard
