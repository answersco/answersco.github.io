---
title: "Indications for stent grafts"
---
Current:
-Aortic, peripheral, visceral aneurysm and PsAs
-TIPS 
-Arterial trauma or dissection
-Venous anastomosis stenosis dialysis graft

Developing:
-Primary treatment arterial stenosis (long-segment SFA, bilateral CIA origin)
-Primary recanalization peripheral arterial occlusions (above the knee)
-In-stent restenosis
-Mycotic aneurysms
