---
title: "What determines the softness or &quot;floppiness&quot; of the wire tip"
---
The taper of the core at the leading end of the guidewire determines the softness or &quot;floppiness&quot; of the tip
-The length and rate of transition of the taper define the characteristics of the tip
-Bentson guidewires (or moveable core wires with core retracted) have the softest tips
