---
title: "What occurs in ASCENDING transtentorial herniation?"
---
Usually a POSTERIOR FOSSA Mass will cause vermis to herniate upward through the tentorial incisura (severe obstructive hydrocephalus at the level of the Aqueduct)

- Flattened or reversed SMILE of quadrigeminal plate cistern
- &quot;Spinning top&quot; appearance of the midbrain from bilateral compression posteriorly
