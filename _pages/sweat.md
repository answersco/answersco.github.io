title: "Sweat"
---
perspire

-begins as a protein-free filtrate of blood plasma produced by deep secretory portion of gland

-Some sodium chloride and other small solutes remain in the sweat --- Some sodium chloride reabsorbed by duct

-Some drugs are excreted in sweat

-On average, 99% water, with pH range of 4 to 6---Acid mantle&#8212;inhibits bacterial growth

-Insensible perspiration&#8212;500 mL/day----Does not produce visible wetness of skin
