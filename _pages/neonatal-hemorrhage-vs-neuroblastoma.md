---
title: "Neonatal hemorrhage vs neuroblastoma"
---
Hemorrhage: can occur in setting of birth trauma or stress

US: adrenal hemorrhage is anechoic and avascular, while neuroblastoma is echogenic and vascular.
MRI: adrenal hemorrhage has low T2 signal, neuroblastoma high T2 signal.

Serial US shows gradual decrease in size of hemorrhage.
