---
title: "Radiation effects of fetus for significant exposure (e.g. &gt; 2 Gy)"
---
Weeks 0-2 (pre-implantation): all or nothing effect.
2-6 WGA: congenital abnormalities, neonatal death.
8-15 WGA: highest risk of MR. 40%/Sv.
Increased risk of childhood cancer, microcephaly.
15-25 WGA: smaller risk of MR, 10%/Sv.
Increased risk of childhood cancer.
