---
title: "Waltman loop"
---
Waltman loop: can be formed in any major aortic branch vessel with braided selective catheters
-Particularly useful in pelvis for selection of branches of the IIA on the same side as the arterial puncture.
-Angled catheter positioned over Ao bifurcation. The stiff end of the guidewire is retracted to the level of the catheter apex.
-The catheter is advanced and twisted, forming the loop.
-Catheter is pulled back and directed into ipsilateral IIA.
