---
title: "What are the exits from the PPF?"
---
1. Lateral: pterygomaxillary fissure --&gt; masticator space
2. Medial: sphenopalatine foramen --&gt; nasopharynx via superior meatus (site of JNA!)
3. Posterior: Foramen rotundum --&gt; contains V2 to the middle cranial fossa
* Vidian Canal: directly below foramen rontundum with vidian artery/nerve (e.g. the artery nerve of the pterygoid canal)
4. Anterior: Inferior orbital fissure (also the &quot;Roof&quot;) --&gt; opens anteriorly to orbit

* 5. Floor: Pterygopalatine canal --&gt; oral cavity via greater and lesser palatine foramina (with descending palatine nerve and artery)
