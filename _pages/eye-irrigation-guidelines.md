title: "Eye irrigation guidelines"
---
1) use sterile technique: equipment and solution used are sterile but no need to use sterile gloves 
2) used to remove
-foreign bodies from the eye (keep contacts in eye) --
 sands, fiberglass dust, dirt
-injurious fluids from an eye splash (take contacts out) --
 chemicals
3) secretions caused by infections
-pink eye
