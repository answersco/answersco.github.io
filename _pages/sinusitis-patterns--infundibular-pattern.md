---
title: "Sinusitis patterns: infundibular pattern, frontal recess pattern, sphenoethmoidal recess pattern."
---
Infundibular pattern&#8594; disease limited to maxillary sinus; obstruction in ipsilateral ostium/infundibulum.

Frontal recess pattern&#8594; disease in frontal sinus; occlusion of ipsilateral frontal recess.

Sphenoethmoidal recess pattern&#8594; disease in ipsilateral sphenoid sinus + posterior ethmoid cells.
