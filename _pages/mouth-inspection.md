---
title: "Mouth inspection"
---
-represents a lot of health conditions such as oral hygiene, condition of teeth and gums, hydration, airway patency
-use a penlight and tongue depressor or gauze
-look at palate, uvula, tonsils, under tongue, side cheeks - should be pinkish/red in appearance with a moist smooth surface
-look at piercings and lip enhancements to see if they look okay
-assess lips: moist, smooth, and symmetrical, color
-texture
-hydration
-contour
-lesions-may be due to irritations, skin cancer or infections -- herpes simplex (cold sores)
