---
title: "Osteopathia Striata?"
---
Benign assymptomatic sclerotic dysplasia characterized by linear bands of sclerosis in the long bones and fan like sclerosis in the flat pelvic bones. Normal bone scan.

Aka Voorhoeve disease. Multiple 2- to 3-mm-thick linear sclerotic bands aligned parallel to bone long axis. Usually affects multiple long bones. Generally is asymptomatic and inconsequential.
