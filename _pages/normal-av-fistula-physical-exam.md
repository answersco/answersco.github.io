---
title: "Normal AV Fistula Physical Exam?"
---
- Continuous thrill at the anastomosis 
- Low pitched bruit at the outflow vein

The most commonly used AV fistulas are created by anastomosing the radial artery to the cephalic vein (radiocephalic fistula) or by anastomosing the brachial artery to the cephalic vein (brachiocephalic fistula) or basilic vein (brachiobasilic fistula).
