---
title: "Osteoporosis"
---
-severe loss of bone density 
-bones lose mass and become brittle due to loss of organic matrix and materials
-spongy bone is most affected b/c it is the most metabolically active
-pathological fractures will occur in hips, vertebral column, and wrists which can create kyphosis which is a spine deformity due to bone loss -- &quot;widows hump&quot;
