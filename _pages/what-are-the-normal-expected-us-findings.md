---
title: "What are the normal expected US findings for the infant lumbar spine?"
---
Conus should terminate above L3 (L1-2 by 3 months of age)
Conus should lie ventrally in thecal sac when prone
Anechoic CSF should be seen dorsal to the cord
Conus has a rounded contour
Nerve roots should be freely mobile 
No evidence of mass/lipoma
The normal filum terminale is echogenic &amp; &lt;2mm
