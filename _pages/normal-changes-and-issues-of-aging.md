---
title: "Normal changes and issues of aging"
---
1) Integumentary:
-Thinner epidermis, capillary fragility

2) Sensory:
-Presbycusis, slower reaction time, macular degeneration/cataracts

3) Renal:
-Decreased creatinine clearance and drug toxicity

4) Musculoskeletal:
-Decreased muscle mass and bone density

5) Functional:
-Gait/balance and falls
-Driving
-Handling finances
