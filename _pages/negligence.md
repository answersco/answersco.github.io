---
title: "Negligence"
---
-practice below standard of care 
EX. did not turn pt q2h and pt not has stage 1 pressure ulcer

-the central issue. 
-is defined as &quot;failure to act as a reasonably prudent person would act in the same circumstances.&quot;
-Failure to follow standards of care
-Failure to use equipment in a responsible manner
-Failure to communicate
-Failure to document
-Failure to assess and monitor
-Failure to act as a patient advocate


EX. A circulating nurse in the OR fails to do a sponge count and later it is determined that there was a retained a sponge. That is negligence.
