---
title: "Fetal measurements"
---
Biparietal diameter
-measure at level of thalamus from outer near skull to inner far skull
-can be affected by head shape and craniosynostosis

Head circumference
-don't include skin

Abdominal circumference
-at the level of the umbilical vein and left portal vein
-don't include subcutaneous tissues

Femur length
-exclude epiphyses
