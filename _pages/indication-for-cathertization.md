---
title: "Indication for cathertization"
---
1) incontinence
-usually managed by managing skin and giving them certain products 
-exception is incontinence with a pressure ulcer
2) urinary retention
-accurate assessment of output: pt's who are hemodynamically unstable, liver/kidney/heart failure
3) surgery
-to allow for no accidents during surgery but most are taken out after surgery 
4) trauma
-severe immobility so it is functionally impossible or unsafe to use other measures
