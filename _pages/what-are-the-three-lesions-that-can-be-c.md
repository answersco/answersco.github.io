---
title: "What are the three lesions that can be considered BIRADS 3 on dx mammogram?"
---
1) nonpalpable, circumscribed mass on a baseline
mammogram (unless it can be shown to be a cyst,
an intramammary lymph node, or another benign
finding on dx mammogram or US).
2) A focal asymmetry which partially thins on spot compression.
3) Cluster of punctate calcifications
