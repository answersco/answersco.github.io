---
title: "Thoracic spinal arteries"
---
Arterial supply to the thoracic cord is derived from upper and lower thoracic aorta.
Anterior spinal artery at T4-T5 level: variably supplied by intercostal and bronchial arteries.

Anterior spinal artery at level of T6-T12 vertebral bodies = artery of Adamkiewicz
-Originates from intercostal arteries (usually the left) in 75%

Spinal arteries have a characteristic appearance with a hairpin turn and a midline course.
