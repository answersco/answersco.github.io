---
title: "US criteria for acute DVT"
---
Absolute: noncompressible (most reliable), hypoechoic vein (frequently expanded).

Adjunctive:
-Intraluminal filling defect on color Doppler
-Absent color-flow signal in lumen despite augmentation
-Absent Doppler signal in lumen despite augmentation
-Absent respiratory variation
-Absent response to Valsalva.
