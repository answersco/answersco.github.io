---
title: "What is L- transposition of the great vessels (L-TGA)? Associated findings? Coronary anomalies?"
---
Inversion of ventricles and great arteries: Atrioventricular discordance AND ventriculoarterial discordance. (LV is on the right and connects to the PA). Associated VSD in 60-70%. Can also have subpulmonic outflow obstruction and Ebstein's anomaly. The right coronary has the branching pattern of the left with an anterior descending and a circumflex.
