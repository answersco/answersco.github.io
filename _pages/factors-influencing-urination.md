---
title: "Factors influencing urination"
---
1) fluid intake: influences frequency and output
2) loss of body fluid
-kidneys are good at knowing what is needed and not needed in the body, especially in times of trauma
3) nutrition
4) body position
5) psychological factors
6) cognition
7) obstruction of urine flow
8) infection
9) medications
10) hypotension
11) neurologic injury
12) decreased muscle tone
13) pregnancy
14) surgery
15) urinary diversion
