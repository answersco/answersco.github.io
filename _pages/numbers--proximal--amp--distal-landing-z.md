---
title: "Numbers: Proximal &amp; Distal Landing Zone Criteria for EVAR:"
---
PROXIMAL LANDING ZONE (from inferiormost renal artery to start of aneurysm):
Aneurysmal neck &lt; 3.2 cm, 1.0-1.5 cm long, angle &lt; 45-60 degrees

DISTAL LANDING ZONE (preferably common iliac artery):
Diamater &lt; 2.5 cm, 1.0 cm long

Access site (Ext iliac artery) at least 6 mm
