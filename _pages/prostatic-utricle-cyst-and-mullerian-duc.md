---
title: "Prostatic utricle cyst and Mullerian duct cysts findings?"
---
Both are midline cystic structures near the bladder in a male (prostatic utricle cyst is out pouching of the prostatic urethra= can be seen on RUG)

- Mullerian duct cyst = remnant of male equivalent of the vagina/ cervix
- can get endometrial, clear cell, or squamous cancer
