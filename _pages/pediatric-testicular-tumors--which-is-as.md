---
title: "Pediatric testicular tumors: which is associated with elevated AFP, and which is associated with Peutz-Jeghers?"
---
Yolk-Sac Tumor: heterogeneous testicular mass in child &lt;2
AFP usually very elevated

Sertoli Cell Tumors: usually bilateral. Visualized on US as &quot;burned-out&quot; tumors (dense echogenic foci that represent calcified scars).
Associated with Peutz-Jeghers.
