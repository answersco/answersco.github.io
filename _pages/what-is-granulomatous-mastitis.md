---
title: "What is granulomatous mastitis?"
---
Noninfective granulomatous inflammation of breast typically occurring in parous women characterized by lobulocentric, noncaseating granulomas (irreg solid masses).

- Nipple discharge is not characteristic
mimics malignancy and biopsy is usually required. 

It appears as large, ill-defined areas of hypoechogenicity on ultrasound in woman with subacute or relapsing breast inflammation and discharging cutaneous sinuses

- Mostly resolves w/ PO steroids
