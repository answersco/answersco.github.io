---
title: "Purpose of pt's records"
---
1) communication (continuity of care)
2) diagnostic and therapeutic orders
3) care planning (baseline and ongoing)
4) legal (not charted, not done)
5) historical documentation (past medical history)
6) research (promote evidence based practice)
7) education (learn about manifestations of health problems)
8) credentialing (compliance with standards of care and governing the profession)
9) reimbursement (demonstrate the pt received the quality of care)
10) quality improvement (measures performance outcomes)
