---
title: "Medications affecting bowel elimination"
---
1) aspirin, anticoagulants: pink to red to black stool, GI bleeding
2) iron salts: black stool (patients need accurate assessment of number of BM with stool softener administration when taking iron tx bc it causes constipation)
3) antacids: white discoloration or speckling in stool
4) antibiotics: green-gray color
5) opioids: cause constipation
