---
title: "What are the most common radiotracers for myocardial perfusion scans/SPECT - how do they work? - when do you image?"
---
- Tc-Sestamibi
- Tc-Tetrofosmin

- work by crossing the cell membrane and localizing in mitochondria (passive diffusion)
- don't redistribute (like Thallium)

-image 30-90 minutes after injection (allow clearance from background)
