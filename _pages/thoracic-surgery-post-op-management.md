---
title: "Thoracic surgery post-op management"
---
1) May be on ventilator / May have chest tube

2) Promote airway clearance 
-lot of coughing and deep breathing
-splint incision when doing so
-use of incentive spirometer
-may be on breathing treatments

3) Manage pain 

4) Maintain fluid volume - help liquefy secretions so you can cough them out 

5) Monitor for complications
