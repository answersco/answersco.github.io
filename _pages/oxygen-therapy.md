---
title: "Oxygen therapy"
---
-Is considered a medication

-Indications of need
1) Change in Respiratory pattern 
2) Hypoxemia (which can can lead to hypoxia)
3) Hypoxia
4) CNS changes - Mental status, agitation disorientation confusion, lethargy, &amp; coma
5) Dyspnea, SOB, changes in HR, dysrhythmias, diaphoresis
6) Longstanding hypoxia &#8594; fatigue, drowsiness, apathy, inattentiveness, &amp; delayed reaction time
