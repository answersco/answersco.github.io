---
title: "Erik Erikson's 8 stages of psychosocial development"
---
-Emphasizes how a person's psychological life is embedded in and shaped by social relationships and challenges faced by the developing person.

-Each stage represents the developmental task that the person must master at a particular place in the life span.

-Each task has two possible outcomes&#8212;either a gain in personal competence, or establishing personal weakness and vulnerability.
