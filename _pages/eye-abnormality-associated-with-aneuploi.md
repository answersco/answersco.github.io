---
title: "Eye abnormality associated with aneuploidy:"
---
Coloboma&#8594; incomplete fusion of the embryonic intraocular fissure&#8594; can result in an elongated or malformed globe.
Associated with trisomies 13 and 18, and the CHARGE and VATER syndromes.
Imaging: cone-shaped or notch-shaped deformity.
Optic nerve colobomas can have outpouchings posteriorly.
Iris colobomas are anterior.
Uveal colobomas tend to have microphthalmos and a cyst.
