---
title: "Genus neisseria"
---
-gram (-) 
-diplococci - (in pairs) and bean-shaped 
-most species - strict pathogens, some commensals
-pathogens are fastidious and grow best under high levels of CO2
-not associated with fomites
-need to be on a human surface to grow
-includes neisseria gonorrheoae and neisseria meingitidis
