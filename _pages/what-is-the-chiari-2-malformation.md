---
title: "What is the Chiari 2 malformation?"
---
Small posterior fossa with myelomeningocele (spina bifida). Abnormal neural tube formation results in failure to maintain distention of the 4th ventricle leading to a hypoplastic posterior fossa and abnormal hind brain.

Small post fossa plus spina bifida/myelomeningocele, and low brain stem. 4th vent, medulla, and cerebellum displaced into foramen magnum. Assoc w scoliosis, segmentation anomalies, dysgen of CC, absent septum pellucidum, obstructive hydrocephalus, fenestration of the falx, club foot, luckenschadel skull, club foot.
