---
title: "Hemoglobin"
---
Men: 13-18 g/dL
Women: 12-16 g/dL
-protein inside the RBC that carries oxygen to the tissues and removes CO2
-has iron pigment which is what makes the blood red

-Hb
-four globular proteins surrounding heme groups -- two beta chains and two alpha chains
-function is to carry oxygen and carbon dioxide in the bloodstream

-33% of cytoplasm
-280 million of these molecules on one RBC
-O2 delivery to tissue and CO2 transport to lungs

is a tetramonomer
