---
title: "Placenta accreta"
---
Placenta attaches deeply into myometrium. Ultrasound shows lack of hypoechoic sub placental zone.

Placenta increta- placenta invades the myometrium.

Placenta percreta- placenta penetrates myometrium into/through the serosa

Most common, mildest form 

Patho: VILLI attach to the myometrium WITHOUT INVASION
