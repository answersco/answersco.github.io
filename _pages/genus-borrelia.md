---
title: "Genus borrelia"
---
-Lyme Disease - Borrelia borgdorferi
-prevalent in eastern U.S (~8,000 cases/yr.) - been on the increase since the 70s
-zoonotic disease w/ arthropod vector
-animal reservoirs - deer and mice
-vector - deer tick (genus Ixodes)
-incidence increasing - due to human impact on environment: humans living near woodland and fields and increasing deer populations
