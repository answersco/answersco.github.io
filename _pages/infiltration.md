Whipple (malabsorb), Mastocytosis, Waldenstrom (NHL)

-Fluid in subcutaneous tissue

-Swelling

-Tender 

-Cool to touch

-Pallor

-Tissue has increased firmness

-Absence of blood return (Not Diagnostic)

-Pain or discomfort at site

** remove IV and start a new IV way above the swelling or in other arm
