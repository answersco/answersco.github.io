---
title: "Glaucoma"
---
damage to the optic nerve because of the pressure created by a buildup of fluid in the eye

-associated with increased intra-ocular pressure
-having this constant increased pressure can lead to optic nerve damage and blindness
-usually lose their peripheral vision first and may complain of a halo around the object they are focusing on
-treated with medicated objects to lower intra-ocular pressure
