---
title: "Esophageal findings in scleroderma:"
---
Scleroderma: lack of peristalsis of the distal &#8532; of the esophagus due to smooth muscle atrophy and fibrosis &#8594; marked esophageal dilation
Secondary candidiasis or aspiration PNA due to stasis
Esophageal dilation often apparent before skin changes
