---
title: "Other malignant pediatric renal masses:"
---
Renal cell carcinoma&#8594; typically in setting of VHL
Renal medullary carcinoma&#8594; aggressive variant associated with sickle cell trait

Rhabdoid tumor: rare, aggressive malignancy that may occur in brain or kidney
Ill-defined, aggressive mass. Renal and CNS lesions may be concurrent.

Clear cell sarcoma&#8594; rare renal cancer with propensity to metastasize to bone
Renal metastases: neuroblastoma, leukemia, and lymphoma
