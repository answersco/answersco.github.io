---
title: "3 stages of CHF (wedge pressure and findings)"
---
Stage 1 = redistribution
PCWP of 13-18
Cephalization of vessels, cardiomegaly, enlarged vascular pedicle

Stage 2 = interstitial edema
PCWP = 18-25
Kerley lines, peribronchial cuffing, less distinct contour of the central vessels

Stage 3 = alveolar edema
PCWP &gt; 25
Fluffy airspace opacities, pleural effusion
