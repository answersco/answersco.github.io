---
title: "Penicillin"
---
-the first antibiotic
-discovered by Alexander Fleming
-isolated from the fungi Penicillium
-purified and used as a drug in 1941
-Fleming recognized a clear area where bacteria could not grow next to fungus and started isolating chemicals from the fungus (penicillin) was very good at killing bacteria (Gram positive to be exact)

-prevents protein crosslinks from forming 
-static drug 
-works better for gram positive bc it has less peptidoglycan which they rely on heavily to hold their cell structure
