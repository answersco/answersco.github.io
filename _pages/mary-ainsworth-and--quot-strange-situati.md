---
title: "Mary Ainsworth and &quot;Strange Situation&quot; Experiment"
---
-put infants and moms in strange situations to see how they would react and what level of attachment they had
-Observational Conditions
1) mother and child playing together while play is being observed (child engaged or walking away to clinging to mom)
2) stranger enters (see how baby reacts)
3) mom leaves child with stranger (see baby's reaction)
4) mother returns and stranger leaves (reunion is most important part)
