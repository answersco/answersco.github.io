---
title: "T-helper cells"
---
-a type of T-lymphocyte (CD-4 cell)
-TH1 - helps cytotoxic T lymphocytes 
-TH2 - helps B-lymphocytes
-activated by binding to antigen and MHC on antigen presenting cells 
-once activated: produces chemicals (lymphokines) which stimulate (to divide, increase metabolism) other lymphocytes
-APC helps to Activate CD4 cells
