---
title: "Reserved powers"
---
the term for those powers not specifically given to the national/federal government which belong to the state governments

Those powers that the constitution does not grant to the federal government and does not, at the same time, deny to the states. Belong to the states and people. Gives the individual states rights to govern that the federal government doesn't have.

also known as police powers; powers reserved to the states by the 10th Amendment that lie at the foundation of a state's right to legislate for the public health and welfare of its citizens
