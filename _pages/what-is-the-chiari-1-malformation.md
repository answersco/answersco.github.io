---
title: "What is the Chiari 1 malformation?"
---
Chiari 1: most common (F&gt;M), usually asx, downward descent of the cerebellar tonsils &gt;5-6mm. If &gt;12mm usually sx assoc w/ syrinx and hydrocephalus. Measured ophisthion to basion. If &lt;5mm tonsilar ectopia. No spina bifida. Other associated syndromes: Crouzon and Klippel-Feil.
