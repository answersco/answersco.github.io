---
title: "Seminal vesicle cyst"
---
Associated with renal agenesis

-unilateral, lateral cyst
-congenital or acquired

Congenital:
-unilateral renal agenesis
-unilateral vas deferens agensis
-ectopic ureter insertion

Acquired:
-prior prostate surgery
-chronic infection

Zinner syndrome = sem ves cyst + unilateral renal agenesis

- Unilateraly cust that is LATERAL to the prostate
- These can be congenital OR acquired 

Congenital: associated with polycystic kidney disease
Acquired: Prostatic obstruction, chronic infection of prostate, prior prostate surgery
