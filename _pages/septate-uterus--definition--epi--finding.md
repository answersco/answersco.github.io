title: "Septate uterus, definition, Epi, findings, and associations?"
---
Septate (V)= Incomplete resorption of medial uterovaginal septum. 
Complete: Septum extends to external os. Partial: Variable length. May extend into upper vagina.
Most common MDA, 55% of total. 65% spont Ab rate.
Tx, septoplasty.
Uterine fundal contour is flat, convex or mildly concave &#8804; 1 cm.
