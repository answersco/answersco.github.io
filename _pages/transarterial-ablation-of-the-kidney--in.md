---
title: "Transarterial ablation of the kidney: indications and general treatment goal"
---
Transarterial ablation of kidney as an alternative to nephrectomy can be accomplished with selective embo.

Indications: 
Intractable renal bleeding due to unresectable tumors
Nephrotic syndrome with unmanageable proteinuria.
End-stage PCKD causing mass effect/pain
Severe HTN related to nonfiltering kidney.

Global embo of kidney with small particles or alcohol is usually effective.
