---
title: "What are the types of errors according to IOM?"
---
The 4 categories of errors defined by the 1999 IOM Report, &quot;To Err is Human: Building a Safer Health System&quot; are (1) diagnostic (e.g., a missed diagnosis) (2) treatment (e.g., a delay in initiating treatment) (3) preventative (e.g., inadequate follow-up after a test) (4) other (e.g., failure to communicate).
