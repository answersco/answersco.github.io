---
title: "What are the modes of spread for testicular tumors?"
---
Left testis drains to left paraaortic nodes inferior to renal hilum.
Right testis drains to interaortocaval chain where testicular vein enters inferior vena cava.
These nodes drain to cisterna chyli &#8594; thoracic duct &#8594; left supraclavicular nodes &#8594; lungs
Choriocarcinoma often has early hematogenous spread.
