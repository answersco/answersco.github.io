---
title: "Esophageal candidiasis?"
---
Spectrum from scattered plaque like lesions in mild disease to very shaggy esophogus in severe cases.

Immunocompromised or achalasia
Most common finding = discrete plaque like lesions
Additional findings can be nodularity or fold thickening 
When it is SEVERE it can look like a shaggy esophagus (image)

Glycogenic Acenthosis: MIMIC of candidiasis but BENIGN
You see this in asymptomatic old people with elevated benign nodules

-small nodules
-shaggy appearance later
DDx: glycogen acanthosis (asx old person)
