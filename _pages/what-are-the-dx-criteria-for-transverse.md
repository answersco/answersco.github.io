---
title: "What are the dx criteria for transverse myelitis?"
---
(1) B/L sensorimotor and autonomic spinal cord dysfunction
(2) clearly defined sensory level
(3) Regression of clinical deficits between 4 hours and 21 days after symptom onset
(4) gadolinium-enhancing cord lesion
(5) exclusion of compressive, post-radiation, neoplastic, and vascular causes.
