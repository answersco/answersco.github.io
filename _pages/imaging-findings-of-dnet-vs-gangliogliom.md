title: "Imaging findings of DNET vs Ganglioglioma?"
---
DNET = &quot;Does Not Enhance DNE&quot;
- Usually child &lt; 20 yo; T2 bright Soap Bubbly lesion in temporal lobe causing seizures; FLAIR bright rim

Ganglioglioma = &quot;Gang of appearances&quot; = can appear anywhere, in anyone
* Classically 13 yo kid with seizures; Cyst + Solid temporal lobe mass with focal calcs and overlying bony remodeling
