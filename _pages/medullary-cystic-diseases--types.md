---
title: "Medullary cystic diseases: types"
---
1. Familial nephronophthisis - ciliopathy; most common cause of inherited childhood renal failure
2. Adult medullary cystic disease (autosomal dominant)
3. Renal - retinal dysplasia (associated with retinitis pigmentosa)

Multiple small cysts in medulla - can be hard to see. May increase thickness and echogenicity of central sinus echoes
