---
title: "What is neuroblastoma in the peds neck?"
---
Neuroblastoma is the most common malignant tumor in peds &lt;1 yo. Most often associated iwth the adrenal gland or in the retroperitoneum, occasionally may form in the neck most often associated with the carotid sheath. Imaging with CT/MRI and MIBG-I-123 is usually necessary.
