---
title: "Mediastinal contents:"
---
Anterior mediastinum: 
Thymus
Lymph nodes
Enlarged thyroid gland, if it extends inferiorly into mediastinum

Middle mediastinum: anterior border is anterior pericardium; posterior borders are posterior pericardium and posterior tracheal wall
Heart and pericardium
Ascending aorta and arch
Great vessels (including SVC, IVC, pulmonary arteries and veins, and brachiocephalic vessels)
Trachea and bronchi
Lymph nodes
Phrenic, vagus, and recurrent laryngeal nerves (all pass through AP window)

Posterior mediastinum: anterior border is posterior trachea and posterior pericardium
Esophagus
Descending thoracic aorta
Azygos and hemiazygos veins
Thoracic duct
Vagus nerves
Lymph nodes
