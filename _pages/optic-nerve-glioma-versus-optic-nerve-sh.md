---
title: "Optic nerve glioma versus optic nerve sheath meningioma"
---
Optic nerve glioma (ONG): 90% &lt; 20 years of age
Expansion and enlargement of entire nerve
If bilateral, think NF-1

Optic nerve sheath meningioma: &quot;tram-track&quot; calcifications
&quot;Doughnut&quot; appearance, with circumferential enhancement around optic nerve
