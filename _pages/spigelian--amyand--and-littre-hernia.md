---
title: "Spigelian, Amyand, and Littre hernia"
---
Location is along the semilunar line (Semilunar = Spigelian) through the transversus abdominis aponeurosis close to the level of the arcuate line.

Amyand hernia: hernia with appendix in it.

Littre hernia: hernia containing a Meckel's diverticulum.
