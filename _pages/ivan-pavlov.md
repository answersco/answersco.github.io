-behavior learning theory
-classical conditioning
-learning involuntarily emotional and psychological response to new stimuli, Pavlov was able to condition dogs to salivate after hearing a tuning fork
-learning new behavior through the process of association

UCS = food
UCR = salivation
CS = tuning fork
CR = salivating after hearing tuning fork

-scientist to first systematically study how we form associations b/w stimuli
EX. Two stimulis: bell (neutral) and food (meaningful): close together in time so every time the bell rang, it would cause salivation
