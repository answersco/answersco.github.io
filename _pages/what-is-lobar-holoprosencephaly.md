---
title: "What is Lobar Holoprosencephaly?"
---
The least severe form:
Partial absence of frontal interhemispheric fissure and septum pellicidum.
Anterior CC is abnormal.
The 3rd ventricle is fully developed 
Rudimentary frontal horns are present. 
Absent/hypoplastic olfactory bulbs.
The division between semilobar and lobar is not distinct.
