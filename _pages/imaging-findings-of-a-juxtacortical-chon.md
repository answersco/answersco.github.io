---
title: "Imaging findings of a juxtacortical chondroma?"
---
Females age 10-30, proximal humerus

Lucent lesion involving the cortex with a margin of periosteal new bone formation trying to cover the lesion (looks like someone took a bite out of the humerus)

T1 dark, T2 bright due to cartilage component

- Treatment = local surgical excision
