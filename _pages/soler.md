---
title: "SOLER"
---
-paying attention by hearing and interpreting what is being said
1) S - sitting and facing a patient to convey openness
2) O - observing in an open posture, keeping arms uncrossed
3) L - lean toward the patient to convey that you're involved in the interaction with them
4) E - establish eye contact
5) R - relax so everyone in the situation is comfortable
