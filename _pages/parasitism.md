---
title: "Parasitism"
---
-one species benefits, the other is harmed
-disease causing microbes (pathogens)
-many levels of parasitism meaning different levels of harm that a parasite can do to your body
-mild harm --&gt; death of host

when one organism (the parasite) feeds on the body of, or the energy used by, another organism (the host), usually by living on or in the host (ex. tapeworms)
