title: "PE prevention and treatment"
---
-Prevention:
1) Active leg exercises
2) Early ambulation
3) Anti-embolism hose

-Treatment:
1) Oxygen
2) Patent IV lines
3) Anticoagulation therapy
-Lovenox - extreme 
4) Thrombolytic therapy
-clot buster
-have to know is bleeding or recent bleed such as ulcers b/c they could bleed out
-Recombinant plasminogen activator (tPA)
