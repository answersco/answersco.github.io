---
title: "Palliative care"
---
-focus on providing relief from the symptoms and stress of a serious illness
-goal is to improve quality of life
-a subspecialty of medicine that is helpful for persons of any age, with many different diagnoses
-do not need to be at end of life to receive this

any age, any diagnosis, at any time for as long as the client would like

-evolved from hospice experience
-not restricted to the end of life and can be used from the point of initial diagnosis
-focuses on relief of physical, mental and spiritual distress. 
-goal: prevent and relieve suffering by early assessment and treatment of pain and other physical problems such as difficulty breathing or nausea
