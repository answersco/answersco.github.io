---
title: "Lipoma arborescens imaging findings?"
---
Frond-like depositions of fatty tissue in the synovial lining of joints and bursa (no calcs)
- May show chemical shift artifact (frequency encoding direction)

- on US: Frond-like HYPERECHOIC mass with joint effusion

- Usually adults 50-70s, supra patellar bursa of knee

- MRI: Behaves like fat (T1 and T2 bright with saturation on Fat sat)

* Sequelae of CHRONIC SYNOVITIS
