---
title: "Renal cyst in pediatric patient:"
---
Renal cyst&#8594; isolated simple renal cyst is rare in children, and likely syndromic, associated with tuberous sclerosis or VHL

Tuberous sclerosis: 
Renal cysts and AMLs
Cardiac rhabdomyoma
CNS cortical hamartomas, subependymal nodules, and subependymal giant cell astrocytoma

VHL:
Renal cysts and RCC
Adrenal pheochromocytoma
Pancreatic cysts, islet cell tumors, and serous cystadenomas
Brain and spinal cord hemangioblastomas.
