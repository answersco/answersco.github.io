---
title: "What inserts at the volar and dorsal aspects of the base of the middle phalanges?"
---
Extensor digitorum communis: inserts onto the dorsal base of the middle phalanges and PIP capsule of digits 2-4 and 50% of digit 5.

Flexor digitorum superficialis: inserts onto the volar base of the middle phalanx and PIP joint volar plates of digits 2-5

Both are involved in avulsion fractures.
