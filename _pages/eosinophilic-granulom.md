---
title: "Eosinophilic granulom"
---
Eosinophilic granuloma: solitary or multiple, lytic or blastic, with or without sclerotic border and periosteal response
3 classic appearances:
Vertebra plana in a kid
Skull with lucent beveled edge appearance
Floating tooth with lytic lesion in alveolar ridge
May have osseous sequestrum
