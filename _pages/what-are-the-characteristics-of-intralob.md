---
title: "What are the characteristics of intralobar pulm sequest?"
---
More common, 75% of pulm sequest. Inside normal lung and shares pleural covering. Arterial supply is also Ao/systemic. However, venous drainage is to pulm system and LA causing a L to L shunt. The parenchyma is consolidated and fibrotic. Fluid or pus-filled cysts and foci of infection can be seen. Ddx normal PNA.
