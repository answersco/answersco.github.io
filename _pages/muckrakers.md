---
title: "Muckrakers"
---
Journalists who attempted to find corruption or wrongdoing in industries and expose it to the public

early 20th-century journalists who exposed illegal business practices, social injustices, and corrupt urban political bosses; leading journalists included Upton Sinclair, Jacob Riis, and Ida Tarbell

journalists in the early 20th century whose stock-in-trade was exposure of the corruption of big business and government
