---
title: "Inguinal hernias"
---
-inspect for inguinal hernias
-bulges
-ask client to bear down
-occurs in the groin area when fatty or intestinal tissue pushed through the inguinal canal
-can increase in size when you stand up or cough
-can be sensitive or painful to the touch
-if left untreated, it can cause intestine to be trapped in the buldge, twist, and die bc its blood supply is cut off causing extreme pain and needs immediate medical attention
-treated with surgery
