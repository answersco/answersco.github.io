---
title: "Human genome"
---
1) only 1.5% of human DNA encodes for proteins
2) the rest of the genome includes:
-viral DNA 
-DNA coding for noncoding RNAs (rRNA, tRNA, snoRNA, microRNA, psuedogenes)
-introns
-promoters and other control sequences
-repeated sequences (telomeres, centromeres, rRNA gene clusters, &amp; transposons
