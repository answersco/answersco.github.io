---
title: "Infantile Hepatic Hemangioma"
---
Best diagnostic clue
Heterogeneous, hypervascular liver mass in an infant
Location
Liver
Size
Solitary tumor ranges from 0.5-14 cm
Multifocal lesions are typically ~ 1 cm in diameter
Morphology
3 types
Focal: Well-defined mass that often has central area of necrosis and fibrosis
Multifocal: Multiple small, solid-appearing masses
Diffuse: Liver replaced by innumerable small masses=
