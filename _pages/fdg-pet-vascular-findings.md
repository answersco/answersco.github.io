---
title: "FDG-PET vascular findings?"
---
Increased accumulation of FDG in a vascular distribution has a ddx. Asymmetrical linear uptake in an extremity may be DVT, Phlebitis, cellulitis, or infected graft (hemodialysis)
Symmetrical uptake may be active atherosclerotic disease, or systemic vasculitis, or if focal infected AAA graft(s)
