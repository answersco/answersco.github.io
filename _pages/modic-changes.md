---
title: "Modic changes:"
---
Type 1: hyperintense on T2-weighted images, hypointense on T1, reflecting bone marrow edema and inflammation.
May be associated with active back pain.
Presence predicts better outcome following surgery.

Type 2: hyperintense on T2- and T1-weighted images, and reflect fatty proliferation within the affected marrow.
Less likely to be associated with active symptoms.

Type 3: hypointense on T2- and T1-weighted images, and are thought to represent sclerosis.
Unclear clinical significance.
