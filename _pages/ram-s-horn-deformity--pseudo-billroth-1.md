---
title: "Ram's horn deformity (pseudo-Billroth 1)"
---
Tapering of the antrum causes the stomach to look like a Ram's horn.
Can be seen with scarring from peptic ulcers, granulomatous disease (TB, Crohn's, Sarcoid, syphilis), or scirrhous carcinoma.

The stomach is the most common location for sarcoid in the GI tract.
