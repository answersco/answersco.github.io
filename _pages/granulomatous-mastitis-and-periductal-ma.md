---
title: "Granulomatous mastitis and periductal mastitis:"
---
Granulomatous mastitis&#8594; rare, idiopathic, non-infectious cause of breast inflammation in young women after childbirth.
May be associated with breast feeding or OCPs
Findings may mimic breast cancer

Periductal mastitis (AKA plasma cell mastitis &#8594; seen in post-menopausal women
Classic MM appearance of large, rod-like secretory calcifications
