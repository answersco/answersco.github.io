---
title: "Neutropenia"
---
-lack of neutrophils (one main type of WBC)
-more susceptible to infection
-need isolated to make sure they do not get sick

-neutrophils are a component of the white cell line
-ANC: absolute neutrophil count
-neutropenia: too little neurophils or less than 1000
-reverse isolation when less than 500
-has to have mask going out of the room to protect them from us, - not enough neutrophils in the body to protect themselves, nothing inside to fight illnesses
