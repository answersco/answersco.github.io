---
title: "Ependymoma vs Medulloblastoma imaging findings?"
---
&quot;MR EF&quot;

Ependymoma = Floor of 4th ventricle; &quot;toothpaste&quot; tumor that squeezes out into basal cisterns via Luschka and Magendie; Heterogeneous enhancement and more likely to calcify (can drop met)

Medulloblastoma = More common; VERMIS or ROOF; &quot;Midline Ball-LIke&quot; and CAN RESTRICT!
- More homogeneous enhancement
- &quot;Zuckerguss&quot; = icing like enhancement of brain surface 2/2 drop mets
