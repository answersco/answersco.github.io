---
title: "What are some situations in which identifiable data can be transmitted without individual authorization?"
---
To the individual at his or her request.
In the course of treatment.
For payment activities and to healthcare operations.
Involving quality or competency assurance.
Fraud or abuse detection or compliance activities.
When required by law, information can be released to public health authorities; during investigation of abuse, neglect, or domestic violence; to oversight agencies; for judicial and administrative proceedings; for law enforcement purposes; and for worker's compensation.
