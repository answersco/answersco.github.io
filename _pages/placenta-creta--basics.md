---
title: "Placenta Creta (basics)"
---
This is when the placenta is a CREATURE, i.e., it is invading the myometrium

Risk factors: prior C-section, placenta previa, advanced maternal age 

US: generallys peaking you are looking fora MOTH-EATEN or SWISS CHEESE appearance of the placenta w/ vascular channels extending from the placenta into the myometrium
