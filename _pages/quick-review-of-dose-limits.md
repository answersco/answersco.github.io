---
title: "Quick Review of dose limits!"
---
Annual occupational dose limits are: 
- 5 rem (50 mSv) Total effective dose equivalent
- 50 rem (0.5 Sv) to any organ or tissue
- 15 rem (150 mSv) to the eye

Public dose limits are 
- 0.1 rem (1 mSv) annually or 2 mrem (20 &#956;Sv) per hour

The fetal dose limit is 0.5 rem (5 mSv) after the pregnancy is declared (or 0.5 mSv per month).
