---
title: "Stages of meiosis"
---
-consists of two rounds of cell division
-produces haploid cells
-each round of division contains prophase, metaphase, anaphase, and telophase
1) starts at 4n with 46 chromosomes
2) after meiosis I, it goes to 2n with 2 cells 23 chromosomes each
3) after meiosis II, it goes to 1n with 4 cells each having 23 chromosomes
