---
title: "Insomnia"
---
-chronic difficulty falling asleep or frequent awakenings
-most common sleep disorder
-consistently underestimate how much sleep they are getting
-chronic: experienced longer than 1 month

-a nonspecific sleep disorder
-inability to fall asleep, waking in the night, or waking up too early
-arousal like anxiety, stress, or excitement can prevent sleep
-medication can be used but is known to make insomnia worse when treatment stopped
-meditation is used to minimize anxiety
-melatonin helps to reset body's internal clock
