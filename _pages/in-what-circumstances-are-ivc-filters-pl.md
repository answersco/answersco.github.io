---
title: "In what circumstances are IVC filters placed in non-standard position?"
---
IVC filter usually placed infrarenally (due to increaed risk of renal vein thrombosis if suprarenal)

Pregnancy: placed suprarenal to avoid compression

Clot in renal or gonadal vein: placed suprarenal, above clot

Duplicated IVC: either bilateral iliac veins or suprarenal (above the bifurcation)

Circumaortic left renal vein: placed below the lower renal vein
