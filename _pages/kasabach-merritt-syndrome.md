---
title: "Kasabach-Merritt syndrome"
---
sequestration of thrombocytes in hemangioma causing thrombocytopenia

Sequestration of platelets within a large hemangioma.

Aka &quot;hemangioma with thrombocytopenia&quot;- anemia, thrombocytopenia and consumptive coagulopathy, rare disease, usually of infants, vascular tumor leads to decreased platelet counts and sometimes other bleeding problems, splenic hemangiomas are common
