---
title: "Hypoglycemia and symptoms"
---
-cold, clammy, shaky, dizziness hangry, if extreme then pt will be unconscious
-these are important bc pt is treated based on symptoms
-drug are usually standing orders for any pt with insulin ordered
1) for pt A&amp;O &amp; can swallow: 4 oz juice = 15g carbs, then check blood sugar in 15 min
-if pt is okay after checking, then give pt protein to maintain glucose level 
2) for unconscious pt: IM glucagon or D50 in water (50% dextrose solution) = 25mL IV
3) for minimally conscious: gelpacks - instaglucose - squirt into buccal or sublingual
