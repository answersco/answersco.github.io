title: "Which of the following is caused by ventricular contraction? A) blood flows into the ventricle B) blood flows out of atria C) the atrioventricular valves close, and then the semilunar valves open D) the semilunar valves close, and then the atrioventricular valves open E) Pressure becomes greater in the aorta and pulmonary trunk as compared to inside the ventricles"
