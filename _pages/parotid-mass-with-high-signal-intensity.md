---
title: "Parotid mass with high signal intensity on T2:"
---
Benign mixed tumor (BMT)/pleomorphic adenoma

CT findings
Smoothly marginated, homogeneously enhancing, ovoid mass
Pear-shaped when in deep lobe &amp; extending through stylomandibular notch
Dystrophic calcification may be present

MR findings
&#8593; T1 signal in hemorrhagic lesions
Very high T2 signal specific for BMT
ADC values higher than other parotid tumors
