---
title: "Nursing management for chest tubes"
---
1) Know purpose of tube for patient - is it to drain air or drainage purposes
-If for air, do not clamp it off - chest will expand, air pressure will increase, and pneumothorax will happen

2) Assess and maintain patency 
-DO NOT rely on bubbling 

3) Know how to use / read drainage system

4) Caution before clamping
-May be &quot;vent&quot; for thoracic cavity

5) Assess and manage pain 

6) Complications:
-Atelectasis
-Infection
-Reaccumulation of air in pleural space
