---
title: "What is Scimitar Syndrome?"
---
Hypoplastic right lung with anomalous right pulmonary venous connection to IVC. Often has a systemic arterial supply as well.

Hypoplastic right lung with partial anomalous pulmonary venous return and systemic arterial supply to RLL = congenital pulmonary venolobar syndrome

- associated with absent right PA and anomalous systemic arterial supply to the right lung (Shunt)

- Tx: arterial emoblization or baffling (surgically constructed wall or tunnel) of the common right pulmonary vein to the RA
