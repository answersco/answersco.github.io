---
title: "HMG-CoA reductase inhibitors/statins action"
---
-interfere with how lipids and lipoproteins are metabolized/formed in the liver
-usually have to have LFTs (liver function tests) done monthly - pt needs to be aware of this
-on lab work there will be, increased HDL, decreased LDL and total cholesterol VLDL and triglycerides
