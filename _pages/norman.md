title: "Norman"
---
battered-person syndrome case in which the court rules b/c husband was asleep, threat could NOT have been imminent, barring self-defense; defendant's subjective belief of what might happen in the future &#8800; what she believed would be IMMINENT; not a reasonable fear of imminent death or great bodily harm at time of killing; convicted and affirmed voluntary manslaughter; dissent notes that defendant was living in a constant state of imminent threat
