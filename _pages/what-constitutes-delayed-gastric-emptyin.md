---
title: "What constitutes delayed gastric emptying at 1, 2, 3, and 4 hours? What about rapid gastric emptying?"
---
1 hour: &gt; 90% remains
2 hours: &gt; 60%
3 hours: &gt; 30%
4 hours: &gt; 10%

Rapid means &lt;70% after 30 min and &lt; 30% remains after 1 hour
- rapid gastric emptying in Sprue, ZES, duodenal ulcer, pancreatic insuff
