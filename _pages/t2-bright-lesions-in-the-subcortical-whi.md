title: "T2 bright lesions in the subcortical white matter, sparing gray matter in an AIDS patient"
---
PML: non-enhancing white matter lesions lacking mass effect

- Toxo: usually central, ring enhancement, edema
- Lymphoma: ring enhancement, edema
- Cryptococcus: gelatinous pseudocysts, enhance, mass effect
- Infarction: would affect both gray and white matter
