---
title: "What is Iniencephaly?"
---
Iniencephaly is a rare neural tube defect resulting in:

- occipital bone defect
- partial or total absence (rachischisis) of cervico-thoracic vertebrae 
** fixed fetal head retroflexion &quot;star-gazer&quot; appearance
