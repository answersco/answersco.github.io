title: "Licensure"
---
-you have met the standards and are able to perform your job safely 
-NCLEX-RN (National Council of Licensure Examination)

-licensure is a legal act by the state to recognize that a licensed person is safe to practice.
-primary function is to protect public safety.
-establishes only minimal levels of competency.
-each state can establish its own criteria in its state practice act
-current movement toward national licensure
-enforced by regulatory boards
