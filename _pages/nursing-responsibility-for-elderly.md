---
title: "Nursing responsibility for elderly"
---
-know that elderly may present with different signs of drug interactions (delirium, agitation, incontinence, falls, depression, unusual fatigue, loss of appetite, weight loss)
-especially watch for use of controlled substances -- benzodiazepines and narcotics are usually not tolerated well in the elderly without significant side effects
-fall risk assessment includes meds
-it is not uncommon for these drugs to cause all of the above side effects
