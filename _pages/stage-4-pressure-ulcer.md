title: "Stage 4 pressure ulcer"
---
-full-thickness skin loss with extensive destruction, necrosis, or damage to muscle, bone, or other structures

-full-thickness skin loss with extensive destruction, tissue necrosis, black eschar, damage to the muscle, bone or supporting structures (tendons, etc)
-seen a lot in paralyzed pt's when they cannot feel pressure or pain
-takes a year or longer to heal with treatment
-lots of calories and appropriate nutrition, appropriate circulation
-may require surgery in order to promote healing
-surgery would include sharp debridement (goes in with a sharp medical tool and cuts away the dead skin)
-use clean technique bc open to environment and already a dirty wound
