---
title: "Where do left vs right testicular neoplasms go in terms of LNs?"
---
Left side --&gt; left para-aortic and pre-aortic nodes

Right side --&gt; paracaval, precaval, and aortocaval nodes

Iliac, pelvic, or inguinal nodes are considered M1 disease, unless prior scrotal or inguinal surgery, in which case it's considered N1 regional disease
