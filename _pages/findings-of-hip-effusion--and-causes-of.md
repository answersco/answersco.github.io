---
title: "Findings of hip effusion, and causes of hip effusion and limp in child:"
---
Hip effusion: plain films are not sensitive or reliable; US in modality of choice
Displacement or distortion of gluteal or psoas fat planes
Widening of teardrop distance (between lateral margin of pelvic teardrop and medial margin of femoral head).

Child with hip effusion and limp:
Aseptic toxic synovitis&#8594; self-limited, noninfectious diagnosis of exclusion
Hemarthrosis in trauma or hemophilia
Septic hip arthritis
