---
title: "NUMBERS: What effective doses are considered very low in radiology (and example scans)?"
---
&lt; 0.1 mSv

CXR, DEXA, Extremity XR

0.1 - 1 mSv

Most body XR (C-spine, screening mammo, KUB, Spine XR)

1-10 mSv

Most GI and CT (upper GI, barium enema, head/chest/abd and pelvic CT)

&gt; 10 mSv

IR, retrospective cardiac CT, Torso CT (10 mSv), TIPS (70!)
