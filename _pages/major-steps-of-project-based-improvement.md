---
title: "Major steps of project-based improvement methods?"
---
1. Identify a problem
2. Form a team
3. Assess the current performance (observation)
4. Develop ways to measure performance
5. Establish a specific goal (SMART)
6. Identify causes of the problem
7. Prioritize problem solving efforts (ID which problem causes occurs most and target efforts here)
8. Develop solutions through iterative testing
9. Sustaining The Improvement
