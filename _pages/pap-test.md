---
title: "PAP test"
---
-screens for cervical cancer have reduced many deaths
-how often women should have a pap test depends on their age and health history
-can find early cell changes and treat them before they become cancer and early when it is easy to treat
-age 21-65 -- every 3 years
-age 30 + -- add HPV (human papilloma virus) screening
-age 65 + -- no longer necessary
