---
title: "Thoracic outlet syndrome is most often due to?"
---
Compression of the Brachial plexus nerves (&gt; subclavian vein &gt; subclavian Artery) by the ANTERIOR SCALENE MUSCLE

- But also due to muscular hypertrophy, cervical rib, fibrous bands, tumors, etc
* Tx = SURGICAL REMOVAL of offending rib/muscle

- Classic: Occurs with arms up!
