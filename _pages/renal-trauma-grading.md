---
title: "Renal trauma grading:"
---
Renal trauma: 
OIS/AAST grading
Grade I: renal contusion or subcapsular hematoma
Grade II: superficial laceration (&lt; 1 cm) or confined perinephric hematoma without urinary extravasation.
Grade III: deeper laceration (&gt; 1 cm) without urinary extravasation.
Grade IV: deep laceration that extends into the collecting system (causing urinary extravasation), or injury to the renal artery or vein with contained hemorrhage. Urinary extravasation usually managed surgically to prevent urinoma or abscess.
Grade V: shattered kidney or avulsion or renal hilum.
