---
title: "Ideal body weight"
---
-for women, a waist size of 35 and for men, a waist size of 40 or higher is at risk for cardiovascular disease, HTN, diabetes
-important for setting the caloric needs for the pt in need of nutrition therapy
-if lost 2% of their weight in a week, 5% in a month, or 7.5% of weight in three months, then it is a concern
