---
title: "Reflex sympathetic dystrophy"
---
AKA complex regional pain syndrome- can be seen after stroke, trauma, or acute illness

Classically increased uptake on blood flow and blood pool, with periarticular uptake on delayed.
Uptake often involves entire extremity
1/3 don't show increased perfusion and uptake
Children may show decreased uptake

Reflex sympathetic dystrophy (RSD): can cause severe osteopenia
May look like unilateral RA with preserved joint spaces, commonly involving hand or shoulder
May occur after trauma or infection
3-phase hot on bone scan, with intra-articular uptake that is somewhat characteristic.
