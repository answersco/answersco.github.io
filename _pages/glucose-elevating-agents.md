---
title: "Glucose elevating agents"
---
-two agents: diazoxide and glucagon
-can also give glucose tablets and IV glucose
-if hypoglycemic, give them 15 grams of carbs then check 15 minutes after until normal range is reached then check 1 hour after
-dextrose IV is given when low blood sugar and unresponsive and recheck every 15 minutes until normal range is reached then check 1 hour after
