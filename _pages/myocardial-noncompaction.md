title: "Myocardial noncompaction?"
---
Developmental defect in the embryologic formation of the L ventricle, due to failure of part of L ventricle to form a solid myocardium

Myocardial noncompaction: developmental defect in embryologic formation of LV, due to failure of part of LV to form a solid myocardium.
&#8593; risk of adverse cardiac events, including arrhythmias, thrombus formation, stroke, and cardiomyopathy.
Imaging: LV appears as heavily trabeculated as the RV, with a relatively thin LV wall.
