title: "Findings in intracranial hypotension and HTN"
---
Intracranial hypotension: &#8595; CSF volume&#8594; compensatory &#8593; venous blood volume
Meningeal engorgement (enhancement)
Distension of dural venous sinuses
Prominence of intracranial vessels
Engorgement of pituitary

Idiopathic intracranial HTN (pseudotumor cerebri): fat middle-aged F with headache
&#8593; CSF&#8594; slit-like ventricles, partially empty sella, compressed venous sinuses
May also see vertical tortuosity of optic nerves and flattening of posterior sclera
Hydrocephalus
