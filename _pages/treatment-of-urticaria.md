---
title: "Treatment of urticaria?"
---
1. benadryl

2. Alpha agonist (to constrict the arterioles and venules)

3. Epinephrine SC 1:1,000
0.1-0.3 mL (0.1-3 mg)

Discontinue injection. Usually no treatment needed.
H-1 receptor blocker (diphenhydramine) PO/IM/IV 25-50 mg.
If severe or widely-disseminated, give alpha agonist (arteriolar and venous vasoconstriction) epinephrine SC (1:1000) 0.1-0.3 ml (=0.1-0.3 mg) if no cardiac contraindications.
