---
title: "When to pick coils vs PVA particles? (both permanent)"
---
1. Coils are good for small-medium arteries (don't use if need to re-access, e.g. in bronchial artery)

2. PVA (if &lt; 300 microns) can cause tissue necrosis at capillary level, good for multiple small arteries or capillaries &amp; if you don't want to jail yourself
