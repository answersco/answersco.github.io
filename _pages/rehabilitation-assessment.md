---
title: "Rehabilitation assessment"
---
1) Assess function, as well as biopsychosocial

2) Assess support system

3) Plans for discharge start on admission
-assess potential discharge plans (home, environment)

4) Assess actual and potential exacerbating factors 
-medical issues
-age
-ability to learn

5) Working with rehabilitation team

6) Recognize needs of patient/caregiver/family
-emotional/psychological/educative/financial/physical

7) Identify adaptive device, assistive device, and functional substitution

**Criteria for acute rehab: have to tolerate 3 hours or more of at least two therapy actions a day
