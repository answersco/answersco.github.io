---
title: "Nullification"
---
Refusal by a state to recognize or enforce a federal law within its borders.

the purported right of a state to declare void a federal law

a legal theory that a state in the U.S. has the right to invalidate any federal law that the state deems unconstitutional; John C. Calhoun was foremost proponent, leading a convention in South Carolina to declare both the tariffs of 1828 and 1832 unenforceable
