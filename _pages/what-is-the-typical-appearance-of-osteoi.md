---
title: "What is the typical appearance of osteoid osteoma of the spine?"
---
Sclerosis surrounding central nidus in the posterior elements of the spine (neural arch), with associated soft tissue mass. Focal scoliosis, concave on side of tumor. Appropriate hx of night pain relieved by NSAIDS. Bone scan 3 phase positive. Must be smaller than 1.5cm.
