---
title: "Pancreatic tumor: daughter tumor"
---
Solid and papillary epithelial neoplasm (SPEN): occurs in young women and children&#8594; daughter tumor
Low malignant potential&#8594; typically resected.
Imaging: appears as a large mass with heterogeneous solid and cystic areas
Hemorrhage is typical
Features a capsule (like mucinous cystic neoplasm)
