---
title: "What is the classic appearance of Dandy-Walker spectrum?"
---
Vermian hypoplasia/aplasia with classic posterior fossa cyst communicating with 4th ventricle.
&quot;Open umbrella&quot; or &quot;batwing&quot; 4th ventricle.
&quot;Molar tooth&quot; brainstem&quot;
Enlarged posterior fossa with superiroly displaced tentorium, torcula, and lateral sinuses above the lambdoid.
Hypoplastic cerebellar hemispheres winging anterolaterally.
Hydrocephalus.
