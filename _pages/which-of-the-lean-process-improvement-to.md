---
title: "Which of the Lean Process Improvement tools focuses on standardization of work areas?"
---
Five S

The goal of the Five S tool in the Lean system is the standardization of work areas and to simplify the manner in which work flows across the system. 
Value Stream Mapping helps understanding of the components and information flow within a process. 
Just-in-time refers to a process whereby excess inventory is kept to a minimum. 
Error-proofing is the testing of a process such that sources of error are identified immediately in a new process such that immediate problem solving and variation reduction is possible.
