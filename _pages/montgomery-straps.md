---
title: "Montgomery straps"
---
-anchoring pieces applied to intact skin adjacent to wound
-laces crisscross over wound to secure large or bulky dressing that need changed frequently
-helps to avoid skin irritation caused by frequent tape removal - might need skin barrier before you put them on
-used for large abdominal wounds with copious drainage 
-can stay for long times - as long as they are intact then they will stay
