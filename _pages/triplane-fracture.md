---
title: "Triplane fracture"
---
Salter harris 4 fracture:
1. Vertical component through the epiphysis
2. Horizontal component through the physis
3. Oblique component through the metaphysis (this is the component that distinguishes from a tillaux fracture)

Salter 4 with vertical epiphyseal component, horizontal physeal component, and oblique metaphyseal component
