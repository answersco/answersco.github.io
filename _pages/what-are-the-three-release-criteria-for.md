---
title: "What are the three release criteria for patients after I-131 tx?"
---
1) When no single member of the public is likely to receive more than 500 mrem or 5mSv.
2) If the rate is less than 0/.07 mSv or 7 mrem/hr measured at 1m. 
3) When the dose is &lt;33mCi I-131, ie for low dose administrations.
