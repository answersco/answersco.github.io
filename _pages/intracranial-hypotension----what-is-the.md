---
title: "Intracranial Hypotension: - what is the cause? - findings seen in intracranial hypotension (6)"
---
- leaking of CSF results in decrease in the overall fixed volume --&gt; venous blood will increase to maintain equilibrium

Findings:
- meningeal engorgement (enhancement)
- distension of dural venous sinuses
- prominence of intracranial vessels
- engorgement of pituitary
- subdural hematoma/hygromas
- sagging brainstem
