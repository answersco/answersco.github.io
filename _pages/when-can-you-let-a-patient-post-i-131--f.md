---
title: "When can you let a patient post I-131 (for either cancer or hyperthyroidism) go?"
---
Patient release is authorized by the NRC when a survey instrument at one meter reads
&#8804;7 mrem/h (0.07 mSv/h); or when the oral I-131 dose is &#8804;33 mCi (1.2 GBq); or when a calculation indicates effective doses to other persons (such as family and caregivers) will be &#8804;500 mrem (5 mSv)
