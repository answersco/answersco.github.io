---
title: "Henry of Navarre"
---
the Huguenot/Protestant leader and cousin to the king who found a compromise to end the wars of religion in France, in 1589 he became Henry IV and issued the Edict of Nantes protecting the minority Protestants while he himself converted to Catholicism (saying &quot;Paris is well worth a Mass&quot;) guaranteeing that France would remain a Catholic kingdom
