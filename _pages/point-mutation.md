---
title: "Point mutation"
---
-change (substituition) of a single nucleotide
-includes missense, nonsense, and splice site mutations
-transition = purine replaces purine (A to G or G to A) -or- pyrimidine replaces pyrimidine (C to T or T to C)
-transversion = purine replaces pyrimidine or pyrimidine replaces purine (A or G to T or C &amp; T or C to A or G)
