---
title: "Lasix renogram interpretation: how will the graph look? - no obstruction - indeterminate - obstructed"
---
No obstruction:
- tracer clears from collecting system without need for Lasix
- washout of 50% of tracer within 10 minutes of Lasix

Indeterminate:
- washout of 50% of tracer within 10-20 minutes of Lasix

Obstructed:
- washout taking longer than 20 minutes after Lasix
