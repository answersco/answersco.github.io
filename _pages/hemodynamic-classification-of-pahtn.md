---
title: "Hemodynamic classification of PAHTN:"
---
Classification:
Precapillary causes of pulmonary HTN: primary abnormality is either the PA system or PA blood flow. Abnormalities of pulmonary parenchymal leading to chronic alveolar hypoxia are included in this category.
Postcapillary causes: abnormality of pulmonary veins or elevation of pulmonary venous pressure leading to PAHTN.
