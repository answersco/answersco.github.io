Assessment of practice quality through FPPE and OPPE

Focused Professional Practice Evaluation (FPPE): 3 uses
1. For initial appointment to medical staff, it is process by which a current member of medical staff is assigned to evaluation your overall performance, typically for first 6 months.
2. For existing members desiring to add a new privilege, it is the process in which one's performance is assessed.
3. For existing members of medical staff about whom there is concern about the quality of their practice, it is used to evaluate.
