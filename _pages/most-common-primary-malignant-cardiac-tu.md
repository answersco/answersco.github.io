title: "Most common primary malignant cardiac tumor in adults"
---
Angiosarcoma: like the RA and tend to involve pericardium
Often cause right-sided heart failure and/or tamponade
Bulky and hetergeneous
Buzzwords: &quot;sun-ray&quot; appearance - describes the pattern of enhancement of the diffuse subtype as it grows along the perivascular spaces associated with the epicardial vessels.
