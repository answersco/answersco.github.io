---
title: "What is a pineal cyst? And imaging findings?"
---
A nonneoplastic intrapineal glial-lined cyst, which is a normal finding in nearly all cases. Common in all ages, adults&gt;peds.
Iso(40% to hyperintense (60%) to CSF, 90% have peripheral enhancement &lt;2mm and 25 % calcifications. No diffusion restriction. Size usually &lt;1cm, &gt;1-2cm are sx with hydrocephalus.
