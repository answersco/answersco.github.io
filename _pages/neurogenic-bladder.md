---
title: "Neurogenic bladder"
---
Detrusor muscle innervated by parasympathetic S2-S4 nerves
Spastic bladder: upper motor neuron
Atonic bladder: lower motor neuron

elongated and trabeculated- xmas tree cystogram
sacral anomolies, myelomeningiocele

a dysfunction of the urinary bladder due to disease of the central nervous system or peripheral nerves involved in the control of micturition (urination)
