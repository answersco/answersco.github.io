---
title: "How to remember Aggar Nassi vs Onodi vs Haller vs Concha bullosa?"
---
Aggar Nassi: most anterior ethmoidal air cells (anterolateral and inferior the frontoethmoidal recess) = A for Anterior = may obstruct frontal recess

Onodi cells: sphenoethmoidal cells : &quot;O No! The opposite of Agger Nassi&quot; (posterior most) = may be mistaken for sphenoid sinus (optic nerve at risk)

Haller cells: infraorbital ethmoidal air cells &quot;Heller is Inferior&quot; = may obstruct maxillary infundibulum

Concha bullosa: aerated middle turbinate = may cause septal deviation and narrowed maxillary infudnibulum if large
