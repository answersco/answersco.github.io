---
title: "Jaundice"
---
-yellow color of the skin
-can be seen as a hue in the skin or sclera of eyes
-may suggest liver disease or excessive breakdown of RBC (hemolysis)
-both cause an excess in bilirubin to where the liver is unable to process it all

-liver enzymes not functioning correctly, bilirubin not excreted in feces, stays in blood
-skin and sclera of eyes gets strong yellow tint
-gallstones blocking common bile duct are another cause
