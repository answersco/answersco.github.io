---
title: "Therapy patients who cannot be released"
---
Patient release
- Must remain confined until the dose to a member of the public will not exceed 0.5 rem.

Patients who cannot be released
- Private room with private bathroom (two treated patients can share a room)
- Sign posted on door including distance and time restrictions for visitors and personnel
- RSO notified if medical emergency or patient dies
- Disposable plates, etc
- Place disposable gloves as well as linens, etc in a special container
