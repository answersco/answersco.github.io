---
title: "Thrombolytic agents adverse effects"
---
-vascular collapse (streptokinase)
-hemorrhage (drug will dissolve clot wherever it might be!!! Can cause stroke, bleeding ulcer, etc.

bleeding risk if :
-recent surgery, delivery, biopsy
-cerebrovascular disease
-hypertension
-liver disease: liver helps with clotting so they might not have additional factors when liver is diseased
