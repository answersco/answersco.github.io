---
title: "What are typical MR findings in hepatic encephalopathy?"
---
Chronic:
Bilateral T1WI hyperintensity in basal ganglia (BG), particularly globus pallidus (GP)
Occasionally increased T2 signal in pituitary, hypothalamus, and mesencephalon.
Atrophy, especially cerebellum.

Acute: 
T1- blurring of grey-white junction
T2- high signal in most of cerebral cortex, sparing perirolandic and occipital regions.
