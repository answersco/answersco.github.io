---
title: "Indications for breast US and screening breast MRI:"
---
Indications for performing breast US
Further characterization of MM findings
Characterization of palpable abnormalities
First-line evaluation of a breast abnormality in patient &lt; 30
Pregnant or lactating female
IR guidance
Evaluation of breast implants

Indications for breast MRI
Screening in high-risk patients (&gt;20% lifetime risk)
Evaluation of disease extent
Evaluation of neoadjuvant chemo response
Assessment for residual disease after positive surgical margins
Evaluation for tumor recurrence
Evaluation for occult breast cancer in patient with axillary metastases.
