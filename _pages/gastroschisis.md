title: "Gastroschisis"
---
Prenatal ultrasound: AWD to right of umbilical cord with herniation of free-floating loops of bowel
Herniated viscera
Small bowel (almost always)
Less frequently: Large bowel, stomach, gonads
Typically no other solid abdominal organs

right/paraumbilical, w/o covering
usually isolated 
a/w younger mothers

No covering membrane, always on the right side, usually good outcome
