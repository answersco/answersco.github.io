---
title: "John Snow"
---
-Father of Epidemiology
-through epidemiologic research, he developed death rates as an objective measure to compare populations
-used mapping and natural experiment, comparing rates in groups exposed to different water supplies from two different water companies in London's Soho district
-discovered that foul water from a local pump was the vehicle for transmission of the agent that caused cholera by mapping cholera cases, plotting where cholera deaths were occurring, characterized where the epidemic started, peaked, and subsided.
