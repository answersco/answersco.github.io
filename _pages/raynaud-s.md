---
title: "Raynaud's"
---
Raynaud's disease:
-Young women, stimulated by col dor emotions
-Intermittent vasoconstriction of b/l digital arteries
-Partially responsive to vasodilators and rare ischemic changes

Raynaud's phenomenon
-Underlying condition, collagen vascular disease, thoracic outlet syndrome, endocrine d/o, malignancy, drug related
-Ischemic changes and ulcers more frequent

Role of angiography:
-Evaluate extent of stenoses and occlusion, exclude underlying condition like aneurysms in PAN, hypothenar hammer syndrome
-Assess response to vasodilator
