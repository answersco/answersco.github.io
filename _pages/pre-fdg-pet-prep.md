---
title: "Pre FDG PET prep"
---
Diet: 
want patient to fast for 4 hours prior (if not insulin spike will drive all FDG into the muscles).
If trying to reduce cardiac activity, can do 12 hr fast with low carb/high protein &amp; fat diet

Hydration:
Adequate hydration decreases bladder exposure

Muscle uptake:
Dont exercise for 24-48 hours before the test

Brown fat:
Can decrease brown fat uptake by keep room warm (remember brown fat helps you shiver) or can give drugs: diazepam (valium) or propranolol
