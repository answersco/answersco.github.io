---
title: "Gallium in sarcoidosis"
---
Increased uptake in lungs is 90% sensitive for active disease

Lambda sign: equivalent of 1-2-3 sign on CXR
-Increased uptake in both hila and right paratracheal node

Panda sign: uptake in nasopharyngeal region, parotids, and lacrimals.
-Can also be seen in Sjogren's and treated lymphoma.
