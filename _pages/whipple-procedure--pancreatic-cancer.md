---
title: "Whipple procedure (pancreatic cancer)"
---
What is removed: Pancreatic head, duodenum, gastric antrum, and gallbladder 

Complications:

Most common = delated gastric emptying AND pancreatic fistula (amylase through the surigcal drain &gt; 50mL for longer than 7-10 days)
Less common = wound infection
