---
title: "Ventrogluteal"
---
-preferred IM injection site
-put palm of hand on trochanter, thumb points in towards belly button, find the anterior posterior iliac spine and run the middle finger up along it to the iliac crest
-does not have major blood vessels or nerves or much fat relative to other sites
-a well defined muscle
-easiest to find spot when pt lays in bed on side and flexes knee
-holds 2.5-3 mL
