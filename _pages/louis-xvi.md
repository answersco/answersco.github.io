---
title: "Louis XVI"
---
French king whose monarchy was abolished by the creation of the French Revolution

King of France (r.1774-1792 CE). In 1789 he summoned the Estates-General, but he did not grant the reforms that were demanded and revolution followed. Louis and his queen, Marie Antoinette, were executed in 1793.

King of France who was overthrown with the French Revolution (killed by the guillotine) Wife was Marie Antoinette
