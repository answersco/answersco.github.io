---
title: "Impingement/rotator cuff tears: primary external causes (abnormal coracoacromial arch)"
---
External impingement: abnormal coracoacromial arch (made up of coracoid process, acromion, and coracoacromial ligament)

Hooked acromion (type III bigliani): more associated with external impingement than curved or flat types

Subacromial osteophytes or thickening of coracoacromial ligament

Subcoracoid impingement: impingement of subscapularis between coracoid process and lesser tuberosity&#8594; congenital or posttraumatic
