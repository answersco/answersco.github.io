---
title: "What are lactating adenomas and their management?"
---
- Lactating adenomas are well-differentiated benign breast tumor in pregnant, lactating, or postpartum women

- They are like fibroadenomas and mulitpile (f/u 4-6 mo post partum or after cessation of lactation) - will regress rapidly after stop lactation

- BUT * Must CORE BIOPSY a lactating adenoma b/c can't be distinguished from carcinoma
