---
title: "Guidance and discipline"
---
-parental teaching is key
-be consistent
-simple explanations
-avoid spanking
-use of time-out
-behavior modifications
-encourage parents to use age and developmentally appropriate discipline

1) parent's trying to promote autonomy
2) temper tantrums: range from whiny to crying to screaming and thrashing
-mostly happens when they are tired or hungry or cannot express what they are feeling so they get very frustrated
-avoid whenever possible
3) negative impact of physical punishment
-no spanking, timeout works the best
4) promoting appropriate discipline
-need firm, gentle guidance
