---
title: "Murmurs"
---
-occur when there are cardiac valve problems due to turbulent blood flow
-can be caused by stenosis and valve regurgitation
-majority are asymptomatic
-difficult to hear if not severe
-when found, pt's are taught to notify physician when they have signs and symptoms such as chest pain and SOB
-can be considered something normal
-children and young adults can have asymptomatic heart murmurs and can grow out of it as they become an adult
-can be severe enough where a valve replacement might be necessary
