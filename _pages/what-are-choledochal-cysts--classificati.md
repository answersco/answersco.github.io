---
title: "What are choledochal cysts? Classification?"
---
Type I: fusiform dilatation of CBD; most common &gt;75-95% of cases.
Type II CBD diverticulum.
Type III CBD choledochocele within duodenal wall.
Type IV multiple small CBD cysts (type IVb; if also intrahepatic then type IVa)
Type V is intrahepatic aka Caroli dz.
