---
title: "Malignant hyperthermia"
---
1) symptoms:
-genetic
-jaw muscle rigidity (one of first signs)
-ventricular dysrhythmia
-tachypnea
-cyanosis
-unstable BP
-increase in body temp (later sign)
-can be life threatening

-triggered by exposure of medication used for general anesthesia
-skeletal muscle begins to break down faster just bc this medication was administered
-this overwhelms the body's ability to manage this breakdown, cannot supply oxygen or regulate CO2 enough
