---
title: "Normal and abnormal T1 signal in the vertebral body:"
---
Marrow should always be more hyperintense on T1-weighted images than the intervertebral discs.
Focal &#8595; in intensity on T1 is worrisome for metastasis.
Diffuse &#8595; in intensity on T1 is abnormal, but nonspecific, with differential including leukemia, lymphoma, myelofibrosis, HIV, and idiopathic causes.
