---
title: "Neuroblastoma: associations, and staging factors (what upstages, and what signifies better prognosis)"
---
Neuroblastoma: younger than Wilms (can be born with it)
Crossing the midline and contralateral positive nodes upstage to Stage 3.
Better prognosis: dx in age &lt; 1, thoracic primary, Stage 4S.
Stage 4S: &lt; 1 year, mets confined to skin, liver, and bone marrow (not cortical bone) - excellent prognosis

Associations: NF-1, Hirschprung's, DiGeorge, Beckwith-Wiedemann.
Most are sporadic.
