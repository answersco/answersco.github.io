---
title: "Pseuodmyxoma peritonei:"
---
Pseudomyxoma peritonei: low-grade malignancy that leads to recurrent mucinous ascites
Associated with mucin-producing adenoma or adenocarcinoma of the appendix
Tumor deposits tend to be spread throughout the entire peritoneal cavity
CT shows lobular ascites that is typically of slightly higher attenuation (5-20 HU) than fluid ascites, occasionally with mucus in the region of the appendix
Advanced disease: scalloping of hepatic margin
