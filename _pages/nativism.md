---
title: "Nativism"
---
The belief that old stock values and social patterns were preferable to those of immigrants. Nativists believed that immigrants were a threat to their American traditions.

A belief that placed the interests of native-born individuals over the interests of immigrants, as advocated by the Know-Nothing Party.

the belief that favored the interests of native-born people over the interests of immigrants; first political party was the Know-Nothings
