---
title: "Norethindrone acetate"
---
-available as a pill that they take daily or a vaginal ring that needs to be changed every 3 months
-if your patient is taking the daily pill they need to be aware that they need to take the medication everyday at the same time, if they miss a dose they can take both pills that same day
-if they are on antibiotics than they need to also use an alternative form of contraceptive because their birth control pill will not be as effective.
