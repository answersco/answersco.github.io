---
title: "What occurs in transtentorial herniation (descending)?"
---
Uncus and hippocampus herniate through the tentorial incisura.

1. Duret hemorrhages from compressed basilar artery branches (at midline pontomesencephalic junction)

2. Ipsilateral pupil dilatation and ptosis 2/2 CN III compression between SCA &amp; PCA

3. Kernohan's Notch phenomenon = ipsilateral hemiparesis 2/2 indentation of the contralateral cerebral crus by the tentorium cerebelli.
